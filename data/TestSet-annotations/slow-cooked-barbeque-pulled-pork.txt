Place pork in a slow cooker.
Add molasses, chili powder, salt, paprika, cumin seeds, and ground coriander.
Pour enough water into slow cooker to cover pork.
Cook on High for 6 to 8 hours.
Drain liquid from slow cooker and shred pork with 2 forks.
Add barbeque sauce.
Reduce heat the Low and continue cooking for 1 hour more.
