In a large bowl, combine 1 1/2 cups flour, 3 tablespoons sugar and salt.
Cut in butter until mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
Mix yolk with water and stir in until mixture forms a ball.
Wrap in plastic and refrigerate for 1 hour or overnight.
Roll out and place in a 9 inch pie plate.
Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C.)
Place pecans in the bottom of pie crust.
In a large bowl, Stir together 3/4 cup of sugar and flour.
Mix in the eggs, corn syrup, milk, melted butter and vanilla.
Blend well and pour over pecans in crust.
Bake in the preheated oven for 10 minutes.
Reduce temperature to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) and bake for 40 to 50 minutes, or until golden brown and filling is set.
