Place bacon in a large, deep skillet.
Cook over medium high heat until evenly brown.
Crumble and set aside.
In a preheated skillet, add the chicken, seasoning mix and oil.
Cook until chicken is golden brown.
Remove from heat and set aside.
In a salad bowl, combine Romaine, enough salad dressing to coat, Parmesan cheese and bacon.
Toss and place on individual salad plates.
Top with the sliced chicken and serve.
