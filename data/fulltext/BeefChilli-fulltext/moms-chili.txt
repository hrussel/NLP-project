moms-chili

Steps

In a large saucepan over medium-high heat, cook beef and onion until meat is
brown. Stir in beans, diced tomatoes, chili seasoning, salt, pepper, chili
powder and water. Reduce heat and simmer 2 hours.

Ingredients

1 pound ground beef

1 large onion, chopped

1 (15 ounce) can ranch-style beans

1 (10 ounce) can diced tomatoes with green chile peppers

1 (1.25 ounce) package chili seasoning mix

salt and pepper to taste

2 teaspoons chili powder, or to taste

1 cup water, or as needed
