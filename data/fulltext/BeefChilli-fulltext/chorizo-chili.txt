chorizo-chili

Steps

Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat; cook and stir onion and garlic
in hot oil until tender, about 5 minutes. Stir ground beef and chorizo sausage
into onion mixture; cook, breaking meat apart while stirring, until no longer
pink, 10 to 15 minutes.

Mix ground beef-chorizo mixture, diced tomatoes, chili, butter beans, tomato
sauce, chili powder, chipotle seasoning blend, salt, and black pepper in a
slow cooker.

Cook on High until flavors have blended, 3 to 4 hours.

Ingredients

2 teaspoons olive oil, or as needed

1/4 cup chopped onion

2 cloves garlic, minced

1 pound ground beef

12 ounces chorizo sausage

1 (14 ounce) can diced tomatoes

1 (14 ounce) can prepared chili

1 (14 ounce) can butter beans

3/4 cup tomato sauce

1 tablespoon chili powder

1 tablespoon salt-free seasoning blend (such as Mrs. DashÂ® Chipotle seasoning)

salt and ground black pepper to taste
