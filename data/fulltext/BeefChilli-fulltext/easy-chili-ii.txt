easy-chili-ii

Steps

In a medium saucepan over medium heat, cook beef until brown. Stir in beans,
tomatoes, onion and bell pepper; reduce heat and simmer 30 minutes.

Ingredients

1 pound ground beef

1 (16 ounce) can chili beans, undrained

1 (14.5 ounce) can peeled and diced tomatoes with juice

1 small onion, chopped

1/4 cup chopped green bell pepper
