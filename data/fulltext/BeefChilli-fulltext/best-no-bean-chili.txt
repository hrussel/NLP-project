best-no-bean-chili

Steps

Pour coffee into a slow cooker and set slow cooker to High; add diced
tomatoes, beer, tomato paste, beef broth, brown sugar, chili sauce, jalapeno
peppers, cumin, cocoa powder, chile powder, oregano, cayenne pepper,
coriander, and salt.

Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Cook and stir ground beef,
onions, and garlic in the skillet until the beef is browned completely, 7 to
10 minutes; add to mixture in the slow cooker.

Slowly pour cornmeal into the slow cooker while stirring to assure no lumps.

Reduce heat to Low and cook for 2 hours.
