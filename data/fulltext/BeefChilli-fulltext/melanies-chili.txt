melanies-chili

Steps

In a large saucepan over medium-high heat, cook beef and onion until meat is
no longer pink. Stir in tomatoes, tomato sauce, beans, chili powder, cumin,
cayenne and garlic. Cover, reduce heat and simmer 20 minutes.

Ingredients

1 pound ground beef

1 onion, chopped

1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes

1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce

1 (16 ounce) can pinto beans

1 teaspoon chili powder

1 teaspoon ground cumin

1/2 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper

1 teaspoon minced garlic
