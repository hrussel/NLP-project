cuddle-up-chili

Steps

Heat a large pot over medium-high heat. Cook and stir beef, onion, and garlic
in the hot pot until ground beef is browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes; drain
and discard grease. Mix chili powder, cumin, onion powder, cayenne pepper,
salt, and black pepper into ground beef.

Stir tomatoes, hot chili beans, chili beans, tomato sauce, green chile
peppers, jalapeno peppers, lime juice, and hot sauce into ground beef mixture;
bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer until flavors blend, at least 30
minutes.

Ingredients

1 pound ground beef

1/4 onion, chopped, or to taste

1 tablespoon chopped garlic

2 tablespoons chili powder

1 tablespoon ground cumin

1 teaspoon onion powder

1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper

salt and ground black pepper to taste

1 (28 ounce) can diced tomatoes

1 (16 ounce) can hot chili beans

1 (16 ounce) can chili beans

1 (16 ounce) can tomato sauce

1 (4 ounce) can diced green chile peppers

1 (4 ounce) can diced jalapeno peppers

1 lime, juiced

1 dash hot sauce, or to taste
