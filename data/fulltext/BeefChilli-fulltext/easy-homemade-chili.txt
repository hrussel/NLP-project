easy-homemade-chili

Steps

In a large saucepan over medium heat, combine the beef and onion and saute
until meat is browned and onion is tender. Add the stewed tomatoes with juice,
tomato sauce, beans and water.

Season with the chili powder, garlic powder, salt and ground black pepper to
taste. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low, cover and let simmer for 15
minutes.

Ingredients

1 pound ground beef

1 onion, chopped

1 (14.5 ounce) can stewed tomatoes

1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce

1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans

1 1/2 cups water

1 pinch chili powder

1 pinch garlic powder

salt and pepper to taste
