beefy-beef-chili

Steps

In a 6 quart stock pot over medium-high heat, brown the ground beef with the
onion until no longer pink. Drain grease from the pan, and stir in the chili
beans, tomato juice, and beef stew. Season with chili powder, salt, and pepper
to taste. Cook over medium heat until simmering. Serve hot.

Ingredients

2 pounds ground beef

1 large onion, chopped

2 (16 ounce) cans chili beans, drained

1 (64 fluid ounce) bottle tomato juice

1 (40 ounce) can prepared beef stew

1 tablespoon chili powder, or to taste

salt and pepper to taste
