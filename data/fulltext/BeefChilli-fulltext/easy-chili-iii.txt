easy-chili-iii

Steps

In a large pot over medium heat, cook beef, onion and garlic until meat is
brown. Stir in black beans, kidney beans, pinto beans, tomatoes, tomato paste
and tomato sauce. Season with chili powder, salt, pepper and pepper sauce.
Reduce heat, cover and simmer 2 to 3 hours, until beans are tender.

Ingredients

3 pounds ground beef

1 large onion, chopped

1 medium head garlic, peeled and chopped

1 cup dry black beans

1 cup dry kidney beans

1 cup dry pinto beans

2 (28 ounce) cans diced tomatoes, drained

3 cups tomato paste

1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce

2 tablespoons chili powder, or to taste

1 teaspoon hot pepper sauce, or to taste

salt and pepper to taste
