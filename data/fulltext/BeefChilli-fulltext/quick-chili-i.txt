quick-chili-i

Steps

In a large stock pot lightly brown ground beef, and drain if needed.

Add onion and garlic and cook until onion is translucent.

Add tomatoes, diced tomatoes with chili peppers, tomato sauce, water, kidney
beans, pinto beans, chili powder, cumin, sugar, salt, pepper and hot sauce.
Simmer for 30 minutes and then serve.

Ingredients

2 pounds ground beef

1 onion, finely diced

3 cloves garlic, minced

1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes

2 (14.5 ounce) cans Italian-style diced tomatoes

1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce

1 cup water

1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans

1 (15 ounce) can pinto beans

2 tablespoons chili powder

1 tablespoon ground cumin

2 tablespoons white sugar

1 tablespoon salt

1 teaspoon ground black pepper

1 tablespoon hot pepper sauce
