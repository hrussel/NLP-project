nicoles-accident-chili

Steps

In a skillet over medium heat, brown the ground beef and cook the onion until
tender. Drain grease.

In a pot, mix the beef and onion, beans, vegetable juice, tomato sauce, tomato
paste, and tomatoes. Season with salt, garlic powder, and pepper. Bring to a
boil, reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer 45 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Ingredients

1 pound ground beef

1 teaspoon onion, chopped

1 (16 ounce) can red kidney beans, drained

1 (46 fluid ounce) can canned vegetable juice

1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce

1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste

1 (14.5 ounce) can stewed tomatoes

2 teaspoons salt

garlic powder to taste

ground black pepper to taste
