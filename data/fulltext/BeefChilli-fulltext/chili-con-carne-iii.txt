chili-con-carne-iii

Steps

In a large skillet over medium heat, combine onion, garlic, and tomato paste.
Cover and cook for 5 minutes.

Stir in butter until melted. Stir in flour, oregano, cumin, chili powder,
tomatoes and beef. Cook uncovered for 8 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Stir in the beans and cook for another 4 minutes, stirring occasionally. Salt
and pepper to taste.

Ingredients

1 large onion, finely chopped

1 clove garlic, peeled and crushed

2 teaspoons tomato paste

2 tablespoons butter, cut into pieces

2 tablespoons all-purpose flour

1 teaspoon dried oregano, crushed

1/2 teaspoon ground cumin

1 1/2 teaspoons chili powder

1 (14.5 ounce) can whole peeled tomatoes with liquid, chopped

1 pound lean ground beef

1 (15.25 ounce) can kidney beans, drained and rinsed

salt to taste

ground black pepper to taste
