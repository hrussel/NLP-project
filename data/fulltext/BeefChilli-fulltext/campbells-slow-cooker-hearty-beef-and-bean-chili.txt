Steps

Cook the beef in a 12-inch skillet over medium-high heat until it's well
browned, stirring often. Pour off any fat.

Stir the beef, onion, garlic, soup, tomatoes, water, beans, chili powder and
cumin in a 3 1/2-quart slow cooker.

Cover and cook on LOW for 8 to 9 hours.*
