taco-soup-v

Steps

In a medium sized stock pot, brown ground beef in oil. Add undrained pinto
beans, undrained kidney beans, diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, onion, green bell
pepper, taco seasoning mix and ranch salad dressing mix. Bring to a boil and
then simmer for 10 to 15 minutes, or until heated through.

Ingredients

3/4 pound lean ground beef

1 teaspoon vegetable oil

2 (15 ounce) cans pinto beans

2 (15 ounce) cans kidney beans

2 (14.5 ounce) cans diced tomatoes

6 ounces tomato sauce

1 onion, finely diced

1 green bell pepper, chopped

1 (1.25 ounce) package taco seasoning mix

1 (1 ounce) package ranch dressing mix
