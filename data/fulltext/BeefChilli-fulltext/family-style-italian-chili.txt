family-style-italian-chili

Steps

Brown meat in large skillet; drain. Add pepper and onions; cook 5 min.,
stirring frequently.

Add beans, tomatoes, seasonings and 1/4 cup cheese; stir. Simmer 1 hour,
stirring occasionally.

Served topped with remaining cheese.
