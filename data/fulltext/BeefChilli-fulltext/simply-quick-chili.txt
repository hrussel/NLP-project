simply-quick-chili

Steps

Heat a large pot over medium-high heat. Cook and stir beef and sausage in the
hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 7 to 10 minutes; drain and discard
grease.

Stir corn, kidney beans, and chili seasoning mix into the beef and sausage.
Reduce heat to medium-low and cook at a simmer until hot, 20 to 30 minutes.

Ingredients

1 pound ground beef

1 pound ground sausage

2 (15.25 ounce) cans whole kernel corn, drained

2 (15 ounce) cans kidney beans, rinsed and drained

2 (1.25 ounce) packages chili seasoning mix
