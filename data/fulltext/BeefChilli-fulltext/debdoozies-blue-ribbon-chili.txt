debdoozies-blue-ribbon-chili

Steps

In a large saucepan over medium heat, combine the ground beef and the onion
and saute for 10 minutes, or until meat is browned and onion is tender. Drain
grease, if desired.

Add the ground black pepper, garlic salt, tomato sauce, salsa, chili seasoning
mix and kidney beans. Mix well, reduce heat to low and simmer for at least an
hour.

Ingredients

2 pounds ground beef

1/2 onion, chopped

1 teaspoon ground black pepper

1/2 teaspoon garlic salt

2 1/2 cups tomato sauce

1 (8 ounce) jar salsa

4 tablespoons chili seasoning mix

1 (15 ounce) can light red kidney beans

1 (15 ounce) can dark red kidney beans
