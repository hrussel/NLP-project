chili-soup

Steps

In a large pot over medium heat, combine the ground beef and onions. Saute for
5 minutes, or until meat is browned. Drain excess fat and add the potatoes,
beans, tomato soup, tomato juice, chili powder, water and salt to taste. Bring
just to a boil and reduce heat to low. Simmer for 1 hour and serve.

Ingredients

3 pounds ground beef

1 1/2 cups chopped onion

2 potatoes, cubed

4 (15 ounce) cans kidney beans

4 (10.75 ounce) cans condensed tomato soup

8 cups tomato juice

3 teaspoons chili powder

8 cups water

salt to taste
