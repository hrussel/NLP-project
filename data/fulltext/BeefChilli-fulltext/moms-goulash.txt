moms-goulash

Steps

Season beef with salt, pepper, and paprika.

Brown seasoned beef and onion in oil until all sides are brown. Cover with
water and simmer for 3 to 4 hours or until tender. Add water whenever level
goes below the ingredients.

An hour before serving, add potatoes and chili.

Ingredients

2 pounds beef stew meat, diced into 1 inch pieces

salt and pepper to taste

1/4 teaspoon paprika

1 onion, chopped

1 tablespoon vegetable oil

8 large potatoes, scrubbed and chopped

1 (16 ounce) can chili without beans
