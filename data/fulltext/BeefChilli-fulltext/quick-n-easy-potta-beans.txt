quick-n-easy-potta-beans

Steps

Brown the ground beef in a large skillet over medium heat. Drain any excess
fat and add the soup, beans and tomatoes and green chilies. Reduce heat to
low, cover and simmer for 15 to 20 minutes.

Ingredients

1 pound ground beef

2 (10.75 ounce) cans condensed minestrone soup

1 (15 ounce) can ranch-style beans

1 (10 ounce) can diced tomatoes with green chile peppers
