taco-soup-i

Steps

In a large saucepan over medium heat, cook ground beef and onion until meat is
evenly brown; drain excess fat. Mix in water, tomatoes, kidney beans, tomato
sauce, and taco seasoning mix. Cover, and simmer for 15 minutes. Remove from
heat, and stir in the avocado.

Ladle hot soup into serving bowls. Pass cheese, tortilla chips, and sour cream
to top each serving.

Ingredients

1/2 pound lean ground beef

1/4 cup chopped onion

1 1/2 cups water

1 (16 ounce) can chopped stewed tomatoes, with juice

1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans with liquid

1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce

2 tablespoons taco seasoning mix

1 avocado - peeled, pitted and diced

1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

1 (12 ounce) package corn tortilla chips

1 (8 ounce) container sour cream
