chicken-oneill

Steps

Marinate chicken in salad dressing to cover, for at least 1 hour.

Heat the chicken with Cajun blackening spice in olive oil. Add crushed garlic,
bell peppers, onion, mushrooms, wine and salt and pepper to taste. Saute until
tender. Add chopped plum tomatoes and chopped fresh parsley to taste. Serve.

Ingredients

1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cubed

1 cup Italian-style salad dressing

2 teaspoons Cajun seasoning

1/4 cup olive oil

5 cloves crushed garlic

1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced

1 green bell pepper, thinly sliced

1 onion, chopped

1/2 pound fresh mushrooms, sliced

1 cup white wine

salt and pepper to taste

6 roma (plum) tomatoes, chopped

1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
