grapes-and-rice-stir-fry

Steps

Heat the vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over medium high heat. Stir
in the grapes and chicken; cook and stir until the chicken is hot, and the
grapes are tender, about 3 minutes. Add the rice and chicken broth; continue
cooking until the rice is hot, about 2 minutes more.

Ingredients

1 tablespoon vegetable oil

1 cup sliced red grapes

1 cup cubed cooked chicken

2 cups cooked rice

1/4 cup chicken broth
