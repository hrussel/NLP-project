chicken-with-cashews

Steps

In a skillet over medium-high heat, mix butter or margarine and vegetable oil
until hot. Add chicken and saute, stirring often, until the chicken turns
white. Remove with a slotted spoon and set aside.

In the same skillet, saute onion and peppers. Add the cooked chicken and the
cashews and season with salt and pepper, curry powder and sesame oil. Saute
for another 2 minutes and serve.

Ingredients

4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into strips

3 tablespoons butter

3 tablespoons vegetable oil

1 onion, chopped

1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced

1 green bell pepper, sliced

1/2 cup cashew halves

salt and pepper to taste

1 teaspoon curry powder

4 tablespoons sesame oil
