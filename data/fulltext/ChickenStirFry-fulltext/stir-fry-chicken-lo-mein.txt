stir-fry-chicken-lo-mein

Steps

Prepare Grilled & Ready® Frozen Grilled Chicken Breast Strips according to
package directions.

Meanwhile, in a large skillet, cook and stir mushrooms in hot oil for 4
minutes. Add sweet pepper; cook and stir for 2 minutes. Add chicken and stir-
fry sauce; cook and stir until heated through. Serve over hot cooked linguine.

Ingredients

1 (22 ounce) package Tyson® Grilled and Ready® Fully Cooked Frozen Grilled
Chicken Breast Strips

2 cups sliced fresh mushrooms

2 tablespoons vegetable oil

2 cups frozen sweet pepper stir-fry

2/3 cup stir-fry sauce

1 pound linguine, prepared according to package directions
