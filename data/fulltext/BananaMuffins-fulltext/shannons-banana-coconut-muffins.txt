shannons-banana-coconut-muffins

Steps

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour 12 muffins
cups or line with paper liners.

Beat sugar and margarine together in a bowl using an electric mixer until
smooth and creamy; stir in eggs. Stir bananas into margarine mixture; stir in
vanilla extract.

Whisk flour, baking powder, and salt together in a bowl; stir into margarine
mixture until just combined. Fold oranges and coconut into batter. Spoon
batter into muffin tins.

Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted in the center of a
muffin comes out clean, 20 to 30 minutes.

Ingredients

1 cup white sugar

1/2 cup margarine

2 eggs, beaten

1 cup mashed bananas

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

2 cups all-purpose flour

1 teaspoon baking powder

1/2 teaspoon salt

1 (10 ounce) can mandarin oranges, drained and chopped

1/2 cup shredded coconut
