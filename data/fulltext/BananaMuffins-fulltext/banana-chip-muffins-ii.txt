banana-chip-muffins-ii

Steps

Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (205 degrees C). Coat a 12 cup muffin tin with
nonstick spray.

Whisk together flour, baking powder, soda, cinnamon, salt, and sugar. Mix in
egg whites, applesauce, and banana mash until just moistened. Stir in
chocolate chips. Divide batter into muffin cups.

Bake for 15 to 18 minutes.

Ingredients

1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

1 1/4 teaspoons baking powder

1/2 teaspoon baking soda

1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

1/8 teaspoon salt

2 egg whites

1 cup mashed bananas

3/4 cup white sugar

1/4 cup applesauce

1/2 cup semisweet chocolate chips
