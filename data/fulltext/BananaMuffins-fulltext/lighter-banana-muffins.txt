lighter-banana-muffins

Steps

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease or line 10
muffin cups.

In a medium bowl, combine eggs and bananas. In a separate bowl, mix together
flour, salt, sugar and baking soda. Stir banana mixture into flour mixture.
Fold in walnuts if desired. Pour batter into prepared muffin cups.

Bake in preheated oven for 20 to 25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted
into center of a muffin comes out clean.

Ingredients

2 eggs, beaten

3 very ripe bananas, mashed

2 cups all-purpose flour

1 teaspoon salt

3/4 cup white sugar

1 teaspoon baking soda
