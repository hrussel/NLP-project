carrot-cake-bars

Steps

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a 10x15 inch
jelly roll pan. Sift together the flour, baking soda, cinnamon and salt. Set
aside.

In a large bowl, beat sugar, oil and eggs until smooth. Mix in the flour
mixture. Stir in the baby food carrots.

Spread batter into a 10x15 inch jelly roll pan. Bake in the preheated oven for
20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes
out clean. Allow to cool.

Ingredients

2 cups white sugar

1 1/2 cups vegetable oil

4 eggs

2 cups all-purpose flour

2 teaspoons baking soda

2 teaspoons ground cinnamon

1 teaspoon salt

3 (4 ounce) jars carrot baby food
