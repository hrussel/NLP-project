jalapeno-garlic-onion-cheeseburgers

Steps

Preheat grill for high heat.

Mix jalapeno pepper, garlic, onion, and ground beef in a medium bowl. Form
into four patties.

Brush grill grate with oil. Grill hamburger patties 5 minutes per side, or
until well done. Top with pepperjack cheese, and serve on buns.

Ingredients

1 fresh jalapeno chile pepper, finely chopped

2 cloves garlic, minced

1 small onion, finely chopped

1 pound lean ground beef

4 slices pepperjack cheese

4 hamburger buns
