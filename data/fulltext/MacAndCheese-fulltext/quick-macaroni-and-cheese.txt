quick-macaroni-and-cheese

Steps

Preheat oven broiler. Grease a 1 1/2 quart microwave safe dish.

Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8
to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.

Microwave frozen mac and cheese according to package directions.

Stir together macaroni, mac and cheese, milk and pepper in prepared dish. Mix
well. Microwave on high 2 minutes, until heated through. Sprinkle with Cheddar
and Parmesan and place under oven broiler. Broil until cheese is melted and
beginning to brown, 2 to 5 minutes.

Ingredients

1 cup elbow macaroni

1/4 cup milk

1 (20 ounce) package frozen macaroni and cheese

1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper

1 1/2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

3 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
