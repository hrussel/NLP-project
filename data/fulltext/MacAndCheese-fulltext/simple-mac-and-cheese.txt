simple-mac-and-cheese

Steps

Cook pasta in a large pot of boiling salted water until al dente. Drain.

Mix together condensed soup and cheese. Using the empty soup can, measure 1
can of milk; mix into the soup mixture. Stir in cooked pasta. Dump into a
greased casserole dish, and cover.

Bake at 325 degrees F (165 degrees C) for 45 minutes.

Ingredients

1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup

1 1/4 cups milk

1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

1 1/2 cups macaroni
