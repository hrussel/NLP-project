mexican-mac-and-cheese

Steps

In a medium skillet over medium-high heat, cook beef with onion flakes until
beef is browned. Drain.

In a large saucepan, cook the macaroni and cheese according to package
directions. Stir in the meat and onion mixture, nacho cheese dip, salsa and
green chiles. Reduce heat and simmer 15 minutes, or until heated through.

Ingredients

1 1/2 pounds lean ground beef

2 tablespoons dried onion flakes

2 (7.25 ounce) packages dry macaroni and cheese

15 ounces nacho cheese dip

1 cup medium salsa

1 (7 ounce) can diced green chiles
