baked-macaroni-and-cheese-with-tomato

Steps

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Bring a large pot of lightly
salted water to a boil. Pour in pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al
dente; drain.

In large bowl, combine macaroni, soup, milk, cheese and 6 tablespoons butter.
Pour into 9x13 baking dish. Top with bread crumbs and dot with remaining
butter. Bake for 45 minutes or until golden brown and bubbly.

Ingredients

1 pound macaroni

1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed tomato soup

1 1/4 cups milk

3 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

8 tablespoons butter, divided

1/4 cup dry bread crumbs
