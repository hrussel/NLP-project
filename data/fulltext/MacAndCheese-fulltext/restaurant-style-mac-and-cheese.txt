restaurant-style-mac-and-cheese

Steps

Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8
to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.

Return drained pasta to the pot. Mix in processed cheese, Cheddar cheese, and
cream. Stir until cheeses melt. Sprinkle with salt.

Ingredients

1 1/2 cups macaroni

6 ounces processed cheese, shredded

1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

2 tablespoons heavy cream

salt to taste
