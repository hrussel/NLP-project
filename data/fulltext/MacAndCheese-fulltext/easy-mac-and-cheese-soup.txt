easy-mac-and-cheese-soup

Steps

Cook macaroni according to package directions; drain. Do not stir in the
sauce.

In a medium saucepan, combine broccoli, onion and water. Bring to a boil and
cook until broccoli is tender. Stir in macaroni, cheese mixture from package,
milk, soup and ham. Return to a boil briefly. Serve hot.

Ingredients

1 (14 ounce) package uncooked macaroni and cheese

1 cup chopped broccoli

1/2 cup chopped onion

1 cup water

2 1/2 cups milk

1 (11 ounce) can condensed cream of Cheddar cheese soup

1 cup cubed cooked ham
