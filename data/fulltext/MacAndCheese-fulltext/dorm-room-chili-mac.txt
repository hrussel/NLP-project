dorm-room-chili-mac

Steps

Cook macaroni and cheese in a saucepan on the stovetop according to package
directions.

Once macaroni and cheese is done, stir in chili, and cook over medium heat
until hot, about 2 minutes. Sprinkle with shredded cheese to serve.

Ingredients

1 (7.25 ounce) package macaroni and cheese mix

1 (14 ounce) can hot dog chili

1/4 cup shredded Cheddar cheese, or cheese of choice
