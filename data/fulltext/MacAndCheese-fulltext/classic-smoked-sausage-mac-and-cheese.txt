Steps

Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.

Cook pasta according to package directions; rinse and drain. Set aside.

Melt 1 tablespoon butter in large saucepan on medium heat. Cook and stir
sausage and onions 3 to 5 minutes or until onions are tender. Stir in Alfredo
sauce, and half and half. Bring to boiling, stirring constantly; reduce heat.
Simmer 1 minute.

Remove saucepan from heat. Stir in cheese and black pepper until cheese is
melted. Stir in pasta and sausage mixture.

Pour mixture into lightly sprayed 139-inch baking dish. Sprinkle evenly with
combined bread crumbs and 1/4 cup melted butter. Bake 20 to 25 minutes or
until crumbs are golden brown and pasta mixture is hot and bubbly.
