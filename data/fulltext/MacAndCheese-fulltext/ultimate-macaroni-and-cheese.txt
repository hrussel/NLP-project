ultimate-macaroni-and-cheese

Steps

Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Cook macaroni in large saucepan as directed on
package for al dente pasta. Rinse under cold water; drain well.

Melt butter in same saucepan on medium heat. Sprinkle with flour and
seasonings. Cook and stir 2 minutes or until well blended. Gradually stir in
milk until smooth. Stirring constantly, cook 3 minutes or until sauce starts
to thicken. Stir in cheese until melted and smooth. Remove from heat. Add
macaroni; toss gently to coat. Pour into greased 13x9-inch baking dish. Mix
panko and paprika; sprinkle evenly over top.

Bake 25 to 30 minutes or until bubbly and golden brown on top. Let stand 5
minutes before serving.
