easiest-mac-n-cheese-ever

Steps

Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook until
al dente, 8 to 10 minutes; drain.

Place a saucepan over medium-low heat. Combine the cheese food, milk, and
pepper in the saucepan; cook until the cheese has melted, stirring frequently.
Stir in the drained macaroni until evenly coated.

Ingredients

4 ounces elbow macaroni

4 ounces cubed processed cheese food

2 fluid ounces milk

1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
