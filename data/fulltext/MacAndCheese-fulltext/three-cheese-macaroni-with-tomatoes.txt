three-cheese-macaroni-with-tomatoes

Steps

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a medium baking
dish.

Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Place macaroni in the
pot, cook for 8 to 10 minutes, until al dente, and drain.

In the baking dish, mix the cooked macaroni, Colby-Jack cheese, Cheddar
cheese, white Cheddar cheese, and tomatoes with juice. Sprinkle bread crumbs
evenly over top.

Bake 30 minutes in the preheated oven, until bubbly and lightly brown.

Ingredients

2 pounds elbow macaroni

8 ounces Colby-Jack cheese, cubed

8 ounces Cheddar cheese, cubed

8 ounces white Cheddar cheese, cubed

2 (14 ounce) cans stewed tomatoes, undrained, crushed

1 cup dry bread crumbs
