bevs-mac-and-cheese

Steps

Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8
to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain and reserve.

In a microwave-safe bowl, combine milk, flour and salt and pepper to taste;
whisk or beat until smooth. Add butter and cheese; microwave on high for 5
minutes and whisk until smooth. Microwave for an additional 4 to 5 minutes and
whisk or beat until smooth and no lumps remain.

Add cooked pasta to mixture; stir and serve.

Ingredients

1 cup elbow macaroni

1 cup milk

3 tablespoons all-purpose flour

salt and pepper to taste

2 tablespoons butter

1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
