hot-chicken-salad-iv

Steps

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

In a large bowl blend together the chicken, celery, bell pepper, onion,
pimento, salt, lemon juice and mayonnaise. Spread mixture into the bottom of a
lightly greased 9x13 inch baking dish, sprinkle with cheese and top with
potato chip crumbs.

Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 25 to 30 minutes, or until cheese is
melted and casserole is bubbly.

Ingredients

2 cups chopped, cooked chicken meat

2 cups chopped celery

1 green bell pepper, chopped

1 onion, chopped

3 tablespoons pimento, chopped

1 teaspoon salt

2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice

1/2 cup mayonnaise

1 pound Cheddar cheese, shredded

3 cups crushed potato chips
