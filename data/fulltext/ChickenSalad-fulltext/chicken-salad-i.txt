chicken-salad-i

Steps

Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add potatoes; cook until tender
but still firm, about 15 minutes. Drain, cool and chop.

In a mixing bowl, combine the potatoes, chicken, peas and corn.

Add enough salad dressing to coat, mix well. Top with sliced tomatoes, cover
and refrigerate for 2 hours. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Ingredients

6 potatoes

1 1/2 pounds skinless, boneless, roasted chicken

1 (15 ounce) can peas, drained

1 (15 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained

1 (16 ounce) jar creamy salad dressing

3 large tomatoes, sliced

salt and pepper to taste
