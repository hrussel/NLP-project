cuban-grilled-chicken-salad

Steps

Toss the romaine with the onion, avocado, and peppers in a large bowl. Divide
among four salad plates. Top each salad with a mound of black beans, some
pineapple chunks, and the chopped chicken meat.

Whisk together the olive oil with the garlic, salt, and pepper. Drizzle this
dressing over each salad along with a little lime juice.

Ingredients

3 cups chopped romaine lettuce

1 small red onion, diced

1 (6 ounce) avocado, diced

1/2 cup red or yellow bell pepper, diced

3/4 cup canned black beans, drained

3/4 cup diced fresh or canned pineapple

2 cups cooked chicken meat, chopped

2 tablespoons olive oil

2 teaspoons minced garlic

salt and pepper to taste

4 teaspoons fresh lime juice
