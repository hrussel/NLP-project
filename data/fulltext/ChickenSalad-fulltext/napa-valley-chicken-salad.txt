napa-valley-chicken-salad

Steps

In a small bowl, combine the dressing and mustard and stir until well blended.

In a large bowl, combine the chicken, grapes, celery, pecans and green onion.
Add the dressing to the salad and toss until evenly coated. Chill covered for
two hours before serving.

Ingredients

1/2 cup Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Salad Dressing

1 teaspoon Dijon mustard

8 ounces cooked chicken, diced

1 cup seedless red grapes, halved

1 cup diced celery

1/2 cup chopped toasted pecans

2 green onions, white and green parts, finely chopped
