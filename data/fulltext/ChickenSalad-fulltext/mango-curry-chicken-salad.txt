mango-curry-chicken-salad

Steps

Grill chicken breasts, cut into small pieces and set aside. In a medium bowl,
blend yogurt and curry with a whisk and stir in chicken, mango, cranberries,
walnuts and mozzarella. Mix well and serve on lettuce leaves if desired.

Ingredients

2 1/2 cups (1/2-inch pieces) grilled skinless, boneless chicken breasts

3/4 cup plain nonfat yogurt

1 teaspoon curry powder

1/4 cup cubed mango

1 cup dried sweetened cranberries

1/4 cup walnuts, coarsely chopped

1/3 cup mozzarella cheese, cut into small cubes

Lettuce leaves
