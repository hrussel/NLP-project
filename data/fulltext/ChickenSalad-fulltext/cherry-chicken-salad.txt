cherry-chicken-salad

Steps

In a large bowl, combine the chicken, dried cherries, celery, nuts,
mayonnaise, milk, salt and pepper and apple if desired. Toss together well and
refrigerate until chilled. Serve on toasted cracked wheat bread or croissants.

Ingredients

3 cooked, boneless chicken breast halves, diced

1/3 cup dried cherries

1/3 cup diced celery

1/3 cup toasted, chopped pecans

1/3 cup low-fat mayonnaise

1 tablespoon buttermilk

1/2 teaspoon salt

1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

1/3 cup cubed apples
