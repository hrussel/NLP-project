creamy-chicken-salad

Steps

Whip cream to soft peaks. Chop almonds in food processor. Shred chicken or
chop finely.

In a large bowl combine the cream, almonds, chicken, mayonnaise, tarragon,
salt and pepper. Mix well and serve.

Ingredients

1/2 cup whipping cream

1/2 cup smoked almonds

4 grilled skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

1/2 cup mayonnaise

1 tablespoon minced fresh tarragon

salt and pepper to taste
