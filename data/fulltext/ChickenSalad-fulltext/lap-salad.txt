lap-salad

Steps

Combine chicken, nuts, onion, sugar, sesame seeds, noodles and their flavor
packet and cabbage in large bowl. In small bowl combine oil, salt, pepper and
vinegar. Pour oil mixture over noodle mixture. Let stand overnight in
refrigerator and serve.

Ingredients

2 boneless chicken breast halves, cooked and diced

2 ounces almonds

2 green onions, chopped

1 tablespoon white sugar

2 tablespoons sesame seeds

1 (3 ounce) package ramen noodles, crushed

1/2 medium head cabbage, shredded

1/2 cup vegetable oil

salt and pepper to taste

3 tablespoons distilled white vinegar
