great-chicken-salad

Steps

In a food processor, combine the chicken, mayonnaise, coleslaw dressing, lemon
juice and celery.

Blend until well mixed. Season with salt and pepper to taste and refrigerate
until chilled.

Ingredients

2 cooked, boneless chicken breast halves, chopped

1/4 cup mayonnaise

4 tablespoons cole slaw dressing

1/2 lemon, juiced

1 stalk celery, chopped

salt and pepper to taste
