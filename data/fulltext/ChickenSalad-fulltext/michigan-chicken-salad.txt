michigan-chicken-salad

Steps

Combine the chicken, celery, onion, and cherries in a bowl. Whisk together the
mayonnaise, mustard, and poultry seasoning in a separate bowl; pour over the
chicken mixture and toss to combine. Season with pepper. Chill at least 30
minutes before serving.

Ingredients

3 cups diced cooked chicken

1/2 cup diced celery

1/4 cup diced onion

1/4 cup chopped dried cherries

3/4 cup mayonnaise

3 tablespoons Dijon mustard

1/2 teaspoon poultry seasoning

ground black pepper to taste
