chicken-salad-with-toasted-almonds

Steps

In a large bowl, toss the chicken with the lemon juice. Cover and chill for 2
hours.

Mix the salad dressing, salt, pineapple, grapes, almonds, water chestnuts and
lettuce into the chicken until evenly combined. Chill until serving.
