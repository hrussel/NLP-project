bangin-buffalo-blue-cheese-chicken-salad

Steps

Mix chicken, blue cheese dressing, bacon, mayonnaise, pecans, onion, celery,
hot sauce, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, black pepper, salt, garlic,
parsley, and paprika in a large bowl; refrigerate for flavors to blend, at
least 1 hour.

Ingredients

2 cooked chicken breast halves, cubed

3/4 cup chunky blue cheese dressing

8 slices cooked bacon, crumbled

1/2 cup mayonnaise

1/2 cup chopped pecans

1/2 cup finely chopped onion

1 teaspoon salt

1 teaspoon minced garlic

1 teaspoon dried parsley

1/2 teaspoon paprika
