easy-chinese-chicken-salad

Steps

Toss chicken meat, cabbage, onions, ramen noodles, sesame seeds, and almonds
together in a large bowl. Beat olive oil, vinegar, sugar, salt, and pepper
together in a small bowl; drizzle over the salad and toss to coat.
