chicken-salad-avocado

Steps

Combine lettuce, chicken, black olives, green bell pepper, red bell pepper,
and red onion together in a bowl; cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate
until chilled, about 30 minutes.

Mix oil, red wine vinegar, lemon juice, sugar, salt, pepper, and garlic in a
container with lid; cover and shake until well combined. Refrigerate until
chilled, about 30 minutes.

Mix avocado into salad. Shake dressing and drizzle over salad; toss lightly.

Ingredients

4 cups green leafy lettuce, or to taste

1 1/2 cups thinly sliced cooked chicken breast

1 cup sliced black olives

1/2 cup thinly sliced green bell pepper

1/2 cup thinly sliced red bell pepper

1/4 cup diced red onion

Dressing:

1/3 cup vegetable oil

1/4 cup red wine vinegar

1/4 cup fresh lemon juice

1 tablespoon white sugar

1 teaspoon salt

1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

1/8 teaspoon chopped garlic, or more to taste

1 avocado - peeled, pitted, and thinly sliced
