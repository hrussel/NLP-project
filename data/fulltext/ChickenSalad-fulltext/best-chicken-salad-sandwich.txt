best-chicken-salad-sandwich

Steps

Mix mayonnaise, yogurt, paprika, and seasoned salt together in a bowl; fold in
cranberries, celery, apple, grapes, and pecans. Stir in chicken and season
with black pepper.

Ingredients

1/2 cup light mayonnaise

1/2 cup low-fat plain yogurt

1 teaspoon paprika

1 teaspoon seasoned salt

1 1/2 cups dried cranberries

1 cup chopped celery

1 cup cubed green apple

1 cup sliced red grapes

1 cup chopped pecans

4 cups drained canned chicken

ground black pepper to taste
