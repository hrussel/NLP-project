angels-chunky-chicken-salad

Steps

Combine the chicken, celery, mayonnaise, onion and pickle; mix well.

Season with the garlic powder, salt and pepper. Serve with lettuce on fresh
crusty bread or bun.

Ingredients

1 cooked, boneless chicken breast half, chopped

2 stalks celery, chopped

3 tablespoons mayonnaise

1/2 onion, finely diced

2 dill pickles, chopped

1/4 teaspoon garlic powder

salt and pepper to taste
