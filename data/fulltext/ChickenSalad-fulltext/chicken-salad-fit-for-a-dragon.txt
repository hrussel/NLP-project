chicken-salad-fit-for-a-dragon

Steps

Stir the chicken, mayonnaise, celery, pine nuts, lemon juice, relish, jalapeno
pepper, and black pepper together in a mixing bowl until evenly blended.
Cover, and refrigerate 20 to 30 minutes to allow the flavors to blend.

Ingredients

2 cups cooked chicken, diced

1/2 cup mayonnaise

1 large stalk celery, diced

1/2 cup pine nuts

1 tablespoon lemon juice

1 1/2 tablespoons sweet pickle relish

1 small jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced

1/4 teaspoon cracked black pepper, or to taste
