lemon-chicken-salad

Steps

In a large bowl, whisk together the salad dressing, sour cream, lemon juice,
lemon pepper, basil and parsley. Add chicken, peas, onion, lettuce and almonds
and stir until evenly coated. Refrigerate until serving.
