chicken-and-vegetable-pasta-salad

Steps

In a large pot of salted boiling water, cook pasta until al dente, rinse under
cold water and drain.

In a large bowl, combine the pasta, chicken, green onions, bell pepper, olives
and cucumber.

Pour dressing over salad and toss to coat. Chill for 2 hours before serving;
sprinkle with sunflower seeds if desired.

Ingredients

1 cup seashell pasta

1 cup chopped, cooked chicken meat

3 green onions, chopped into 1 inch pieces

1 red bell pepper, chopped

1 cup sliced black olives

1 cucumber, peeled and chopped

2/3 cup Italian-style salad dressing

1/4 cup sunflower seeds
