spinach-and-chicken-salad

Steps

Place equal portions of spinach onto four salad plates. Arrange chicken,
zucchini, bell pepper, and black olives over spinach, and top with cheese.
Drizzle dressing over salad.

Ingredients

1 (10 ounce) bag fresh spinach, rinsed and dried

4 cooked skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, sliced

1 zucchini, halved lengthwise and sliced

1 red bell pepper, chopped

1/2 cup black olives

3 ounces fontina cheese, shredded

1/2 cup fat-free roasted garlic salad dressing
