citrus-tarragon-chicken-salad

Steps

Mix the chicken, celery, green onions, and tarragon together in a large bowl.
Stir in the lemon juice, lemon zest, orange zest, and mayonnaise. Season to
taste with salt and pepper. Refrigerate at least 30 minutes before serving.

Ingredients

5 cups chopped cooked chicken breast meat

1 celery stalk, chopped

4 green onions, chopped

2 tablespoons fresh tarragon, chopped

2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice

2 teaspoons lemon zest

2 teaspoons orange zest

1 cup mayonnaise

salt and pepper to taste
