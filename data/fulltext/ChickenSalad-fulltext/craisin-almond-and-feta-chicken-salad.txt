craisin-almond-and-feta-chicken-salad

Steps

Mix chicken, celery, red bell pepper, red onion, and cranberries in a large
bowl.

Stir mayonnaise and sour cream together in a separate bowl; pour over the
chicken mixture and stir to coat. Season mixture with salt and pepper.

Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate 8 hours to overnight.

Stir feta cheese and almonds through salad to serve.

Ingredients

3 cups diced cooked chicken

2 large stalks celery, diced

1 red bell pepper, seeded and diced

1/4 red onion, diced

6 tablespoons mayonnaise

6 tablespoons sour cream

1/3 cup toasted sliced almonds
