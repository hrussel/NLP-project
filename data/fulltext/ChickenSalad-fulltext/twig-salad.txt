twig-salad

Steps

In small bowl, whisk together the oil, sugar, vinegar, salt, seasoning blend,
and pepper. Set aside.

In a large bowl, combine the lettuce, chicken, chow mein noodles, almonds,
sesame seeds, green onions, and poppy seeds. Toss to blend. Pour salad
dressing over the salad just before serving.

Ingredients

1/2 cup vegetable oil

1/4 cup white sugar

1/4 cup white vinegar

1/2 teaspoon salt

1 teaspoon salt-free herb and spice blend

1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

1 1/2 heads iceberg lettuce, torn into bite-sized pieces

1 pound chopped cooked chicken breast meat

1 (5 ounce) can chow mein noodles

1 (2.25 ounce) package blanched slivered almonds

1/4 cup sesame seeds, lightly toasted

4 green onions, chopped

2 teaspoons poppy seeds
