just-peachy-chicken-salad

Steps

In a large bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, peach juice and whipped cream.
Add salt and pepper to taste. Stir in chicken and peaches and chill until
ready to serve.

Ingredients

1 cup mayonnaise

1/4 cup peach juice

2/3 cup whipped heavy cream

salt and pepper to taste

4 cups cubed, cooked chicken

3 cups pitted and diced fresh peaches
