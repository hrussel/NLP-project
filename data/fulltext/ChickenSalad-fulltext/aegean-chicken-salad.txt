aegean-chicken-salad

Steps

Break up chicken in a large bowl using a fork. Stir in onion, lemon juice,
yogurt, hummus, olives, red bell pepper, and dill. Sprinkle with feta cheese.

Ingredients

1 (12.5 fl oz) can chicken breast chunks, drained

1/4 cup finely chopped red onion

1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice

2 tablespoons Greek yogurt

1/4 cup hummus

1/4 cup kalamata olives, chopped

1/4 cup finely chopped red bell pepper

1 teaspoon chopped fresh dill

1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
