Steps

In a large bowl, mix the chicken, creamy salad dressing, pickle relish, apple,
pecans, celery, and raisins. Season with salt and pepper. Chill until serving.

Ingredients

1 (5 ounce) can chunk chicken, drained and flaked

2 tablespoons creamy salad dressing

1 teaspoon sweet pickle relish

1 large apple, cored and diced

1 cup chopped pecans

1/2 stalk celery, chopped

2/3 cup raisins

salt and pepper to taste
