Steps

Mix broth, mayonnaise, cheese and dill weed.

Toss pasta, tomatoes, peas, mushrooms, onion, chicken and broth mixture until
evenly coated. Cover and refrigerate at least 2 hours. Serve on lettuce.

Ingredients

1 (14 ounce) can Swanson® Chicken Broth (Regular or Natural Goodness™)

1/2 cup reduced-calorie mayonnaise

1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

1 teaspoon dried dill weed or dried basil leaves

3 cups cooked corkscrew-shaped pasta

1 cup cherry tomatoes, cut in half

1 cup cooked peas

1/2 cup sliced mushrooms

1 small red onion, chopped

2 cups cubed cooked chicken

Lettuce leaves
