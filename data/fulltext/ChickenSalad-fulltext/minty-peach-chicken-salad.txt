minty-peach-chicken-salad

Steps

Combine peaches, chicken, and cucumber in a large bowl.

Blend vinegar, mint, sugar, lemon juice, salt, pepper, and hot sauce in a
blender until vinaigrette is smooth. Drizzle vinaigrette over peach mixture;
toss to coat. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate until chilled, at
least 30 minutes. Fold avocado into salad before serving.

Ingredients

3 fresh peaches - peeled, pitted, and cubed

2 cups cubed cooked chicken breast

1 cucumber, seeded and chopped

Dressing:

1/4 cup white wine vinegar

1/4 cup minced fresh mint

1/3 cup white sugar

1 tablespoon lemon juice

1/4 teaspoon salt

1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper

3 dashes hot sauce, or to taste

2 avocados - peeled, pitted, and cubed
