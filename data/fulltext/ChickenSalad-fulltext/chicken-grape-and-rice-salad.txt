chicken-grape-and-rice-salad

Steps

Bring water and margarine to a boil in a saucepan; reduce heat to low and stir
in long grain and wild rice mix. Cover and simmer until rice is tender, about
25 minutes. Fluff with fork and allow rice mix to cool thoroughly.

Mix cooled rice mix with chicken, red onion, grapes, cashews, and mayonnaise.
Serve immediately or chill before serving.
