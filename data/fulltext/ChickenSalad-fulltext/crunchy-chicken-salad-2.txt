crunchy-chicken-salad-2

Steps

Mix chicken, onion, mayonnaise, water chestnuts, sweet pickle relish, pecans,
salt, and black pepper together in a bowl. Serve on lettuce leaves and garnish
each serving with a slice or 2 of hard-boiled egg.

Ingredients

1 1/2 cups cubed cooked chicken

3 tablespoons chopped onion

3 tablespoons mayonnaise

2 tablespoons chopped water chestnuts

4 teaspoons sweet pickle relish

1 tablespoon chopped pecans

1/4 teaspoon salt

1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper

4 lettuce leaves

1 hard-boiled egg, sliced
