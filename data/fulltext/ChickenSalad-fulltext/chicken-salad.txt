chicken-salad

Steps

Combine the chicken, celery, spice blend and mayonnaise. Mix all together;
more or less mayonnaise can be added, depending on your preference. Voila,
your summer salad is ready to serve!

Ingredients

6 boneless chicken breast halves, cooked and diced

1 cup chopped celery

1 teaspoon salt-free herb and spice blend

1 cup mayonnaise
