hawaiian-chicken-salad

Steps

In a medium bowl, beat cream cheese until fluffy. Mix in salad dressing and 2
tablespoons reserved pineapple juice. Stir in the pineapple tidbits, chicken,
almonds, and grapes until evenly coated. Chill until serving.
