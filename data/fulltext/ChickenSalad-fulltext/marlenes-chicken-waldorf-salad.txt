marlenes-chicken-waldorf-salad

Steps

Combine chicken, pineapple, Red Delicious apple, Golden Delicious apple,
celery, red grapes, green grapes, and walnuts in a large bowl.

Whisk milk, mayonnaise, and ranch dressing mix together in a separate bowl
until well blended.

Pour ranch dressing over chicken mixture; toss gently to coat.

Serve salad on a bed of lettuce leaves.

Ingredients

4 cups cubed, cooked chicken breast

1 (20 ounce) can pineapple chunks, drained

1 Red Delicious apple, chopped

1 Golden Delicious apple, chopped

1 cup sliced celery

1/2 cup seedless red grapes

1/2 cup seedless green grapes

1/2 cup chopped walnuts

3/4 cup milk

3/4 cup mayonnaise

1 (1 ounce) packet ranch dressing mix

6 leaves lettuce, or as needed
