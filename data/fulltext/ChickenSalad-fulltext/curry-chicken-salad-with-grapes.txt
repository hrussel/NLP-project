curry-chicken-salad-with-grapes

Steps

Heat butter in a large saucepan over low heat until melted; remove from heat
and cool to room temperature.

Stir mayonnaise, garlic, parsley, and curry powder into butter until evenly
incorporated. Fold in chicken, grapes, and almonds. Transfer mixture to a
serving bowl and cover with plastic wrap. Refrigerate until flavors have
blended, at least 1 hour.

Ingredients

1/2 cup butter

2 cups mayonnaise

1/4 cup minced garlic

2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

1 teaspoon curry powder

4 cups shredded cooked chicken

2 cups halved red grapes

1/2 cup toasted slivered almonds
