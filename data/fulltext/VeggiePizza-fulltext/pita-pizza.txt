pita-pizza

Steps

Preheat grill for medium-high heat.

Spread one side of the pita with olive oil and pizza sauce. Top with cheese
and mushrooms, and season with garlic salt.

Lightly oil grill grate. Place pita pizza on grill, cover, and cook until
cheese completely melts, about 5 minutes.

Ingredients

1 pita bread round

1 teaspoon olive oil

3 tablespoons pizza sauce

1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

1/4 cup sliced crimini mushrooms

1/8 teaspoon garlic salt
