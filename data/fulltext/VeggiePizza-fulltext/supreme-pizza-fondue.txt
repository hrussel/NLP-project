Steps

In a large skillet cook the Johnsonville速 Mild Italian Ground Sausage, onion,
and garlic until meat is brown. Drain off fat.

In a 3-1/2- or 4-quart slow cooker combine Classico速 Tomato and Basil pasta
sauce, mushrooms, pepperoni, and basil. Stir in the sausage mixture.

Cover and cook on low-heat setting for 3 hours. If desired, stir in ripe
olives and sweet pepper; cover and cook for 15 minutes more. To serve, spear
the dippers with fondue forks and dip into the fondue.
