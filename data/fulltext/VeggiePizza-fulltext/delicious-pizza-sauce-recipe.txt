delicious-pizza-sauce-recipe

Steps

Heat olive oil in a saucepan. Pour crushed tomatoes into the saucepan and
place over low heat. Stir basil, garlic, salt, and black pepper into the
tomatoes; bring to a simmer. Add Parmesan cheese to sauce to serve.

Ingredients

2 tablespoons olive oil

1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes

2 leaves basil, chopped

3 cloves garlic, chopped

1 pinch salt and ground black pepper to taste

1 pinch grated Parmesan cheese
