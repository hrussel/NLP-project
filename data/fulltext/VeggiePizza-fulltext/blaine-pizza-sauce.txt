blaine-pizza-sauce

Steps

Stir tomato sauce, tomato paste, vegetable oil, garlic, salt, oregano, sugar,
and pepper together in a bowl; refrigerate until chilled completely, at least
15 minutes.

Ingredients

1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce

1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste

1 tablespoon vegetable oil

1 clove garlic, minced

1 teaspoon salt

1/2 teaspoon oregano

1/2 teaspoon white sugar

1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
