pizza-eggs

Steps

Scrape the cheese and toppings off the pizza crust, it may help to heat the
pizza in the microwave. Discard the crust. Heat the toppings and milk in a
small saucepan over medium heat. Stir until the topping and milk combine, it
should be a soft clumpy consistency. Remove from the heat.

Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat. Whisk the eggs, Italian seasoning,
onion powder, salt, and pepper in a bowl. Pour in eggs and cook without
stirring for 1 minute. Add the pizza toppings to the eggs; cook, stirring
constantly, until eggs reach the desired consistency.

Ingredients

3 slices leftover pizza

2 tablespoons milk

3 eggs

1 teaspoon Italian seasoning

1/8 teaspoon onion powder

salt and black pepper to taste

2 tablespoons vegetable oil
