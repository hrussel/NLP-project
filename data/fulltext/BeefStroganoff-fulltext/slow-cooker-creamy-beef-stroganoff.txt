slow-cooker-creamy-beef-stroganoff

Steps

Stir the soup, water, Worcestershire sauce, mushrooms, onions, garlic and
black pepper in a 6-quart slow cooker. Add the beef and stir to coat.

Cover and cook on LOW for 8 to 9 hours or until the beef is fork-tender. Stir
the sour cream in the cooker. Serve the beef mixture with the noodles.
Sprinkle with the parsley, if desired.
