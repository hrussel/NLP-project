easy-slow-cooker-stroganoff

Steps

Mix cream of mushroom soup, mushrooms, butter, and onion in a slow cooker; add
beef tips.

Cook for 8 hours on Low.

Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook egg noodles in the
boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the
bite, about 5 minutes. Drain.

Serve beef tips and sauce over cooked egg noodles.

Ingredients

2 (10.75 ounce) cans cream of mushroom soup

1 (8 ounce) package sliced fresh mushrooms

1/2 cup butter

1 small onion, chopped

3 pounds beef tips, cut into bite-size pieces

1 (8 ounce) package wide egg noodles
