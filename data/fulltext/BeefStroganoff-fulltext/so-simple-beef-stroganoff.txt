so-simple-beef-stroganoff

Steps

Whisk cream of mushroom soup, water, and dry onion soup mix in a slow cooker;
stir meat into soup mixture. Cover and cook on High for 1 hour. Reduce setting
to Low and cook until beef is tender, 2 to 3 more hours. Test for doneness
after 2 hours. Stir cream cheese into beef until smooth.

Ingredients

1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup

1/2 cup water, or more as needed

1 (1 ounce) package dry onion soup mix

1 pound cubed beef stew meat

4 ounces cream cheese
