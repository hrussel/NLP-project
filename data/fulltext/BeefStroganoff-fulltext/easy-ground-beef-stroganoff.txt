easy-ground-beef-stroganoff

Steps

Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add ground beef, add onions,
garlic, and mushrooms; saute until onion is golden brown. Season with salt and
black pepper.

Stir 2 cups hot water, bouillon cubes, and tomato paste into meat mixture.
Stir together 1 1/2 cups cold water and flour; stir into pan. Reduce heat to
low, and simmer for 1 hour.

Ingredients

2 pounds ground beef

2 onions, chopped

1 clove garlic, minced

1 (4.5 ounce) can mushrooms, drained

2 teaspoons salt

1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

2 cups hot water

6 cubes beef bouillon

4 tablespoons tomato paste

1 1/2 cups water

4 tablespoons all-purpose flour
