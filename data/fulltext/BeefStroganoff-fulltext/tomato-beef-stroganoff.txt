tomato-beef-stroganoff

Steps

Spray inside of large slow cooker with cooking spray. Combine roast, tomatoes,
tomato sauce, tomato paste, mushrooms and seasoning mix in slow cooker. Cook
on LOW 8 hours or on HIGH 4 hours or until meat is tender.

Stir in noodles the last 20 minutes of cook time and cook for remainder of
time on HIGH. Stir in sour cream just before serving.
