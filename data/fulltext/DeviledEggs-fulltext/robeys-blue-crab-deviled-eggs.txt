robeys-blue-crab-deviled-eggs

Steps

Place eggs in a saucepan with enough water to cover. Bring water to a boil and
remove saucepan from heat. Let eggs stand in hot water for 15 minutes. Drain,
cool, and peel.

Cut the eggs in half lengthwise and remove the yolks. In a bowl, mash the
yolks and mix with mayonnaise, paprika, mustard powder, and Old Bay seasoning.
Fold in the crabmeat. Spoon the mixture evenly into the hollowed egg white
halves. Chill at least 1 hour before serving.

Ingredients

12 eggs

1/4 cup mayonnaise

1 teaspoon paprika

1/2 teaspoon mustard powder

1/2 teaspoon Old Bay Seasoning TM

1 (6 ounce) can lump crabmeat, drained
