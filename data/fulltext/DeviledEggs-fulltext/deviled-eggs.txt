deviled-eggs

Steps

Place eggs in saucepan and cover with water. Bring to boil. Cover, remove from
heat, and let eggs sit in hot water for 10 to 12 minutes. Remove from hot
water and cool.

Peel and cut in half lengthwise. Remove yolks and combine with mustard, salad
dressing and salt and pepper. Mix together until smooth.

Refill each egg half with the yolk mixture and sprinkle with paprika.

Ingredients

8 eggs

1/2 teaspoon prepared mustard

1 tablespoon creamy salad dressing

salt and pepper to taste

1 pinch paprika
