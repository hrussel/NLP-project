bread-and-butter-pickle-deviled-eggs

Steps

Halve eggs lengthwise. Carefully remove yolks to a small bowl and mash with a
fork; add mayonnaise, pickles, honey mustard, and black pepper. Blend the
mixture until smooth.

Scoop yolk mixture into egg halves; garnish with paprika. Keep refrigerated
until ready to serve.

Ingredients

6 hard-boiled eggs

1/4 cup mayonnaise

2 tablespoons sliced bread-and-butter pickles

1 tablespoon honey mustard, or more to taste

1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

1 pinch paprika, or as needed
