zippy-deviled-eggs-2

Steps

Combine egg yolks, mayonnaise, mustard, and hot sauce in a food processor;
puree until smooth.

Spoon egg yolk mixture into a pastry bag with a decorative tip; pipe filling
into the cavity of the egg white halves.

Ingredients

12 hard-boiled eggs, halved lengthwise with yolks and whites separated

1/2 cup mayonnaise

3 tablespoons Dijon mustard

1 tablespoon hot yellow pepper sauce
