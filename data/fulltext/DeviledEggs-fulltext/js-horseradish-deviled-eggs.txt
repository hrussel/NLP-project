js-horseradish-deviled-eggs

Steps

Cut eggs in half lengthwise; remove yolks and set whites aside.

Mix egg yolks, mayonnaise, mustard, horseradish, shallots, and seasoned salt
in a bowl until smooth. Pipe or spoon yolk mixture into egg whites; top each
with a leaf of cilantro.

Ingredients

12 hard cooked eggs

2/3 cup mayonnaise

1/4 cup Dijon mustard

2 tablespoons prepared horseradish

1/2 cup finely chopped shallots

3/4 teaspoon seasoned salt

1/4 cup fresh cilantro leaves
