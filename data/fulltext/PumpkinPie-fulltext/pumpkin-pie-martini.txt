pumpkin-pie-martini

Steps

Place graham cracker crumbs in a shallow dish. Coat rim of martini glass with
honey, and dip into graham cracker crumbs to coat.

Combine milk and pumpkin puree in a cocktail shaker, and shake to combine.
Pour in vodka and creme de cacao, and add ice. Shake well, and then strain
into prepared martini glass. Garnish with a sprinkle of pumpkin pie spice.

Ingredients

1 graham cracker, crushed

1 tablespoon honey

1/3 cup milk

2 tablespoons pumpkin puree

3/4 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger vanilla flavored vodka

1/2 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger creme de cacao

1 pinch pumpkin pie spice

1 cup crushed ice
