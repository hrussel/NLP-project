frosty-pumpkin-pie

Steps

Combine pumpkin, brown sugar, salt, and spices with a rotary beater. Blend in
ice cream. Pour into pie shell. Freeze until firm. Serve frozen, garnished
with whipped cream and walnut halves.

Ingredients

1 (9 inch) pie crust, baked

1 cup pumpkin puree

1/2 cup packed brown sugar

1/2 teaspoon salt

1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

1/2 teaspoon ground ginger

1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg

1 quart vanilla ice cream, softened
