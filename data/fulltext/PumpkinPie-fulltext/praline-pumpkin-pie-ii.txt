praline-pumpkin-pie-ii

Steps

Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C.) In a small bowl, combine
pecans, 1/3 cup brown sugar and butter. Press firmly into bottom of unbaked
pie shell. Bake in oven 10 minutes. Remove crust from oven and reduce
temperature to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C.)

In a large bowl, combine eggs, pumpkin, 2/3 cup brown sugar, flour, cloves,
mace, cinnamon, salt and ginger. Blend in cream until mixture is smooth and
creamy. Pour into partially baked shell.

Bake in the preheated oven for 40 to 45 minutes, or until filling is set.

Ingredients

1 (9 inch) unbaked pie crust

1/3 cup ground pecans

1/3 cup brown sugar

2 tablespoons butter, softened

2 eggs

1 cup canned pumpkin puree

2/3 cup brown sugar

1 tablespoon all-purpose flour

1/4 teaspoon ground cloves

1/4 teaspoon ground mace

1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

1/2 teaspoon salt

1/2 teaspoon ground ginger

1 cup light cream
