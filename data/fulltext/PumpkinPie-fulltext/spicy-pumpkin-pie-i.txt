spicy-pumpkin-pie-i

Steps

Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (220 degrees C).

In a large bowl, mix together butter or margarine, pumpkin, cinnamon, ginger,
cloves, and salt. In a separate bowl, beat eggs until foamy. Mix flour, brown
sugar, white sugar, and milk into eggs. Blend egg mixture into pumpkin
mixture. Pour half of mixture into each pastry lined pan.

Bake in preheated oven for 15 minutes. Reduce heat to 375 degrees F (190
degrees C) and bake an additional 45 minutes, until toothpick inserted in
center comes out clean.

Ingredients

2 (9 inch) unbaked pie crusts

2 tablespoons butter, melted

1 (29 ounce) can pumpkin puree

1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

1/4 teaspoon ground ginger

1/4 teaspoon ground cloves

1/2 teaspoon salt

2 eggs

2 tablespoons all-purpose flour

1/2 cup brown sugar

1/2 cup white sugar

1 cup milk
