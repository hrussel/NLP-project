no-bake-pumpkin-pie-ii

Steps

In a large bowl, mix the milk and pumpkin until smooth. Add the pudding mix,
pumpkin pie spice and cinnamon and mix until well combined. Fold in the
whipped topping and pour into pie shell. Refrigerate until serving.

Ingredients

2/3 cup milk

1 cup pumpkin puree

1 (3.4 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix

1/2 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice

1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

1 cup frozen whipped topping, thawed

1 (9 inch) pie shell, baked
