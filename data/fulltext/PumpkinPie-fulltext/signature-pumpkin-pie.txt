Steps

Preheat oven to 425 degrees F. Place pie crust on large foil-lined baking
sheet.

Mix pumpkin, milk, eggs and pumpkin pie spice in large bowl until smooth. Pour
into crust.

Bake 15 minutes. Reduce oven temperature to 350 degrees F. Bake 40 minutes
longer or until knife inserted 1 inch from crust comes out clean. Cool
completely on wire rack. Serve with Vanilla Whipped Cream, if desired.
