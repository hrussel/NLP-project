easy-freezy-pumpkin-pie

Steps

In a large bowl, stir together the brown sugar, salt, pumpkin and cinnamon.
Mix in the softened ice cream until well blended. Spoon into the pie crusts
and place them in the freezer. Freeze for at least 3 hours before serving.

Ingredients

1/2 cup packed brown sugar

1/2 teaspoon salt

1 cup canned pumpkin puree

1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

1 quart vanilla ice cream, softened

2 (9 inch) prepared graham cracker crusts
