gingersnap-pumpkin-pie

Steps

Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).

Combine cookie crumbs, granulated sugar, and melted butter in a 9 inch pie
pan. Press into sides. Bake for 5 minutes. Cool completely.

Combine pumpkin, brown sugar, cornstarch, cinnamon, nutmeg, salt, vanilla,
eggs, and milk. Blend with wire whisk until combined.

Pour into crust. Bake at 325 degrees F (165 degrees C) for 1 hour. Let cool.
Refrigerate to chill.

Ingredients

1 3/4 cups gingersnap cookie crumbs

2 1/2 tablespoons butter, melted

2 tablespoons white sugar

1 1/2 cups canned pumpkin

3/4 cup packed brown sugar

1 tablespoon cornstarch

1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg

1/4 teaspoon salt

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

2 eggs

1 (12 fluid ounce) can evaporated milk
