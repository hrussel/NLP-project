pumpkin-cream-pie

Steps

Combine milk, pie filling mix, pumpkin, spices, and whipped topping in a deep,
narrow-bottom bowl. Beat at lowest speed of an electric mixer for about 1
minute. Pour filling into cooled pie shell.

Chill until set, about 3 hours.

Ingredients

2 cups cold milk

2 (3.5 ounce) packages instant vanilla pudding mix

1 cup canned pumpkin

1 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice

1 cup frozen whipped topping, thawed

1 (9 inch) pie crust, baked
