Steps

Heat oven to 425 degrees F. Place pie crust in 9-inch glass pie pan as
directed on box for One-Crust Filled Pie.

In large bowl, beat eggs with wire whisk. Stir in remaining ingredients until
well blended. Pour into crust-lined pan.

Bake 15 minutes. Reduce oven temperature to 350 degrees F; bake 40 to 50
minutes longer or until knife inserted near center comes out clean. Cool
completely, about 2 hours. Store in refrigerator.
