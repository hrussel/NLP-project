pumpkin-chiffon-pie-iv

Steps

In a large bowl, combine pumpkin puree, pudding mix, pumpkin pie spice and
evaporated milk. Mix well. Fold in 1 cup of the whipped topping. Pour filling
into pie shell. Chill for 2 hours, or until set.

Spread remaining 1/2 cup whipped topping over filling.

Ingredients

1 (9 inch) prepared graham cracker crust

1 cup pumpkin puree

1 (1 ounce) package instant sugar-free vanilla pudding mix

1/2 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice

1 cup nonfat evaporated milk

1 1/2 cups lite frozen whipped topping, thawed
