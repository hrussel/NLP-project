maple-walnut-pumpkin-pie

Steps

Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.

In large mixing bowl, combine pumpkin, sweetened condensed milk, eggs,
cinnamon, maple flavoring, ginger, nutmeg and salt; mix well. Pour into pie
shell.

Bake at 425 degrees F for 15 minutes. Reduce oven to 350 degrees F; continue
baking 30 minutes.

In medium mixing bowl, combine brown sugar, flour and cinnamon; cut in butter
until crumbly. Stir in nuts.

Remove pie from oven; top evenly with crumb mixture. Return to oven 10
minutes. Cool. Garnish as desired. Store covered in refrigerator.
