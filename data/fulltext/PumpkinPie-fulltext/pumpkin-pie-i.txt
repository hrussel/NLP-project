pumpkin-pie-i

Steps

Brown the pie crusts ahead of time, and cool.

Mix together eggs, sugar, spices, sweetened condensed milk, half and half, and
pumpkin; mix until lumps are gone. Do not overbeat.

Bake pies at 400 degrees F (205 degrees C) for 45 minutes. Let cool, and
serve. Store leftovers in refrigerator.

Ingredients

3 eggs

3/4 cup white sugar

1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg

1/4 teaspoon ground cloves

1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk

2/3 cup half-and-half

1 (29 ounce) can pumpkin puree

1 recipe pastry for a 9 inch single crust pie
