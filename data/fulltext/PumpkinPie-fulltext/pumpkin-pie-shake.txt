pumpkin-pie-shake

Steps

Combine the evaporated milk, ice cream, and pumpkin pie filling in a blender
and blend until smooth. Pour into a glass; top with whipped topping and ground
cinnamon to serve.

Ingredients

5 ounces evaporated milk

2 scoops vanilla ice cream

1/4 cup pumpkin pie filling

2 tablespoons sweetened whipped cream

1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
