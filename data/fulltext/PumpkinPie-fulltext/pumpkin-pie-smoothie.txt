pumpkin-pie-smoothie

Steps

Remove pumpkin from can and freeze for 1 hour.

In a blender combine partially frozen pumpkin, frozen apple juice concentrate,
nutmeg and cinnamon. Blend until smooth. Continue to blend while adding water
to fill the blender.

Ingredients

1 (15 ounce) can solid pack pumpkin puree

1 (12 fluid ounce) can frozen apple juice concentrate

1/8 teaspoon ground nutmeg

1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

2 1/2 cups water
