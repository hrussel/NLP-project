sugarless-pumpkin-pie-i

Steps

Beat egg, sugar substitute and spices until fluffy. Add milk and pumpkin; mix
well.

Pour into unbaked 8-inch pie shell. Bake at 350 degrees F for 30 minutes or
until inserted knife comes out clean.

Ingredients

1 recipe pastry for a 9 inch single crust pie

1 cup solid pack pumpkin puree

1/2 teaspoon ground cloves

1 egg

1 cup milk

1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

6 packets granulated artificial sweetener
