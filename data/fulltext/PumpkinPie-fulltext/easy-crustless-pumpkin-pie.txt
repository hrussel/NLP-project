easy-crustless-pumpkin-pie

Steps

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a pie dish.

Beat pumpkin puree, milk, sweetener, egg substitute, vanilla extract,
cinnamon, ginger, and nutmeg in a bowl until smooth; pour into prepared pie
dish.

Bake in preheated oven until set, about 30 minutes.
