pumpkin-pie-cocktail

Steps

Combine the ice cream, ice, pumpkin, half-and-half, rum, and 1/4 teaspoon
pumpkin pie spice in a blender; blend until smooth. Pour into a serving glass;
top with whipped topping, sprinkle with pinch of pumpkin pie spice.

Ingredients

2 scoops vanilla ice cream

1/2 cup crushed ice

1 tablespoon canned pumpkin

1 fluid ounce half-and-half

1 fluid ounce spiced rum

1/4 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice

2 tablespoons whipped topping

1 pinch pumpkin pie spice
