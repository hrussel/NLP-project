spiced-pumpkin-pie

Steps

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

In a medium bowl, mix together the pumpkin, egg substitute, brown sugar,
pumpkin pie spice and nonfat evaporated milk.

Pour the pumpkin mixture into the pie crust. Bake in the preheated oven 1
hour, or until a knife inserted in the middle comes out clean.

Ingredients

2 cups pumpkin

1/2 cup egg substitute

1/2 cup brown sugar

1 tablespoon pumpkin pie spice

12 fluid ounces nonfat evaporated milk

1 (9 inch) unbaked pie crust
