broccoli-slaw

Steps

In a medium bowl combine broccoli, dressing (use more or less to achieve
desired creaminess), dried cranberries, pistachios, salt and pepper. Mix well,
cover and refrigerate until ready to serve.

Ingredients

2 (16 ounce) packages broccoli coleslaw mix

1 (16 ounce) bottle cole slaw dressing

1 (8 ounce) package sweetened dried cranberries

2 cups pistachio nuts

salt and pepper to taste
