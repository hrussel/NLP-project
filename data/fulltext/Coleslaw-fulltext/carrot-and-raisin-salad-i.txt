carrot-and-raisin-salad-i

Steps

In a mixing bowl, combine the carrots, celery, raisins, mayonnaise and
vinegar. Mix together and refrigerate until chilled.

Ingredients

2 cups shredded carrots

1/2 cup diced celery

1/2 cup raisins

1/3 cup mayonnaise

1 tablespoon distilled white vinegar
