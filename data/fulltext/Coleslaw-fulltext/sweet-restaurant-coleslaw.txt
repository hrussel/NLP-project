sweet-restaurant-coleslaw

Steps

Pour coleslaw mix into a large bowl.

Whisk salad dressing, sugar, vegetable oil, horseradish, vinegar, salt, and
poppy seeds together in a bowl until smooth; drizzle over coleslaw mix and
toss to coat.

Cover bowl with plastic wrap; refrigerate at least 2 hours before serving.
