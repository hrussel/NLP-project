pineapple-slaw

Steps

Mix cabbage, pineapple, green onions, and bell pepper together in a large
bowl.

Whisk vinegar, mayonnaise, sugar, salt, and pepper together in a separate
bowl; drizzle over cabbage mixture and toss to coat.

Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate at least 2 hours before serving.

Ingredients

1 medium head green cabbage, chopped

1 fresh pineapple - peeled, cored, and cut into small pieces

4 green onions, chopped

1 bell pepper, chopped

1/3 cup white vinegar

1/4 cup mayonnaise

1 tablespoon white sugar

1 teaspoon salt

1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
