sherrys-hot-slaw

Steps

In a saucepan, combine the vinegar and sugar; cook over medium heat until
sugar is dissolved. Remove from heat and refrigerate until chilled.

In a large bowl, combine cabbage, lettuce, bell pepper, onion, tomatoes,
ketchup, cayenne pepper and hot sauce. Mix and pour cooled vinegar and sugar
mixture over slaw. Toss and serve chilled.

Ingredients

1 medium head cabbage, chopped

1/2 head iceberg lettuce - rinsed, dried, and chopped

1 green bell pepper, chopped

1 onion, chopped

2 tomatoes, chopped

1 (20 ounce) bottle ketchup

1 cup white wine vinegar

1 cup white sugar

1 tablespoon ground cayenne pepper

1 tablespoon hot pepper sauce
