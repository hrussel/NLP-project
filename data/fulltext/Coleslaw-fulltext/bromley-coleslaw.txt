bromley-coleslaw

Steps

In a large bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise and honey. Mix in the coleslaw
mix, celery seed, and green onions. Season with salt and pepper. Chill in the
refrigerator until ready to serve.

Ingredients

2 cups mayonnaise

1/2 cup honey

1 (16 ounce) package shredded coleslaw mix

4 tablespoons celery seed

1 bunch green onions, chopped

salt and pepper to taste
