creamy-spiced-coleslaw

Steps

In a large bowl, mix mayonnaise, sugar, salt, pepper, celery seed, garlic
powder, onion powder and cider vinegar. Toss coleslaw mix with dressing to
coat. Chill at least 1 hour before serving.

Ingredients

1 cup mayonnaise

2 tablespoons sugar

1/2 teaspoon salt

1/2 teaspoon pepper

1/2 teaspoon celery seed

1/2 teaspoon garlic powder

1/2 teaspoon onion powder

2 tablespoons cider vinegar

1 (16 ounce) package shredded coleslaw mix
