jaymes-cole-slaw

Steps

In a large bowl, whisk together the brown sugar, apple cider vinegar,
mayonnaise, and celery seed. Add cabbage and toss to coat.

Ingredients

1/2 cup brown sugar

1/2 cup apple cider vinegar

1/2 cup mayonnaise

1 teaspoon celery seed

4 cups chopped cabbage
