snow-pea-and-napa-cabbage-slaw

Steps

In a large pot of boiling water, blanch snow peas 15 seconds, strain and
transfer to a bowl of cold water to chill. Drain and slice thin.

In a large bowl, combine the snow peas, cabbage, carrots, bell pepper, green
onions, lemon juice, vinegar and olive oil. Add salt and pepper to taste; toss
and serve chilled.

Ingredients

1/2 pound snow peas

1 1/2 pounds shredded cabbage

2 carrots, shredded

1 green bell pepper, thinly sliced

3 green onions, chopped

1 tablespoon lemon juice

1 tablespoon rice wine vinegar

3 tablespoons olive oil
