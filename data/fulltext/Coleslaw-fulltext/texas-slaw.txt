texas-slaw

Steps

In a large bowl, toss together the coleslaw mix, corn, Cheddar cheese,
jalapeno and cilantro. In a separate bowl, stir together the Ranch-style
dressing, cumin, coriander, garlic powder and lime juice. Pour over the
coleslaw mixture, and toss to coat. Serve right away, or refrigerate until
serving.

Ingredients

1 (16 ounce) package coleslaw mix

1 (7 ounce) can Mexican-style corn, drained

1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

2 fresh jalapeno peppers, seeded and chopped

1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro

1 cup Ranch-style salad dressing

1/4 teaspoon ground cumin

1/4 teaspoon ground coriander

1/2 teaspoon garlic powder

1 lime, juiced
