sauerkraut-salad-2

Steps

In a saucepan combine the salt, vinegar, caraway seeds and sugar. Bring to a
boil over medium heat. Stir in the sauerkraut, onion, celery, pepper and
pimento. Remove from heat and let marinate overnight before serving.

Ingredients

1/4 cup cider vinegar

1 teaspoon caraway seeds

1 cup white sugar

1 (20 ounce) can sauerkraut

1 cup chopped celery

1 cup chopped onion

1 cup chopped green bell pepper

2 ounces pimento
