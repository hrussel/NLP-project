creamy-german-coleslaw

Steps

Place cabbage in a large bowl.

Mix sugar, vinegar, celery seed, and salt together in a separate bowl. Stir
mayonnaise into sugar-vinegar mixture until dressing is smooth and creamy.
Pour dressing over cabbage; toss to coat.

Marinate coleslaw in refrigerator for 2 to 3 hours; stir well before serving.

Ingredients

1/2 head cabbage, thinly sliced

3 tablespoons white sugar

3 tablespoons cider vinegar

1/2 teaspoon celery seed

1/2 teaspoon salt

1/2 cup mayonnaise, or more to taste
