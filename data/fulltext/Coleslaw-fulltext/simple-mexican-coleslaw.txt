simple-mexican-coleslaw

Steps

Place cabbage and carrots in a colander and rinse in cold water; let drain for
5 minutes.

Whisk rice vinegar, olive oil, and cilantro together in a large salad bowl.
Stir cabbage and carrots into dressing; season with salt. Slaw can be served
immediately or covered and refrigerated for up to 1 day.

Ingredients

6 cups very thinly sliced green cabbage

1 1/2 cups grated carrots

1/4 cup rice vinegar

2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil

1/3 cup chopped cilantro

1/4 teaspoon salt
