southern-coleslaw-2

Steps

In a large bowl, stir together the vinegar, sugar, mayonnaise and mustard. Add
the cabbage, eggs and bacon, and toss lightly to coat with the dressing.
Season with salt and pepper. Refrigerate for 1 hour before serving to blend
flavors.

Ingredients

1 tablespoon red wine vinegar

1 teaspoon white sugar

1/3 cup mayonnaise

1 tablespoon coarse-grain brown mustard

1 head cabbage, cored and shredded

2 hard-cooked eggs, peeled and chopped

6 slices crisply cooked bacon, crumbled

salt and pepper to taste
