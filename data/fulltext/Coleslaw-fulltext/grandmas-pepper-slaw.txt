grandmas-pepper-slaw

Steps

Toss the cabbage and bell pepper together in a large bowl. Season the mixture
liberally with salt; stir. Allow to sit 45 minutes; drain and return to the
bowl.

Bring the vinegar and sugar to a boil together in a saucepan. Immediately
remove from heat and stir into the cabbage mixture. Add the celery seed; mix
thoroughly. Chill completely before serving.

Ingredients

1 medium head green cabbage, shredded

2 green bell peppers, diced

salt to taste

1 cup vinegar

1 1/2 cups sugar

2 tablespoons celery seed
