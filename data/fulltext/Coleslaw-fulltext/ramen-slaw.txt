ramen-slaw

Steps

Combine broccoli coleslaw mix, sunflower seeds, almonds, and ramen noodles
together in a bowl. Whisk canola oil, sugar, ramen noodle seasoning packet,
and vinegar together in a separate bowl; pour over slaw mixture. Fold in green
onions. Chill in refrigerator for 1 1/2 hours before serving.

Ingredients

1 (12 ounce) package broccoli coleslaw mix

1/2 cup sunflower seeds

1/2 cup slivered almonds

1 (3 ounce) package Oriental-flavored ramen noodles, broken into small pieces

1/2 cup canola oil

1/4 cup white sugar

1/4 cup white wine vinegar

4 green onions, chopped
