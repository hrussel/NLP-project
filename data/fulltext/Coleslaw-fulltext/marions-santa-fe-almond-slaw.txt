marions-santa-fe-almond-slaw

Steps

In a large bowl, mix the cabbage, green onions, cilantro, and tomato.

In a skillet over medium heat, cook and stir the sesame oil, black pepper, and
chili pepper until heated through. Mix in the bacon bits and almonds. Cook and
stir about 5 minutes, until almonds are lightly browned. Remove from heat, and
toss with the cabbage mixture. Serve immediately.

Ingredients

1/2 head cabbage, coarsely chopped

4 green onions, chopped

1/2 cup finely chopped cilantro

1 large tomato, diced

1/2 cup sesame oil

1 teaspoon coarsely ground black pepper

1/3 teaspoon crumbled dried red chile pepper

1/2 cup imitation bacon bits

1 cup slivered almonds
