5-minute-slaw

Steps

Whisk red wine vinegar, sugar, salt, celery seed, and green onions in a salad
bowl until sugar and salt have dissolved. Toss coleslaw mix with dressing.
Refrigerate 4 to 6 hours.

Ingredients

1/4 cup red wine vinegar

2 tablespoons white sugar

2 teaspoons salt

1/3 teaspoon celery seed

3 green onions, thinly sliced

2 (8 ounce) packages coleslaw mix
