traditional-creamy-coleslaw

Steps

Whisk mayonnaise, sugar, rice wine vinegar, lemon juice, horseradish, onion
powder, dry mustard, celery salt, salt, and black pepper in a large mixing
bowl, stirring to dissolve sugar. Fold coleslaw mix into dressing. Cover bowl
and refrigerate 1 hour before serving.

Ingredients

1 cup mayonnaise

1/4 cup white sugar

2 tablespoons seasoned rice wine vinegar

1 1/2 tablespoons lemon juice

1 tablespoon prepared horseradish

1/2 teaspoon onion powder

1/2 teaspoon dry mustard

1/2 teaspoon celery salt

1/2 teaspoon salt

1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

1 (28 ounce) package coleslaw mix
