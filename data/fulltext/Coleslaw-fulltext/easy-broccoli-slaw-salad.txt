easy-broccoli-slaw-salad

Steps

Whisk olive oil, vinegar, sugar, and ramen noodle seasoning packets together
in a bowl. Combine broccoli coleslaw mix, almonds, raisins, green onions,
green bell pepper together in a bowl; fold in dressing. Refrigerate at least 2
hours. Stir again before serving.

Ingredients

2/3 cup olive oil

1/3 cup cider vinegar

2/3 cup white sugar

2 (3 ounce) packages chicken ramen noodles, broken

10 ounces broccoli coleslaw mix

3/4 cup sliced almonds

3/4 cup golden raisins

1/2 cup sliced green onions

1/2 cup chopped green bell pepper
