cindys-coleslaw

Steps

Place coleslaw mix in a large bowl. Combine mayonnaise, sugar, vinegar, onion
powder, salt, and pepper in a small bowl. Pour mayonnaise mixture over
coleslaw mix and toss to coat. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes; serve with
a slotted spoon.

Ingredients

1 (16 ounce) bag coleslaw mix

1 cup mayonnaise

1/4 cup white sugar, or to taste

2 tablespoons cider vinegar

1/2 teaspoon onion powder

salt and ground black pepper to taste
