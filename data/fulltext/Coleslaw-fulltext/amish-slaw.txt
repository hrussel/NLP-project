amish-slaw

Steps

In a large bowl, toss together the cabbage, onion, and 1 cup sugar. In a small
saucepan, combine the vinegar, salt, celery seed, 1 teaspoon white sugar,
mustard and oil. Bring to a boil, and cook for 3 minutes. Cool completely,
then pour over cabbage mixture, and toss to coat. Refrigerate overnight for
best flavor.

Ingredients

1 medium head cabbage, cored and shredded

1 medium onion, finely chopped

1 cup white sugar

1 cup vinegar

1 teaspoon salt

1 teaspoon celery seed

1 teaspoon white sugar

1 teaspoon prepared mustard

3/4 cup vegetable oil
