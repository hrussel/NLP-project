pineapple-coleslaw---quick-and-easy

Steps

Mix marshmallows, mayonnaise, pineapple, salt, and pepper together in a bowl;
fold in coleslaw mix. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate until
flavors blend, at least 1 hour.

Ingredients

2 cups miniature marshmallows

1 1/2 cups mayonnaise

1 (8 ounce) can crushed pineapple, drained

salt and ground black pepper to taste

2 (8.5 ounce) packages coleslaw mix
