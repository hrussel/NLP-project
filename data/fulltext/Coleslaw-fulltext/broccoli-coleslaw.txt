broccoli-coleslaw

Steps

In a small bowl combine oil, vinegar, sugar and the seasoning packet from the
ramen noodles. Mix well and refrigerate at least one hour before serving, or
overnight.

In a large bowl combine broccoli, carrots, green onions and sunflower seeds.
Crush ramen noodles and stir in. Pour dressing over salad about 10 minutes
before serving.

Ingredients

1 cup olive oil

1/3 cup distilled white vinegar

1/2 cup white sugar

1 (3 ounce) package chicken flavored ramen noodles, crushed, seasoning packet
reserved

1 large head fresh broccoli, diced

2 carrots, grated

2 bunches green onions, chopped

1 cup sunflower seeds
