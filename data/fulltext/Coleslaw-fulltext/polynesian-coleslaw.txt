polynesian-coleslaw

Steps

Stir together peanut butter, garlic, salt, rice vinegar, soy sauce, and hot
pepper sauce until smooth. Fold in cilantro, chives, and red pepper. Add
shredded cabbage, and toss until well coated. Scoop into a serving bowl, cover
with plastic wrap, and refrigerate 1 hour. Sprinkle with sesame seeds before
serving.
