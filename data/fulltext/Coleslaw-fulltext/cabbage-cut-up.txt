cabbage-cut-up

Steps

Toss the cabbage and tomato together in a bowl. Pour the orange juice and lime
juice over the mixture. Season with salt and pepper; stir.

Ingredients

2 cups shredded cabbage

1 small tomato, diced

1/2 cup fresh orange juice

1/4 cup fresh lime juice

salt and pepper to taste
