original-blue-cheese-coleslaw

Steps

Lightly mix together the blue cheese, lemon juice, mayonnaise, and salt and
pepper in a large salad bowl. Stir in the celery, green onions, and bagged
coleslaw, and mix until well-coated.

Ingredients

1 cup crumbled blue cheese

2 lemons, juiced

1/3 cup mayonnaise

salt and black pepper to taste

6 stalks celery, thinly sliced

4 green onions with tops, thinly sliced

1 (16 ounce) package shredded coleslaw mix with red and green cabbage and
carrots
