zesty-coleslaw

Steps

In a large bowl, mix together the mayonnaise, creamy salad dressing,
horseradish, vinegar, dill weed and sugar. Add coleslaw mix, and stir to coat.
Refrigerate at least 3 hours before serving to blend flavors.

Ingredients

1/2 cup mayonnaise

3/4 cup creamy salad dressing (e.g. Miracle Whip)

2 tablespoons prepared horseradish

1 1/2 teaspoons white vinegar

1 1/2 teaspoons dill weed

3 1/2 tablespoons sugar

1 (16 ounce) package coleslaw mix
