coleslaw-with-a-difference

Steps

In a large bowl, combine coleslaw, seasoning salt, lemon juice, olive oil,
mayonnaise and salsa. Toss well and serve.

Ingredients

1/2 (16 ounce) package shredded coleslaw mix

1/2 teaspoon seasoning salt

1 tablespoon lemon juice

2 tablespoons olive oil

1/2 cup mayonnaise

1/2 cup salsa
