tangy-ranch-apple-slaw

Steps

Whisk sour cream, sweetener, apple cider vinegar, and ranch dressing mix in a
bowl until thoroughly combined. Toss green apples, shredded cabbage, and green
onions in a salad bowl and pour dressing over the slaw; toss to coat. Season
with salt and black pepper. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and chill at least 20
minutes or up to 4 hours before serving.

Ingredients

1/3 cup sour cream

2 tablespoons granular sucralose sweetener (such as Splenda®)

1 1/2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar

1 tablespoon dry ranch salad dressing mix (such as Hidden Valley Ranch®)

2 large green apples, cored and diced

1/2 (8 ounce) package shredded cabbage

2 green onions, thinly sliced

salt and ground black pepper to taste
