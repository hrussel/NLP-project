southern-coleslaw

Steps

Place the tomato in a large bowl, and season with salt and pepper. Stir in the
coleslaw mix and lime juice. Refrigerate for 10 minutes before serving.

Ingredients

1 medium tomato, diced

1 teaspoon salt

1 teaspoon pepper

1 (16 ounce) package shredded coleslaw mix

1/4 cup lime juice
