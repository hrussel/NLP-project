amish-coleslaw

Steps

In a large bowl, toss together the cabbage, onion, and 1 cup SPLENDA速
Granulated Sweetener. In a small saucepan, combine the vinegar, salt, celery
seed, 1 teaspoon SPLENDA速 Granulated Sweetener, mustard and oil. Bring to a
boil, and cook for 3 minutes. Cool completely, then pour over cabbage mixture,
and toss to coat. Refrigerate overnight for best flavor.
