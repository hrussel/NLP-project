sweet-cole-slaw

Steps

Stir mayonnaise, sour cream, sugar, white balsamic vinegar, orange juice, and
pepper together in a bowl.

Toss cabbage and carrots together in a large bowl. Drizzle dressing over the
cabbage mixture and toss to coat.

Ingredients

1 1/4 cups mayonnaise

1/4 cup sour cream

1/4 cup white sugar

2 tablespoons white balsamic vinegar

1 tablespoon orange juice

1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

2 1/2 heads cabbage, shredded

2 carrots, shredded
