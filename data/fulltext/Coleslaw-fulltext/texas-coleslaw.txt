texas-coleslaw

Steps

In a large bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, lime juice, cumin, salt and
pepper. Add the cabbage, carrot, green onions and radishes and stir until
well-combined. Chill at least an hour before serving.

Ingredients

1 cup mayonnaise

1 tablespoon lime juice

1 tablespoon ground cumin

1 teaspoon cayenne pepper

1 teaspoon salt

1 teaspoon ground black pepper

1 medium head green cabbage, rinsed and very thinly sliced

1 large carrot, shredded

2 green onions, sliced

2 radishes, sliced
