fruit-and-nut-slaw

Steps

Drain pineapple, reserving 2 tablespoons juice. Cut pineapple into thin
strips, and place in a large bowl.

In a medium bowl, combine reserved pineapple juice and lemon juice. Toss with
banana, and add to pineapple. Add cabbage, celery, mandarin oranges, walnuts
and raisins; toss to combine.

Blend yogurt and salt together, and add to cabbage mixture; toss lightly.
Cover, and refrigerate until thoroughly chilled.

Ingredients

1 (8 ounce) can sliced pineapple, drained with juice reserved

2 tablespoons lemon juice

1 banana, peeled and sliced

3 cups shredded cabbage

1 cup diagonally sliced celery

1 (11 ounce) can mandarin oranges, drained

1/2 cup chopped walnuts

1/4 cup raisins

1 (8 ounce) container orange flavored yogurt

1/2 teaspoon salt
