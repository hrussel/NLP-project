red-white-and-blue-slaw-salad

Steps

Place bacon in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium high heat until evenly
brown. Crumble and set aside.

In a large bowl, combine the bacon, cabbage and dressing. Mix well. Sprinkle
with halved cherry tomatoes and blue cheese. Refrigerate and serve chilled.

Ingredients

12 slices bacon

6 cups shredded cabbage

1 cup cole slaw dressing

1/2 cup blue cheese, crumbled

1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
