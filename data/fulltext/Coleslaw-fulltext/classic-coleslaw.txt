classic-coleslaw

Steps

In a large bowl, combine cabbage, carrot, onion, vegetable oil, vinegar and
SPLENDA® Granulated Sweetener. Stir until the ingredients are well mixed.
Chill in the refrigerator until serving.

Ingredients

1 medium head cabbage, shredded

1 carrot

1/4 onion, chopped

1/4 cup vegetable oil

1/4 cup vinegar

1/4 cup SPLENDA® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
