cats-famous-coleslaw

Steps

In a large bowl, mix the onion, cabbage, carrots, Greek yogurt, and pineapple.
Season with salt and pepper, paprika, and garlic powder. Chill 30 minutes in
the refrigerator before serving.

Ingredients

1 onion, finely chopped

1 head cabbage, finely chopped

4 carrots, grated

1 cup Greek yogurt

1 (8 ounce) can sliced pineapple, drained and chopped

salt and pepper to taste

paprika to taste

garlic powder to taste
