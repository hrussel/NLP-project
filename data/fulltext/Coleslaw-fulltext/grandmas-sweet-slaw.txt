grandmas-sweet-slaw

Steps

In a large bowl, stir together the sugar and mayonnaise. Add the carrots and
cabbage, and mix until well coated. Serve chilled.

Ingredients

1/2 cup white sugar

1/2 cup mayonnaise

3 medium carrots, shredded

1 medium head cabbage, cored and shredded
