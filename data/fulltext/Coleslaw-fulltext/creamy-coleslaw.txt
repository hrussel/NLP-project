creamy-coleslaw

Steps

In a large bowl, combine the onion, red pepper, cabbage and carrot. Add
raisins and toss well.

In a small bowl, whisk the mayonnaise, sour cream, mustard and vinegar
together.

Pour dressing over vegetable mixture and refrigerate for at least 2 hours
before serving.

Ingredients

1 red onion, thinly sliced

1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced

3 cups shredded cabbage

1 large carrot, shredded

1/2 cup raisins

1/4 cup mayonnaise

1/2 cup sour cream

2 tablespoons Dijon-style prepared mustard

1 teaspoon white wine vinegar
