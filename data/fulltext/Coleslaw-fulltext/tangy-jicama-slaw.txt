tangy-jicama-slaw

Steps

Combine the jicama, cilantro, lime juice, lemon juice, and mandarin orange
segments with a small amount of the syrup from the can in a bowl; mix to
evenly coat. Allow mixture to sit 10 minutes. Season with salt and stir just
before serving.

Ingredients

1 jicama, peeled and chopped

1/4 cup fresh cilantro leaves, minced

1 large lime, juiced

1 lemon, juiced

1 (11 ounce) can mandarin orange segments, drained, liquid reserved

salt to taste
