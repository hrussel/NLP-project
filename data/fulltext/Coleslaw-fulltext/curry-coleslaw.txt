curry-coleslaw

Steps

In a large bowl, mix the cabbage, green onions, peas, and peanuts.

In a separate bowl, mix the sour cream, mayonnaise, vinegar, curry powder,
ginger, and cayenne pepper. Toss with the slaw to coat. Cover and refrigerate
until serving.

Ingredients

1 head cabbage, cored and shredded

2 bunches green onions, chopped

1 (16 ounce) package frozen green peas

1 cup dry roasted peanuts

1 cup sour cream

1 cup mayonnaise

1/4 cup white vinegar

2 tablespoons curry powder

1/2 teaspoon ground ginger

1 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
