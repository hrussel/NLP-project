apple-slaw-with-pineapple

Steps

Whisk cream, mayonnaise, and sugar in a large salad bowl until sugar has
dissolved. Gently fold in cabbage, apple, and pineapple to coat with dressing.
Refrigerate until serving time.

Ingredients

1/2 cup heavy whipping cream

1/2 cup mayonnaise

1 tablespoon white sugar

4 cups finely shredded cabbage

1 large red apple, cored and diced

1 (8 ounce) can crushed pineapple, drained
