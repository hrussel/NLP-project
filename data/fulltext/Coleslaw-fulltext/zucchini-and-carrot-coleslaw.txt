zucchini-and-carrot-coleslaw

Steps

Place zucchini in a colander and let drain thoroughly, about 30 minutes. Toss
with carrot in a large salad bowl; stir in creamy salad dressing and sugar.
Chill the coleslaw for 1 hour to blend flavors, stir again, and season with
salt and black pepper.
