quick-and-tart-cabbage-side-salad

Steps

Toss coleslaw mix, tomato, and cucumber together in a mixing bowl.

Whisk vinegar, Dijon mustard, and agave nectar together in a bowl until
emulsified; season with salt and pepper. Drizzle dressing over the vegetable
mixture. Turn the salad with a fork until vegetables are evenly coated in
dressing.

Ingredients

2 cups coleslaw mix

1 tomato, diced small

1/2 cucumber, diced small

1 tablespoon rice vinegar

1 teaspoon Dijon mustard

1/2 teaspoon agave nectar

salt and ground black pepper to taste
