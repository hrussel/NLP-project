slow-cooker-pork-tenderloin-with-beer-and-veggies

Steps

Combine the pork tenderloin, beer, vinegar, potatoes, carrots, mushrooms,
garlic, onion, salt, peppercorns, and sage in a slow cooker. Cover and cook on
low for 4 hours.
