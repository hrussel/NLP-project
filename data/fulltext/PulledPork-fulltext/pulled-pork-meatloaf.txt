pulled-pork-meatloaf

Steps

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Spray a loaf pan with cooking
spray.

Whisk eggs and milk together in a bowl. Add pork, bread crumbs, bell pepper,
green onion, mustard, and pepper; stir. Transfer pork mixture to the prepared
loaf pan. Press pork tightly into pan and spread barbeque sauce on top.

Bake in the preheated oven until heated through, about 1 hour. Let meatloaf
cool 5 to 10 minutes before slicing and serving.

Ingredients

cooking spray

2 eggs

1/2 cup milk

3 cups cooked pulled pork without sauce, chopped

1 cup bread crumbs

1/4 green bell pepper, chopped

1 green onion, chopped, or to taste

1 teaspoon dry mustard

1 teaspoon ground black pepper

1/2 cup barbeque sauce
