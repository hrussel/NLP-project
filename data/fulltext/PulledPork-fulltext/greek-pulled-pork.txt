greek-pulled-pork

Steps

Place pork tenderloin into a slow cooker, sprinkle meat generously with Greek
seasoning, and pour the jar of peperoncini peppers with their juice over the
pork. Cook 4 hours on High.

Shred the pork using 2 forks and allow to cook 10 to 15 more minutes.

Ingredients

1 (2 pound) pork tenderloin, fat trimmed

2 tablespoons Greek seasoning (such as Cavender's®), or more to taste

1 (16 ounce) jar peperoncini peppers (such as Mezzetta®)
