eatons-easy-pulled-pork

Steps

Arrange onion slices in the bottom of a slow cooker. Season pork with salt and
pepper and place over onion. Add vinegar and water.

Cook pork on Low for 8 hours. Transfer pork to a platter and shred with two
forks. Remove and discard about half the pork juices from slow cooker and stir
in shredded pork, barbeque sauce, and brown sugar.

Ingredients

1 onion, thinly sliced

4 1/2 pounds bone-in pork loin end roast

salt and ground black pepper to taste

3/4 cup cider vinegar

1/4 cup water

1/2 (18 ounce) bottle hickory brown sugar barbeque sauce

3 tablespoons brown sugar, or to taste
