spicy-pulled-pork-pushover

Steps

Mix tomato paste, vinegar, onion flakes, yellow mustard, molasses, garlic,
olive oil, and liquid smoke in a bowl to create a thick paste. Stir brown
sugar, kosher salt, cumin, chipotle pepper, paprika, black pepper, garlic
powder, mustard powder, and cayenne pepper into tomato paste mixture.

Place pork shoulder into a slow cooker and add paste mixture.

Cook on High for 4 hours. Shred pork with 2 forks and remove bone. Reduce heat
to Low and continue cooking for 1 to 2 more hours.

Ingredients

1/2 cup apple cider vinegar

2 tablespoons dried onion flakes

2 tablespoons prepared yellow mustard

2 tablespoons molasses

4 cloves garlic, minced

1 tablespoon olive oil

2 teaspoons liquid hickory smoke flavoring

7 tablespoons brown sugar

2 tablespoons kosher salt
