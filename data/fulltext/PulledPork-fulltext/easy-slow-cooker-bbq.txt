easy-slow-cooker-bbq

Steps

Place pork roast in a slow cooker. Pour vinegar over pork, coating all sides;
season with salt and pepper.

Cook on Low for 10 to 12 hours. Transfer pork to a platter. Drain and discard
juices from the slow cooker. Chop pork and return to the slow cooker. Add
barbeque sauce and continue to cook for 30 minutes.

Ingredients

1 (3 pound) boneless pork shoulder (Boston Butt) roast

3/4 cup cider vinegar

salt and ground black pepper to taste

1 cup barbeque sauce, or to taste
