memphis-pulled-pork

Steps

Spread onion slices and garlic cloves in the bottom of a slow cooker; stir in
beef broth, barbeque sauce, brown sugar, cider vinegar, and pepper. Add pork
roast.

Cover and cook on Low for 6 to 8 hours. Remove pork from slow cooker. Strain
juices and return to the slow cooker. Mix water and cornstarch in a bowl; stir
into juices until sauce is thickened.

Ingredients

1 onion, thinly sliced

6 cloves garlic, peeled

2 (14 ounce) cans beef broth

1 (18 ounce) bottle barbeque sauce

1/3 cup brown sugar

1/3 cup cider vinegar

1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

1 (4 pound) Boston butt pork shoulder roast

2 tablespoons cornstarch

3 tablespoons water
