newcastle-bbq-pulled-pork

Steps

Place onion, olive oil, and garlic in a slow cooker. Arrange pork over onion
mixture and add ale. Season with black pepper.

Cook on Low for 6 to 8 hours. Drain and discard liquid from slow cooker. Shred
pork with 2 forks and add barbeque sauce and liquid smoke.

Continue to cook on Low for 2 more hours.
