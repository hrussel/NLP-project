bbq-pork-sliders

Steps

Cut roast into 3 to 4 pieces. Place pork in a greased or lined slow cooker.
Combine remaining ingredients and pour over pork.

Cover and cook on HIGH for 3 to 4 hours or LOW for 6 to 7 hours until pork is
very tender. Shred with 2 forks. Serve on rolls, topping with coleslaw if
desired.

Ingredients

1 1/2 pounds shoulder or butt pork roast

3/4 cup barbecue sauce

1/2 cup water

1 tablespoon honey

1 tablespoon fresh grated orange peel

1 bay leaf, whole

1/2 teaspoon thyme leaves

1/4 teaspoon Spice Islands® Crushed Red Pepper

1/4 teaspoon Spice Islands® Fine Grind Black Pepper

12 dinner rolls

Optional topping: coleslaw
