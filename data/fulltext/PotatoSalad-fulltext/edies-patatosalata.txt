edies-patatosalata

Steps

In a small bowl, combine olive oil, wine vinegar, oregano, parsley, and onion.
Mix and set aside.

Add potatoes to a large pot of boiling, lightly salted water. Boil for about
20 minutes, or until potatoes are tender. Drain water and let cool.

When cooled, peel and dice. Place in a large bowl, add dressing and season
with salt and pepper to taste. Toss to coat, cover bowl and refrigerate for
several hours or overnight.

Ingredients

1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil

3 tablespoons red wine vinegar

1 teaspoon dried oregano

1 red onion, thinly sliced

5 large red potatoes

2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
