Steps

Place potatoes into a large saucepan, and fill with enough water to cover.
Bring to a boil, and cook for about 8 minutes, or until tender. Drain, and
cool slightly.

In a large bowl, stir together the mayonnaise and brown sugar. Stir in the
sweet potatoes, walnuts and raisins until evenly coated. Chill for at least 1
hour before serving.

Ingredients

2 pounds sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed

1 cup mayonnaise

1/2 cup packed brown sugar

1 cup chopped walnuts

1/2 cup raisins
