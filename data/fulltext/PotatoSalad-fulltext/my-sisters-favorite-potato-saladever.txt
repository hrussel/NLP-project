my-sisters-favorite-potato-saladever

Steps

Place potatoes in a pot with enough water to cover, and bring to a boil. Cook
15 minutes, or until tender. Drain and cool.

In a bowl, gently toss potatoes, mayonnaise, red wine vinegar, Dijon mustard,
dill, celery salt, salt, and pepper. Cover, and refrigerate at least 30
minutes before serving.

Ingredients

6 red potatoes, cut into chunks

3/4 cup mayonnaise

1/4 cup red wine vinegar

1 tablespoon Dijon mustard

1 tablespoon dried dill weed

1 teaspoon celery salt

salt and pepper to taste
