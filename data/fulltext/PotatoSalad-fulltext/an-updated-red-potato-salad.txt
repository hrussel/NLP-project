an-updated-red-potato-salad

Steps

Place potatoes into a large pot and cover with salted water; bring to a boil.
Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until tender, about 20 minutes. Drain and
cube potatoes.

Transfer potatoes to a large bowl; stir in mayonnaise, sour cream, vinegar,
sugar, onion, and celery seed until well coated. Gently mix in eggs and season
with salt and black pepper. Refrigerate until chilled.

Ingredients

8 red potatoes

1 1/2 cups mayonnaise (such as Hellmann's®/Best Foods®)

2/3 cup sour cream

3 tablespoons red wine vinegar

3 tablespoons white sugar

2 teaspoons dried minced onion

1 teaspoon celery seed

4 hard-cooked eggs, chopped

salt and ground black pepper to taste
