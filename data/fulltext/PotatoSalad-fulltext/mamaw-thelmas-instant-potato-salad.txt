mamaw-thelmas-instant-potato-salad

Steps

Pour 2 cups of warm water into a bowl. Stir in the instant potato flakes until
well blended. Mix in the mayonnaise, milk, relish, mustard and onion. Season
with salt and pepper. Mix well, and refrigerate until needed.

Ingredients

2 cups warm water

1 cup instant potato flakes

1 tablespoon mayonnaise

1/2 cup milk

1 tablespoon dill pickle relish

1 teaspoon prepared yellow mustard

1 small onion, chopped

salt and pepper to taste
