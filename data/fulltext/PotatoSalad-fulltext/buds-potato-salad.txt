buds-potato-salad

Steps

Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add potatoes and cook until
tender but still firm, about 15 minutes. Drain and allow to cool.

Slice cooled potatoes and layer in a large bowl or dish.

In a saucepan combine water, vinegar and sugar. Bring to a boil and cook for
one minute. Remove from heat and pour over potatoes. Cover and chill for at
least 12 hours.

Drain excess marinade and add onions, salt, pepper, celery seed and
mayonnaise. Mix well and serve chilled.

Ingredients

10 pounds potatoes

1 cup water

1 cup white wine vinegar

1 cup white sugar

2 onions, diced

salt and pepper to taste

1 tablespoon celery seed

2 cups mayonnaise
