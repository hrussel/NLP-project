dressing-for-potato-salad

Steps

Mix together the mayonnaise, sour cream, mustard, ketchup, Worcestershire
sauce, onion and salt and pepper. Refrigerate until ready to toss with
potatoes.

Ingredients

1/2 cup mayonnaise

1/2 cup sour cream

2 teaspoons prepared mustard

1 tablespoon ketchup

1 1/2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce

1/2 cup diced onion

salt and pepper to taste
