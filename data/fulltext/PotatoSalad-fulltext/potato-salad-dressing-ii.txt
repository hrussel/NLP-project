potato-salad-dressing-ii

Steps

In a saucepan, combine mustard, flour, butter, eggs, sugar, water and vinegar.
Cook over medium heat stirring often until mixture becomes thick and smooth.
Remove from heat and allow to cool.

Combine dressing mixture with creamy salad dressing, mix well. Fold into
cooked potatoes.

Ingredients

1/2 cup creamy salad dressing

1/2 teaspoon mustard powder

2 tablespoons all-purpose flour

1 teaspoon butter

2 eggs, beaten

3/4 cup white sugar

1/4 cup water

1/4 cup white wine vinegar
