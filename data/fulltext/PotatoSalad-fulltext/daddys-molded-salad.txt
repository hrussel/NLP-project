daddys-molded-salad

Steps

Grease or butter one 2 quart gelatin mold pan.

In a small saucepan, bring 1 cup of water to a boil; add gelatin and mix until
dissolved. Remove from heat and let cool to room temperature. Stir in
mayonnaise, cottage cheese, pineapples, walnuts and marshmallows.

Pour into molded pan and chill until firm. Remove onto serving dish and
garnish with halved cherries.
