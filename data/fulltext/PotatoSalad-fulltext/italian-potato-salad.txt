italian-potato-salad

Steps

Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add potatoes and cook until
tender but still firm, about 15 minutes. Drain, cool and chop.

In a large bowl, mix together the garlic, olive oil, vinegar and parsley. Add
potatoes and toss to evenly coat.

Cover and refrigerate overnight.

Ingredients

5 large potatoes, peeled and chopped

2 cloves garlic, minced

2/3 cup extra virgin olive oil

1/2 cup white wine vinegar

1/3 cup chopped fresh parsley
