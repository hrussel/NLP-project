ranch-potato-salad

Steps

Boil the potatoes for 10 to 15 minutes or until tender. Drain and set aside.

In a mixing bowl, mix together the mayonnaise, ranch salad dressing, celery,
peas, paprika, scallions and salt and pepper.

Add the potatoes, toss and refrigerate 1 hour before serving.

Ingredients

3 potatoes, peeled and cubed

1/4 cup low-fat mayonnaise

1/2 cup fat free ranch dressing

3/4 cup diced celery

1/2 cup frozen green peas, thawed

1 teaspoon paprika

1/4 cup chopped green onions

salt and pepper to taste
