dill-potato-salad

Steps

Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add potatoes and cook until
tender but still firm, about 15 minutes. Drain, cool, peel and chill.

Meanwhile, in a medium bowl combine sour cream, dill, parsley, Dijon, salt and
pepper.

Pour dressing over potatoes and toss gently. Chill before serving.

Ingredients

7 cups chopped new potatoes

1 (8 ounce) container sour cream

2 teaspoons chopped fresh dill weed

1 teaspoon dried parsley

2 tablespoons Dijon mustard

1/2 teaspoon salt

1/4 teaspoon pepper
