red-white-and-blue-potato-salad

Steps

Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil; add potatoes and cook until
tender, about 15 minutes. Drain, cool and dice the potatoes.

Combine the potatoes, radishes, onion, blue cheese and mayonnaise in a large
bowl; mix until the mayonnaise evenly coats the ingredients.

Ingredients

1 pound medium purple potatoes, scrubbed

2 bunches red radishes, trimmed and diced

1/2 white onion, diced

1/2 cup crumbled blue cheese

3 tablespoons mayonnaise
