warm-dijon-potato-salad-2

Steps

Place potatoes into a large pot and cover with salted water; bring to a boil.
Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until tender, about 20 minutes.
Drain and cut into cubes.

Combine mayonnaise, Parmesan cheese, Dijon mustard, vinegar, cayenne pepper,
salt, black pepper, and paprika in a large bowl. Add potatoes, red onion, and
green onions; gently toss to combine.

Ingredients

5 red potatoes

1/2 cup reduced-fat mayonnaise

2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese

1 1/2 tablespoons Dijon mustard

4 teaspoons cider vinegar

1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper

1/2 teaspoon salt

1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

1/4 teaspoon paprika

1/2 red onion, thinly sliced

2 green onions, chopped
