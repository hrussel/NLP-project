potato-salad

Steps

Place potatoes into a large pot and cover with salted water; bring to a boil.
Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until barely tender, about 10 minutes.
Drain. Rinse potatoes with cold water to cool completely; drain.

Stir mayonnaise, pickles, eggs, red onion, celery, mustard, vinegar, salt, and
pepper together in a large bowl. Fold potatoes into the mayonnaise mixture.

Ingredients

5 pounds red potatoes, chopped

3 cups mayonnaise

2 cups finely chopped pickles

5 hard-cooked eggs, chopped

1/2 cup chopped red onion

1/2 cup chopped celery

3 tablespoons prepared mustard

1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar

1 teaspoon salt

1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
