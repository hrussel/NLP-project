potato-salad-dressing-i

Steps

In a saucepan, combine the dry mustard, flour, butter, eggs, sugar, water and
vinegar. Cook over medium-high heat until thick and nice and smooth, stirring
often. If necessary, add up to 1 tablespoon additional flour. Set aside to
cool.

Blend together cooled dressing with one equal part creamy salad dressing. Pour
enough dressing over cooked potatoes to coat.

Ingredients

1/2 teaspoon mustard powder

1 tablespoon all-purpose flour

1 teaspoon butter

2 eggs, beaten

3/4 cup white sugar

1/4 cup water

1/4 cup white wine vinegar

1/4 cup creamy salad dressing (e.g. Miracle Whip)
