authentic-russian-salad-olivye

Steps

Bring a large pot of water to a boil; add potatoes and carrot. Return mixture
to a boil and add eggs; cook until potatoes are tender, 20 to 30 minutes.
Drain and slightly cool mixture. Chop potatoes and carrot; peel and chop eggs.

Mix potatoes, carrot, eggs, pickles, peas, ham, and parsley together in a
large bowl; stir in mayonnaise until salad is evenly coated.

Ingredients

6 potatoes, peeled

1 carrot, or more to taste

4 eggs

6 large pickles, cut into cubes

1 (15 ounce) can peas, drained

1/2 cup cubed fully cooked ham, or to taste

1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley, or to taste

1/2 cup mayonnaise, or to taste
