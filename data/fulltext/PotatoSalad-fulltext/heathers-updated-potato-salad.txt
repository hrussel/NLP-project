heathers-updated-potato-salad

Steps

Place potatoes into a large pot and cover with salted water; bring to a boil.
Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until tender, about 20 minutes. Drain and
let cool.

Mix mayonnaise, buttermilk, Dijon mustard, dill, red onion, and celery in a
large salad bowl; gently fold in the potatoes. Season with salt and black
pepper. Chill for 4 hours before serving.

Ingredients

5 pounds red potatoes, peeled and diced

1/2 cup mayonnaise

1/4 cup buttermilk

2 tablespoons Dijon mustard

2 tablespoons dried dill weed

1/2 cup diced red onion

1/2 cup diced celery

salt and ground black pepper to taste
