Steps

Place potatoes in saucepan. Cover with water. Heat to a boil. Cook over low
heat 20 minutes or until potatoes are tender. Drain. Cool and cut into cubes.

Mix broth, vinegar, flour, sugar, celery seed, salt and black pepper in
saucepan. Add onion. Cook until mixture boils and thickens, stirring
constantly. Cook over low heat 5 minutes or until onion is tender.

Toss potatoes, parsley and broth mixture until evenly coated. Serve warm.
