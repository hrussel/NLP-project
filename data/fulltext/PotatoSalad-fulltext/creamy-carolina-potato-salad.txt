creamy-carolina-potato-salad

Steps

Place potatoes into a large pot and cover with salted water; bring to a boil.
Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until tender, 20 to 25 minutes. Drain,
cool, and chop potatoes into 1-inch cubes. Combine chopped potatoes and eggs
in a large bowl.

Mix sour cream, mayonnaise, pimento peppers, green onions, bacon, mustard,
sugar, vinegar, salt, black pepper, celery seed, and garlic powder together in
a bowl. Pour dressing over potatoes and eggs; toss gently to coat. Sprinkle
potato salad with paprika.

Ingredients

2 pounds red potatoes

3 hard-cooked eggs, chopped

1/2 cup sour cream

1/2 cup mayonnaise

1/2 (4 ounce) jar chopped pimento peppers

2 green onions, chopped

2 slices cooked bacon, chopped

2 tablespoons yellow mustard

1 tablespoon white sugar

1 tablespoon red wine vinegar

1/2 teaspoon salt

1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

1/2 teaspoon celery seed

1/4 teaspoon garlic powder

1 pinch paprika, or as desired
