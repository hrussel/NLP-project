ez-kids-potato-salad

Steps

Whisk together the salad dressing, apple juice, onion flakes, dill, mustard
and salt and pepper. Pour over potatoes and toss to coat.

Ingredients

1/4 cup creamy salad dressing

1/4 cup apple juice

1 tablespoon dried minced onion

1 teaspoon dried dill weed

1 teaspoon prepared mustard

salt and pepper to taste

1 (15 ounce) can sliced potatoes, drained
