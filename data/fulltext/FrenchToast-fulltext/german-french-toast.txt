german-french-toast

Steps

Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. In a shallow bowl, beat together
eggs and milk.

Dip bread slices one at a time in egg mixture, and fry in butter until golden.
After turning once, top with a slice of muenster cheese, and cover until
cheese is melted.

Ingredients

1 teaspoon butter

2 eggs

1/2 cup milk

8 slices white bread

8 slices Muenster cheese
