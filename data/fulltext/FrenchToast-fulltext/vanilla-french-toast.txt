vanilla-french-toast

Steps

Whisk eggs, milk, sugar, vanilla and cinnamon in a medium bowl.

Melt butter in skillet over medium-high heat. Dip bread slices in egg mixture
and brown each side in skillet. Serve with maple syrup.
