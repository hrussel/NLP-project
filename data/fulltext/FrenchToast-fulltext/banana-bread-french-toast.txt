banana-bread-french-toast

Steps

In a shallow bowl, whisk together the eggs, sweetened condensed milk and
vanilla with a fork. Set aside.

Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Slice banana bread into 4
thick slices. Dip each slice into the egg mixture, then place in the hot pan.
Cook on each side until golden brown. Dust with confectioners' sugar just
before serving, if desired.

Ingredients

3 eggs

3 tablespoons sweetened condensed milk

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

2 tablespoons butter

1 loaf banana bread

confectioners' sugar for dusting
