peanut-butter-french-toast

Steps

Whisk together milk, eggs, peanut butter, sugar, vanilla extract, and cinnamon
in a large bowl.

Heat the oil in a griddle or frying pan over medium heat.

Dunk each slice of bread in egg mixture, soaking both sides. Place in pan, and
cook on both sides until golden, about 3 to 4 minutes per side. Serve hot.

Ingredients

1/2 cup milk

3 eggs

1/4 cup peanut butter

2 tablespoons white sugar

1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon

1 tablespoon vegetable oil

4 slices bread
