ultimate-french-toast

Steps

Combine eggs, milk and cinnamon; beat well. Dip bread into egg mixture until
completely coated.

Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat. Cook bread
slices until they are golden brown on both sides. Serve hot.

Ingredients

4 eggs

2 tablespoons milk

1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon

8 slices bread
