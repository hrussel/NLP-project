skiers-french-toast

Steps

Combine light corn syrup, butter and sugar in saucepan; simmer until syrup-
like. Pour mixture over the bottom of a 9"x 13" pan.

Slice bread into 12-16 slices; place over the syrup. Layer as needed.

Beat together the eggs, milk, vanilla, and salt. Pour over bread. Cover with
saran wrap. Refrigerate overnight.

Bake in a preheated oven 350 degree F (175 degrees C) for 45 minutes.

Cut into squares. Invert and serve.

Ingredients

2 tablespoons light corn syrup

1/2 cup butter

1 cup packed brown sugar

1 (1 pound) loaf unsliced white bread, with crust trimmed

5 eggs

1 1/2 cups milk

1 tablespoon vanilla extract

1/4 teaspoon salt
