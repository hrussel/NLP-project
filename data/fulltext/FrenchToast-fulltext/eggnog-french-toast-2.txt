eggnog-french-toast-2

Steps

Whisk the eggs. eggnog, cinnamon, and pumpkin pie spice together in a mixing
bowl until well blended. Pour the mixture into a shallow dish.

Preheat an electric skillet to 300 degrees F (150 degrees C). Lightly grease
the skillet.

Dip one slice of bread at a time into the eggnog mixture, being sure to coat
each side of the bread thoroughly.

Place the prepared bread slices into the preheated skillet, and cook, turning
once, until golden brown on each side. Place cooked slices on a serving plate
and cover with foil to keep warm until all French toast is cooked. Serve
immediately.

Ingredients

2 eggs, beaten slightly

1 1/2 cups eggnog

1 1/2 tablespoons ground cinnamon

1 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice

12 slices French bread
