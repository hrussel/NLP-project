french-toast-corners

Steps

Cut each slice of bread twice diagonally to make each slice into 4 triangles.

Beat together eggs, milk, vanilla and cinnamon. Dip the bread slices into egg
mixture.

Heat butter over medium-high heat in a frying pan or skillet. Cook bread until
golden brown. Serve warm with your favorite topping.

Ingredients

6 slices white bread

2 eggs

2/3 cup milk

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

1 tablespoon butter
