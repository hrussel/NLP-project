reduced-fat-french-toast

Steps

Beat together egg substitute, milk, vanilla and cinnamon. Dip bread slices in
egg mixture until both sides are soaked.

Spray a skillet or frying pan with cooking spray and heat over medium high
heat. Place bread slices into pan and cook until golden brown on both sides

Ingredients

1/2 cup egg substitute

2/3 cup skim milk

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

6 slices reduced calorie white bread
