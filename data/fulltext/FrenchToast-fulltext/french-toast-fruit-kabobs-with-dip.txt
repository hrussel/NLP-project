french-toast-fruit-kabobs-with-dip

Steps

Kabobs: Combine first 4 ingredients. Dip bread into mixture and fry in melted
butter until golden on both sides. Cut into strips and thread strips on
skewers alternating with the fruit of your choice.

Dip: Combine all ingredients and serve with kabobs.

Ingredients

French Toast:

2 eggs

1 tablespoon milk

1/4 teaspoon cinnamon

1 teaspoon maple syrup

4 slices bread

Butter for frying

Fresh fruit such as strawberries, kiwi, pineapple, bananas

Dip:

1/4 cup plain Greek style yogurt

1 tablespoon Smucker's® Simple Blends® Summer Fruit Fruit Spread
