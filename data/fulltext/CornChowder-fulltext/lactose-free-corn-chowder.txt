lactose-free-corn-chowder

Steps

In a 3 quart pot over high heat, combine the potatoes with enough water to
cover. Boil for one hour, adding water to cover potatoes as necessary. Add the
celery, leeks and scallions and boil for another hour.

Reduce heat to low, add the corn and parsley and heat through. Add the non-
dairy creamer just before serving. Enjoy!

Ingredients

5 potatoes, peeled and cubed

1 cup chopped celery

1/2 cup sliced leeks

1/2 cup chopped green onions

2 cups fresh corn kernels

1 tablespoon dried parsley

1 cup non-dairy creamer

salt and pepper to taste
