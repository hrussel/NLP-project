corn-chowder-iii

Steps

In a large skillet over medium heat, cook bacon until crisp. Drain, reserving
1 tablespoon of bacon grease.

Place bacon grease in a large stock pot over medium heat. Cook bell pepper,
onion and celery leaves in grease until just tender. Stir in bacon, corn and
water and bring slowly to a boil, stirring frequently. Stir in milk and heat
gently, without boiling. Serve hot.

Ingredients

1 pound bacon, diced

2 green bell peppers, chopped

2 onions, chopped

1 bunch fresh celery leaves

6 (15 ounce) cans cream-style corn

5 cups water

4 (12 fluid ounce) cans evaporated milk
