mexican-corn-chowder

Steps

In a large saucepan over medium heat, melt butter. Cook celery, onion and
carrot in butter until onion begins to soften. Stir in garlic, oregano, salt
and pepper and cook 2 minutes more. Stir in corn, chicken broth, cooked
chicken and chile pepper and let simmer until heated through. Stir in milk and
heat gently just before serving.

Ingredients

1/4 cup butter

1 stalk celery, chopped

1 onion, chopped

1 carrot, chopped

1 clove garlic, minced

1 teaspoon dried oregano

salt and pepper to taste

3 1/2 cups fresh corn kernels

1 cup chicken broth

2 boneless chicken breast halves, cooked and cubed

2 teaspoons chopped green chile pepper

2 cups milk
