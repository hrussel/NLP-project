easy-vegetarian-corn-chowder

Steps

In a large saucepan over medium heat, melt butter. Cook onions and celery in
butter 3 minutes. Whisk in flour and cook 6 minutes more, until light brown.
Whisk in broth and simmer 10 minutes.

Stir in creamed corn, corn, carrot, half-and-half, milk, nutmeg, pepper and
salt. Simmer over low heat 10 minutes more.

Ingredients

6 tablespoons butter

1/4 cup diced onion

1/2 cup diced celery

6 tablespoons all-purpose flour

2 (14.5 ounce) cans vegetable broth

2 (15 ounce) cans creamed corn

1 (15 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained

2 tablespoons shredded carrot

1 cup half-and-half cream

3/4 cup skim milk

1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg

1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

1 pinch salt
