conh-chowdah-corn-chowder

Steps

Remove corn kernels from the ears of corn.

Saute bacon, onions, and corn in a medium pot until the onions are translucent
and the bacon is crispy.

Add the heavy cream. Heat through over medium low heat. Season with salt and
pepper. Serve.

Ingredients

1 white onion, chopped

4 slices bacon, diced

6 ears fresh corn

2 quarts heavy whipping cream

salt to taste

ground black pepper to taste
