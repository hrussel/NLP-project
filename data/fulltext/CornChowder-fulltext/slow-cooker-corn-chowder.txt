slow-cooker-corn-chowder

Steps

In a slow cooker, place the potatoes, onions, ham, celery, corn, butter or
margarine and salt and pepper to taste. Add water to cover and two bouillon
cubes.

Cook on low setting for 8 to 9 hours and then stir in the evaporated milk.
Cook for 30 more minutes.

Ingredients

5 potatoes, peeled and cubed

2 onions, chopped

2 cups diced ham

3 stalks celery, chopped

1 (15.25 ounce) can whole kernel corn, undrained

2 tablespoons margarine

salt and pepper to taste

2 cubes chicken bouillon

1 (12 fluid ounce) can evaporated milk
