depression-era-corn-chowder

Steps

In a large pot over medium heat, combine broth, corn, onion and potatoes.
Bring to a boil, then reduce heat, cover and simmer 15 to 20 minutes, until
potatoes are just tender.

Stir in evaporated milk and butter until butter is just melted. Season with
salt and pepper and serve at once.

Ingredients

2 (14.5 ounce) cans chicken broth

2 (15 ounce) cans whole kernel corn

1 large white onion, diced

3 cups diced potatoes

2 (12 fluid ounce) cans evaporated milk

1/3 cup butter

salt and pepper to taste
