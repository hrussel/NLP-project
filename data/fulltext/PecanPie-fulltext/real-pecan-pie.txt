real-pecan-pie

Steps

In a large mixing bowl, slightly beat the eggs.

Stir in corn syrup, sugar, and vanilla. Mix well.

Lightly stir in pecans, till well-coated with egg mixture.

Pour into pie shell. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 20 minutes.
Turn the oven down to 300 degrees F (150 degrees C), and bake for another 30
minutes.

Cool before serving. Store leftovers covered in refrigerator.

Ingredients

2 eggs

3/4 cup corn syrup

3/4 cup white sugar

1 1/2 cups pecan halves

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

1 recipe pastry for a 9 inch single crust pie
