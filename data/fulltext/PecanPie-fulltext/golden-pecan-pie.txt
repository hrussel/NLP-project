golden-pecan-pie

Steps

Heat oven to 375 degrees F. Place pie crust in 9-inch glass pie pan as
directed on box for One-Crust Filled Pie.

In large bowl, mix brown sugar, flour, corn syrup, vanilla and eggs until well
blended. Stir in pecans and butter. Pour into crust-lined pan.

Bake 40 to 50 minutes or until filling is puffed and pie is golden brown. Cool
completely, about 2 hours. Store in refrigerator.
