old-fashioned-honey-pecan-pie

Steps

In a saucepan bring the honey to a boil. Quickly beat the eggs into the honey.
Add butter, vanilla, nuts, and nutmeg. Pour into the pie shell.

Bake at 325 degrees F (165 degrees C) for 25 minutes or until set.

Ingredients

1 cup honey

3 eggs, beaten

3 tablespoons butter

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

1 cup chopped pecans

1 pinch ground nutmeg

1 recipe pastry for a 9 inch single crust pie
