pecan-pie-vii

Steps

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C.)

In a large bowl, combine brown sugar, flour, margarine, milk, vanilla and
eggs. Blend until smooth. Stir in chopped pecans. Pour into pie crust. Place
whole pecan meats gently on top in decorative manner.

Bake in the preheated oven for 35 to 40 minutes, or until golden brown and
filling is set.

Ingredients

1 (9 inch) deep dish pie crust

2 cups light brown sugar

1 tablespoon all-purpose flour

1/2 cup melted margarine

1 cup evaporated milk

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

3 eggs

1 cup chopped pecans

1 cup pecan halves
