peanut-butter-chip-pecan-pie

Steps

Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C.)

In a large bowl, combine flour and sugar. Blend in melted butter, eggs and
vanilla. Stir in peanut butter chips and chopped pecans. Pour filling into pie
shell.

Bake in the preheated oven for 30 to 40 minutes, or until golden brown.

Ingredients

1/2 cup all-purpose flour

1 cup white sugar

1/2 cup melted butter

2 eggs

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

1 cup peanut butter chips

1 cup chopped pecans

1 (9 inch) unbaked pie crust
