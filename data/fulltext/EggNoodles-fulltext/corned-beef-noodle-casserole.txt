corned-beef-noodle-casserole

Steps

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

Cook noodles until tender and drain.

Combine noodles, corned beef, cheese, cream of chicken soup, milk and onion.
Pour into a 2 quart casserole dish. Top with buttered bread crumbs.

Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 45 minutes.

Ingredients

1 (8 ounce) package wide egg noodles

1 (12 ounce) can corned beef

4 ounces processed cheese food (eg. Velveeta)

1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup

1 cup milk

1/2 cup chopped onion

1 cup seasoned dry bread crumbs
