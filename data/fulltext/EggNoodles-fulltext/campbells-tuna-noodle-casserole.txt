campbells-tuna-noodle-casserole

Steps

Stir soup, milk, peas, tuna and noodles in 3-quart casserole.

Bake at 400 degrees F for 30 minutes or until hot. Stir.

Mix bread crumbs with butter in bowl and sprinkle over tuna mixture. Bake for
5 minutes more.
