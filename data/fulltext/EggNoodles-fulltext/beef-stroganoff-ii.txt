beef-stroganoff-ii

Steps

Brown the beef strips in a large skillet over medium high heat and drain
excess fat. Add the onion and saute for 5 minutes, or until tender. Stir in
the flour, salt, paprika and ground black pepper.

Add the soup, reduce heat to medium low and let simmer for 15 minutes. Then
add the sour cream, stirring well.

Prepare egg noodles according to package directions. Serve beef mixture over
the egg noodles.

Ingredients

1 pound sirloin steak, cut into bite size strips

1 onion, chopped

1 1/2 tablespoons all-purpose flour

3/4 teaspoon salt

1/4 teaspoon paprika

1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper

1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup

1 1/4 cups fat free sour cream

1 (8 ounce) package egg noodles
