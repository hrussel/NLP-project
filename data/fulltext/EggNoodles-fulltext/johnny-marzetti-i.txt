johnny-marzetti-i

Steps

Cook noodles in a large pot of boiling water until done. Drain.

Brown meat in a large skillet. Add onions, pepper, mushrooms, and celery; cook
until soft.

Combine hamburger mixture with tomatoes, beans, and cooked noodles in a 3 - 4
quart baking dish.

Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 30 minutes. Top with cheddar cheese,
and bake 30 minutes longer.

Ingredients

1 pound lean ground beef

2 onions, chopped

1 green bell pepper, chopped

1/2 pound fresh mushrooms, sliced

1 cup chopped celery

1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes

1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans

1 (16 ounce) package wide egg noodles

1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
