fairy-godmother-rice

Steps

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 2 quart casserole
dish.

Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Brown noodles in the butter.

In a large bowl combine browned noodles, rice, soup mix, broth, water
chestnuts and soy sauce. Mix well and transfer to prepared casserole dish.

Bake for 45 minutes, or until liquid has been absorbed and casserole is
browned and crispy on top.

Ingredients

1/2 cup butter

5 ounces thin egg noodles

2 cups uncooked instant rice

2 (1 ounce) packages dry onion soup mix

4 cups vegetable broth

1 (5 ounce) can water chestnuts, drained and sliced

soy sauce to taste
