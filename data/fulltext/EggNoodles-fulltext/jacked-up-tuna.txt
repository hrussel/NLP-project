jacked-up-tuna

Steps

Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add the egg noodles, and
cook until tender, about 6 minutes. Drain.

While the noodles are cooking, mix together the sour cream, milk, garlic and
salt; taste and adjust seasoning if desired. Stir into drained noodles along
with peas and tuna. Heat through over low heat, and serve.

Ingredients

1 (8 ounce) package uncooked egg noodles

1 (15 ounce) can peas, drained

1 (6 ounce) can tuna, drained and flaked

1/2 cup sour cream

1/4 cup milk

garlic powder to taste

salt and pepper to taste
