corn-noodle-casserole

Steps

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a medium
casserole dish.

Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add noodles, cook for 6
to 8 minutes, until al dente, and drain.

Place turkey in a skillet over medium heat, and cook until evenly brown. Drain
grease.

In a bowl, gently mix cooked noodles and turkey, corn, and soup. Season with
salt and pepper. Transfer to the prepared baking dish.

Bake 20 minutes in the preheated oven, until bubbly.

Ingredients

1 (16 ounce) package uncooked egg noodles

1 pound ground turkey

1 (15 ounce) can canned or frozen corn

1 (10.75 ounce) can cream of mushroom soup

salt and pepper to taste
