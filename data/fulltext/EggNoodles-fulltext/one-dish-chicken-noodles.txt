one-dish-chicken-noodles

Steps

Heat oil in large skillet. Cube chicken into bite size pieces and brown in hot
oil. Meanwhile, in a large saucepan, boil noodles in water until soft, then
drain. Add hot cooked noodles to chicken in skillet and pour in gravy and
peas. Stir together until ingredients are covered/coated with gravy. Cover
skillet, reduce heat to medium low and let simmer for 10 minutes until gravy
and peas are hot. Serve directly from the skillet.

Ingredients

1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breast meat

2 (12 ounce) jars chicken gravy

1 (12 ounce) package egg noodles

3 cups frozen green peas, thawed
