easy-turkey-and-noodles-skillet

Steps

In 10-inch skillet, melt butter over medium-high heat. Cook onion in butter 4
to 6 minutes, stirring frequently, just until tender.

Stir in all remaining ingredients except turkey and potato chips. Heat to
boiling. Reduce heat; cover and simmer 10 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add
turkey. Cover and continue to cook 5 to 8 minutes, stirring occasionally,
until noodles are tender. Sprinkle with potato chips.

Ingredients

2 tablespoons butter or margarine

1/2 cup onion, chopped

1 (18 ounce) can Progresso™ Recipe Starters™ creamy roasted garlic with
chicken stock cooking sauce

1 1/2 cups uncooked medium egg noodles

1 cup Green Giant® Valley Fresh Steamers® frozen sweet peas

1/2 cup milk

1 teaspoon Italian seasoning

2 cups cubed cooked turkey

Garnishes, If Desired:

Coarsely crushed sour cream and onion potato chips
