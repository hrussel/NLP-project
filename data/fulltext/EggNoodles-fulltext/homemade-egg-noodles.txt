homemade-egg-noodles

Steps

Sift together the flour, salt and baking powder. Add egg yolks and mix until
dry ingredients are moistened.

Press into a ball and cut in quarters. Roll out on floured surface 1/8 to 1/4
inch thick; cut to desired width and length. Lay on linen dish towel or wooden
dowel to dry.

Add to broth such as chicken or turkey and cook until done.

Ingredients

2 cups all-purpose flour

1/4 teaspoon salt

1/4 teaspoon baking powder

4 egg yolks
