egg-noodles

Steps

In a large bowl, stir together the flour and salt. Add the beaten egg, milk,
and butter. Knead dough until smooth, about 5 minutes. Let rest in a covered
bowl for 10 minutes.

On a floured surface, roll out to 1/8 or 1/4 inch thickness. Cut into desired
lengths and shapes.

Allow to air dry before cooking.

To cook fresh pasta, in a large pot with boiling salted water cook until al
dente.

Ingredients

2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

1 pinch salt

2 eggs, beaten

1/2 cup milk

1 tablespoon butter
