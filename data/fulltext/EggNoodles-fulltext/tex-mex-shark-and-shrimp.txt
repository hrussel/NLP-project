tex-mex-shark-and-shrimp

Steps

Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add egg noodles, cook for
6 to 8 minutes, until al dente, and drain.

Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Mix in the shark, shrimp,
and tomatoes with green chiles. Cover, and cook 15 minutes, or until shark is
easily flaked with a fork.

Serve the shark mixture over the cooked egg noodles. Sprinkle with mozzarella
cheese, and season with pepper.

Ingredients

1 (16 ounce) package uncooked wide egg noodles

1 teaspoon olive oil

1 pound shark steaks, cut into chunks

1 pound frozen medium shrimp

1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes and green chiles

2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

ground black pepper to taste
