sesame-peanut-noodle-salad

Steps

Stir together the peanut butter, peanut oil, tea, ginger, garlic, jalapeno
pepper, salt, sugar, soy sauce, vinegar, and sesame oil in a large mixing bowl
until incorporated. Place the egg noodles in a large bowl; add the cucumber
and cilantro and mix together with your hands. Pour the peanut sauce over the
noodles; mix with your hands until evenly coated.

Ingredients

1 cup natural peanut butter

2 tablespoons peanut oil

1/2 cup brewed black tea

1/2 tablespoon minced fresh ginger

3 cloves garlic, minced

1/2 jalapeno pepper, minced

1/2 teaspoon salt

1 1/2 teaspoons white sugar

1 tablespoon light soy sauce

2 tablespoons rice vinegar

1 1/2 tablespoons sesame oil

2 pounds cooked egg noodles

1 cup peeled, seeded, and julienned cucumber

1 cup fresh cilantro leaves
