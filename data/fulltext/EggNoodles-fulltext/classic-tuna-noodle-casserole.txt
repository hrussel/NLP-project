classic-tuna-noodle-casserole

Steps

Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.

Mix soup, milk, peas, pimiento, tuna and noodles in 1 1/2-quart baking dish.

Bake for 20 minutes.

Mix bread crumbs with butter. Sprinkle on top. Bake 5 minutes or until hot.

Ingredients

1 (10.75 ounce) can Campbell's® Condensed Cream of Celery Soup or Campbell's®
Condensed 98% Fat Free Cream of Celery Soup

1/2 cup milk

1 cup cooked peas

2 tablespoons chopped pimentos

2 (6 ounce) cans tuna, drained and flaked

2 cups hot cooked medium egg noodles

2 tablespoons dry bread crumbs

1 tablespoon butter or margarine, melted
