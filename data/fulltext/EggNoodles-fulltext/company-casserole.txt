company-casserole

Steps

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Bring a large pot of lightly
salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al
dente; drain.

In a skillet over medium heat, brown the ground beef with the onion until no
pink shows; drain. Mix in 1 of the cans of tomato sauce, salt, pepper and
cinnamon. Pour into shallow 3 quart casserole baking dish.

Pour in noodles in an even layer. Top with cottage cheese; sprinkle with
onions and Cheddar cheese. Pour on remaining can of tomato sauce.

Bake in a preheated over for 30 minutes.

Ingredients

1 (8 ounce) package egg noodles

1 pound lean ground beef

1 onion, chopped

2 (7 ounce) cans tomato sauce with mushrooms

1 teaspoon salt

1/4 teaspoon black pepper

1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon

1 cup cottage cheese

1/2 cup chopped green onions

1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
