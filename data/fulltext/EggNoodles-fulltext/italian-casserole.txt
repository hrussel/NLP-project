italian-casserole

Steps

In large skillet, brown ground beef and onion. Stir in tomatoes, tomato paste,
salt, parsley, garlic salt and pepper, and simmer over low heat for 3 hours.

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Bring a large pot of lightly
salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al
dente; drain.

In a 2 quart casserole dish, combine noodles and meat mixture. Top with cheese
slices and bake for 15 to 20 minutes, or until cheese is melted.

Ingredients

3/4 pound lean ground beef

1 onion, chopped

1 (28 ounce) can whole peeled tomatoes, chopped

1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste

1 teaspoon salt

1 tablespoon dried parsley

1/2 teaspoon garlic salt

black pepper to taste

8 ounces wide egg noodles

1 (12 ounce) package process sharp cheddar cheese singles
