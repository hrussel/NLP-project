swanson-sensational-chicken-noodle-soup

Steps

Heat broth, black pepper, carrot and celery in 2-quart saucepan over medium-
high heat to a boil.

Stir noodles and chicken in saucepan. Reduce heat to medium. Cook 10 minutes
or until noodles are tender, stirring often.

Ingredients

4 cups Swanson® Chicken Broth (Regular, Natural Goodness™ or Certified
Organic)

Generous dash ground black pepper

1 medium carrot, sliced

1 stalk celery, sliced

1/2 cup uncooked extra-wide egg noodles

1 cup shredded cooked chicken or turkey
