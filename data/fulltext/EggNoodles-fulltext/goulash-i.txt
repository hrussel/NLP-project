goulash-i

Steps

Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8
to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.

In a large skillet, over medium heat, brown the ground beef and onion
together. Drain off the grease. Add salt, pepper, and garlic salt to taste.

Stir in the corn, kidney beans, and tomato soup. Stir in the cooked egg
noodles and mix thoroughly.

Ingredients

3 cups dry egg noodles

1 pound ground beef

1 medium onion, chopped

salt to taste

ground black pepper to taste

garlic salt to taste

1 (15 ounce) can corn

1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans

1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed tomato soup
