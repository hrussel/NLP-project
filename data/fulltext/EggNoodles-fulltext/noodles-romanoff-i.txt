Steps

In a large pot with boiling salted water cook egg noodles until al dente.
Drain.

In a large bowl mix together the cottage cheese, sour cream, minced onion,
garlic, Worcestershire sauce, red pepper sauce, and salt. Stir in cooked egg
noodles. Pour into a greased 2 quart casserole dish.

Bake in a preheated 350 degree F (175 degrees C) for 30 minutes, or until
heated through and bubbly. Serve warm.

Ingredients

1 (8 ounce) package wide egg noodles

1 (8 ounce) container cottage cheese

1 (8 ounce) container sour cream

1/4 tablespoon finely chopped onion

1/4 teaspoon minced garlic

1/2 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce

1 dash hot pepper sauce

1/2 teaspoon salt

2 tablespoons shredded Cheddar cheese
