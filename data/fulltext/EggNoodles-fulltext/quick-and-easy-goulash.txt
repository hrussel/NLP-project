quick-and-easy-goulash

Steps

Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add egg noodles and cook
for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.

In a skillet over medium heat, brown the beef; drain.

Heat through over medium the beef, spaghetti sauce, corn, chopped onion and
cheese.

Ingredients

1 (12 ounce) package egg noodles

1 pound lean ground beef

1 (26 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce

1 (15.25 ounce) can whole kernel corn

1 onion, chopped

1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
