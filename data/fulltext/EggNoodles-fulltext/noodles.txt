noodles

Steps

Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8
to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.

Return pasta to pot with chicken, soup, garlic salt and pepper over medium
heat. Heat through, 5 minutes.

Ingredients

1 (16 ounce) package wide egg noodles

2 (5 ounce) cans chunk chicken, drained

2 (10.75 ounce) cans condensed cream of mushroom soup

1/2 teaspoon garlic salt

1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
