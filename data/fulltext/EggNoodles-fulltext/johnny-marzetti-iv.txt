johnny-marzetti-iv

Steps

Cook pasta in a large pot of boiling salted water until al dente. Drain.

In a large skillet, cook ground beef, onion, and mushrooms. Drain grease. Mix
in garlic salt, ground black pepper, sugar, Worcestershire sauce, and tomato
sauce. Simmer for 30 minutes.

In a greased 2 quart casserole dish, layer half of the cooked egg noodles,
then half of the sauce mixture, followed by half of the grated cheese. Repeat.

Bake in a preheated 375 degree (190 degree C) oven for 20 to 30 minutes.

Ingredients

1 (8 ounce) package wide egg noodles

1 pound lean ground beef

1 onion, chopped

1 (4.5 ounce) can sliced mushrooms

1 pinch garlic salt

ground black pepper to taste

1 1/2 tablespoons white sugar

1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce

2 (15 ounce) cans tomato sauce

8 ounces sharp Cheddar cheese, shredded
