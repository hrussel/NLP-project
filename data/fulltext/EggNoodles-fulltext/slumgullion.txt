slumgullion

Steps

In a large skillet, cook and stir ground meat until browned. Drain off excess
fat.

Stir in corn and tomato soup. Simmer while the noodles are cooking, about 10
minutes.

Cook pasta in a large pot of boiling water until al dente. Drain.

Stir noodles into ground beef mixture, and sprinkle parmesan cheese on top.
Serve immediately.

Ingredients

1 pound lean ground beef

1 (11 ounce) can whole kernel corn

1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed tomato soup

1 (8 ounce) package wide egg noodles

1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
