broccoli-noodles-and-cheese-casserole

Steps

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Bring a large pot of lightly
salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al
dente; drain.

Steam broccoli until bright green and tender, 5 to 10 minutes. Combine
broccoli, pasta and cottage cheese in 2 quart baking dish; mix well.

Sprinkle pasta mixture with Cheddar cheese and bake for 8 to 10 minutes, until
cheese is bubbly.

Ingredients

1 (16 ounce) package egg noodles

1 head broccoli, cut into florets

2 cups cottage cheese

2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
