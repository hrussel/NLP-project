leftover-ham-and-noodles

Steps

In a medium saucepan, combine ham, black pepper, garlic, and 1 1/2 quarts
water. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer for 15 minutes.

Add onion and celery; continuing cooking until vegetables are tender, about 5
minutes.

Add noodles, and cook until al dente. Serve hot.

Ingredients

1 slice ham

ground black pepper to taste

1 clove garlic, minced

1 1/2 quarts water

1 onion, chopped

2 stalks celery, chopped

1 (16 ounce) package wide egg noodles
