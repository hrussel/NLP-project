anns-sisters-meatloaf-recipe

Steps

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

In a large bowl combine the beef, eggs, crumbs, ketchup, MSG, water and soup
mix. Mix well and spoon mixture into loaf pan. Cover with 2 strips of bacon,
then cover with tomato sauce.

Bake in preheated oven for 1 hour.

Ingredients

2 pounds lean ground beef

2 eggs

1 1/2 cups dry bread crumbs

1/4 cup ketchup

1 teaspoon monosodium glutamate (MSG)

1/2 cup warm water

1 (1 ounce) package dry onion soup mix

2 slices bacon

1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
