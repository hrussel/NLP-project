meatloaf-with-truvia-natural-sweetener

Steps

Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.

Mix all loaf ingredients well and place in a baking dish. Shape into a loaf.

Mix ingredients for sauce and spread on top of loaf. Bake for 1 hour.

Ingredients

For the Loaf:

1/2 pound ground sirloin

1/2 pound ground chuck

2 garlic cloves, smashed and chopped

1/2 cup chopped onion

1/2 cup chopped celery

1 teaspoon chili pepper

1/2 cup chopped bell pepper

2 eggs, lightly beaten

2 tablespoons Truvia® natural sweetener spoonable*

1 lemon zested

8 ounces canned diced tomatoes with juice

1/2 cup bran flakes, ground

1 teaspoon salt

1/2 teaspoon cracked black pepper

For the Sauce:

1/2 cup ketchup

1/2 cup canned pureed tomatoes

1 teaspoon cumin

1/2 cup Truvia® natural sweetener spoonable**

1 dash Worcestershire sauce

1 tablespoon prepared mustard
