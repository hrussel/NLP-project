banana-meatloaf

Steps

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.

In a large bowl, combine ground beef, bread crumbs, onion, banana, salt,
pepper, paprika and dry mustard until well mixed. Shape into a loaf and place
in prepared pan.

Bake in preheated oven 1 hour, or until no longer pink in the center.

Ingredients

1 pound lean ground beef

1 cup fresh bread crumbs

1 tablespoon minced onion

3/4 cup mashed banana

1/2 teaspoon salt

1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper

1/4 teaspoon paprika

1/2 teaspoon ground mustard
