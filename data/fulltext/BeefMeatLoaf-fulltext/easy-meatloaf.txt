easy-meatloaf

Steps

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

In a large bowl, combine the beef, egg, onion, milk and bread OR cracker
crumbs. Season with salt and pepper to taste and place in a lightly greased
5x9 inch loaf pan, OR form into a loaf and place in a lightly greased 9x13
inch baking dish.

In a separate small bowl, combine the brown sugar, mustard and ketchup. Mix
well and pour over the meatloaf.

Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 1 hour.

Ingredients

1 1/2 pounds ground beef

1 egg

1 onion, chopped

1 cup milk

1 cup dried bread crumbs

salt and pepper to taste

2 tablespoons brown sugar

2 tablespoons prepared mustard

1/3 cup ketchup
