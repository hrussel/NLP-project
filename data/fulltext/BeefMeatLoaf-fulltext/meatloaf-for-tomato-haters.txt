meatloaf-for-tomato-haters

Steps

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

Mix onion, bell pepper, bread crumbs, hot pepper sauce, egg, salt, and pepper
together in a large bowl. Add ground beef; mix.

Press beef mixture into a loaf pan. Sprinkle brown gravy mix over meat.

Bake in preheated oven until cooked through and browned, 1 hour. Remove from
oven and let rest for 10 minutes before serving.

Ingredients

1 cup diced onion

1 cup diced green bell pepper

3/4 cup Italian-style bread crumbs

1/4 cup hot pepper sauce

1 egg

1 teaspoon salt

1 teaspoon ground black pepper

2 pounds ground beef

1 (.75 ounce) packet dry brown gravy mix
