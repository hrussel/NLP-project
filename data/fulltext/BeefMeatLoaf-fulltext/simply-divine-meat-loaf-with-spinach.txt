simply-divine-meat-loaf-with-spinach

Steps

Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).

In a large bowl, mix the ground beef, eggs, sour cream, Worcestershire sauce,
dry onion soup mix, Parmesan cheese, salad dressing, bread crumbs, and
spinach. Transfer to a 5x9 inch pan, and cover with aluminum foil.

Bake 45 minutes in the preheated oven. Remove foil, and continue baking 15
minutes, to a minimum internal temperature of 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).
Let stand 5 minutes before slicing.

Ingredients

1 1/2 pounds ground beef

2 eggs

1/2 cup sour cream

2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce

1 (1 ounce) package dry onion soup mix

1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

1/2 cup Italian salad dressing

1 1/2 cups Italian seasoned bread crumbs

1 cup chopped fresh spinach
