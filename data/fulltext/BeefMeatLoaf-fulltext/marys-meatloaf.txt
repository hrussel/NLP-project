marys-meatloaf

Steps

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

In a large bowl, thoroughly mix ground beef, onion, salt, pepper and garlic
powder. Mix in bread, milk, egg, 2 tablespoons mustard and 1/4 cup ketchup.

Press the meat mixture into a 9x5 inch loaf pan. In a small bowl, stir
together remaining mustard, ketchup and brown sugar; pour on top of the meat
loaf.

Bake in preheated oven for 2 hours.

Ingredients

1 1/2 pounds lean ground beef

1 large onion, chopped

salt to taste

ground black pepper to taste

garlic powder to taste

6 slices white bread, cut into cubes

1/2 cup milk

1 egg

3 tablespoons yellow mustard, divided

1/2 cup ketchup, divided

3 tablespoons brown sugar
