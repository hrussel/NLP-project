turffaloaf

Steps

Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Lightly grease a 5x9 inch loaf
pan.

In a large bowl, mix the turkey, buffalo, bread crumbs, dry onion soup mix,
Parmesan cheese, Italian seasoning, egg, dill dip, blue cheese dressing, and
Worcestershire sauce. Transfer to the prepared loaf pan.

Cover with aluminum foil, and bake 45 minutes in the preheated oven. Remove
foil, and continue baking 15 minutes, to an internal temperature of 160
degrees F (70 degrees C). Let stand 10 minutes before slicing.

Ingredients

1 pound ground turkey

1/2 pound ground buffalo

4 slices day-old bread, processed into crumbs

1 (1 ounce) package dry onion soup mix

1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

2 teaspoons Italian seasoning

1 egg, lightly beaten

1/2 cup creamy dill dip

1/2 cup blue cheese dressing

2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
