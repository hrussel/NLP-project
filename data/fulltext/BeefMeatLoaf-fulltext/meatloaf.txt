meatloaf

Steps

In a large bowl, combine ground meat, soup, chopped onion, rolled oats, eggs,
steak sauce, ketchup and mustard. Mix well.

Shape into a loaf, and pat Into a loaf pan.

Bake in a preheated 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) oven for 1 hour or until
done.

Ingredients

2 pounds lean ground beef

1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed tomato soup

1 onion, finely diced

1 cup rolled oats

2 eggs, beaten

1 tablespoon steak sauce

1/2 cup ketchup

3 tablespoons prepared mustard
