easy-salsa-meatloaf

Steps

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

In a large bowl, mix the ground sirloin, salsa, bread crumbs, cheese, egg, and
garlic. Season with parsley, salt, and pepper. Transfer to a 5x9 inch loaf
pan.

Bake 45 minutes in the preheated oven, or to a minimum internal temperature of
160 degrees F (72 degrees C).

Ingredients

1 pound ground beef sirloin

1/2 cup salsa

1/2 cup Italian seasoned bread crumbs

1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

1 egg, lightly beaten

3 cloves garlic, minced

dried parsley to taste

salt and pepper to taste
