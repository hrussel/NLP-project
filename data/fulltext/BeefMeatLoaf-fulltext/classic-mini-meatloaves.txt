classic-mini-meatloaves

Steps

Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. Line shallow baking pan with aluminum foil;
spray foil with cooking spray.

Mix beef, tomato sauce, Egg Beaters, oats and soup mix in large bowl until
well blended. Shape into 4 mini meatloaves, about 4x3 inches; place in
prepared pan. Spread ketchup evenly over tops of meatloaves.

Bake 25 minutes or until meatloaves are cooked through (160 degrees F). Let
stand 5 minutes before serving.
