simply-amazing-meatloaf

Steps

Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).

In a mixing bowl, combine ground beef, Spanish rice and sauce mix, eggs, 1
tablespoon of ketchup, and mustard. Mix thoroughly.

Coat the inside of a 9x5 inch loaf pan with 2 tablespoons of the ketchup.
Transfer the meat mixture to the loaf pan. Top with the remaining ketchup.

Bake in a preheated oven for 1 hour or until cooked through.

Ingredients

1 1/2 pounds lean ground beef

1 (4.4 ounce) package Spanish rice and sauce mix

2 eggs

1 tablespoon ketchup

1 tablespoon yellow mustard

2 tablespoons ketchup
