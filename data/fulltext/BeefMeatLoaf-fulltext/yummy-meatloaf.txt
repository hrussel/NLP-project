yummy-meatloaf

Steps

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

In a large bowl, combine the beef, onion, crushed crackers, salt, ground black
pepper, steak sauce, eggs and water and mix well. Transfer to an ungreased
9x13 inch baking dish. Form into a loaf about 6x10 inches.

Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 1 hour, or until center is
completely cooked.

Ingredients

2 pounds ground beef

1/2 onion, chopped

1 (10 ounce) package saltine crackers, crushed

salt to taste

1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

1 tablespoon steak sauce

2 eggs

3/4 cup water
