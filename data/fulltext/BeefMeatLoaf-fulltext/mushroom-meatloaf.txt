mushroom-meatloaf

Steps

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

In a large mixing bowl, combine ground meat, minced mushrooms, bread crumbs,
onion, ketchup, eggs, and salt and pepper. Mix well. Spread 1/2 of the mixture
into the bottom of a loaf pan. Arrange 6 whole mushrooms stem down into meat.
Top with rest of meat, patting to combine both halves.

Bake for 1 hour and 45 minutes, or until done. Internal temperature should
measure 160 degrees F (70 degrees C) when done.

Ingredients

2 pounds lean ground beef

1/2 pound fresh mushrooms, all minced except for 6

3/4 cup fresh bread crumbs

1/2 cup minced onion

1/2 cup ketchup

2 eggs, beaten

1 1/2 teaspoons salt

1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
