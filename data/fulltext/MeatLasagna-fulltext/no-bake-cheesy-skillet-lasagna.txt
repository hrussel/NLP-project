no-bake-cheesy-skillet-lasagna

Steps

In 12-inch skillet, cook sausage over medium-high heat 5 to 7 minutes,
stirring occasionally, until no longer pink; drain.

Stir in 2 cups water and all remaining ingredients except cheese and basil.
Heat to boiling, stirring occasionally.

Reduce heat to medium; simmer uncovered about 10 minutes, stirring
occasionally, until pasta is tender. Remove from heat; sprinkle with cheese.
Cover, let stand 2 to 3 minutes or until cheese is melted. Sprinkle with
basil.

Ingredients

1 pound bulk Italian pork sausage

1 (16 ounce) bag frozen red bell pepper and onions for stir-fry, thawed and
drained

1 (18 ounce) can Progresso™ Recipe Starters™ creamy three cheese cooking sauce

3 cups uncooked mini lasagna (mafalda) noodles or medium egg noodles

1 teaspoon Italian seasoning

1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Garnishes, if Desired:

Chopped fresh or whole basil leaves
