Steps

Cook beef with garlic in skillet until browned. Pour off fat.

Add broth, tomatoes and Italian seasoning. Heat to a boil.

Stir in pasta. Cook over medium heat 10 min. or until pasta is done. Stir in
cheese. Serve with additional cheese if desired.
