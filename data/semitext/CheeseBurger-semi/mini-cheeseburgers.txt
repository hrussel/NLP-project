Preheat an oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Line a baking sheet withaluminum foil.
Mix ground beef, 1/2 cup ketchup, 1/2 cup shredded cheese, egg, and garlicpowder in a large bowl.
Form mixture into 1 inch balls, and gently press ontoprepared baking sheet to form mini-burgers.
Bake in preheated oven for 20 minutes.
Spread remaining ketchup on burgers,and sprinkle with remaining cheese.
Return burgers to the oven, and bake untilcheese is melted and bubbly, about 5 minutes.
