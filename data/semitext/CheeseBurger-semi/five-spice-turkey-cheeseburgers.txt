Whisk together the bread crumbs, egg white, onion, soy sauce, minced ginger,five-spice powder, ground ginger, and pepper in a large bowl.
Mix in turkeyuntil just combined.
Shape turkey mixture into four patties.
Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat, and lightly oil the grate.
Cook on the preheated grill until the patties are no longer pink in thecenter, about 5 minutes per side for well done.
An instant-read thermometerinserted into the center should read 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).
Place aslice of gouda cheese on each patty; continue grilling until cheese hasmelted, about 1 minute.
While the turkey patties are cooking, place thepineapple rings on the grill until softened, 2 to 3 minutes per side.
Assemble the burgers by placing a turkey patty on half of an onion roll, thentop with the grilled pineapple.
Divide the spinach leaves evenly among theburgers, then top with the other half of the onion roll.
