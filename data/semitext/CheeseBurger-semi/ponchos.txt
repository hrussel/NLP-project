Preheat an oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Combine ground beef and onion in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
Cookand stir until meat is well browned and onion is soft.
Drain grease.
Cut each French roll into quarters.
Spread the processed cheese sauce on cutsides of each piece; place on a baking sheet.
Evenly spoon the cooked beefonto the cheese topped rolls.
Top each roll with a dab of ketchup.
Bake in preheated oven until rolls are crisp and filling is hot, about 20minutes.
