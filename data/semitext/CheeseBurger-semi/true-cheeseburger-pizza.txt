Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.
Cook and stir beef, salt, andpepper in the hot skillet until meat is browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes;drain and discard grease.
Mix ketchup and mustard in a bowl; spread mixture over pizza crust.
Spreadmozzarella cheese and Cheddar cheese over ketchup mixture and top with beefand onions.
Transfer pizza to a large baking sheet.
Bake in preheated oven until cheese is melted, about 20 minutes.
Top withpickles and allow pizza to cool for 5 minutes.
Top with shredded lettuce andtomatoes before slicing and serving.
