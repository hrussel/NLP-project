Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Place the ground beef in a skillet over medium-high heat.
Cook, stirring tocrumble, until no longer pink.
Add the onion to the skillet; cook and stiruntil tender.
Drain off grease.
Unroll the crescent roll dough on a clean surface.
Separate the triangles andthen cut each triangle in half diagonally to make 2 smaller triangles.
Stackthe slices of cheese and cut diagonally into 4 triangles.
Place one triangleof cheese onto each triangle of dough.
Spoon about a tablespoon of ground beefover the cheese and then top with a slice of pickle.
Fold the points of thedough towards the center like a diaper and place on a baking sheet.
Bake for 10 to 15 minutes in the preheated oven, until golden brown.
