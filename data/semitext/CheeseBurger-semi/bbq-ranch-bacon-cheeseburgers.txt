Mix ground beef with seasoning mix in large bowl until well blended.
Shapebeef into 6 oblong patties, about 6 x 3-inches each.
Whisk together barbecuesauce and dressing in small bowl; set aside.
Spray grate of outdoor grill and utensils with grilling spray.
Preheat grillto medium heat.
Grill patties 5 minutes on one side or until juices begin toappear on surface.
Turn patties over; top each with 3 slices bacon.
Cook about4 minutes longer; top each patty with 2 triangle pieces of cheese, overlappingto cover patty.
Remove patties when cheese begins to melt.
Spread barbecue-dressing mixture on bottom of each roll.
Add a lettuce leaf.
Place cooked patty with bacon and cheese on lettuce.
Spoon additionalbarbecue-dressing mixture over cheese.
Sprinkle with fried onions, if desired.
Serve immediately.
