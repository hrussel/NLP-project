Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Mix cream cheese, sour cream, canned jalapeno peppers with juice, greenchiles, diced jalapeno pepper, cilantro, and ranch dressing mix together in abowl until smooth.
Add about half the Cheddar cheese and half the Parmesancheese; stir.
Spread the cheese mixture into a baking dish.
Arrange jalapenoslices atop the cheese mixture; top with remaining Cheddar and Parmesancheeses.
Cover baking dish with aluminum foil.
Bake in preheated oven until top is warm and bubbling, about 25 minutes.
