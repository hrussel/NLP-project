Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Lightly grease a 9x13-inchbaking dish.
Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.
Cook and stir beef with blackpepper, salt, and garlic powder in the hot skillet until meat is browned andcrumbly, 5 to 7 minutes; drain and discard grease.
Reduce heat to low and mix1/2 cup ketchup into beef.
Lay a tortilla onto a work surface and place about 1/3 cup beef mixture and1/4 cup shredded cheese onto tortilla in a line down the center.
Roll tortillaover meat and cheese, fold in top and bottom to enclose filling, and finishrolling into a burrito shape.
Repeat with remaining tortillas and filling.
Place filled tortillas into prepared baking sheet and sprinkle remainingMexican cheese blend on top.
Cover dish with aluminum foil.
Bake in the preheated oven until dish is heated through and cheese topping hasmelted, 20 to 25 minutes.
Stir sour cream with 1/3 cup ketchup in a bowl andtop each serving with the sour cream sauce.
