Preheat oven to 375 degrees C (165 degrees C).
Place carrots, potato, celery, pepper, and garlic into a food processor, andpuree to a wet paste.
Add eggs, mustard, and tomato paste, and process foranother few seconds.
Place the ground beef in a large mixing bowl, and add pureed vegetablemixture, thyme, basil, rosemary, cayenne pepper, and salt and pepper.
Kneadtogether until thoroughly mixed.
Form into two loaves, and place in a 9x13inch baking dish.
Bake for 50 to 60 minutes, or until done.
Allow to cool for 10 minutes beforeslicing.
You can also press the meat into bread pans to cook.
