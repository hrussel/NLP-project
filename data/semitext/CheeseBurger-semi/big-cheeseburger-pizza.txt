Place the ground beef and onion in a skillet over medium-high heat.
Cook,stirring to crumble, until evenly browned.
Season with seasoning salt, drainoff grease, and set aside.
Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
Spread salad dressing onthe pizza crust.
Top with a layer of ground beef and onion.
Sprinkle shreddedcheese over the top.
Bake for 8 to 10 minutes in the preheated oven, until cheese is melted.
Allowpizza to cool for about 5 minutes, then slice into wedges and top with lettucetomato and pickles, if desired.
