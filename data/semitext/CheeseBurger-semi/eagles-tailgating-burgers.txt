Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat.
Combine the ground beef, bread crumbs, Romano cheese, barbeque sauce, beer,egg, green onion, basil, Worcestershire sauce, garlic, salt and pepper in alarge bowl.
Mix lightly using your hands, then form into 8 patties.
Lightly brush the grill grate with oil and place patties on the grill.
Cookuntil no longer pink in the center, 7 to 8 minutes per side for well done.
