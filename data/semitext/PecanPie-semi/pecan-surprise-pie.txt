Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a small mixing bowl, beat cream cheese until fluffy.
Add 1/3 cup sugar andbeat until smooth.
Thoroughly mix in 1 egg and 1 teaspoon vanilla extract.
Donot overbeat.
Spread mixture into pastry shell.
Sprinkle with chopped pecans.
In a medium bowl, combine 3 beaten eggs, corn syrup, 1/4 cup sugar, 1 teaspoonvanilla extract, salt, cinnamon, and cloves.
Mix until incorporated but notfrothy.
Pour mixture over pecan layer.
Bake in preheated oven for 35 to 45 minutes, until filling is set and pecansare browned.
Pie will be quite high, but will settle as it cools.
