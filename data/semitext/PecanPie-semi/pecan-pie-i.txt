Preheat the oven to 350 degree F (175 degree C).
In a medium bowl, mix together the sugar and flour until flour is blended in.
Stir in the eggs, corn syrup and vanilla.
Mix in the pecans and pour thefilling into the pie crust.
Bake for 50 to 55 minutes in the preheated oven, until a knife insertedhalfway between center and edge comes out clean.
Cool.
