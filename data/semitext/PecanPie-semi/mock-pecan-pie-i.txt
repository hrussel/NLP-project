Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a medium bowl combine eggs, brown sugar, and white sugar.
Beat well.
Stirin vanilla extract and melted butter or margarine.
Mix thoroughly.
Fold inoats and coconut.
Pour into pie shell.
Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes.
Cool before serving.
Top with whippedcream if desired.
