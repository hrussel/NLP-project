In a medium skillet, melt butter or margarine over medium heat.
Add coconutand pecans.
Toss well, and saute until coconut is lightly browned.
Set asideto cool.
In a large mixing bowl, beat cream cheese until fluffy.
Add condensed milk andmix until smooth.
Fold in whipped topping.
Spread 1/4 of cream cheese mixtureinto each pastry shell.
Sprinkle 1/4 of coconut mixture over each pie.
Drizzle1/2 of caramel topping over each coconut layer.
Follow with remaining creamcheese mixture, then remaining coconut mixture.
Pies may be served chilled or frozen.
