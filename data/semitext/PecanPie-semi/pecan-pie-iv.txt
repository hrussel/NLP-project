Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Place pie shell in a 9 inch piepan.
In a medium bowl, gently beat eggs.
Stir in sugar and flour, then the syrup,butter and vanilla.
Fold in pecans.
Pour mixture into pie shell.
Bake for 50to 60 minutes; knife inserted in center of pie should come out clean.
