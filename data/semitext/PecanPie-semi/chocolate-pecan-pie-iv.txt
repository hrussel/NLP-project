Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Heat butter and chocolate over low heat, stirring constantly, until chocolateis melted; cool slightly.
With hand beater, beat eggs, sugar, salt, chocolate mixture and corn syrup.
Stir in pecans and pour mixture into pie shell.
Bake until set, 40 to 50 minutes.
Cool slightly.
Serve warm, or refrigerateand serve with nondairy whipped topping.
