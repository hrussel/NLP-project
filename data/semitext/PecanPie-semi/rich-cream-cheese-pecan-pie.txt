In a small bowl, blend together cream cheese, 1 egg, 1 teaspoon vanilla, 1/3cup sugar, and salt.
Mix by hand until mostly smooth.
Spread mixture into thebottom of the unbaked pie shell.
Sprinkle with chopped pecans.
In another bowl, slightly beat 3 eggs.
Stir in 1/4 cup sugar, corn syrup, and1 teaspoon vanilla; blend well.
Fold in chopped chocolate or chocolate chips.
Very CAREFULLY pour corn syrup mixture over pecans.
Cover pie crust edge witha strip of foil to prevent excessive browning.
Bake at 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) for 35 to 45 minutes, or until center isset.
Cool completely.
Store in refrigerator.
