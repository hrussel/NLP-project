Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C.
) In a saucepan over low heat,combine caramels, butter and milk.
Cook, stirring frequently, until smooth.
Remove from heat and set aside.
In a large bowl, combine sugar, eggs, vanilla and salt.
Gradually mix in themelted caramel mixture.
Stir in pecans.
Pour filling into unbaked pie crust.
Bake in the preheated oven for 45 to 50 minutes, or until pastry is goldenbrown.
Allow to cool until filling is firm.
