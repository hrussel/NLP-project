Heat oven to 375 degrees F.
Place pie crust in 9-inch glass pie pan asdirected on box for One-Crust Filled Pie.
In large bowl, mix brown sugar, flour, corn syrup, vanilla and eggs until wellblended.
Stir in pecans and butter.
Pour into crust-lined pan.
Bake 40 to 50 minutes or until filling is puffed and pie is golden brown.
Coolcompletely, about 2 hours.
Store in refrigerator.
