Bake pie shell at 450 degrees F (230 degrees C) for 5 minutes.
Cool.
Turn ovendown to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
Beat eggs and salt until very light and lemon colored.
Beat in sugar a littleat a time.
With a wire whisk, fold in melted butter or margarine, and cornsyrup.
Pour into partially baked shell.
Arrange pecan halves on top, brokenside down.
Bake for 10 minutes, and reduce heat to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
Bakefor 30 minutes.
