Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
Press pie crusts into two9-inch pie pans.
Melt chocolate chips in the top of a double boiler over simmering water,stirring frequently and scraping down the sides with a rubber spatula to avoidscorching.
Mix sugar, flour, and salt together in a bowl.
Whisk buttermilk, eggs, and vanilla extract in a separate bowl; stirbuttermilk mixture into sugar mixture.
Slowly pour melted chocolate intomixture, while beating with an electric mixer, until filling is smooth.
Pourhalf the filling into each prepared pie crust.
Sprinkle pecans over each pie.
Bake pies in the preheated oven until cooked through, about 1 hour.
Cool forat least 1 hour before serving.
