Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Prick pastry shell in several places with a fork and bake in preheated ovenfor 5 minutes.
Remove and allow to cool.
In a large bowl, combine sugar, corn syrup, eggs, vanilla, salt, and pecans.
Mix well, then pour into pie shell.
Bake in preheated oven for 45 minutes.
Check after 30 minutes; if crust isgetting too brown, cover edges with aluminum foil.
When done, pie will be alittle loose in center, but will set as it cools; do not overbake.
