Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Press pie crust into a 9-inchpie dish.
Beat butter, eggs, lemon juice, and vanilla extract together in a bowl; beatin sugar until smooth.
Fold coconut and pecans into butter mixture; pour intopie crust.
Bake in the preheated oven until pie is set in the middle, 45 to 50 minutes.
