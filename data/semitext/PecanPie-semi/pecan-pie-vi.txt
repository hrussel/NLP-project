Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C.
)In a large bowl, cream the butter and brown sugar.
Beat in the corn syrup,eggs, salt and vanilla.
Stir in the chopped pecans.
Pour filling into pieshell.
Bake in the preheated oven for 10 minutes.
Reduce temperature to 325 degrees F(165 degrees C) and bake for 30 to 40 minutes, or until golden brown.
