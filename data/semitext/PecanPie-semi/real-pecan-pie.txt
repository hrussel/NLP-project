In a large mixing bowl, slightly beat the eggs.
Stir in corn syrup, sugar, and vanilla.
Mix well.
Lightly stir in pecans, till well-coated with egg mixture.
Pour into pie shell.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 20 minutes.
Turn the oven down to 300 degrees F (150 degrees C), and bake for another 30minutes.
Cool before serving.
Store leftovers covered in refrigerator.
