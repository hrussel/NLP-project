Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees F).
In a small saucepan combine sugar, corn syrup, and butter or margarine.
Cookover medium heat, stirring constantly, until butter or margarine melts andsugar dissolves.
Cool slightly.
In a large bowl combine eggs, bourbon, vanilla, and salt.
Mix well.
Slowlypour sugar mixture into egg mixture, whisking constantly.
Stir in chocolatechips and pecans.
Pour mixture into pie shell.
Bake in preheated oven for 50 to 55 minutes, or until set and golden.
May beserved warm or chilled.
