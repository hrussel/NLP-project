Beat egg whites until slightly stiff.
Add sugar, vanilla, baking powder, Ritzcrackers and pecans.
Mix well, and pour into greased 9 inch pie plate.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 25 minutes exactly.
Allow pie to cool to room temperature, and then cover with nondairy whippedtopping.
Refrigerate for 2 hours before serving.
