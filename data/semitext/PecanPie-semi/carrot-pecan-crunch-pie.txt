Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
To Make Topping: In a medium bowl combine brown sugar, melted butter ormargarine, and chopped pecans.
Mix well and set aside.
To Make Carrot Custard: Steam carrots until tender.
Drain and cool.
Placecooled carrots in a blender or food processor and add eggs, condensed milk,pumpkin pie spice, and salt.
Blend until smooth.
Pour carrot mixture into pieshell.
Sprinkle with pecan topping.
Bake in preheated oven for 45 minutes or until toothpick inserted in centercomes out clean.
