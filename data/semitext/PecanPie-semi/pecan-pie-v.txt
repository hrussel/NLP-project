Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (205 degrees C).
In a large bowl, beat eggs until foamy, and stir in melted butter.
Stir in thebrown sugar, white sugar and the flour; mix well.
Last add the milk, vanillaand nuts.
Pour into an unbaked 9-in pie shell.
Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes at400 degrees, then reduce temperature to 350 degrees and bake for 30 to 40minutes, or until done.
