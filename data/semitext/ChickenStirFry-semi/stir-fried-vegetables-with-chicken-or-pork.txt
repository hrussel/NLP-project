Heat oil in a wok or large heavy skillet.
Add chicken, garlic and oystersauce, and stir-fry for 10 minutes.
Stir in broccoli, green pepper, carrots,cabbage, celery, bean sprouts, zucchini and green onions.
Season with salt,and stir-fry for 6 to 8 minutes.
In a small bowl, mix together water, soysauce and cornstarch.
Stir into vegetables, and cook for 1 to 2 minutes, oruntil sauce is thickened.
