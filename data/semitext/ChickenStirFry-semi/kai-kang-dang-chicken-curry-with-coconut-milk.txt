Bring 1/2 cup coconut milk to a boil in a heavy saucepan.
Whisk in the currypaste and cook until fragrant, about 5 minutes.
Add the chicken and cook untilbrowned, about 5 minutes.
Stir in the 2 cups coconut milk, fish sauce, sugar,vegetables, bamboo shoots, and basil; simmer until the chicken is cookedthrough, about 15 minutes.
Pour the lime juice over the dish and remove fromheat.
Serve warm.
