Fry the curry paste in the oil in a large skillet or wok over medium heatuntil fragrant.
Stir the coconut milk into the curry paste and bring to aboil.
Add the chicken; cook and stir until the chicken is nearly cookedthrough, 10 to 15 minutes.
Stir the palm sugar, fish sauce, and lime leavesinto the mixture; simmer together for 5 minutes.
Taste and adjust thesaltiness by adding more fish sauce if necessary.
Garnish with sliced redchile peppers and Thai basil leaves to serve.
