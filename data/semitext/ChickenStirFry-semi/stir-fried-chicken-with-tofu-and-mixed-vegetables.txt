In a medium bowl, mix the soy sauce, sugar, cornstarch, and rice wine.
Placethe green onion and chicken in the mixture.
Allow to marinate at least 15minutes.
In a wok over medium-high heat, cook and stir the chicken with the marinademixture about 5 minutes until almost done.
Toss in the garlic, onion, andpeppers.
Continue to cook and stir 5 minutes, or until vegetables are crispbut tender and chicken is no longer pink and juices run clear.
Mix the tofu, water, oyster sauce, and chili paste into the wok.
Cook and stiruntil heated through.
