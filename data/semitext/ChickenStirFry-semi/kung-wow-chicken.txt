Combine white vinegar, rice vinegar, 1 tablespoon soy sauce, sambal chilepaste, sesame oil, 2 tablespoons brown sugar, ketchup, 2 tablespoon whitewine, and garlic in a bowl.
Whisk thoroughly; set aside.
Mix cornstarch with cold water in a small bowl.
Heat peanut oil in a heavy duty, nonstick skillet over high heat.
Stir inchicken; cook and stir until chicken begins to brown, about 2 minutesStir in zucchini and red bell pepper; cook for 2 more minutes.
Add chickenbroth and stir.
Stir in vinegar mixture; cook and stir until vegetables are tender and chickenis no longer pink in the center, about 2 minutes.
Stir in cornstarch mixtureuntil sauce is thickened, about 30 seconds; remove from heat.
Toss in peanuts; season with salt and pepper to taste.
Stir in green oniontops.
