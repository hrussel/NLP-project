Preheat an indoor grill, and coat with cooking spray.
Place the chicken stripson the grill, and cook for about 7 minutes.
Allow them to cool slightly, thencut into cubes.
While the chicken is cooking, heat the oil in a large skillet over mediumheat.
Place the onion rings in the skillet, and cook for a few minutes.
Addthe red and yellow pepper, baby corn, and the stir-fry vegetables.
Increasethe heat to medium-high; cook and stir for about 15 minutes.
Sprinkle in the sugar and salt.
Stir in the chicken.
Dissolve the cornstarchin water, and pour into the skillet along with the soy sauce.
Cook and stiruntil the sauce thickens.
