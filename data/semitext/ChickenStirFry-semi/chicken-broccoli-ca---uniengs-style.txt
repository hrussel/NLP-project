In a large bowl, combine chicken, oyster sauce and soy sauce.
Set aside for 15minutes.
Heat oil in a wok or large heavy skillet over medium heat.
Saute garlic andonion until soft and translucent.
Increase heat to high.
Add chicken andmarinade, then stir-fry until light golden brown, about 10 minutes.
Stir inwater, pepper and sugar.
Add bok choy and broccoli, and cook stirring untilsoft, about 10 minutes.
Pour in the cornstarch mixture, and cook until sauceis thickened, about 5 minutes.
