Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
Add onion and saute until soft,then add chicken and 2 tablespoons soy sauce and stir-fry for 5 to 6 minutes.
Stir in carrots, celery, red bell pepper, pea pods and green bell pepper andstir-fry another 5 minutes.
Then add rice and stir thoroughly.
Finally, stir in scrambled eggs and 1/3 cup soy sauce, heat through and servehot.
