In a small bowl combine the orange juice, orange zest, soy sauce, salt, garlicand brown sugar.
Mix well.
Heat oil in a large skillet or wok over medium high heat.
When oil begins tobubble, add chicken.
Saute until cooked through (no longer pink inside), about7 to 10 minutes.
Add orange sauce mixture to chicken and cook until sauce begins to bubble.
Addflour, a little bit at a time, until sauce has thickened to your liking.
Addbean sprouts and cook for 1 minute; serve hot over chow mein noodles.
