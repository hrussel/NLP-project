Lightly coat the bottom of a nonstick skillet or wok with oil.
Over mediumheat, cook and stir the chicken strips until lightly brown.
Add carrots andonion, and cook for 3 minutes.
Add mushrooms, zucchini, and squash.
Cook until squash begins to soften.
Addpecans, and sprinkle season with pepper.
Toss in pan for 2 to 3 minutes andserve.
