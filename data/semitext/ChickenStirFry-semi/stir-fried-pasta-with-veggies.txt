Bring a large pot of water to a boil.
Cook spaghetti pasta in boiling wateruntil al dente.
Drain.
Meanwhile, heat oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
Cookgarlic in oil for 1 minute.
Stir in onion, and cook until soft.
Stir inchicken, and cook until juices run clear.
Mix in the broccoli, cauliflower,and carrots, and cook for 2 to 5 minutes, stirring frequently.
Season with soysauce, salt, and pepper.
Toss pasta with vegetables, and serve warm.
