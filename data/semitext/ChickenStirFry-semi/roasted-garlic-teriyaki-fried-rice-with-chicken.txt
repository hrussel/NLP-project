Cut chicken into thin strips.
Heat oil in hot wok or large skillet over high heat.
Add chicken, green onionsand carrot.
Stir-fry 3 minutes, or until chicken is thoroughly cooked.
Add egg; cook, stirring gently, until firm.
Stir in rice and cook until heatedthrough.
Add roasted garlic teriyaki sauce; remove pan from heat.
Stir mixture untilwell mixed.
Serve immediately.
