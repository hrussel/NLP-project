Stir orange juice, soy sauce, garlic, orange zest, ground ginger, and redpepper flakes together in a bowl until thoroughly combined.
Heat oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
Cook and stirchicken and orange juice mixture in the hot oil until the chicken is no longerpink in the middle and the juices run clear, 7 to 10 minutes.
Whisk chicken broth and cornstarch together in a small bowl; stir into thechicken and sauce mixture in small amounts until sauce has thickened to yourliking.
Mix stir-fry vegetables, sugar snap peas, broccoli, and carrot into thechicken and sauce mixture; cook and stir until the vegetables are slightlysoftened, another 7 to 10 minutes.
