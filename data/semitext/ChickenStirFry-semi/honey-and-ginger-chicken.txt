Heat olive oil in a skillet or wok over medium heat.
Add chicken cubes, 1/4cup honey, and ginger.
Cook and stir until chicken is golden brown, about 10minutes.
Add bell peppers, onion, broccoli, pineapple, and remaining 1/2 cuphoney.
Cover and cook over medium-high heat until vegetables are tender, 5 to10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
