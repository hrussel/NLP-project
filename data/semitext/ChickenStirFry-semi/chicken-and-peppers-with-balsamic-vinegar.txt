Heat 2 tablespoons olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
Placethe chicken in the skillet, season with salt and pepper, and brown on bothsides.
Remove from heat, and set aside.
Heat remaining oil in the skillet over medium heat, and stir in the red bellpepper, yellow bell pepper, orange bell pepper, and onion.
Cook about 5minutes, until tender.
Mix in the garlic, and cook and stir about 1 minute.
Mix in the basil and 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar.
Return the chicken to the skillet.
Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer 20minutes, or until chicken is no longer pink and juices run clear.
Stir inremaining balsamic vinegar just before serving.
