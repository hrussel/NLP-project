In a bowl, mash the yellow curry paste with about 2 tablespoons of chickenbroth to help dissolve the paste; whisk in remaining chicken broth, sugar,curry powder, salt, light soy sauce, and coconut milk.
Set aside.
Heat a wok or large skillet over high heat for about 30 seconds; pour in theoil.
Let the oil heat until shimmering, about 30 more seconds.
Stir thechicken, garlic, and ginger into the hot oil; cook and stir until the chickenhas begun to brown and the garlic and ginger are fragrant, about 2 minutes.
Stir in the onion and potatoes, toss all ingredients in the hot oil, and pourin the sauce mixture.
Bring the sauce to a boil, reduce heat, and cover thewok.
Simmer until the chicken is cooked through and the potatoes are tender,20 to 25 minutes.
