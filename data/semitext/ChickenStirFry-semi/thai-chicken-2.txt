Place the rice noodles in a bowl.
Pour the boiling water over the noodles; setaside.
Heat the oil in a large wok or skillet over medium-high heat.
Cook and stirthe onion, zucchini, and mushrooms until softened; remove from skillet,reserving the oil, and set aside.
Reheat the remaining oil in the skillet.
Cook and stir the chicken in the hotoil until no longer pink, about 5 minutes.
Return the vegetable mixture to theskillet and add the fish sauce, curry paste, and sugar; stir to coat thechicken and vegetables with sauce.
Drain the rice noodles and add to the mixture.
Pour the coconut milk into theskillet and stir to combine.
Sprinkle with cilantro to serve.
