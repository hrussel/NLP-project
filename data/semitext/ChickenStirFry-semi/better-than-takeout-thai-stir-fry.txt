Bring the chicken broth to a boil in a large, deep skillet over medium-highheat.
Add the mushrooms, onion, and broccoli; cover, and steam until tender,about 5 minutes.
Stir in tamari, peanut butter, and pepper flakes untilsmooth.
Add the coleslaw mix, bean sprouts, and chicken; cook until heatedthrough.
Sprinkle with toasted sesame seeds to garnish.
