Preheat slow cooker on Low for 15 minutes.
Season pork shoulder with salt and pepper; place pork in preheated slowcooker.
Mix ketchup, brown sugar, and vinegar in a bowl; pour over pork.
Cook on Low for 8 hours.
Transfer pork to a large platter and slice into 3 to4 pieces.
Shred meat with 2 forks and return to slow cooker.
Continue to cookfor 1 hour.
