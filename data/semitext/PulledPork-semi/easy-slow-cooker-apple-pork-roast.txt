Place the apples and onion into the bottom of a slow cooker, and sprinkle with1/4 teaspoon cinnamon.
Rub the pork roast with salt and pepper, and place it on the layer of applesand onions in the slow cooker.
Pour the applesauce over the roast, and dustthe top with 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon.
Cook on High setting for 8 hours.
Serve the roast topped with the sauce,apples, and onions.
