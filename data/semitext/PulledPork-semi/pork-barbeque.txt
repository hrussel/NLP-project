In a bowl, mix the wine, white pepper, liquid smoke, garlic, black pepper,pepper sauce, and Worcestershire sauce.
Place pork in a slow cooker, and coverwith the sauce mixture.
Cover, and cook 4 to 5 hours on Low, or until the meatshreds easily.
Shred the pork with a fork.
Mix in the barbeque sauce, onion, green bellpepper, red bell pepper, and yellow bell pepper.
Continue cooking 30 minutes,or until vegetables are tender.
Place pork in a strainer to remove excess liquid, pushing down with a largespoon to extract as much moisture as possible.
Liquid may be reserved as adipping sauce.
