Preheat oven to 250 degrees F (120 degrees C).
Score fat layer on pork shoulder by making shallow cuts in a diamond pattern.
Spread garlic, parsley, sage rosemary, and thyme over pork.
Place pork in alarge roasting pan, fat side up.
Pour cider over pork.
Bake in the preheated oven for 8 hours.
Transfer pork to a large platter;remove and discard any large pieces of fat and skin.
Pour pan drippings into aseparate pot.
Shred meat and return to the roasting pan.
Whisk flour into water in a bowl.
Bring drippings to a boil and stir in flour mixture; cook and stir until gravyis thickened, about 5 minutes.
Pour gravy over pork.
