Arrange onion slices in the bottom of a slow cooker.
Season pork with salt andpepper and place over onion.
Add vinegar and water.
Cook pork on Low for 8 hours.
Transfer pork to a platter and shred with twoforks.
Remove and discard about half the pork juices from slow cooker and stirin shredded pork, barbeque sauce, and brown sugar.
