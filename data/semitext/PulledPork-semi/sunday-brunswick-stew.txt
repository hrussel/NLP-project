Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
Cook and stir onion and celeryin hot oil until onion is translucent, 5 to 7 minutes.
Stir garlic into themixture; cook and stir another 2 minutes.
Transfer mixture to slow cooker.
Stir butternut squash, pork, diced tomatoes, baked beans, chicken stock, corn,peas, cayenne pepper, cumin, paprika, salt, and barbeque sauce into the onionmixture in the slow cooker.
Cover and cook on High for 4 hours.
