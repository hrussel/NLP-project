Combine hard apple cider and 1/4 cup brown sugar in a slow cooker; mix untildissolved.
Add pork tenderloin.
Cook tenderloin on Low until very tender, about 6 hours.
Shred pork with afork.
About 20 minutes for the pork is finished, melt butter in a large skillet overmedium-low heat; cook and stir onion until well browned, about 10 minutes.
Combine 1/2 cup brown sugar, cinnamon, and onion powder; season with salt andpepper.
Stir apples and sugar mixture into cooked onion.
Cover and cook untilapples are tender and sauce has thickened, about 10 minutes more.
Spoon pork and apple-onion sauce over rolls to serve.
