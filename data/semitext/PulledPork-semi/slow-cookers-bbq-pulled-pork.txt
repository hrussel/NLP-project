Place pork in slow cooker.
Mix Seasoning Mix, ketchup, brown sugar and vinegar until blended.
Pour overpork.
Cover.
Cook 8 hours on LOW or 4 hours on HIGH.
Remove pork from slow cooker.
Shred pork, using 2 forks.
Return pork to slow cooker.
Mix and heat with saucebefore serving.
Serve on sandwich rolls, if desired.
