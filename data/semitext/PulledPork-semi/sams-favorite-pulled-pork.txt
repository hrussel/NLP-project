Season pork tenderloin with salt, black pepper, and 1/2 teaspoon cayennepepper.
Heat oil in a skillet over medium-high heat; fry pork in hot oil until brownedon all sides, about 10 minutes.
Transfer meat to a slow cooker.
Add water,brown sugar, cider vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, garlic, and hot sauce.
Cook on Low for 7 hours.
Remove tenderloin to a platter and drain cookingliquid, reserving about 1/2 cup.
Shred pork and return to slow cooker.
Addenough reserved liquid to moisten meat.
Whisk mayonnaise, white vinegar, garlic powder, lemon pepper, and 1 pinchcayenne pepper together in a bowl.
Serve mayonnaise sauce with shredded pork.
