Season pork roast with half the steak seasoning and place in the center of aslow cooker.
Layer about half the pork rib meat around pork roast and seasonwith about half the remaining steak seasoning, paprika, and chili pepper.
Layer the remaining pork ribs in the slower and season with the remainingsteak seasoning.
Add soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, and liquid smoke.
Slowly pour water over pork, being careful not to wash away the seasoning.
Cook on Low for 10 hours.
Transfer meat to a roasting pan; remove and discardany bones and large chunks of fat.
Shred pork with tongs or forks.
Drain and discard liquid from slow cooker; wipe away any remaining fat.
Returnshredded pork to slow cooker and add about 1 1/2 bottles barbeque sauce.
Stir.
Continue to heat on Low until ready to serve.
Serve with remaining 1/2 bottlebarbeque sauce.
