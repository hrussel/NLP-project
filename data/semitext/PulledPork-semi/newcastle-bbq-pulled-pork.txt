Place onion, olive oil, and garlic in a slow cooker.
Arrange pork over onionmixture and add ale.
Season with black pepper.
Cook on Low for 6 to 8 hours.
Drain and discard liquid from slow cooker.
Shredpork with 2 forks and add barbeque sauce and liquid smoke.
Continue to cook on Low for 2 more hours.
