Place pork roast in a slow cooker.
Pour vinegar over pork, coating all sides;season with salt and pepper.
Cook on Low for 10 to 12 hours.
Transfer pork to a platter.
Drain and discardjuices from the slow cooker.
Chop pork and return to the slow cooker.
Addbarbeque sauce and continue to cook for 30 minutes.
