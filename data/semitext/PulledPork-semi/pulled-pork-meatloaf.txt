Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Spray a loaf pan with cookingspray.
Whisk eggs and milk together in a bowl.
Add pork, bread crumbs, bell pepper,green onion, mustard, and pepper; stir.
Transfer pork mixture to the preparedloaf pan.
Press pork tightly into pan and spread barbeque sauce on top.
Bake in the preheated oven until heated through, about 1 hour.
Let meatloafcool 5 to 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
