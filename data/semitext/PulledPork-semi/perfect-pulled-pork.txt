Combine all the seasonings in a small bowl and rub evenly over roast.
Placemeat in a 6-quart slow cooker.
Add water.
Cover and cook on LOW for 6-8 hoursor HIGH for 4-5 hours or until pork is very tender.
Remove pork to a large cutting board or platter and let rest for 10-15minutes.
Pull, slice or chop to serve.
Serve in buns with barbecue sauce.
