Mix beer, mustard, honey, Worcestershire sauce, garlic, cayenne pepper, andsalt in a bowl until honey is dissolved.
Pour a small amount of the beermarinade into a slow cooker; add pork.
Pour remaining marinade over pork.
Cover and refrigerate overnight, turning pork occasionally.
Cook pork on Low in the slow cooker for 8 hours, basting occasionally.
Addwater if the pork becomes dry.
Drop onion around pork and continue to cookuntil pork is very tender, about 2 hours more.
Pull and shred pork with two forks, discarding any pieces of fat.
Transferpork to a serving dish.
Skim remaining onion from the slow cooker using asieve and add to pork.
