Combine coleslaw mix, pepperoncini peppers, red onion, mayonnaise, lemonjuice, red wine vinegar, 1 tablespoon granulated garlic, Dijon mustard, whitesugar, salt, 1/2 teaspoon granulated onion, and lemon pepper in a large bowl.
Toss until well combined.
Cover and refrigerate until ready to use.
Preheat oven to 300 degrees F (150 degrees C).
Combine kosher salt, black pepper, 2 tablespoons granulated garlic, 2tablespoons granulated onion, 2 tablespoons brown sugar, 1 tablespoon cayennepepper, nutmeg, allspice, and cinnamon together in a bowl.
Place pork shoulder in a large roasting pan.
Rub with 1/4 cup molasses on allsides and between any crevices.
Sprinkle with kosher salt seasoning mixtureand rub over all sides.
Cover pan tightly with heavy-duty aluminum foil and roast pork in thepreheated oven until tender and a thermometer inserted reaches 200 degrees F(95 degrees C), 3 to 4 hours.
Transfer pork to a cutting board, reserving panjuice, and allow to cool slightly before pulling.
Using two forks, shred thepork shoulder into bite-size pieces.
Transfer meat to a large bowl and tosswith some of the pan juices to keep it moist.
Pour reserved pan juices into a large pot over medium heat.
Add barbequesauce, coffee, 1 tablespoon molasses, 1 tablespoon brown sugar, dry mustard,and a pinch of cayenne pepper.
Bring to a simmer, reduce heat, and cook for 15minutes.
Add shredded pork and stir until warm, about 5 minutes.
Fill each hoagie bun with pork mixture.
Top with 1 to 2 cups coleslaw.
Garnishwith 1 tablespoon onion and 1 tablespoon jalapeno.
