Cut several slits into the fatty side of the pork shoulder.
Insert garlicslices into slits.
Transfer pork to a slow cooker, fat side up.
Mix coffee, onion, Worcestershire sauce, and liquid smoke in a bowl; pour overpork.
Cook pork on Low until very tender, about 8 hours.
Carefully remove pork fromslow cooker to a large plate; discard excess fat.
Shred pork with 2 forks orby hand.
Reserve about 1/2 cup juices from the slow cooker; discard remaining liquid.
Return shredded pork and juices to slow cooker and season with red pepperflakes, salt, and pepper.
Add barbeque sauce and stir.
Heat pork on Low until sauce is hot, about 30 minutes more.
Pile pork overtoasted buns to serve.
