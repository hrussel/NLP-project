Stir the soup, water, Worcestershire sauce, mushrooms, onions, garlic andblack pepper in a 6-quart slow cooker.
Add the beef and stir to coat.
Cover and cook on LOW for 8 to 9 hours or until the beef is fork-tender.
Stirthe sour cream in the cooker.
Serve the beef mixture with the noodles.
Sprinkle with the parsley, if desired.
