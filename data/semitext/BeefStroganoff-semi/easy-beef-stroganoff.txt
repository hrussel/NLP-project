Bring a large pot of water to a boil.
Cook egg noodles in boiling water untildone, about 8 minutes.
Drain.
Meanwhile, prepare the sauce.
In a large skillet, cook mushrooms and onions in2 tablespoons of butter over medium heat until soft; remove from pan.
Using the same pan, melt remaining butter.
Cook ground beef in melted butteruntil browned.
Mix in flour.
Stir in beef broth, and cook until slightlythickened.
Add mushroom and onion mixture; stir in sour cream.
Season to tastewith salt and pepper.
Continue cooking until sauce is hot, but not boiling.
Serve sauce over egg noodles.
