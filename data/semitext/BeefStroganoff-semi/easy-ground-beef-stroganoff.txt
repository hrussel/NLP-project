Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.
Add ground beef, add onions,garlic, and mushrooms; saute until onion is golden brown.
Season with salt andblack pepper.
Stir 2 cups hot water, bouillon cubes, and tomato paste into meat mixture.
Stir together 1 1/2 cups cold water and flour; stir into pan.
Reduce heat tolow, and simmer for 1 hour.
