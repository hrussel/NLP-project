Stir golden mushroom soup, water, onion, Worcestershire sauce, and onion soupmix together in a slow cooker; add beef and stir to coat.
Cook on Low for 6 hours.
Stir cream cheese and sour cream into the beef mixture.
Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat.
Cook and stir mushrooms in meltedbutter until tender, 3 to 5 minutes; add to beef mixture.
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Cook egg noodles in theboiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to thebite, about 5 minutes.
Drain.
Ladle beef mixture over noodles to serve.
