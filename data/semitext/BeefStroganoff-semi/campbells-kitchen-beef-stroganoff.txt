Slice beef into very thin strips.
Heat half the oil in skillet over medium-high heat.
Cook beef until browned,stirring often.
Set beef aside.
Add remaining oil.
Add onion and cook over medium heat until tender.
Pour offfat.
Add soup and paprika.
Heat to a boil.
Stir in sour cream and return beef toskillet.
Heat through.
Serve over noodles.
Sprinkle with parsley.
