Heat the oil in a 12-inch skillet over medium-high heat.
Add the beef and cookin batches until it's well browned, stirring often.
Add the onion, garlic,parsley and mushrooms and cook until the mushrooms are tender.
Remove the beefmixture from the skillet.
Stir the broth and wine in the skillet and heat to aboil.
Reduce the heat to medium.
Stir the sour cream and flour in a small bowl.
Add the sour cream mixture tothe skillet.
Cook and stir until the mixture boils and thickens.
Return thebeef mixture to the skillet and cook until hot.
Serve over the noodles withthe black pepper, if desired.
