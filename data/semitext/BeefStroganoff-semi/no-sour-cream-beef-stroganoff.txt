Brown ground beef in a large skillet over medium heat.
Season with salt.
Stirin butter, mushrooms, onion, and garlic; cook until the onions are soft.
In a small bowl, stir together white wine and flour.
Stir into meat, alongwith mushroom soup and beef broth.
Simmer for 10 to 15 minutes, allowing tothicken.
(If you need more liquid, stir in additional broth.
) Serve seasonedwith a dash of Worcestershire sauce.
