Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Cook the bow-tie pasta ata boil, stirring occasionally, until cooked through yet firm to the bite,about 12 minutes; drain and transfer to a serving bowl.
Heat olive oil and butter together in a large frying pan; cook and stirmushrooms, onions, and garlic in the oil-butter mixture until mushrooms aretender, 5 to 10 minutes.
Stir cream cheese, white wine, tomato paste, lemon juice, cornstarch, beefbouillon, parsley, seasoned salt, and paprika together in a bowl; add steak.
Pour cream cheese mixture into frying pan with mushroom mixture; cook andgently stir mixture over low heat just until sauce is warmed and smooth, 5 to10 minutes.
Add enough milk to reach desired consistency.
Pour sauce over pasta and toss to coat.
Season with black pepper.
