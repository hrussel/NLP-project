Toss cabbage, red bell pepper, onion, celery, jalapeno pepper, and freshginger in a large salad bowl.
Whisk peanut butter, soy sauce, lime juice,sugar, sesame oil, and salt in a separate bowl until dressing is smooth.
Pourdressing over slaw and toss to combine.
Let stand at least 1 hour; servesprinkled with chopped peanuts.
