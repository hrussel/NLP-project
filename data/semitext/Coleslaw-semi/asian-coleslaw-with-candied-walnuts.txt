In a large bowl, toss together cabbage, jicama, carrots and cilantro.
Whisktogether ponzu, sesame oil, orange juice and ginger; pour over cabbage mixtureand toss together.
Sprinkle with candied walnuts.
To make candied walnuts, place 1 cup walnuts and 1/2 cup sugar in a skilletover medium heat.
Cook, stirring constantly, until sugar dissolves and turnslight brown.
Toss to coat walnuts.
Remove skillet from heat; stir in 1/2teaspoon vanilla and 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon.
Spread walnuts on aluminum foil tocool.
