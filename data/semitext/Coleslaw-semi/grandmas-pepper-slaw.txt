Toss the cabbage and bell pepper together in a large bowl.
Season the mixtureliberally with salt; stir.
Allow to sit 45 minutes; drain and return to thebowl.
Bring the vinegar and sugar to a boil together in a saucepan.
Immediatelyremove from heat and stir into the cabbage mixture.
Add the celery seed; mixthoroughly.
Chill completely before serving.
