Place cabbage, jicama, and apple in a large bowl and toss to combine.
Whisk mayonnaise, pineapple juice, sugar, hot sauce, salt, and pepper in abowl until mixture is smooth and fluffy, 1 to 2 minutes.
Pour mayonnaise mixture over cabbage, jicama, and apple; toss to combine.
Letsit for 5 minutes.
Mix in cilantro and toss again.
Garnish with toasted corn bits and serve immediately.
