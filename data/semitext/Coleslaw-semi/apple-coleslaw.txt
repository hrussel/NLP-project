Place the shredded cabbage and carrot together in a bowl with the slicedapple, and toss to combine.
In a separate bowl, stir together the honey, brownsugar, vinegar, pineapple juice, and mayonnaise until the honey and sugar havedissolved completely.
Pour over the salad, and toss to coat.
Season with saltand pepper, and toss again.
Cover and chill until ready to serve.
