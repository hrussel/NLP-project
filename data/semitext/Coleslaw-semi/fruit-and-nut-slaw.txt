Drain pineapple, reserving 2 tablespoons juice.
Cut pineapple into thinstrips, and place in a large bowl.
In a medium bowl, combine reserved pineapple juice and lemon juice.
Toss withbanana, and add to pineapple.
Add cabbage, celery, mandarin oranges, walnutsand raisins; toss to combine.
Blend yogurt and salt together, and add to cabbage mixture; toss lightly.
Cover, and refrigerate until thoroughly chilled.
