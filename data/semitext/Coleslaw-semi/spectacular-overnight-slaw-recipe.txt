Mix cabbage, onion, green bell pepper, red bell pepper, carrots, and olivestogether in a large bowl.
Whisk vinegar, oil, sugar, Dijon mustard, salt, celery seed, and mustard seedtogether in a saucepan.
Bring to a boil; cook and stir until sugar isdissolved, about 1 minute.
Pour hot dressing over cabbage mixture and tossgently.
Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 8 hoursor overnight.
