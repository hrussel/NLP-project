Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat; cook and stir sunflowerkernels and ramen noodles in hot butter until sunflower kernels are lightlybrowned and fragrant, 3 to 5 minutes.
Remove from heat and transfer into abowl to cool.
Stir sunflower seeds and ramen noodles with napa cabbage and spring onions ina large salad bowl.
Whisk vinegar, vegetable oil, sugar, and soy saucetogether in a separate bowl until sugar has dissolved; pour dressing oversalad and toss.
