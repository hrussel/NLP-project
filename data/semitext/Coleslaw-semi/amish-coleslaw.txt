In a large bowl, toss together the cabbage, onion, and 1 cup SPLENDA®Granulated Sweetener.
In a small saucepan, combine the vinegar, salt, celeryseed, 1 teaspoon SPLENDA® Granulated Sweetener, mustard and oil.
Bring to aboil, and cook for 3 minutes.
Cool completely, then pour over cabbage mixture,and toss to coat.
Refrigerate overnight for best flavor.
