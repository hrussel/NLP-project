Mix mayonnaise, vinegar, sugar, celery seed, and ramen noodle seasoning packetin a bowl; chill in refrigerator.
Heat olive oil and sesame oil in a skillet over medium heat; cook and stirramen noodles and almonds until toasted, stirring frequently, 3 to 5 minutes.
Remove from heat and cool.
Combine ramen noodles, almonds, broccoli coleslaw mix, onion, sunflower seeds,and dressing in a bowl; stir to coat.
