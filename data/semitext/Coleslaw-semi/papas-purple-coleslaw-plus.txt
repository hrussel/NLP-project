Toss red cabbage, tomato, baby carrots, pineapple, pecans, apple, red bellpepper, dried cherries, green onion, cilantro, parsley, dry mustard, salt, andblack pepper together in a large salad bowl until thoroughly combined.
Gentlymix mayonnaise into the slaw and drizzle with honey; stir again to combine allingredients.
Cover bowl and chill before serving.
