Toss the cabbage, red bell pepper, red onion, carrots, mango, and cilantrotogether in a large bowl.
Whisk the yogurt, mayonnaise, mustard, cider vinegar, agave nectar, salt,pepper, and hot sauce together in a small bowl; pour over the cabbage mixtureand toss to coat.
Allow the slaw to marinate in the refrigerator for at least1 hour to allow the flavors to combine.
