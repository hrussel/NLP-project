In a large bowl, mix the cabbage, green onions, cilantro, and tomato.
In a skillet over medium heat, cook and stir the sesame oil, black pepper, andchili pepper until heated through.
Mix in the bacon bits and almonds.
Cook andstir about 5 minutes, until almonds are lightly browned.
Remove from heat, andtoss with the cabbage mixture.
Serve immediately.
