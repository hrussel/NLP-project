Place the beets into a large saucepan with enough water to cover the beets by1 inch.
Bring to a boil.
Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer untilthe beets are easily pierced with a fork, about 40 minutes; drain.
Chill beetsin refrigerator until cool enough to handle.
Peel the beets and grate into a large bowl.
Stir the prunes, walnuts, andpickle with the beets.
Add the mayonnaise and stir until all the ingredientsare evenly coated.
Chill completely before serving.
