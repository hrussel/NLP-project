Place the shredded bok choy, cucumber, carrots, and cherry peppers into alarge salad bowl.
Place the jalapeno peppers into the work bowl of a foodprocessor, then pour in the apple cider vinegar, brown mustard, soy sauce, andagave syrup.
Pulse several times, then process for a few seconds to combine.
Pour the dressing over the vegetables and toss.
Refrigerate from 1 hour toovernight.
Before serving, sprinkle with roasted ginger and black pepper; tossagain to serve.
