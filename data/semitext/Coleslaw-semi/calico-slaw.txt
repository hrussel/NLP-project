Toss the cabbage, carrots, green bell pepper, red bell pepper, Red Deliciousapple, and Golden Delicious apple together in a large bowl.
Whisk the apple cider vinegar, sugar, and sea salt together in a small bowl;season with black pepper.
Pour the vinegar mixture over the cabbage mixtureand gently toss to coat.
Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate atleast 30 minutes.
