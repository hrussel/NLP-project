In a large bowl, toss together the cabbage, onion, and 1 cup sugar.
In a smallsaucepan, combine the vinegar, salt, celery seed, 1 teaspoon white sugar,mustard and oil.
Bring to a boil, and cook for 3 minutes.
Cool completely,then pour over cabbage mixture, and toss to coat.
Refrigerate overnight forbest flavor.
