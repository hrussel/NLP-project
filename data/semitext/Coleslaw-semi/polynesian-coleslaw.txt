Stir together peanut butter, garlic, salt, rice vinegar, soy sauce, and hotpepper sauce until smooth.
Fold in cilantro, chives, and red pepper.
Addshredded cabbage, and toss until well coated.
Scoop into a serving bowl, coverwith plastic wrap, and refrigerate 1 hour.
Sprinkle with sesame seeds beforeserving.
