Preheat an oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
Mix the beef, pork, water, eggs, picante sauce, onion soup mix, bread crumbs,and bell pepper together in a mixing bowl until evenly blended.
Pack into theprepared loaf pan, then poke holes into the loaf with a fork nearly to thebottom of the pan.
Pour the undiluted cheese sauce over the top of the roastand spread evenly.
Bake in the preheated oven until no longer pink in the center, about 1 1/2hours.
An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read 160degrees F (72 degrees C).
