Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
In a large bowl, combine the beef, egg, sour cream and Worcestershire sauce.
Mix in soup mix, cheese, and bread crumbs.
Form mixture into a loaf, and placein a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
Cover with foil.
Bake at 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) for 45 minutes.
Remove foil and continuebaking for another 10 to 15 minutes.
Let stand 5 to 10 minutes before servingfor easier slicing.
