Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large bowl, mix together ground beef, eggs, bread crumbs and ketchup.
Season with Italian-style seasoning, oregano, basil, garlic salt, dicedtomatoes and cheese.
Press into a 9x5 inch loaf pan, and cover loosely withfoil.
Bake in the preheated oven approximately 1 hour, or until internal temperaturereaches 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).
