Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
In a mixing bowl, combine ground beef, Spanish rice and sauce mix, eggs, 1tablespoon of ketchup, and mustard.
Mix thoroughly.
Coat the inside of a 9x5 inch loaf pan with 2 tablespoons of the ketchup.
Transfer the meat mixture to the loaf pan.
Top with the remaining ketchup.
Bake in a preheated oven for 1 hour or until cooked through.
