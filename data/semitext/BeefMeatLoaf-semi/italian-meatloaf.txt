Put tomatoes in a bowl.
Pour hot water over tomatoes and soak until softened,about 30 minutes; drain.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Prepare a loaf pan with cookingspray.
Mix drained tomatoes, ground beef, bread crumbs, onion, basil, ketchup,provolone cheese, egg whites, and garlic together in a bowl; pack intoprepared loaf pan.
Bake in the preheated oven until no longer pink in the center, about 1 hour.
An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 160degrees F (70 degrees C).
