Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large mixing bowl, combine ground meat, minced mushrooms, bread crumbs,onion, ketchup, eggs, and salt and pepper.
Mix well.
Spread 1/2 of the mixtureinto the bottom of a loaf pan.
Arrange 6 whole mushrooms stem down into meat.
Top with rest of meat, patting to combine both halves.
Bake for 1 hour and 45 minutes, or until done.
Internal temperature shouldmeasure 160 degrees F (70 degrees C) when done.
