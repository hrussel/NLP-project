Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, bread crumbs, egg, garlic powder andWorcestershire sauce.
Mix well, and place into a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
Bake in preheated oven for 30 to 50 minutes.
Meanwhile, in a separate medium bowl, stir together the ketchup, brown sugarand pineapple preserves.
Pour over the meatloaf about 20 minutes beforeremoving from oven.
