Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Lightly grease an 8 1/2 x 4 1/2inch loaf pan.
In a mixing bowl, combine the ground beef, salt, egg, black pepper and breadcrumbs.
Pour in the milk, 3 tablespoons of the steak sauce, onion and greenbell pepper.
Place the mixture into the prepared loaf pan and shape into a loaf.
Brush thetop with the remaining steak sauce.
Bake in the preheated oven for 1 hour or until done.
Allow to stand 5 minutesbefore slicing.
