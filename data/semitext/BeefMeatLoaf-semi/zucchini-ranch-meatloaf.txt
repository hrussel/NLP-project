Preheat an oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Lightly grease a 9x13-inchbaking dish.
Mix the ground beef, ground turkey, zucchini, onions, green bell pepper, egg,Worcestershire sauce, Cheddar cheese, bread crumbs, and ranch dressing mixuntil well combined.
Form the mixture into a loaf in the middle of theprepared baking dish.
Stir together the ketchup and steak sauce in a small bowl.
Pour the sauce overthe top of the meatloaf.
Bake in the preheated oven until no longer pink inthe center, about 1 hour.
An instant-read thermometer inserted into the centershould read at least 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).
