Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a small bowl, combine the ketchup, brown sugar, 1 tablespoon lemon juiceand mustard powder.
In a separate large bowl, combine the ground beef, bread, onion, egg,bouillon, remaining lemon juice and 1/3 of the ketchup mixture from the smallbowl.
Mix this well and place in a 5x9 inch loaf pan.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 1 hour, drain any excess fat, coatwith remaining ketchup mixture and bake for 10 more minutes.
