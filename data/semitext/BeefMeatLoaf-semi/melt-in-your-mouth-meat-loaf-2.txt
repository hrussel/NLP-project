Combine eggs, milk, bread crumbs, onion, salt, sage, and mushrooms in a largebowl.
Crumble ground beef over mixture and stir well to combine.
Shape into around loaf; place in a 5-quart slow cooker.
Cover and cook on Low until a meatthermometer reads 160 degrees F (71 degrees C), 5 to 6 hours.
Whisk ketchup, brown sugar, mustard, and Worcestershire sauce in a small bowl;spoon sauce over meat loaf.
Return to slow cooker and cook on Low until heatedthrough, about 15 minutes.
Let stand 10 minutes before cutting.
