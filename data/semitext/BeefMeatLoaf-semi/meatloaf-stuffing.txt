Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Mix meatloaf meat, apples, onion, stuffing mix, celery, milk, water, eggs,garlic powder, salt, and black pepper together in a bowl until ingredients areevenly combined.
Spread cream of mushroom soup into a 9x5-inch loaf pan.
Spoon meat mixtureinto the loaf pan and use a spatula to gently flatten the top of the loaf.
Bake in the preheated oven until no longer pink in the center, about 1 1/2hours.
An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read atleast 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).
Cool in pan for 5 to 10 minutes.
Flip meatloaf pan upside-down onto a serving platter so the cream of mushroomsoup is on the top of the loaf.
