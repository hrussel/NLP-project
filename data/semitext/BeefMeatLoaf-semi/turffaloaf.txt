Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Lightly grease a 5x9 inch loafpan.
In a large bowl, mix the turkey, buffalo, bread crumbs, dry onion soup mix,Parmesan cheese, Italian seasoning, egg, dill dip, blue cheese dressing, andWorcestershire sauce.
Transfer to the prepared loaf pan.
Cover with aluminum foil, and bake 45 minutes in the preheated oven.
Removefoil, and continue baking 15 minutes, to an internal temperature of 160degrees F (70 degrees C).
Let stand 10 minutes before slicing.
