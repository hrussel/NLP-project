Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Lightly grease a 9x5-inch loafpan.
Press brown sugar in the bottom of prepared loaf pan and spread ketchup oversugar.
Mix ground beef, milk, eggs, salt, ground pepper, onion, ginger, bread crumbs,and parsley thoroughly in a mixing bowl.
Shape into a loaf and place on top ofketchup.
Bake in preheated oven until no longer pink in the center, about 1 hour.
Aninstant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 160degrees F (70 degrees C).
