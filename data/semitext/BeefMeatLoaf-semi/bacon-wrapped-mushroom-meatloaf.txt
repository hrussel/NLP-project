Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Thoroughly mix beef, pork, lamb, mushrooms, bread crumbs, eggs, garlic, thyme,soy sauce, soup base, parsley, and sage in a large bowl; shape into 2 loaves.
Wrap bacon strips around the loaves to cover completely.
Place loaves on abaking sheet.
Bake in the preheated oven until the loaves are no longer pink in the center,90 minutes to 2 hours.
An instant-read thermometer inserted into the centershould read at least 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).
