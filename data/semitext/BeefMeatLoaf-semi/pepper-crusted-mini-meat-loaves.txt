Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Lightly grease a baking dish.
Mix ground beef, rice, parsley, egg, Worcestershire sauce, garlic powder,onion powder, and 1/4 teaspoon salt together in a bowl; form into 4 oblongmini-loaves.
Season the outside of each loaf generously with pepper.
Arrangeloaves in the prepared baking dish.
Bake in the preheated oven until no longer pink in the center, 35 to 45minutes.
An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center of each loafshould read at least 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).
Heat olive oil in a skillet over low heat; saute onion in oil until soft andgolden brown, 15 to 20 minutes.
Season onion with 1 pinch salt; serve overmeat loaves.
