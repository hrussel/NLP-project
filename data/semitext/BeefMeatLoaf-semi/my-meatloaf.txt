Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
In a large bowl, combine the beef, ketchup, soup mix, bread, Italian-styleseasoning and water.
Mix well.
Shape into a loaf and place into a 9 inch piepan OR a 9x13 inch baking dish.
Baste the top of the meatloaf with ketchup.
Bake at 425 degrees F (220 degrees C) for one hour, or until the edges arejust crispy and the top has a crust.
Cool for 15 to 20 minutes before cutting.
