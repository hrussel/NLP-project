Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
Combine onion, green bell pepper, milk, 1/4 cup ketchup, and eggs in a blenderand puree until smooth.
Stir ground beef, crushed saltine crackers, kosher salt, dry mustard in alarge bowl.
Add onion and egg mixture; stir to combine.
Transfer beef mixture to a shallow baking dish and shape into a loaf.
Drizzletop of the meatloaf with 2 tablespoons ketchup.
Bake in the preheated over for 45 minutes.
Remove from oven and let meatloafto rest uncovered for 15 minutes.
Slice and serve.
