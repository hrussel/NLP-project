Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Mix ground beef, Mexican-style tomato sauce, eggs, onion soup mix, breadcrumbs, green chiles, garlic and herb seasoning, and garlic powder in a largebowl until thoroughly combined.
Place mixture into a large loaf pan and shapeinto a loaf.
Bake in the preheated oven until meatloaf is no longer pink inside and aninstant-read meat thermometer inserted into the middle of the loaf shows atleast 160 degrees F (70 degrees C), about 1 hour.
