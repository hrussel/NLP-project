Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large bowl, mix the ground sirloin, salsa, bread crumbs, cheese, egg, andgarlic.
Season with parsley, salt, and pepper.
Transfer to a 5x9 inch loafpan.
Bake 45 minutes in the preheated oven, or to a minimum internal temperature of160 degrees F (72 degrees C).
