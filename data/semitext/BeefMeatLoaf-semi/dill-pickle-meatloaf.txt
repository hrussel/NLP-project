Preheat an oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Grease a 8x8 inch bakingdish.
Beat the egg in a mixing bowl.
Whisk in the onion, pickle juice, salt, andpepper.
Stir in the bread until soggy, then mix in the ground beef untilevenly combined.
Form into a loaf and place into the prepared pan.
Stirtogether the chopped pickles, ketchup, water, sugar, and Worcestershire sauce;pour over the meatloaf.
Bake in the preheated oven until no longer pink in the center, about 1 hour.
An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 160degrees F (70 degrees C).
