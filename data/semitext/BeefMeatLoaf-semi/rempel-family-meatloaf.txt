Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Stir the ground beef, crushed crackers, Cheddar cheese, and onion soup mix ina large bowl until well combined.
Whisk the eggs, ketchup, and steak sauce ina separate bowl until smooth.
Mix the eggs into the meat until evenlycombined, if the mixture seems too dry, add a little water.
Press into a 9x5inch loaf pan.
Bake in preheated oven until the meatloaf reaches 160 degrees F (71 degrees C)and is no longer pink in the center, 45 to 60 minutes.
