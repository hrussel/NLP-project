In a medium skillet, melt butter over medium heat.
Add garlic, onion, andgreen pepper and saute until onions are soft.
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
In a large mixing bowl, place ground beef, crushed crackers, Cheddar cheese,taco seasoning, and sauteed onion and green pepper.
Mix thoroughly.
Press beef mixture into a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
Bake in a preheated oven for 45to 55 minutes or until done.
Check with a meat thermometer for doneness.
Internal temperature should read 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
