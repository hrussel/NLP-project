Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Mix beef, pork, 1 cup spaghetti sauce, and dry soup mix together in a bowl.
Add bread crumbs and eggs; stir to incorporate.
Divide meat mixture betweentwo loaf pans.
Stir remaining 1 cup spaghetti sauce and condensed cream of mushroom souptogether in a bowl.
Bake meat loaves in the preheated oven for 30 minutes.
Pour cream of mushroomsoup mixture over the top of each loaf and return to oven.
Continue bakingloaves until no longer pink in the center, about 30 minutes more.
An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 160 degrees F(70 degrees C).
