Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large bowl, combine the beef, rice, brown sugar, onion and 1 tablespoonketchup.
Mix well and place in a 5x9 inch loaf pan.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 30 minutes.
Spread remaining 1/2 cupketchup over the top of the loaf.
Bake for 15 more minutes.
