Mix thoroughly beef, bread crumbs and egg.
Shape firmly into 8x4-inch loaf inbaking pan.
Bake at 350 degrees F for 30 minutes.
Spread 1/2 can soup over top of meatloaf.
Bake 30 minutes or until done.
Mix 2 tablespoons drippings, remaining soup and water in saucepan.
Heatthrough and serve with meat loaf.
