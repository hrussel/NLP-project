Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large bowl, mix ground beef, eggs, onion, barbeque sauce, green bellpepper, diced tomatoes with green chile peppers, Salsa Verde flavored tortillachips and Worcestershire sauce.
Shape into a loaf and place in an 11x7 inchbaking pan.
Bake in the preheated oven 75 to 90 minutes, or until center of the loaf is nolonger pink.
