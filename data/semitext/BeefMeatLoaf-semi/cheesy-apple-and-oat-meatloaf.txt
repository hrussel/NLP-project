Preheat an oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
Mix the beef, pork, egg, apple, onion, Worcestershire sauce, salt, and pepperin a mixing bowl until evenly mixed.
Add the Cheddar cheese, bread crumbs, andoats; mix again until evenly mixed.
Pack the meat mixture into the preparedloaf pan.
Bake in the preheated oven until no longer pink in the center, about 1 hour.
An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 160degrees F (72 degrees C).
Allow the meatloaf to rest for 10 minutes beforeslicing to keep the meat from crumbling.
