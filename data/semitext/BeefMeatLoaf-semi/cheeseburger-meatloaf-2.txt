Preheat an oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Mix ground beef, onion, bell pepper, 1 cup Cheddar cheese, Worcestershiresauce, sour cream, and crackers together in a bowl.
Place mixture on a bakingdish and shape into a loaf.
Bake in the preheated oven until no longer pink in the center, 35 to 40minutes.
An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read atleast 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).
Heat the cream of mushroom soup, milk, and 1 1/2 cup Cheddar cheese in asaucepan over medium heat until the cheese has melted, about 10 minutes.
Poursauce over the meatloaf before serving.
