Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Whisk eggs, milk, salt, and ground black pepper in a large bowl.
Add crumbledbread and stir until dissolved.
Mix ground beef, onion, Cheddar cheese, andcarrot into bread mixture; transfer mixture to a 9x5-inch loaf pan.
Combinebrown sugar, ketchup, and mustard in a small bowl; spread over meat mixture.
Bake in the preheated oven until no longer pink in the center, 1 to 1 1/4hours.
An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read atleast 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).
