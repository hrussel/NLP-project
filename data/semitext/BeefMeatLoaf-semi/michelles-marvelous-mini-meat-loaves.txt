Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Lightly grease a 9x13-inchbaking dish with cooking spray.
Stir ground beef, onion, oats, Cheddar cheese, milk, egg, and salt together ina bowl; mix well.
Divide meat mixture into 8 equal portions and form each intoa loaf shape.
Place loaves in prepared baking dish.
Stir ketchup, brown sugar, and mustard together in a bowl until smooth.
Spreadketchup mixture over meat loaves.
Bake in the preheated oven until no longer pink in the center, about 40minutes.
An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read atleast 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).
