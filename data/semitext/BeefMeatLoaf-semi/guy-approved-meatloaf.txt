Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
In a large bowl, combine the beef, eggs, onion, barbecue sauce, crackercrumbs, potato chips, liquid smoke, ground black pepper, salt, garlic powderand cayenne pepper and mix well.
On a lightly greased cookie sheet, shape the mixture into a loaf.
Lay thebacon slices across the width of the loaf so that the entire surface iscovered.
Cover the loaf loosely with foil.
Bake at 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) for 1 1/2 hours.
