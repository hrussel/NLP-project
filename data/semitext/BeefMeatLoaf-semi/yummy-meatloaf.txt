Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large bowl, combine the beef, onion, crushed crackers, salt, ground blackpepper, steak sauce, eggs and water and mix well.
Transfer to an ungreased9x13 inch baking dish.
Form into a loaf about 6x10 inches.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 1 hour, or until center iscompletely cooked.
