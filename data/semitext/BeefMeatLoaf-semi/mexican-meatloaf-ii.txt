Preheat an oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Combine the eggs, tomatoes, onion, Worcestershire sauce, salt, and pepper in alarge mixing bowl.
Gently stir the ground beef and crackers into the eggmixture.
Press into a 8x8 inch square baking pan.
Bake in preheated oven for35 minutes.
Remove the loaf from the oven, and drain any liquid from the pan.
Spread alayer of salsa on top of the loaf, and sprinkle with shredded cheese.
Returnthe loaf to the oven; bake until the meatloaf reaches 160 degrees F (71degrees C) and is no longer pink in the center, about 15 minutes.
