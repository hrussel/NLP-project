Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Mix ground beef, eggs, bread crumbs, salt, and black pepper together in alarge bowl.
Add green bell pepper, red bell pepper, yellow bell pepper, andonion; mix.
Spread the beef mixture into a 13x9-inch glass baking dish.
Make a trough in the middle of the meat mixture.
Put 3/4 of the Cheddar cheeseand half the tomato sauce into the trough.
Fold the beef mixture around thecheese and sauce filling to form a loaf, pinching to seal.
Loosely cover themeat loaf with aluminum foil.
Bake in preheated oven until the beef is cooked through and the cheese in themiddle is melted, about 45 minutes.
Remove and discard aluminum foil.
Top meatloaf with remaining Cheddar cheese and tomato sauce.
Continue baking until thecheese melts, about 15 minutes.
Rest meat loaf 10 to 15 minutes before slicingto serve.
