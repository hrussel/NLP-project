Thoroughly mix 1/2 cup tomato soup, beef, onion soup mix, bread crumbs and eggin a large bowl.
Place the mixture into a 13x9x2-inch baking pan and firmlyshape into an 8x4-inch loaf.
Bake at 350 degrees F for 1 hour 15 minutes or until the meatloaf is cookedthrough.
Heat 2 tablespoons pan drippings, remaining tomato soup and water in a 1-quartsaucepan over medium heat until the mixture is hot and bubbling.
Serve thesauce with the meatloaf.
