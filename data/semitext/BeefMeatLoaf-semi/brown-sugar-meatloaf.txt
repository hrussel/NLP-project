Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Lightly grease a 5x9 inch loafpan.
Press the brown sugar in the bottom of the prepared loaf pan and spread theketchup over the sugar.
In a mixing bowl, mix thoroughly all remaining ingredients and shape into aloaf.
Place on top of the ketchup.
Bake in preheated oven for 1 hour or until juices are clear.
