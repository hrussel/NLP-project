Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Place bacon in a large deep skillet over medium-high heat.
Add onions, andcook, stirring frequently, until bacon is crisp and onions are transparent.
Drain off excess grease, and set aside to cool.
In a large bowl, mix together the ground beef, pork sausage, bacon and onions,spinach, olives, pistachio nuts, 1 cup of ketchup, eggs, and meatloafseasoning until well blended.
Pat meat into a loaf shape, and place in a largebaking pan.
A roasting pan will work well.
Poke cheese cubes into the top ofthe loaf so they are completely covered.
Brush remaining ketchup generouslyover the top.
Bake for 1 1/2 hours in the preheated oven, or until the internal temperatureof the loaf is 180 degrees F (82 degrees C).
