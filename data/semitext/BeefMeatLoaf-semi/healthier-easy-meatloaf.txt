Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Lightly grease a 9x5-inch loafpan.
Combine beef, egg, onion, milk, bread crumbs, parsley, and carrot in a largebowl.
Season with salt and black pepper.
Place beef mixture in loaf pan.
Stir together brown sugar, mustard, and ketchup in a small bowl.
Pour overmeatloaf.
Bake in preheated oven until no longer pink in the center, about 1 hour.
Aninstant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 160degrees F (70 degrees C).
