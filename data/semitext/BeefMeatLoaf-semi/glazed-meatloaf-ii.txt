Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a small bowl, combine ketchup, brown sugar, 1 tablespoon lemon juice anddry mustard until smooth.
In a large bowl, combine ground beef, shredded bread, onion, egg, bouillon, 3tablespoons lemon juice, and 1/3 cup of the ketchup mixture until well mixed.
Form into a loaf and place in a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
Bake 1 hour.
Pour off fat.
Pour reserved ketchup mixture over loaf.
Bake 10minutes more.
