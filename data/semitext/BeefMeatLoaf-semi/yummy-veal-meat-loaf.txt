Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
In a bowl, mix the veal, bread crumbs, egg, baby carrots, 1/2 cup ketchup,garlic, and onion.
Season with salt, parsley, chili powder, and pepper.
Transfer to an 8x8 inch pan, and form into a loaf shape.
Top with remainingketchup.
Cover, and bake 45 minutes in the preheated oven.
Remove cover, and continuebaking 15 minutes, or to an internal temperature of 160 degrees F (70 degreesC).
Let sit 10 minutes before serving.
