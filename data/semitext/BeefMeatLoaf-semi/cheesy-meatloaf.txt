Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large bowl combine the beef, pork, soup mix, eggs, bread crumbs, pepperand water.
Stir in 1/2 cup of the cheese.
Mix well and spread half of mixtureinto a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
Spread remaining 1 1/2 cups cheese over meat loafmixture.
Top with remaining half of meat loaf mixture.
Bake in preheated oven for 60 minutes.
Let cool 10 to 15 minutes beforeserving.
