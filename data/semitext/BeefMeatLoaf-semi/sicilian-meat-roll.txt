In a large bowl, combine eggs and tomato juice.
Stir in the bread crumbs,parsley, oregano, salt, pepper, garlic and ground beef.
Mix thoroughly.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
On a piece of foil or waxed paper, pat and shape meat into a 10x8 inchrectangle.
Arrange ham slices on top of meat, leaving a small margin aroundedges.
Tear up the cheese slices, reserving 1 slice whole, and sprinkle overthe ham.
Starting from the short end, carefully roll up meat, using the foil or waxedpaper to lift.
Seal the edges and ends of the meat.
Place roll, seam sidedown, in a 9x13 inch baking dish.
Bake in a preheated oven for about 75 minutes.
Cut the reserved slice ofcheese into 4 triangles.
Overlap the triangles on top of loaf.
Bake foranother 2 minutes or until cheese melts.
