Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
Line shallow baking pan with aluminum foil;spray foil with cooking spray.
Mix beef, tomato sauce, Egg Beaters, oats and soup mix in large bowl untilwell blended.
Shape into 4 mini meatloaves, about 4x3 inches; place inprepared pan.
Spread ketchup evenly over tops of meatloaves.
Bake 25 minutes or until meatloaves are cooked through (160 degrees F).
Letstand 5 minutes before serving.
