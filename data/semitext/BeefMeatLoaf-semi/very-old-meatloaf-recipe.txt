Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Mix ground beef with water and milk in a large bowl using your hands untilbeef is evenly moistened.
Mix bread crumbs, onion, carrot, apple, and egg into the beef mixture untilevenly integrated.
Form the beef mixture into a loaf.
Transfer the meatloaf to a deep baking dish; tent with a sheet of aluminumfoil.
Bake in preheated oven for 1 hour; remove foil tent and bake until no longerpink the center, about 30 minutes more.
