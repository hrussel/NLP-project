Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
In a large bowl, mix together ground beef, salsa, cheese, egg, and crackercrumbs.
Form into a loaf, and place in prepared pan.
Bake for approximately 1 hour, or until done.
Internal temperature shouldmeasure at least 160 degrees F (70 degrees C), the meat should be brownedthrough, and the juices clear.
