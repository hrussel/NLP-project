Cook ground beef, onion and green pepper in large skillet over medium heat 5minutes or until beef is no longer pink; pour off drippings.
Add tomatoes,water and taco seasoning; simmer 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Toss pasta with meat mixture.
Spoon 3 cups of mixture into an 11x7-inch bakingdish.
Sprinkle with 1 cup cheese; top with remaining meat mixture.
Cover withfoil; bake in preheated 375 degrees F oven 30 minutes.
Uncover; sprinkle withremaining cheese.
Return to oven 5 minutes or until cheese is melted.
