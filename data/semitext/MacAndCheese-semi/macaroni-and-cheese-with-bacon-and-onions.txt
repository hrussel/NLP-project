Follow the recipe for Creamy Macaroni and Cheese using Vermont white cheddar.
Fry bacon in a large skillet over medium-high heat until crisp, about 5minutes.
Transfer to a paper towel-lined plate.
Pour off all but 3 tablespoonsof bacon drippings.
Add onions and thyme; saute until soft and golden brown, 8to 10 minutes.
Stir bacon and onions into macaroni.
Serve hot.
