Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
Cook macaroni in large saucepan as directed onpackage for al dente pasta.
Rinse under cold water; drain well.
Melt butter in same saucepan on medium heat.
Sprinkle with flour andseasonings.
Cook and stir 2 minutes or until well blended.
Gradually stir inmilk until smooth.
Stirring constantly, cook 3 minutes or until sauce startsto thicken.
Stir in cheese until melted and smooth.
Remove from heat.
Addmacaroni; toss gently to coat.
Pour into greased 13x9-inch baking dish.
Mixpanko and paprika; sprinkle evenly over top.
Bake 25 to 30 minutes or until bubbly and golden brown on top.
Let stand 5minutes before serving.
