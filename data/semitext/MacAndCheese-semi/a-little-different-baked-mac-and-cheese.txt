Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Cook elbow macaroni inthe boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to thebite, 8 minutes.
Drain.
Spread half the macaroni into a 9x13-inch casserole dish; top with half thetomatoes and half the Cheddar cheese.
Repeat layering with remaining macaroni,tomatoes, and Cheddar cheese.
Pour milk over entire casserole; sprinkle withcrushed crackers.
Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is melted and crackers are golden,about 30 minutes.
