Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
Cook pasta according to package directions; rinse and drain.
Set aside.
Melt 1 tablespoon butter in large saucepan on medium heat.
Cook and stirsausage and onions 3 to 5 minutes or until onions are tender.
Stir in Alfredosauce, and half and half.
Bring to boiling, stirring constantly; reduce heat.
Simmer 1 minute.
Remove saucepan from heat.
Stir in cheese and black pepper until cheese ismelted.
Stir in pasta and sausage mixture.
Pour mixture into lightly sprayed 139-inch baking dish.
Sprinkle evenly withcombined bread crumbs and 1/4 cup melted butter.
Bake 20 to 25 minutes oruntil crumbs are golden brown and pasta mixture is hot and bubbly.
