Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Bring a large pot of lightlysalted water to a boil.
Cook elbow macaroni in boiling water, stirringoccasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, 8 minutes.
Drain.
Melt 2 tablespoons butter in a saucepan over medium heat.
Stir in flour tomake a roux.
Slowly add milk to roux, stirring constantly.
Stir in Cheddar andParmesan cheeses and cook over low heat until cheese is melted and sauce isthick, about 3 minutes.
Place macaroni in large baking dish and pour sauceover macaroni.
Stir well.
Melt 2 tablespoons butter in a skillet over medium heat.
Add breadcrumbs andstir until butter is absorbed, 2 to 3 minutes.
Spread over macaroni to cover.
Sprinkle with paprika.
Bake in preheated oven until cheese sauce is hot and breadcrumbs are browned,about 30 minutes.
