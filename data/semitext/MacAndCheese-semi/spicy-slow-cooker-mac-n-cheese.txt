Stir the condensed soup and water together in a slow cooker until smooth.
Addthe shell pasta and andouille sausage.
Set the slow cooker to High; cook 2hours, stirring frequently to prevent sticking.
Once the pasta is tender, stir in the sour cream, Cheddar, and mozzarellauntil the cheeses melt.
Season to taste with salt and pepper.
Remove fromheat, and allow to rest 15 minutes before serving.
