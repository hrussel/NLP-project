Heat a large, deep skillet over medium-high heat.
Cook and stir beef in thehot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes; drain and discardgrease.
Return skillet to heat.
Stir salsa, taco seasoning mix, and black pepper intothe beef; simmer the mixture until hot, about 5 minutes.
Stir water, macaroni, and milk into the beef mixture; bring to a boil, reduceheat to medium-low, place a cover on the skillet, and simmer the mixture untilthe pasta is tender, about 10 minutes.
Stir cheese food and Cheddar cheese into the pasta mixture; cook until thecheese melts completely, 5 to 7 minutes.
