Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Bring a large pot of lightlysalted water to a boil, add pasta, and cook until done; drain.
In 9x13 inch baking dish, stir together macaroni, shredded Cheddar cheese,cottage cheese, sour cream, Parmesan cheese, salt and pepper.
In a small bowl,mix together bread crumbs and melted butter.
Sprinkle topping over macaronimixture.
Bake 30 to 35 minutes, or until top is golden.
