Preheat oven broiler.
Grease a 1 1/2 quart microwave safe dish.
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Add pasta and cook for 8to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
Microwave frozen mac and cheese according to package directions.
Stir together macaroni, mac and cheese, milk and pepper in prepared dish.
Mixwell.
Microwave on high 2 minutes, until heated through.
Sprinkle with Cheddarand Parmesan and place under oven broiler.
Broil until cheese is melted andbeginning to brown, 2 to 5 minutes.
