Heat the butter in a skillet over medium heat.
Stir in the onion; cook andstir until the onion has softened and turned translucent, about 5 minutes.
Stir in flour and salt and cook, stirring constantly, for 2 minutes more.
Add milk and macaroni to saucepan, and bring to a boil.
Reduce heat and cover.
Simmer for 15 minutes or until pasta is tender, stirring occasionally.
Mix in cheese, and stir until cheese melts.
