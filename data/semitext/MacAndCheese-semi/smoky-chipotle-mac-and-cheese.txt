Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Grease a 2-quart casseroledish.
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Cook elbow macaroni inthe boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to thebite, 8 minutes.
Drain.
Stir soup, evaporated milk, Colby cheese, and chipotle chile into the cookedpasta until well combined.
Spoon mixture into the prepared casserole dish.
Stir together panko bread crumbs and olive oil in a small bowl.
Sprinkle breadcrumb mixture over the top of casserole.
Bake in the preheated oven until the top is browned and the casserole isbubbling, 25 to 30 minutes.
