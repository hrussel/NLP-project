In a 12-inch nonstick skillet, brown sausage over medium-high heat for 3-5minutes.
Transfer to a plate and keep warm.
In the same skillet, bring water to a boil.
Add pasta, reduce heat, stir oftenuntil pasta is al dente, about 8-10 minutes.
(Do not drain).
In a small bowl, whisk cornstarch, milk and hot pepper sauce.
Gradually add topasta; cook and stir until slightly thickened, about 2 minutes.
Remove fromheat; add cheese and sausage.
Stir until blended and cheese melts.
Sprinklewith crumbs if desired.
Serve.
