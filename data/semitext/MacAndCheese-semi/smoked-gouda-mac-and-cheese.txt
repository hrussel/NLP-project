Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Lightly grease a 10 inchcasserole dish.
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Add pasta and cook for 8to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
Melt butter in a small saucepan over medium heat.
Stir in the flour and cookuntil a roux forms.
Stir in the milk, salt and pepper; cook, stirringconstantly, until sauce is smooth and thick and coats the back of a spoon.
Remove from heat and stir in cheese.
Combine cooked pasta and cheese sauce; transfer to prepared dish.
Bake in preheated oven for 15 minutes, or until heated through.
