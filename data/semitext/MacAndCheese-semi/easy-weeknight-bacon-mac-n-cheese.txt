Heat oven to 400 degrees F.
Spray 2-quart casserole with cooking spray.
Cookand drain pasta as directed on package.
Meanwhile, in 3-quart saucepan, heat cooking sauce, milk and garlic toboiling.
Remove from heat; stir in cheeses until melted.
Stir in pasta, baconand bell pepper.
Spoon into casserole.
Bake uncovered 20 minutes or until edges are bubbly.
Sprinkle with breadcrumbs.
