Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil and stir in macaroni fromthe packages, retaining cheese mix packets for later use.
Cook macaroni at arolling boil over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until tender but stillslightly firm, 8 to 10 minutes.
Drain and rinse macaroni with cold water.
Return cooked macaroni to the pot.
Mix butter, chicken broth, and tomatoes with macaroni and bring mixture to asimmer over medium heat; stir in garlic powder, cumin, dry mustard, pepper,and contents of cheese mix packets.
Stir in milk and reduce heat to low; heatsoup almost to a simmer.
Do not boil.
Serve soup in bowls and top each serving with about 2 tablespoons of sourcream.
