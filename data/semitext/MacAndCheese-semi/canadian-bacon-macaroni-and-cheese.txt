Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Add macaroni and cook for8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
While macaroni is boiling, fry bacon and place on paper towels to drain.
Cutinto bite size pieces.
Melt margarine in a large saucepan over medium low heat.
Stir in flour, thentomatoes and wait for sauce to thicken, stirring occasionally.
When thicknessis to your liking, stir in cheese until it has melted.
Stir in cooked macaroniand bacon and heat through.
Serve hot.
