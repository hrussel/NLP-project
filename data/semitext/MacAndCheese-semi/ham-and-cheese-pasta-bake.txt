Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
In a large bowl, combine dry pasta, spaghetti sauce, ham, garlic, onion powderand pepper.
Fill sauce jar with water, and pour over mixture.
Mix well, andspoon into 9x13 inch casserole dish.
Cover tightly with foil.
Bake in preheated oven for 35 minutes.
Carefully remove foil, then sprinklewith mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
Bake for 5 minutes, or until cheese ismelted.
