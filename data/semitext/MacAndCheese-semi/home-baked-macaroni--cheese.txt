Heat oven to 375 degrees F.
Prepare Dinner as directed on package.
Stir in 1/4 cup shredded cheese, sourcream and pepper.
Spoon into greased 1-1/2-L baking dish; top with remaining shredded cheese,then combined cracker crumbs and margarine.
Bake 20 minutes Let stand 5 minutes before serving.
Serve with peas.
