Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Add pasta and cook for 8to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain and reserve.
In a microwave-safe bowl, combine milk, flour and salt and pepper to taste;whisk or beat until smooth.
Add butter and cheese; microwave on high for 5minutes and whisk until smooth.
Microwave for an additional 4 to 5 minutes andwhisk or beat until smooth and no lumps remain.
Add cooked pasta to mixture; stir and serve.
