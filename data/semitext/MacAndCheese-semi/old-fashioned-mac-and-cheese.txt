Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Prepare the elbow macaroni according to package directions.
Meanwhile, melt the butter in a small saucepan over medium high heat.
Stir inthe flour until a cream colored paste forms.
Then pour in the milk and stirconstantly until this comes to a hard boil, then stir for 1 more minute.
Remove from heat and set aside.
When the macaroni is cooked, spread 1/2 of it into the bottom of a lightlygreased 9x13-inch baking dish.
Then layer 1/2 of the grated onion, 1/2 of thesalt and pepper and 1/2 of each of the cheeses.
Repeat this one more time:macaroni, onion, salt and pepper and cheeses, and then pour the reserved whitesauce over all.
Top off with small pats of butter to taste.
Cover and bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 45 minutes.
