Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Cook elbow macaroni inthe boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to thebite, 8 minutes.
Drain.
Stir together macaroni, shredded Cheddar cheese, cottage cheese, sour cream,Parmesan cheese, and salt and pepper in a 9x13-inch baking dish.
Mix togetherbread crumbs and melted butter in a small bowl; sprinkle topping over macaronimixture.
Bake until top is golden in the preheated oven, 30 to 35 minutes.
