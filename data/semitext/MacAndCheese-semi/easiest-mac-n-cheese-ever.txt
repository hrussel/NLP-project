Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Add pasta and cook untilal dente, 8 to 10 minutes; drain.
Place a saucepan over medium-low heat.
Combine the cheese food, milk, andpepper in the saucepan; cook until the cheese has melted, stirring frequently.
Stir in the drained macaroni until evenly coated.
