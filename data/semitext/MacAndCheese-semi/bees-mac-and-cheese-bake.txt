Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Lightly grease a largecasserole dish.
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Stir in shell pasta andcook 12 minutes, or until tender but still firm.
Drain, and transfer to alarge bowl.
Mix cream of mushroom soup, Cheddar cheese, and pimentos with the pasta.
Pourinto the prepared casserole dish.
Top with crushed crackers.
Cover dish, and bake 30 minutes in the preheated oven.
Remove cover, andcontinue baking 15 minutes, until cheese is bubbly and crackers are lightlybrowned.
