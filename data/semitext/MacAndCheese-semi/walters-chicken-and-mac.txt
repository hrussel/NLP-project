Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Add macaroni and cook for8 minutes or until al dente; drain.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large bowl combine cooked macaroni, chicken, cheddar cheese, soup, milkand mushrooms.
Place mixture in a 9x13 inch baking dish.
Bake uncovered in preheated oven for 50 to 60 minutes; serve.
