Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Lightly grease 24 muffin cups.
In a bowl, cream together the butter, sugar, and vanilla.
Mix in banana andsour cream.
Stir in the eggs.
In a separate bowl, mix the flour, bakingpowder, and baking soda.
Mix dry ingredients into the banana mixture untilevenly moist.
Fold in chocolate chips.
Transfer batter to the prepared muffincups, filling each cup about 3/4 full.
Bake 25 minutes in the preheated oven, or until a knife inserted in the centerof a muffin comes out clean.
