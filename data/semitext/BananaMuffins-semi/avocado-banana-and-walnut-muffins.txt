Heat oven to 375 degrees F.
Grease 12 muffin tin wells or line with papercups.
In a large bowl, mix avocado, bananas, eggs and milk.
In a separate bowl,whisk or sift together flour, sugar, baking soda and salt.
Stir into avocadomixture; do not over-mix.
Stir in walnuts.
Spoon batter into prepared muffin tin; bake 15 to 20 minutes or until topsstart to brown and a toothpick inserted into a muffin comes out clean.
