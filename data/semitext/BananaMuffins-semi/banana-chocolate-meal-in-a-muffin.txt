Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Spray a 12-cup muffin tin withcooking spray.
Mix mashed bananas and brown sugar together in a large bowl.
Add eggs, oliveoil, and vanilla extract; mix well.
Mix flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and cinnamon in a separate bowl.
Stirbanana mixture into flour mixture.
Fold chocolate chips and walnuts intobatter.
Distribute batter into the muffin cups.
Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted in the center of amuffin comes out clean, 35 to 40 minutes.
