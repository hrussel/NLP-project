Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Blend butter or margarine, sugar, egg, banana, dissolved coffee, and vanillain food processor for 2 minutes.
Add flour, salt, baking powder, and soda, andblend just until flour disappears.
Add chocolate chips and mix in with woodenspoon.
Spoon mixture into 15 to 18 paper-lined muffin cups.
Bake for 25 minutes.
Cool on wire racks.
