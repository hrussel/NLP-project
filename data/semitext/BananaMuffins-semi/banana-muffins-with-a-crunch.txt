Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Line 24 muffin cups with paperliners.
In a bowl, mix the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
Mix inthe eggs, milk, vanilla, and butter.
Fold in mashed bananas, chopped banana,granola, walnuts, and coconut.
Scoop into the prepared muffin cups.
Sprinklewith banana chips.
Bake 25 minutes in the preheated oven, or until a knife inserted in the centerof a muffin comes out clean.
