Combine flour, oats, sugar, baking powder, soda, and salt.
In a large bowl, beat the egg lightly.
Stir in the milk, oil, and vanilla.
Addthe mashed banana, and combine thoroughly.
Stir the flour mixture into thebanana mixture until just combined.
Line a 12-cup muffin tin with paper bakecups, and divide the batter among them.
Bake at 400 degrees F (205 degrees C) for 18 to 20 minutes.
