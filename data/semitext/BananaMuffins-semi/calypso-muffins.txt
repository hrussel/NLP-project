In a saucepan, bring water to a boil.
stir in oats, and cook 1 minute.
Cover,and remove from heat; allow to cool.
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Grease and flour a muffin pan,or use paper liners.
In a large bowl, combine flour, bran, sugar, baking powder, and nutmeg.
Make awell in the center, and add mashed banana, cooked and cooled oatmeal, egg,pineapple, coconut milk, and coconut extract.
Mix until smooth.
Scoop into 12muffin cups.
Bake in preheated oven for 25 to 30 minutes, or until golden brown, and topsspring back when lightly tapped.
