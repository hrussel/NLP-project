Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
Prepare a muffin tin with paper liners.
Prepare muffins according to package instructions using water, eggs and oil,but use only 1/4 cup oil instead of 1/2 cup, and add 1/4 cup applesauce.
Stirin banana, 3/4 cup almonds and chocolate.
Divide evenly among muffin cups andsprinkle with remaining 1/4 cup almonds.
Bake 15-20 minutes, until a tester comes out clean.
Cool muffin tin on a rackfor 10 minutes, then remove muffins and continue cooling on rack.
Store airtight at room temperature for up to 4 days.
