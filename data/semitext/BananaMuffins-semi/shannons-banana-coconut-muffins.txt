Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Grease and flour 12 muffinscups or line with paper liners.
Beat sugar and margarine together in a bowl using an electric mixer untilsmooth and creamy; stir in eggs.
Stir bananas into margarine mixture; stir invanilla extract.
Whisk flour, baking powder, and salt together in a bowl; stir into margarinemixture until just combined.
Fold oranges and coconut into batter.
Spoonbatter into muffin tins.
Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted in the center of amuffin comes out clean, 20 to 30 minutes.
