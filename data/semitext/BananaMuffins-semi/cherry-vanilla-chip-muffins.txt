Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (205 degrees C).
Lightly grease or line a 12-cupmuffin tin with paper liners.
In a large bowl blend the banana, egg substitute, soy milk, oil and applesaucetogether.
Stir in the rice flour, sucanat, baking powder and salt.
Mix untiljust moistened then stir in the dried cherries and vanilla chips.
Pour batterinto the prepared muffin tin, filling each cup 2/3 full.
Bake at 400 degrees F (205 degrees C) for 30 minutes.
