Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
Grease 12 muffin cups or linewith paper liners.
Stir whole wheat flour, rolled oats, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, andchocolate chips together in a bowl.
Make a well in the center.
Whisk eggs in aseparate bowl until frothy; mix applesauce, skim milk, and banana into eggs.
Pour moist ingredients into well made in dry ingredients; stir just tomoisten.
Batter will be lumpy.
Fill prepared muffin cups about 3/4 full.
Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center ofseveral muffins comes out clean, 16 to 18 minutes.
Cool in pan for 5 minutesbefore removing to finish cooling on wire racks.
