Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Spray 16 muffin cups withcooking spray.
Sift all-purpose flour, whole wheat flour, baking powder, baking soda, andsalt together in a large bowl.
Mix bananas, white sugar, canola oil, brown sugar, and egg together in aseparate large bowl; fold banana mixture into flour mixture until justincorporated.
Scoop batter into prepared muffin cups.
Bake in the preheated oven until muffins spring back when lightly tapped inthe middle, 14 to 16 minutes.
