Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Grease 12 muffin cups or linewith paper muffin liners.
Stir the flour, baking soda, baking powder, and sugar together in a bowl.
Addthe bananas, mayonnaise, and chocolate chips; stir until well combined.
Pourinto prepared muffin cups to about 3/4 full.
Bake in the preheated oven until golden and the tops spring back when lightlypressed, about 20 minutes.
