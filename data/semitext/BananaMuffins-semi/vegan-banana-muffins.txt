Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Grease 12 muffin cups or linewith paper liners.
Mix flour, white sugar, brown sugar, cinnamon, baking powder, baking soda,nutmeg, and salt together in a large bowl.
Stir bananas, canola oil, andcoconut milk together in a separate bowl; mix banana mixture into flourmixture until just combined.
Fill muffin cups with batter.
Bake in the preheated oven until a tooth pick inserted in the center of amuffin comes out clean, 30 to 35 minutes.
