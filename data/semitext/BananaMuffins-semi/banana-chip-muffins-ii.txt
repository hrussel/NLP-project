Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (205 degrees C).
Coat a 12 cup muffin tin withnonstick spray.
Whisk together flour, baking powder, soda, cinnamon, salt, and sugar.
Mix inegg whites, applesauce, and banana mash until just moistened.
Stir inchocolate chips.
Divide batter into muffin cups.
Bake for 15 to 18 minutes.
