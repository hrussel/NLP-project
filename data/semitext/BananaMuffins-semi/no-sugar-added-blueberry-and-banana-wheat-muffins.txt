Preheat an oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Grease 18 muffin cups, orline with paper muffin liners.
Mix together mashed bananas, egg, water, and oil in a large bowl.
Mix inflour, baking soda, and baking powder until mostly smooth (you will still seelumps from the banana, but that's okay!).
Gently fold the blueberries into thebatter.
Bake in the preheated oven until golden and the tops spring back when lightlypressed, about 15 minutes.
Remove muffins from tins, and cool on a wire rack.
