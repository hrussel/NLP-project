Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Coat muffin pans with non-stickspray, or use paper liners.
Sift together the flour, baking powder, bakingsoda, and salt; set aside.
Combine bananas, sugar, egg, and melted butter in a large bowl.
Fold in flourmixture, and mix until smooth.
Scoop into muffin pans.
Bake in preheated oven.
Bake mini muffins for 10 to 15 minutes, and largemuffins for 25 to 30 minutes.
Muffins will spring back when lightly tapped.
