Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Lightly grease a 12 cup muffinpan.
In a large bowl, mix whole wheat flour, wheat bran, wheat germ, baking soda,baking powder, and salt.
In a medium bowl, beat together eggs, honey, and vegetable oil.
Stir inbananas, milk, lemon juice, and poppy seeds.
Make a well in the center of the wheat flour mixture, and pour in egg andbanana mixture.
Stir until just blended.
Spoon into the prepared muffin pan.
Bake 25 to 30 minutes in the preheated oven, until a toothpick inserted in thecenter comes out clean.
