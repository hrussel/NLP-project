Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Grease muffin cups or line withpaper muffin liners.
Mix together flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt.
In a separate bowl,beat together banana, sugar, egg and vanilla.
Stir in the sour cream.
Stirbanana mixture into flour until just combined.
Scoop batter into preparedmuffin cups.
Bake in preheated oven for 15 to 20 minutes, or until a toothpick insertedinto center of a muffin comes out clean.
Let cool before serving.
For bestflavor, place in an airtight container or bag overnight.
