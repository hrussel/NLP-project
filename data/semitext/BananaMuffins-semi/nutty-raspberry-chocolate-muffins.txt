Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
Grease 12 muffin cups or fillwith paper liners.
Mix almond meal, all-purpose flour, whole wheat flour, baking powder, bakingsoda, cinnamon, and salt together in a bowl.
Beat butter and sugar together in a separate bowl until light and fluffy.
Addeggs, one by one, beating until each is incorporated.
Stir vanilla into thebutter mixture.
Mix milk and yogurt together in a separate bowl.
Add butter mixture,alternating with milk mixture, to the flour mixture until just incorporated.
Stir mashed banana into batter; fold in raspberries and chocolate chips.
Fillmuffin cups 3/4-full with batter.
Bake on the middle rack in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted inthe middle of the muffins comes out clean, 18 to 25 minutes.
