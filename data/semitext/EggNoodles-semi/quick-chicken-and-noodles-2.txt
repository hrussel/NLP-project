Season the chicken with the garlic powder and paprika.
Cook the chicken in a12 inch nonstick skillet over medium-high heat until it's well browned on bothsides.
Add the stock, basil, black pepper and vegetables to the skillet and heat to aboil.
Stir in the noodles.
Reduce the heat to low.
Cover and cook for 10minutes or until the chicken is cooked through and the noodles are tender.
