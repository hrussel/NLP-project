Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Bring a large pot of lightlysalted water to a boil.
Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until aldente; drain.
In a skillet over medium heat, brown the ground beef with the onion until nopink shows; drain.
Mix in 1 of the cans of tomato sauce, salt, pepper andcinnamon.
Pour into shallow 3 quart casserole baking dish.
Pour in noodles in an even layer.
Top with cottage cheese; sprinkle withonions and Cheddar cheese.
Pour on remaining can of tomato sauce.
Bake in a preheated over for 30 minutes.
