Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Cook egg noodles inboiling water, stirring occasionally, until cooked through yet firm to thebite, about 5 minutes; drain.
Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat.
Add kohlrabi, season with salt andpepper, and cook until the kohlrabi is tender, 7 to 10 minutes.
Stir thedrained egg noodles into the kohlrabi; cook and stir until the noodles areslightly fried, 5 to 7 minutes.
