Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Grease a 2-quart baking dish.
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Cook egg noodles in theboiling water, stirring occasionally, until cooked through but firm to thebite, about 5 minutes.
Drain.
Stir melted butter and noodles in a large bowl.
Mix apples, egg yolks, sugar, raisins, cinnamon, and vanilla extract intonoodle mixture until well blended.
Beat egg whites in a large bowl until stiff peaks form.
Lift your beater orwhisk straight up: the egg whites should form a sharp peak that holds itsshape.
Fold egg whites into noodle mixture; pour into prepared baking dish.
Bake in the preheated oven until browned, about 40 minutes.
