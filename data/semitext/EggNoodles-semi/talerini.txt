Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Bring a large pot oflightly salted water to a boil.
Add the noodles, and cook until tender, about7 minutes.
Drain, and return to the pot.
Crumble the ground beef into a large skillet over medium-high heat.
Cook andstir until evenly browned.
Drain off grease.
Stir in the tomato sauce, cornand olives.
Season with chili powder.
Stir the beef mixture in with thenoodles.
Pour into a 9x13 inch baking dish.
Top with Cheddar cheese.
Bake for 20 minutes in the preheated oven, until the cheese is melted.
