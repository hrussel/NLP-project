Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Cook egg noodles in theboiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to thebite, about 5 minutes.
Drain.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Combine mushroom soup, milk, and tuna in a saucepan over medium heat; cookuntil well blended and warmed through, about 5 minutes.
Pour mixture into a9x13-inch baking dish; add noodles and mix well.
Top noodles with cheese foodand sprinkle potato chips over cheese.
Bake in the preheated oven until casserole is bubbling, 30 to 45 minutes.
