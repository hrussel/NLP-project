Heat oil in large skillet.
Cube chicken into bite size pieces and brown in hotoil.
Meanwhile, in a large saucepan, boil noodles in water until soft, thendrain.
Add hot cooked noodles to chicken in skillet and pour in gravy andpeas.
Stir together until ingredients are covered/coated with gravy.
Coverskillet, reduce heat to medium low and let simmer for 10 minutes until gravyand peas are hot.
Serve directly from the skillet.
