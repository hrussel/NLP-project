Heat oil in a large skillet over medium high heat.
Roll the beef in flour andsaute in the hot oil until well browned, about 5 minutes.
Transfer the meat to the slow cooker and top with the soup, water andbouillon.
Cook on high setting for 8 hours.
Stir in the sour cream during the last 30minutes.
Cook the egg noodles according to package directions.
Serve the meat over thenoodles.
