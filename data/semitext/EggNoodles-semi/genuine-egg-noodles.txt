Combine flour, salt and baking powder.
Mix in eggs and enough water to makethe dough workable.
Knead dough until stiff.
Roll into ball and cut intoquarters.
Using 1/4 of the dough at a time, roll flat to about 1/8 inch useflour as needed, top and bottom, to prevent sticking.
Peel up and roll fromone end to the other.
Cut roll into 3/8 inch strips.
Noodles should be about 4to 5 inches long depending on how thin it was originally flattened.
Let dryfor 1 to 3 hours.
Cook like any pasta or, instead of drying first cook it fresh but make surewater is boiling and do not allow to stick.
It takes practice to do thisright.
