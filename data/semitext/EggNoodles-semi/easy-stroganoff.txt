In a large, heavy skillet, brown ground beef with Worcestershire sauce,seasoning salt, and garlic.
Drain well, set aside.
Cook noodles in a large pot of boiling water until done.
Drain.
Return noodles to pot.
Gently stir in ground beef, mushrooms, condensed soupand milk.
Place the pan over low heat, and heat through.
Stir in sour cream,and serve immediately.
