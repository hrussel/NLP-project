Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Add pasta and cook for 8to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
In a mixing bowl combine soup, milk, and water.
Pour a small amount of themixture into a 9x13 inch baking dish; enough to just cover the bottom.
Layer the cooked noodles, flaked tuna, peas, carrots, and potatoes until allused up.
Pour the remaining soup mixture over the layers.
Sprinkle with salt,pepper, and paprika.
Lightly coat the entire casserole with bread crumbs.
Cover and bake in preheated oven for 45 minutes.
