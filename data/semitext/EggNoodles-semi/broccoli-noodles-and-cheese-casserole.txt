Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Bring a large pot of lightlysalted water to a boil.
Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until aldente; drain.
Steam broccoli until bright green and tender, 5 to 10 minutes.
Combinebroccoli, pasta and cottage cheese in 2 quart baking dish; mix well.
Sprinkle pasta mixture with Cheddar cheese and bake for 8 to 10 minutes, untilcheese is bubbly.
