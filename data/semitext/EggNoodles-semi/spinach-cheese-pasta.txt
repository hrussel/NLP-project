Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Add egg noodles and cookfor 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente.
Drain, and return to the pot.
Heat the olive oil in a skillet, and cook the garlic and spinach 3 to 5minutes, until well coated.
Transfer to the pot with the drained pasta.
Tossin the cottage cheese.
Top with Parmesan cheese to serve.
