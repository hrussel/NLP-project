Cook pasta in a large pot of boiling salted water until al dente.
Drain.
In a large skillet, cook ground beef, onion, and mushrooms.
Drain grease.
Mixin garlic salt, ground black pepper, sugar, Worcestershire sauce, and tomatosauce.
Simmer for 30 minutes.
In a greased 2 quart casserole dish, layer half of the cooked egg noodles,then half of the sauce mixture, followed by half of the grated cheese.
Repeat.
Bake in a preheated 375 degree (190 degree C) oven for 20 to 30 minutes.
