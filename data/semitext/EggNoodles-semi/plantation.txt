Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Add noodles, and cookuntil tender, about 7 minutes.
Drain.
Meanwhile, crumble the ground beef into a large skillet over medium-high heat.
Season with onion powder, garlic powder, salt and pepper; cook and stir untilevenly browned.
Drain off grease.
Stir the cream cheese and cream of mushroom soup into the ground beef untilwell blended.
Mix in the drained noodles and corn.
Stir in evaporated milk toreach your desired consistency.
Top with mozzarella cheese, and serve.
