Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
Lightly grease a largecasserole dish.
In a skillet over medium heat, cook the ground beef until evenly brown.
Draingrease.
Mix in garlic, and season with salt and pepper.
Stir in spinach, andcook until heated through.
Stir in the uncooked egg noodles, and transfer tothe prepared casserole dish.
Mix in cream of mushroom soup and milk, andsprinkle with cheese.
Bake 45 minutes in the preheated oven, until bubbly.
