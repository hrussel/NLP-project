Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat.
Cook and stir onion in meltedbutter until softened, 7 to 10 minutes.
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Cook egg noodles in theboiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to thebite, about 5 minutes.
Drain and return to the pot.
Stir butter and onion mixture, cottage cheese, sour cream, sea salt, and blackpepper into the noodles.
Place the pot over medium heat; cook and stir untilheated through and warm, 5 to 8 minutes.
