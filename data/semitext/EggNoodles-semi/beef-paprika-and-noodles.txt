Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat.
Cook and stir steak in hot oil untilbrowned completely, about 5 minutes.
Stir water, ketchup, onion, Worcestershire sauce, brown sugar, paprika,mustard, garlic, salt, and pepper with the beef in the pot; bring to a boil,reduce heat to low, and cook at a simmer until meat is tender and the saucethickens, 2 to 3 hours.
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Cook egg noodles in theboiling water, stirring occasionally, until cooked through but firm to thebite, about 5 minutes; drain.
Ladle beef mixture over noodles to serve.
