In a large pot with boiling salted water cook egg noodles until al dente.
Drain.
In a large bowl mix together the cottage cheese, sour cream, minced onion,garlic, Worcestershire sauce, red pepper sauce, and salt.
Stir in cooked eggnoodles.
Pour into a greased 2 quart casserole dish.
Bake in a preheated 350 degree F (175 degrees C) for 30 minutes, or untilheated through and bubbly.
Serve warm.
