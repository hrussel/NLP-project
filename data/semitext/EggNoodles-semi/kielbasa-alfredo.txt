Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Add egg noodles, and cookuntil tender, about 8 minutes.
Drain.
Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat.
Add sliced kielbasa, and fryuntil toasty at the edges.
Drain excess grease, and stir in the alfredo sauce.
Stir in the noodles, and season with salt and pepper.
Serve with a sprinklingof Parmesan cheese.
