Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Add the egg noodles, andcook until tender, about 6 minutes.
Drain.
While the noodles are cooking, mix together the sour cream, milk, garlic andsalt; taste and adjust seasoning if desired.
Stir into drained noodles alongwith peas and tuna.
Heat through over low heat, and serve.
