Sift together the flour, salt and baking powder.
Add egg yolks and mix untildry ingredients are moistened.
Press into a ball and cut in quarters.
Roll out on floured surface 1/8 to 1/4inch thick; cut to desired width and length.
Lay on linen dish towel or woodendowel to dry.
Add to broth such as chicken or turkey and cook until done.
