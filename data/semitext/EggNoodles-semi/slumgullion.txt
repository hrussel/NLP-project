In a large skillet, cook and stir ground meat until browned.
Drain off excessfat.
Stir in corn and tomato soup.
Simmer while the noodles are cooking, about 10minutes.
Cook pasta in a large pot of boiling water until al dente.
Drain.
Stir noodles into ground beef mixture, and sprinkle parmesan cheese on top.
Serve immediately.
