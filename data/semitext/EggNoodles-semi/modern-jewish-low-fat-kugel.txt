Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Spray a 9x13-inch baking dishwith cooking spray.
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Cook egg noodles in theboiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to thebite, about 5 minutes.
Drain.
Gently stir egg noodles, sour cream, cottage cheese, pineapple with juice,eggs, orange juice, cranberries, melted butter, sugar, applesauce, orangezest, vanilla extract, and cinnamon together in a large bowl until thoroughlycombined.
Spoon the noodle mixture into prepared baking dish.
Bake in the preheated oven until kugel is set and browned on top, 60 to 75minutes.
