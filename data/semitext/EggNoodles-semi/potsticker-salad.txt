Cook egg noodles in a large pot of boiling water for 5 to 7 minutes, or untilal dente.
Drain, and set aside.
Heat the oil in a large frying pan over medium heat, cook potstickers untilgolden brown, turning once or twice.
Add the water to the pan, reduce heat tolow, cover, and cook for another 3 minutes, or until liquid has evaporated.
Rinse with cold water to cool, drain, and cut in half.
In a large mixing bowl, toss together the water chestnuts, baby corn, carrot,mushrooms, peanut sauce, noodles, and potstickers.
Chill 1 hour.
Toss withpeanuts just before serving.
