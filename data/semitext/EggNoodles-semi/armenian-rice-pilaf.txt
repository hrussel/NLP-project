Melt butter in large saucepan over medium-high heat.
Add the egg noodles andstir until they begin to brown, being careful not to burn the butter.
Stir inthe rice.
Continue stirring until rice is coated with butter.
Pour in the chicken broth and add the bouillon cubes.
Bring to a boil; reduceheat to low and cover tightly.
Cook without uncovering pot until all theliquid has been absorbed, about 20 minutes.
