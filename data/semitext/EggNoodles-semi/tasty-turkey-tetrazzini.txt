Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Butter a 9 x 13-inch dish.
For topping, combine all ingredients and set aside.
For filling, heat oil in a saute pan, add onions and cook for 3 to 4 minutesor until they are soft.
Add yellow squash and saute for 3 to 4 minutes.
Transfer to large bowl and add remaining ingredients and toss gently.
Placemixture into prepared pan.
Cover with topping and bake 30 to 35 minutes oruntil thoroughly heated.
Serve.
