Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Add pasta and cook for 8to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
Immediately add sour cream and cottagecheese to pasta; mix together.
Add Worcestershire sauce, onion and garlic to mixture, if desired.
Pourmixture into a lightly greased two-quart baking dish and top with breadcrumbs.
Bake in preheated oven for 45 minutes.
