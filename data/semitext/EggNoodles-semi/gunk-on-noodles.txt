Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Place the egg noodles inthe pot, cook 6 to 8 minutes, until al dente, and drain.
In a large skillet over medium-high heat, cook the ground beef 10 minutes, oruntil evenly brown.
Mix in the onion and garlic, and cook until onion istender.
Mix in the tomato paste, mushrooms and juice, sugar, butter, andWorcestershire sauce.
Season with salt and pepper.
Continue to cook and stir 5minutes, until bubbly and heated through.
Serve over the cooked noodles.
