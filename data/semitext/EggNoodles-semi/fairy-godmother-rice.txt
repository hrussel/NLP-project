Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Grease a 2 quart casseroledish.
Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat.
Brown noodles in the butter.
In a large bowl combine browned noodles, rice, soup mix, broth, waterchestnuts and soy sauce.
Mix well and transfer to prepared casserole dish.
Bake for 45 minutes, or until liquid has been absorbed and casserole isbrowned and crispy on top.
