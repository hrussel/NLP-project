Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
In a 13x9 inch casserole dish, combine the mushroom and beef onion soups,canned mushrooms and water.
Mix thoroughly and add beef tips.
Turn to coatwell.
Bake in a preheated oven for 1 hour.
While beef tips are baking, bring a large pot of lightly salted water to aboil.
Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
Servebeef tips and sauce over noodles.
