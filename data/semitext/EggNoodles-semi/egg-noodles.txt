In a large bowl, stir together the flour and salt.
Add the beaten egg, milk,and butter.
Knead dough until smooth, about 5 minutes.
Let rest in a coveredbowl for 10 minutes.
On a floured surface, roll out to 1/8 or 1/4 inch thickness.
Cut into desiredlengths and shapes.
Allow to air dry before cooking.
To cook fresh pasta, in a large pot with boiling salted water cook until aldente.
