Preheat an oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Grease a 2-quart casserole.
Bring water to a full rolling boil in a pot.
.
Cook the egg noodles in theboiling water, stirring occasionally, for 3 minutes; remove from heat, cover,and let stand until the noodles are tender, about 10 minutes.
Drain.
Stir the noodles, onion, sour cream, chicken soup, ham, and Swiss cheesetogether in a large bowl.
Season with salt and pepper.
Spoon into the preparedcasserole.
Sprinkle the top with bread crumbs.
Bake in the preheated oven until the casserole is bubbling and the breadcrumbs have browned, about 40 minutes.
