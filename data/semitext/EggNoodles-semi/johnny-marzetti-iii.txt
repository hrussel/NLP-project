In a large pot with boiling salted water cook pasta until al dente.
Drain.
Meanwhile, in a large skillet cook ground beef until no pink remains.
Addonions and celery and cook until they are softened.
Add salt and pepper totaste.
Stir in tomato soup, tomato sauce, and water.
Simmer for 10 minutes.
In a casserole dish, mix together the cooked and drained pasta with the meatmixture.
Bake in a preheated 350 degree F (175 degree C) oven for 20 to 30 minutes.
Sprinkle the top with grated Cheddar cheese and continue to cook until cheeseis melted, about 3 to 5 minutes.
