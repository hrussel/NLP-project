In a large slow cooker, mix together the cream of celery soup, French onionsoup, and sour cream.
Stir in the meatballs.
Cook on high heat for 3-4 hours.
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Add pasta and cook for 8to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
In a large bowl, toss the pasta withbutter.
Serve meatballs and sauce over the cooked pasta.
