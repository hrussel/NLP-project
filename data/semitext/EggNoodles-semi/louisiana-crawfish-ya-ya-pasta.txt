Bring a large pot of lightly-salted water to a rolling boil; stir in the eggnoodles and return to a boil.
Cook the pasta uncovered, stirring occasionally,until the pasta has cooked through but is still firm to the bite, about 5minutes.
Drain well in a colander set in the sink.
Meanwhile, melt the butter in a large skillet over medium heat.
Stir in thechopped onion, green pepper, garlic, and parsley.
Cook and stir until theonion has softened and turned translucent, about 5 minutes.
Add the crawfishtails and Cajun seasoning; simmer 5 minutes longer.
Pour in the heavy cream, mushrooms, and 1/2 cup of Cheddar cheese; stir untilthe cheese has melted.
Toss the pasta with the crawfish sauce and greenonions; sprinkle with the remaining Cheddar cheese.
