Combine the turkey drumsticks and water in a large soup pot and bring to aboil.
Stir in onions, carrots, celery, garlic, salt, black pepper, sage,thyme, rosemary, and celery salt; reduce heat to a simmer.
Cook until theturkey meat is very tender, about 2 hours.
Remove the turkey drumsticks andallow to cool.
When cool enough to handle, strip the meat from the bones andtendons, chop the meat, and return to the soup.
Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil.
Cookthe egg noodles a boil until cooked through yet firm to the bite, about 5minutes.
Drain.
Scoop noodles into soup bowls and fill the bowls with turkeysoup.
