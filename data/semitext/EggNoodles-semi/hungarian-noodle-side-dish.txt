Cook egg noodles in a large pot with boiling salted water.
Drain well.
In a large bowl dissolve chicken bouillon cube in boiling water.
Mix in thecream of mushroom soup, chopped onion, Worcestershire sauce, poppy seeds,garlic powder, and hot pepper sauce.
Stir in cottage cheese, sour cream, andcooked egg noodles.
Transfer to lightly greased slow cooker and sprinkle the top with parmesancheese and paprika.
Cover and cook on high for 3 to 4 hours.
Serve immediately.
