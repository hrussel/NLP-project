Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Cook egg noodles in theboiling water, stirring occasionally, until cooked through but firm to thebite, about 5 minutes.
Drain and let noodles cool.
Beat eggs with cinnamon and vanilla extract in a large bowl; gently stir innoodles.
Mix pineapple, raisins, and apple into noodle mixture.
Transfermixture into a large casserole dish and scatter pieces of margarine over thenoodle mixture.
Spread apricot preserves over the casserole.
Bake in the preheated oven until noodles on top are golden brown and preservesare bubbling, about 15 minutes.
Cool slightly before serving.
