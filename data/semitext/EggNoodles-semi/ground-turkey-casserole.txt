Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large skillet over medium-high heat, saute the ground turkey for 5 to 10minutes, or until browned.
Drain the turkey, stir in the tomato sauce andsugar, and set aside.
In a medium bowl, combine the sour cream and creamcheese.
Mix well and set aside.
Cook noodles according to package directions.
Place them into a 9x13-inchbaking dish, then layer the turkey mixture over the noodles.
Then layer thesour cream mixture over the turkey, and top with cheese.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 20 to 35 minutes, or until cheese ismelted and bubbly.
