Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Add pasta and cook for 8to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
In a saucepan over medium heat, combine cheddar cheese, milk, butter, cottagecheese, and sour cream; stir until melted.
Pour cooked noodles into the cheese mixture and stir until well mixed.
Stir incanned tuna and green peas; heat thoroughly.
