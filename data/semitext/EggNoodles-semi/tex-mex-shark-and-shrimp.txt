Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Add egg noodles, cook for6 to 8 minutes, until al dente, and drain.
Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
Mix in the shark, shrimp,and tomatoes with green chiles.
Cover, and cook 15 minutes, or until shark iseasily flaked with a fork.
Serve the shark mixture over the cooked egg noodles.
Sprinkle with mozzarellacheese, and season with pepper.
