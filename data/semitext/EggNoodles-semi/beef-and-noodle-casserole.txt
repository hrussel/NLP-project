Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Cook the noodles according to package directions.
Brown the ground beef in a large skillet over medium high heat.
Stir in thetomato soup, Worcestershire sauce and garlic, bring to a boil, reduce heat tolow and let simmer.
When noodles are done, stir them and the cheese into the simmering sauce untilcheese is melted.
Stir the sherry into the sauce and stir for 1 minute, thenplace in a 2 quart casserole dish and sprinkle with the Parmesan cheese totaste.
Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes.
