Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Lightly grease a mediumcasserole dish.
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Add noodles, cook for 6to 8 minutes, until al dente, and drain.
Place turkey in a skillet over medium heat, and cook until evenly brown.
Draingrease.
In a bowl, gently mix cooked noodles and turkey, corn, and soup.
Season withsalt and pepper.
Transfer to the prepared baking dish.
Bake 20 minutes in the preheated oven, until bubbly.
