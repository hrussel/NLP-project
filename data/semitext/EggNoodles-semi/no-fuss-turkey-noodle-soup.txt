Combine chicken stock, summer savory, basil, thyme, chipotle powder, salt,black pepper.
parsley, and sherry in a stock pot; bring to a boil.

Melt butter in a skillet.
Cook and stir celery, onion, and garlic in hotbutter until onions are translucent, about 5 minutes; stir into stock mixture.
Add turkey, reduce heat to low, and cook at a simmer until turkey is hot andhas flavored the soup, about 30 minutes.
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Cook egg noodles in theboiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to thebite, about 5 minutes; drain and add to soup.
Season with salt and blackpepper to serve.
