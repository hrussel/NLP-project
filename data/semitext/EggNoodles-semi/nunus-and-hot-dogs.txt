Bring a large pot of lightly-salted water to a boil; add the pasta and cookuntil al dente, about 5 minutes; drain.
Melt 1 tablespoon butter in a skillet.
Cook the hot dog pieces in the butteruntil browned.
Add the hot dogs to the drained noodles with 2 tablespoonsbutter, parsley, and Parmesan cheese; stir until butter is melted and the hotdogs are coated.
