Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Lightly grease a 9x13 inchbaking dish.
Bring a large pot of water to a boil.
Add egg noodles, and cookfor 8 to 10 minutes, or until done; drain.
Layer noodles, mixed peas and carrots, onions, and ham in prepared bakingdish.
Mix together soup and milk, then pour mixture over casserole.
Seasonwith salt and pepper, then press with the back of a fork to help soup mixtureseep through casserole layers.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 30 minutes, or until bubbly and hot.
