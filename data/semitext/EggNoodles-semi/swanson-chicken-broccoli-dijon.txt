Spray a 10-inch skillet with the cooking spray and heat over medium-high heatfor 1 minute.
Add the chicken and cook for 10 minutes or until it's wellbrowned on both sides.
Add the stock, garlic and broccoli to the skillet and heat to a boil.
Reducethe heat to low.
Cover and cook for 5 minutes or until the chicken is cookedthrough.
Remove the chicken from the skillet and keep warm.
Stir the flour, milk, mustard and black pepper in a small bowl until smooth.
Gradually stir the flour mixture into the skillet.
Increase the heat tomedium-high.
Cook and stir until the mixture boils and thickens.
Serve withthe chicken and noodles.
