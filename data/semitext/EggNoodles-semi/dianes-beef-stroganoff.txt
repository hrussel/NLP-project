Heat olive oil and butter in a large, heavy pot or Dutch oven over mediumheat.
Combine flour, salt, and black pepper in a bowl and toss beef stew meatin seasoned flour.
Brown the beef in the hot oil and butter, stirring often,and remove meat from pan.
Pour white wine into pan and scrape up and dissolve any small bits of brownedfood; stir onion and garlic into white wine.
Cook until onion is translucent,stirring often, about 5 minutes.
Return browned beef to pan.
Stir ketchup,Worcestershire sauce, and juice from canned mushrooms with beef mixture.
Reduce heat to low, cover pan, and simmer until beef is tender, about 3 hours.
Mix button mushrooms and sour cream into beef mixture and heat through, about5 minutes.
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Cook egg noodles in theboiling water, stirring occasionally, until cooked through but firm to thebite, about 5 minutes.
Drain.
Serve Stroganoff over cooked egg noodles.
