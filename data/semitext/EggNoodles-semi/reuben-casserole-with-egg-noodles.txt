Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Cook egg noodles in theboiling water, stirring occasionally, until cooked through but firm to thebite, about 5 minutes; drain, reserving a few tablespoons of the water.
Transfer noodles and reserved water to a large bowl.
Mix salad dressing and mayonnaise into the noodles about a tablespoon each ata time, stirring to coat the noodles, until the mixture has a creamy textureto your liking.
Add corned beef, sauerkraut, Swiss cheese, and Cheddar cheeseto the noodles; stir.
Pour the mixture into a 13x9-inch baking dish.
Bake in preheated oven until the cheese melts completely and the casserole ishot in the center, 45 minutes to 1 hour.
