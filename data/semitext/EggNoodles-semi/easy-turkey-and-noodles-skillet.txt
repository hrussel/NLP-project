In 10-inch skillet, melt butter over medium-high heat.
Cook onion in butter 4to 6 minutes, stirring frequently, just until tender.
Stir in all remaining ingredients except turkey and potato chips.
Heat toboiling.
Reduce heat; cover and simmer 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Addturkey.
Cover and continue to cook 5 to 8 minutes, stirring occasionally,until noodles are tender.
