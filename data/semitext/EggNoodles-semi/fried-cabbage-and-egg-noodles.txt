Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Cook the egg noodles inthe boiling water until the pasta is tender yet firm to the bite, about 5minutes.
Drain.
While the noodles cook, melt the butter in a large skillet over low heat.
Addthe cabbage to the melted butter; season with salt and pepper.
Cover and cookuntil the cabbage begins to brown, 5 to 7 minutes.
Stir the drained noodlesinto the cabbage; cook and stir until the noodles begin to brown, about 5minutes.
