Poach chicken in a large pot of simmering water.
Cook until no longer pink incenter, about 12 minutes.
Remove from pot and set aside.
Bring chicken cookingwater to a boil and cook pasta in it.
Drain.
Cut chicken into small pieces,and mix with noodles.
In a separate bowl, mix together mushroom soup, chicken soup, and sour cream.
Season with salt and pepper.
Gently stir together cream soup mixture with thechicken mixture.
Place in a 2 quart baking dish.
Melt butter in a small saucepan, and remove from heat.
Stir in crumbledcrackers.
Top casserole with the buttery crackers.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for about 30 minutes, until heatedthrough and browned on top.
