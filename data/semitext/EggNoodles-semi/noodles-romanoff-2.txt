Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Add noodles and cook for8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
While noodles are cooking, combine the sour cream, 1/4 cup cheese, chives,salt and pepper in a medium bowl.
Mix together.
Stir butter into drained noodles, then stir in sour cream mixture.
Place on awarm platter and sprinkle with remaining 1/4 cup cheese.
