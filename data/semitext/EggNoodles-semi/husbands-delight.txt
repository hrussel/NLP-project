Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Add pasta and cook for 8to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
In a large skillet over medium heat, brown the ground beef.
Add the tomatoeswith juice, salt and sugar and simmer for 15 minutes.
In a separate medium bowl, combine the sour cream, cream cheese and onion andmix well.
In a 9x13 inch baking dish, place half of the egg noodles, then allof the meat mixture, then the cream mixture.
Top with the remaining noodlesand sprinkle with the cheese.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 25 minutes.
