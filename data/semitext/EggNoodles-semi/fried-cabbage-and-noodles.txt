Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil.
Stirin kluski noodles and return to a boil.
Cook kluski noodles uncovered,stirring occasionally, until tender but still slightly firm, 7 to 10 minutes.
Drain well and set noodles aside.
Place chopped bacon in a large, deep skillet, and cook over medium-high heat,stirring often, until evenly browned, 5 to 8 minutes.
Cook and stir cabbage and onion into the bacon and drippings until the cabbageis wilted and tender, about 10 minutes.
Gently mix the cooked kluski noodles into cabbage mixture; season with hotsauce.
