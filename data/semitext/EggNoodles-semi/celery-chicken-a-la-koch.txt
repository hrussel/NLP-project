Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large mixing bowl combine the noodles, chicken meat, soup, 2 cans of themilk and celery.
Mix well and spread mixture into the bottom of a 9x13 inchbaking dish.
Pour the remaining can of milk over all; do not mix in.
Then top all withpotato chip crumbs.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 50 minutes.
Serve hot.
