Beat eggs and sugar, add Ricotta, milk, and vanilla.
Mix thoroughly.
Cook macaroni, drain, put in large mixing bowl and melt butter over top.
Add egg/Ricotta mixture, stirring in thoroughly.
Pour into thoroughly buttered6x10 inch pan.
Bake for 1 hour at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C), let cool, then refrigeratebefore serving.
