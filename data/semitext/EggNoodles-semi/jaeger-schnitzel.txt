Pound out cubed pork, and cut in half.
Heat oil in a large skillet or Dutch oven over medium heat.
Dip pork in egg,then bread crumbs, and place in hot oil.
Cook, turning, until golden brown.
Remove to a warm plate.
Prepare gravy mix according to package directions.
Stir in mushrooms, and cookwith gravy.
Meanwhile, bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Add the eggnoodles, and cook until al dente, about 8 to 10 minutes; drain.
Serve pork over noodles and smother with gravy.
