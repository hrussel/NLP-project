Melt butter in a large pot over medium heat.
Add the stew meat, caraway seeds,onion and paprika.
Cook and stir until the meat is browned, and the onions aretranslucent.
Add the garlic and lemon zest, and cook for about a minute, thenstir in the tomato paste and beef consomme, adding water if necessary to coverthe meat.
Cover, and simmer over medium-low heat for 1 1/2 hours.
Just before the meat is done cooking, bring a large pot of salted water to aboil.
Add egg noodles, and cook until tender, about 8 minutes.
Drain and servethe goulash spooned over the egg noodles.
