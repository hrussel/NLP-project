Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Bring a large pot of water to a boil.
Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutesor until al dente; drain.
In a large bowl, combine noodles, sugar, raisins, pecans, salt and eggs.
Meltbutter in an 8x12 inch baking dish.
Tilt to coat dish, then pour remainingbutter into noodle mixture, stirring to coat evenly.
Spread mixture into dish.
Sprinkle with cinnamon.
Bake in preheated oven 55 minutes, until top is browned.
