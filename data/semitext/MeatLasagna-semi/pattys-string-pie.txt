Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Brown ground beef in a large skillet over medium heat until crumbly and nolonger pink, about 10 minutes.
Drain and set aside in a bowl.
Cook and stironion and green bell pepper in the same skillet until onion is translucent,about 5 minutes.
Stir onion, green pepper, and spaghetti sauce into groundbeef.
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Cook spaghetti in theboiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to thebite, about 10 minutes.
Drain and transfer to a bowl.
Let pasta cool slightlyand stir beaten eggs and Parmesan cheese into spaghetti.
Spread spaghetti mixture into a 9x13-inch baking dish.
Spread ricotta cheeseover pasta mixture in an even layer; top with ground beef mixture.
Sprinklemozzarella cheese evenly over the top of the casserole.
Bake in the preheated oven until the casserole is set in the center and thecheese topping is melted and lightly browned, about 35 minutes.
