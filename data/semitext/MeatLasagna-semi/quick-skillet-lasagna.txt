Cook pasta according to package directions; drain.
Meanwhile, cook Johnsonville® Mild Italian Ground Sausage in a 10-inchnonstick skillet until meat is brown; drain.
Set meat aside.
Wipe skillet withpaper towel.
Spread about half of the cooked pasta in the skillet.
Cover with about half ofthe Classico® Tomato and Basil pasta sauce.
Spoon cooked meat over Classico®Tomato and Basil pasta sauce.
Sprinkle with 1 cup of the mozzarella cheese.
Top with remaining pasta and sauce.
Sprinkle remaining mozzarella and Parmesancheese over top.
Cook, covered, over medium heat for 5 to 7 minutes or until heated through andcheese melts.
Remove skillet from heat and let stand, covered, for 1 minute.
