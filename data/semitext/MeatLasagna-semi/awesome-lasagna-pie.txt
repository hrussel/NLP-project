Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Crumble the ground beef into a large skillet over medium-high heat.
Cook andstir until no longer pink.
Drain and stir in the tomato paste, salt, andItalian seasoning.
Set aside a little bit of the mozzarella and Parmesancheeses for the top; stir the rest into the ground beef.
Set aside.
Spread cottage cheese into the bottom of a 9 inch pie plate.
Spoon the groundbeef mixture over the cheese.
In a small bowl, stir together the milk andeggs.
Blend in the biscuit mix until smooth.
Pour over the top of the pie,covering completely.
Bake in the preheated oven until top is browned, about 30 minutes.
Remove fromthe oven and sprinkle the reserved cheese over the top.
