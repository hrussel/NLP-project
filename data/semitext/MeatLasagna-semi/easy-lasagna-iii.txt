Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Add lasagna noodles andcook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
In a skillet, brown ground beef.
Using a fork, mix cottage cheese, eggs,garlic powder, parsley, salt and pepper.
In a separate bowl combine tomatopaste and tomato sauce.
In a 9x13 inch baking pan, layer noodles, ground beef, tomato sauce mixtureand cottage cheese mixture.
Two layers of each will fit.
Cover with tin foiland bake for 30 minutes.
Remove tin foil the last 8 minutes of baking time.
