Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
In a skillet over medium heat, brown ground beef with onion; drain grease.
Pour in spaghetti sauce and simmer 5 minutes.
In a 9x13 inch baking dish, spread enough sauce and beef to cover bottom andput a layer of ravioli down end to end.
Spread a layer of sauce and cheesejust like you would with regular lasagna.
Keep layering and finish with sauceon top.
Bake uncovered in a preheated oven for 1 hour.
Let sit for 5 minutes beforeserving.
