Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees) C.
Spray a 9x13 pan with cookingspray.
Brown beef in a skillet over medium heat and drain excess grease.
Add theolives, mushrooms, and onion.
Stir and cook until the onions are soft.
Stir inthe spaghetti sauce, pepper, oregano, and Italian seasoning.
In a small bowl combine the peas and corn.
To assemble, lay 3 noodles on bottom of prepared pan, then one half of thebeef mixture, half of the corn and pea mixture.
Repeat.
End the layers withthe remaining noodles.
Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
Sprinkle the top with cheese andbake for an additional 5 minutes.
Let stand 10 minutes before serving.
