In a large pot over medium heat, cook and stir the ground beef until evenlybrown; drain.
Mix the water, tomatoes, mushrooms, green bell pepper, onion, and garlic intothe pot with the beef.
Season with basil, salt, and pepper.
Bring to a boil,reduce heat, and simmer 45 minutes.
Mix the feta cheese into the soup, and continue cooking about 15 minutesbefore serving.
