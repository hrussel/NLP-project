Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Place ground beef in a large, deep skillet.
Cook over medium high heat untilevenly brown.
Drain, crumble and set aside.
In a medium saucepan over medium-high heat, combine spaghetti sauce mix,mushroom gravy mix, tomato paste, water and oil.
Bring to a boil and stir inground beef.
Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
In a 9x13 inch baking dish layer the noodles, sauce and cheese; repeat layers,ending with cheese.
Bake in preheated oven for 20 minutes.
