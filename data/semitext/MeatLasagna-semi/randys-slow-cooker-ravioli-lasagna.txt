Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.
Cook and stir beef, garlic, garlicpowder, salt, and pepper in the hot skillet until meat is browned and crumbly,5 to 7 minutes.
Drain and discard grease.
Stir pasta sauce, Italian seasoning,basil, and oregano into ground beef mixture.
Ladle a generous layer of meat sauce into the bottom of a slow cooker; add alayer of ravioli.
Ladle another layer of meat sauce over ravioli layer;alternate with remaining ravioli and meat sauce until all ingredients areused.
Cook on Low for 3 to 5 hours.
Sprinkle ravioli mixture with mozzarella cheeseand continue cooking until cheese is melted, 45 minutes to 1 hour more.
