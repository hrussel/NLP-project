Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large heavy skillet over medium heat, cook beef, onion and garlic untilbeef is evenly brown.
Drain excess fat.
Remove from heat, and stir in tomatoesand jalapenos.
Pour half of beef mixture in a 9x13 inch pan.
Top with a layerof tortilla chips, 1/2 of black olives, 1/2 of tomato and 1/2 of shreddedcheese.
Repeat layers.
Bake in preheated oven for 10 to 15 minutes, or until cheese is melted.
