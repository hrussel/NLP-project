Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Cook lasagna in theboiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to thebite, about 8 minutes.
Drain.
Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat; stir cream cheese into hot butteruntil it begins to melt, about 5 minutes.
Season with salt, onion powder, andblack pepper.
Whisk warm milk gradually into cream cheese until smooth; stirin 1 1/2 cup Swiss cheese.
Bring sauce to a simmer, stirring constantly, untilcheese has melted and sauce has thickened, 5 to 10 minutes.
Remove from heat.
Spread 3/4 cup of cheese sauce into bottom of a 9x9-inch baking dish.
Arrange1/3 of the lasagna noodles over the sauce and spread half the cooked chickenand half the ham over the noodles.
Pour 1 cup sauce over chicken and ham.
Repeat layers starting with half the remaining lasagna noodles, remainingchicken, remaining ham, and 1 more cup of cheese sauce.
Finish by layeringremaining lasagna noodles, remaining sauce, 1/2 cup Swiss cheese, and breadcrumbs; sprinkle top with Italian seasoning.
Cover dish with aluminum foil.
Bake in the preheated oven until casserole is bubbling, about 25 minutes.
Remove foil and continue to bake until crumbs are browned, about 10 moreminutes.
Let casserole stand 5 to 10 minutes before serving.
