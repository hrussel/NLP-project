Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
Cook ground beef and sausage in a large, deep skillet until well browned,about 5 minutes.
Drain fat.
Stir in pasta sauce.
Bring to a simmer.
While sauce is simmering, bring a large pot of salted water to a boil overhigh heat.
Stir in the egg noodles.
Boil until cooked through, but is stillfirm to the bite, about 5 minutes.
Drain well.
Spoon half of the meat sauce into the prepared baking dish.
Top with half ofthe cooked noodles, and 1 1/2 packages of cheese slices.
Repeat with remainingsauce, noodles, and cheese.
Place uncovered dish in preheated oven.
Bake, until cheese is melted andbrowned, about 40 minutes.
Let stand 5 minutes before serving.
