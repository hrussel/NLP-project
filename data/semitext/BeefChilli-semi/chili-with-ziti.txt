In a medium pot over medium heat, brown the ground beef, onion and garlicuntil the meat is no longer pink; drain fat.
Pour in tomato sauce, chili powder, oregano and cinnamon; mix thoroughly.
Pourin kidney beans and water; stir well.
Salt and pepper to taste.
Simmer on lowfor 30 minutes.
Bring a medium pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Pour in the ziti pastaand cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
Serve the chili over the ziti and top with cheddar cheese.
