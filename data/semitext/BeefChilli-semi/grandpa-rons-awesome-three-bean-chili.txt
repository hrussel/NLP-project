Heat a large stock pot over medium-high heat.
Cook and stir beef until brownedand crumbly, 10 to 15 minutes; drain and discard grease.
Mix beer, tomatosauce, diced tomatoes, pinto beans, red beans, kidney beans, 2 chopped onions,green bell pepper, red bell pepper, brown sugar, garlic, chili seasoning mix,cornstarch, and mustard into ground beef; bring to a boil.
Reduce heat, coverpot, and simmer, stirring frequently, until flavors have blended, about 1hour.
Top each serving with about 1 tablespoon Cheddar cheese, 1 1/2 teaspoonsonion, and hot sauce; season with salt and pepper.
