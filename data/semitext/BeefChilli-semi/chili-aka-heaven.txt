Heat a large soup pot over medium-high heat.
Cook and stir beef in the potuntil browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes; drain and discard grease.
Cook and stir onions, jalapeno peppers, habanero peppers, and garlic with theground beef until onions are slightly softened, about 5 minutes.
Add chilibeans, tomatoes, water, chili powder, and cumin; bring to a boil.
Reduce heatand simmer until flavors blend, about 1 hour.
