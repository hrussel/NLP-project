Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat and stir in the ground beef andground pork.
Cook and stir until the meat is crumbly, evenly browned, and nolonger pink.
Drain and discard any excess grease.
Set meat aside.
Melt thebutter in the pot over medium heat.
Stir in the garlic, bell pepper, andonion; cook and stir until the onion has softened and turned translucent,about 5 minutes.
Stir in the cooked meat, pinto beans, tomatoes, parsley,chili powder, pepper, monosodium glutamate, salt, and cumin.
Cover and bringto a boil, then reduce heat, and simmer for 1 hour.
