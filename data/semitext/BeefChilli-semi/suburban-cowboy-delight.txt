In a large skillet over medium heat, fry bacon pieces until browned.
Crumblethe ground beef into the skillet with the bacon, and stir in the green pepperand onion.
Continue to cook until the beef is no longer pink.
Drain off anyexcess grease.
Stir in the chili seasoning mix until the meat is coated.
Add the tomatoes and beans to the skillet, mix well, and simmer for 15 to 20minutes.
(I always put my biscuits in just then, and they finish together.
