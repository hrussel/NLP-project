Cook bacon and onion together in a large pot over medium heat until the onionis translucent, about 5 minutes.
Add ground beef, garlic and cumin to the pot;cook and stir until the beef is completely browned, 7 to 10 minutes.
Stir kidney beans, diced tomatoes, water, chili powder, liquid smoke, salt,black pepper, and hot pepper sauce into the beef mixture; bring to a simmerand cook until thick, about 1 hour.
Skim excess grease if desired.
