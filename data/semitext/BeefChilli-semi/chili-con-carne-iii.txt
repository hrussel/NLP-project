In a large skillet over medium heat, combine onion, garlic, and tomato paste.
Cover and cook for 5 minutes.
Stir in butter until melted.
Stir in flour, oregano, cumin, chili powder,tomatoes and beef.
Cook uncovered for 8 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Stir in the beans and cook for another 4 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Saltand pepper to taste.
