Heat the oil in a large stock pot over medium heat.
Crumble the beef and porkinto the pot.
Cook, stirring frequently, until no longer pink.
Drain off thegrease, leaving just a thin layer.
Cook the onion, garlic, bell pepper, andserrano pepper in the remaining oil until the onions soften, about 5 minutes.
Add the lime juice, chili powder, cayenne pepper, black pepper, salt, and 11/2 bottles of dark beer.
Slowly add the last 1/2 bottle of beer whilestirring.
Stir in the tomatoes and tomato juice.
Reduce heat to low.
Simmer 1hour.
