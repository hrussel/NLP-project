Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
In a large skillet over medium high heat, saute the ground beef, onion andbacon for 5 to 10 minutes, or until the meat is browned and the onion istranslucent; drain excess fat.
In a 3 quart casserole dish, combine the vinegar, mustard, ketchup, brownsugar, green bell pepper, kidney beans, chili beans, pork and beans and themeat mixture.
Stir together until well blended.
Bake at 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) for 45 to 50 minutes, remove from ovenand top with the cheese and crushed tortilla chips.
Return to oven and bakefor 5 to 6 more minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
