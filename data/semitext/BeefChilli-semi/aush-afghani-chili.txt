Brown ground beef in a skillet over medium heat; remove with slotted spoon toa large pot; reserving drippings in the skillet.
Cook and stir the onion inthe reserved drippings until golden brown; remove with slotted spoon and addto beef in the pot.
Stir the tomatoes with juice, garlic, red pepper, cumin,chili powder, mint, garam masala, garbanzo beans, and spinach into the beefmixture and place the pot over low heat; simmer 3 to 6 hours.
Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil overhigh heat.
Once the water is boiling, stir in the fettuccini, and return to aboil.
Cook the pasta uncovered, stirring occasionally, until the pasta hascooked through, but is still firm to the bite, about 8 minutes.
Drain well ina colander set in the sink.
Stir into the chili along with the sour cream andserve hot.
