Heat a large saucepan or Dutch oven over medium heat.
Add ground beef, andcook until evenly browned.
Stir occasionally to crumble.
Season the beef withchili powder, onion flakes, cumin, paprika, garlic and red pepper flakes, andmix well.
Pour in the tomatoes, chili beans, and tomato sauce.
Reduce heat to low, andsimmer for at least 30 minutes, or longer for thicker chili.
Season to tastewith salt and pepper.
