Place a large skillet over medium-high heat.
Cook the ground beef in the hotskillet until completely browned, 7 to 10 minutes; drain.
Combine the drained beef in a slow cooker with the bell peppers, onions,kidney beans, black beans, tomato sauce, and diced tomatoes.
Cook on High for30 minutes.
Stir the garlic, chili powder, paprika, oregano, rosemary,coriander, garlic powder, salt, cumin, cilantro, and bay leaves into the beefmixture.
Reduce heat to Low and cook another 6 1/2 hours.
