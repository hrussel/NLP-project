Place the pork sausage, beef, and onion into a large skillet over medium heat,and cook, stirring frequently, until the meat is browned, about 15 minutes.
Break the meat up into crumbles as it cooks.
Drain off excess fat, then placethe meat into a soup pot.
Mix in the diced tomatoes with green peppers, chilibeans, tomato sauce, chili powder, masa harina, cumin, salt, paprika, oregano,garlic powder, and cayenne pepper.
Bring the chili to a simmer, reduce heat,and simmer for 1 hour.
