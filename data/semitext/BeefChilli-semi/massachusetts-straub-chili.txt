Heat oil in a large stockpot over medium heat.
Saute onions and bell pepperuntil tender.
Add garlic, ground beef and stew meat and cook until the meat isno longer pink, about 10 minutes.
Add the tomatoes, beef broth and kidney beans to the stockpot, cover and bringto a boil.
Let the mixture boil for 4 to 5 minutes then stir in the chilipowder, Italian seasoning, salt and brown sugar.
Simmer uncovered for about 2hours, or until the chili thickens and beans are tender.
