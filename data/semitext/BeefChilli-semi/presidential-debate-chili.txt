Crumble beef into a large pot over medium-high heat; add onion and garlic.
Cook and stir beef until completely browned, 7 to 10 minutes.
Drain anddiscard excess grease.
Return pot to medium-high heat.
Stir diced tomatoes, beef broth, diced green chilies, chili powder, brownsugar, oregano, cumin, and salt through the beef mixture.
Add beans and bringto a boil.
Reduce heat to medium-low, place a cover on the pot, and simmer for 1 hour.
