Brown the ground beef in a large soup pot over medium-high heat until cookedand crumbly; drain and set aside.
Brown sausages, then set aside.
Reduce heatto medium and stir in bacon.
Cook until the bacon has released its fat and isbeginning to turn crispy.
Stir in garlic and onions, and cook until the onionssoften and turn translucent, about 5 minutes.
Stir in tomato paste to coat theonions.
Pour in beef broth, diced tomatoes, chili beans, and pinto beans.
Add theAnaheim peppers, jalapeno peppers, and serrano peppers.
Season withWorcestershire, brown sugar, chipotle powder, and cumin.
Bring to a boil overhigh heat, then reduce heat to medium-low, and simmer for 2 hours, or untilthickened, stirring occasionally.
Season to taste with salt and pepper, thensimmer for 5 additional minutes before serving.
