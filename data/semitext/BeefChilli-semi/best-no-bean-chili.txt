Pour coffee into a slow cooker and set slow cooker to High; add dicedtomatoes, beer, tomato paste, beef broth, brown sugar, chili sauce, jalapenopeppers, cumin, cocoa powder, chile powder, oregano, cayenne pepper,coriander, and salt.
Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.

Slowly pour cornmeal into the slow cooker while stirring to assure no lumps.
