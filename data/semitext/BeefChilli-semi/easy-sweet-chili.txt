Crumble ground beef into a skillet over medium heat.
Stir garlic, oregano,chili powder, and basil into the beef; cook and stir until beef is completelybrowned, 7 to 10 minutes.
Combine light red kidney beans, dark red kidney beans, diced tomatoes, andcorn in crock of a slow cooker.
Stir cooked ground beef into the bean mixture.
Cook on Medium-Low for 2 hours.
Stir sugar into the chili and continue cookingas long as you can wait, at least 6 hours.
Season with salt and black pepperto serve.
