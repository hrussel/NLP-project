In a large saucepan or stockpot, cook ground beef, onion and red pepper untilbeef is browned.
Drain off excess fat.
Stir in the chili powder, garlic, bay leaf, diced tomatoes, spaghetti sauce,salsa and taco sauce.
Lower heat and simmer for 1 1/2 hours, stirringoccasionally.
Stir in beans just before serving and heat through.
