Place the ground beef, onion and garlic in a large saucepan over medium heat.
Cook, stirring to crumble the beef, until the beef is no longer pink and theonion is tender.
Drain off the fat and return the pan to the stove.
Combinethe chili powder, cumin and basil; sprinkle over the beef.
Cook and stir tocoat the meat and toast the spices a little.
Pour in the tomatoes, green chilies, tomato sauce, beer and vinegar.
Bring toa boil and stir to loosen any bits that are stuck to the bottom of the pan.
Mix in the brown sugar, hot pepper sauce, salt and pepper.
Reduce the heat tolow, cover and simmer for 3 hours.
Remove the lid for the last 30 minutes ofcooking.
