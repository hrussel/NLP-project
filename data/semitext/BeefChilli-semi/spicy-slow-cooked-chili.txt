Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat and stir in ground beef.
Cook andstir until beef is crumbly, evenly browned, and no longer pink, about 7minutes.
Drain and discard any excess grease.
Combine ground beef, kidney beans, diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, onions, bellpepper, garlic, chili powder, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper in a slowcooker.
Cover and cook on Low for 10 hours, or High for 4 hours.
Garnish each serving with Cheddar cheese.
