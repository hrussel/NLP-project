Place the ground beef in a large pot over medium-high heat.
Cook, stirring tocrumble, until evenly browned.
Drain off grease.
Place the pot over mediumheat, and add the onion, garlic, green pepper, red pepper and kidney beans.
Cook and stir for a few minutes.
Stir in the tomato sauce and water, andseason with chili powder.
Simmer over low heat for 40 minutes uncovered.
