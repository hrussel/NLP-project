Cook sausage and ground beef in a large pot over medium-high heat untillightly browned and crumbly.
When the meat has released its grease, and hasbegun to brown, drain off accumulated grease, and season with chili powder,cumin, and coriander.
Cook and stir for 1 minute until fragrant, then stir inthe garlic and onion.
Cook until the onion has softened and turnedtranslucent, about 4 minutes.
Stir in the diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, kidney beans, chipotle peppers,salt, and pepper.
Bring to a simmer, then pour the chili into a slow cooker.
Cover, and cook on Low for 8 to 10 hours.
Stir in tomato paste an hour beforethe chili is done.
