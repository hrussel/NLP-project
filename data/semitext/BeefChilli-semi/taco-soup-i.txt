In a large saucepan over medium heat, cook ground beef and onion until meat isevenly brown; drain excess fat.
Mix in water, tomatoes, kidney beans, tomatosauce, and taco seasoning mix.
Cover, and simmer for 15 minutes.
Remove fromheat, and stir in the avocado.
Ladle hot soup into serving bowls.
Pass cheese, tortilla chips, and sour creamto top each serving.
