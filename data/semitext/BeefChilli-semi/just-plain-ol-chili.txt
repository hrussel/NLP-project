Heat a large skillet over medium heat and add ground beef; cook and stir untilmeat is crumbly, evenly browned, and no longer pink, about 10 minutes.
Drainand discard any excess grease.
Heat olive oil in a soup pot over medium-high heat.
Cook and stir green pepperin the hot oil until it starts to soften, 5 to 7 minutes.
Reduce heat to low,stir in garlic, cumin, and chili powder, and season with salt and black pepperto taste.
Mix cooked ground beef and tomatoes into green bell pepper mixture, breakingup tomatoes with a spatula.
Add 4 cups of water or enough to cover theingredients, reduce heat to low, and simmer at least 2 hours to blend flavors.
