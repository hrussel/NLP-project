In a 4 quart saucepan over medium heat, cook ground beef, green pepper, onion,chili powder and garlic, until beef is browned and vegetables are tender.
Drain fat off of beef/vegetable mixture.
Add undrained kidney beans, tomato soup, vinegar and cinnamon to soup, andbring to a boil.
Simmer for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Heat through.
Serve with sprinkled cheese on top.
