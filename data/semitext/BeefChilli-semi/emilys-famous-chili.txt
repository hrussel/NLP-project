In a large pot over medium-high heat, cook beef until brown.
Drain and returnto pot.
Stir in onions, bell peppers and jalapenos and cook until tender.
Stirin garlic and cook 1 minute more.
Season with chili powder, cumin, salt andpepper.
Stir in the tomato paste and kidney beans with their liquid.
In a food processor or blender, puree the tomatoes with the chipotle peppersuntil smooth.
Stir into the pot with 3 cups of water.
Combine the remaining 1 cup water with the flour in a jar and shake tocombine.
Pour into the chili and stir in the vinegar.
Simmer about 45 minutesbefore serving.
