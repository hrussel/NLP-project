Place ground beef in a large, deep skillet.
Cook over medium-high heat untilevenly brown.
Drain excess fat, and crumble.
Stir in onion, green pepper,tomato sauce, tomato paste and chili beans.
Season with chili powder, hotsauce, seasoning salt, cayenne pepper and garlic powder.
Cook over low heat, stirring occasionally, until desired consistency (at least1 hour for best flavor).
