In a large skillet over medium heat, cook beef until brown.
Stir in onion,celery and bell pepper and cook until vegetables are tender.
Combine beef mixture with tomatoes, kidney beans and salsa in a slow cooker.
Season with sugar, cayenne, chili powder, basil and oregano.
Cook 8 hours onlow.
Then refrigerate 8 hours or overnight before reheating and serving.
