Brown ground beef in skillet with onions.
Drain fat.
Place the whole peppercorns, bay leaves, small red peppers, and crushed redpepper in a spice bag.
In a large pot combine the garlic, tomato paste, water, chili powder, groundred pepper, vinegar, cinnamon, allspice, Worcestershire sauce and salt withthe ground beef, onions and spice bag.
Cook over low heat for 3 to 4 hours.
When ready to serve, don't forget to remove spice bag and whole garlic cloves.
