Crumble the ground beef into a stock pot or large Dutch oven over medium-highheat.
Add the onion and garlic, and cook stirring frequently until beef isevenly browned.
Drain off excess grease.
Season with chili powder, oregano, cumin and hot sauce.
Stir in the tomatoesand vinegar.
Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, and simmer for about 1hour - or longer if you have time.
Stir occasionally to prevent burning on thebottom.
