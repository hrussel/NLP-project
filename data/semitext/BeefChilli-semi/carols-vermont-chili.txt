Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
Stir in the garlic, onion,red bell pepper, and jalapeno peppers.
Cook and stir until the onion istender, about 7 minutes.
Add the tomato sauce, crushed tomatoes, maple syrup,and kidney beans.
Season with black pepper and chili powder.
Bring to a boil,then reduce heat to a simmer.
Meanwhile, heat a large skillet over medium-high heat and stir in the groundbeef.
Sprinkle beef with salt.
Cook and stir until the beef is crumbly, evenlybrowned, and no longer pink.
Drain and discard any excess grease; stir intothe chili along with 1 beer.
Cook and stir for 1 hour, stirring frequently.
Season to taste with salt, and thin to desired consistency with 1 beer beforeserving.
