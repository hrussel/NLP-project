Sprinkle steak pieces evenly with salt and pepper.
Heat oil in 6-quart saucepan over medium-high heat 1 minute.
Add steak pieces,onion, garlic and chili powder.
Cook 5 minutes, or until steak is browned onall sides and onion is tender, stirring frequently.
Add beans, both diced tomatoes with their juice, tomato paste, water, sugarand red pepper; blend well.
Bring to a boil.
Reduce heat to low; simmer,uncovered, 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Serve with optional toppings, ifdesired.
