In a large pot over medium high heat, combine the oil and steak and saute forabout 5 minutes, or until steak is browned.
Add the onions, garlic and chilepepper and saute for another 5 minutes, or until the onions are translucent.
Add the chili powder, cumin, cayenne pepper, tomatoes and kidney beans.
Reduceheat to low, cover and simmer for about one hour.
Remove cover and simmer foranother 30 minutes, or until you've reached your desired thickness.
