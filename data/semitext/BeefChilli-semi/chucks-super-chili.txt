Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat and stir in Italian sausage andground sirloin.
Cook and stir until meat is crumbly, evenly browned, and nolonger pink.
Drain and discard any excess grease.
Transfer meat to a large Dutch oven; place over medium-high heat.
Stir inchopped onion; add red, yellow, and green bell peppers, garlic, and jalapenopeppers.
Cook and stir until onion is translucent, about 5 minutes.
Mix in chili powder, brown sugar, cumin, tomato paste, oregano, salt, blackpepper, and bay leaves.
Cook and stir until fragrant, about 3 minutes more.
Stirring constantly, pour in wine, chopped tomatoes and their juice, kidneybeans, tomato sauce, and chopped chipotle chiles in sauce.
Bring to a boil.
Cover, reduce heat to low, and simmer, stirring occasionally, for 1 hour.
Uncover and simmer, stirring occasionally, until thickened, about 30 minutes.
Discard bay leaves before serving.
