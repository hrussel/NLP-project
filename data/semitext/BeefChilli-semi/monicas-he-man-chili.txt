In a skillet over medium-high heat, cook the bacon until evenly brown andcrisp.
Reserve juices in skillet, and set bacon aside.
Brown the sausage inthe bacon juices.
Set aside.
Cook the ground beef in the skillet until evenlybrown.
Set aside.
Saute the onion and green pepper in the skillet juices untiltender.
In a large pot over medium heat, mix the bacon, sausage, beef, onion, greenpepper, and jalapeno pepper.
Pour in the wine and tomatoes, and season withchili powder.
Cook 20 minutes, stirring often.
Mix in the beans, and continueto cook and stir 25 minutes.
