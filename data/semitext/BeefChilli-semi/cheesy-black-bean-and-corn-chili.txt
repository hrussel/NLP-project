Place ground beef into a large skillet and cook, stirring often, until thebeef is browned and crumbled, about 10 minutes.
Stir in green onions after 5minutes.
Drain excess grease.
Place ground beef mixture into a large soup pot over medium heat.
Stir in black beans, corn and its liquid, chili seasoning mix, crushedtomatoes, and water; stir to combine.
If chili seems too thick, mix in alittle more water.
Cover the chili and cook 1 hour; add processed cheese cubes, allow to melt,and stir into the chili until smooth just before serving.
