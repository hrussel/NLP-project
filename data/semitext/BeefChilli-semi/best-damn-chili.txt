Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat; cook and stir onion, bell pepper,Anaheim pepper, jalapeno peppers, and garlic in the hot oil until softened.
Meanwhile, heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.
Cook and stir beef inthe hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes; add Worcestershiresauce and garlic powder.
Crumble bouillon cubes over beef and add beer.
Continue to cook , scraping any browned bits from the bottom of the skillet,until liquid is hot, about 3 minutes.
Stir beef mixture into pepper mixture.
Stir crushed tomatoes, diced tomatoes, tomato paste, and wine to the beefmixture.
Season with chili powder, 2 tablespoons cumin, brown sugar, peppersauce, basil, paprika, salt, oregano, and black pepper.
Bring to a boil andreduce heat to medium-low.
Cover and simmer until meat and vegetables are verytender and flavors have developed in the chili, about 90 minutes, stirringoccasionally.
Mix kidney beans into beef and vegetables.
Continue to simmer until beans arehot, about 30 minutes more.
Blend sour cream, cilantro, and remaining 1/2 teaspoon cumin in a foodprocessor until smooth.
Serve sour cream mixture with chili.
