Heat vegetable oil over medium heat in a large pot.
Add ground beef, onion,bell pepper, and garlic.
Cook until ground beef is browned and vegetables arestarting to become tender.
If desired, drain off fat.
Stir black beans, red kidney beans, diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomatojuice, chili powder, cumin, basil, oregano, salt, and black pepper into pot.
Bring to a boil.
Reduce the heat to low.
Cover and simmer, stirringoccasionally, for 30 minutes.
Serve with sour cream and cheese.
