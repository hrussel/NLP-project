Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat; cook and stir onion and garlicin hot oil until tender, about 5 minutes.
Stir ground beef and chorizo sausageinto onion mixture; cook, breaking meat apart while stirring, until no longerpink, 10 to 15 minutes.
Mix ground beef-chorizo mixture, diced tomatoes, chili, butter beans, tomatosauce, chili powder, chipotle seasoning blend, salt, and black pepper in aslow cooker.
Cook on High until flavors have blended, 3 to 4 hours.
