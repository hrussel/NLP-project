Brown beef and onion in a large skillet over medium high heat.
Place brownedmixture in a large pot and stir in the tomato sauce, garlic, Worcestershiresauce, cinnamon, vinegar, chili powder, cayenne pepper, salt and pepper.
Simmer, uncovered, over low heat for 1 1/2 hours.
When meat mixture has about 20 minutes cooking time left, bring a large pot oflightly salted water to a boil.
Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes oruntil al dente; drain and set aside.
Serve meat mixture over cooked spaghetti topped with cheese, beans, olives andchopped onion.
