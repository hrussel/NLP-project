In a large pot, cook the ground beef over medium heat until evenly browned.
Drain off grease, and set aside.
Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat.
Saute the onions, green pepper andhabanero pepper until onions are translucent.
Remove from heat.
Transfer theonion mixture to the pot with the ground beef, and set the heat to medium.
Add the kidney beans and tomato sauce to the beef mixture, and season withchili powder, salt, garlic salt and hot pepper sauce.
Bring to a simmer, andadjust seasonings to taste if necessary.
Cover, reduce heat to low, and simmerfor 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
