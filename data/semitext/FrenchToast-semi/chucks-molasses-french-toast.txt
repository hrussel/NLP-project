Beat together the eggs, molasses, cream, vanilla, ginger, and cinnamon in abowl until smooth.
Dip the challah slices in the batter until coated on allsides.
Melt half of the butter in a large skillet over medium heat.
Place half of thechallah slices into the pan, and cook until golden brown on each side, about 3minutes per side.
Repeat with remaining butter and challah slices.
