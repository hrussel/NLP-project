Beat the eggs, pumpkin puree, cinnamon, and nutmeg together in a bowl.
Dip thebread slices into the pumpkin mixture until coated on both sides.
Melt 1 teaspoon of butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
Place 4 ofthe bread slices into the skillet and cook until browned on both sides, about3 minutes per side.
Repeat with the remaining bread and butter.
