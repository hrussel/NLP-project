Combine light corn syrup, butter and sugar in saucepan; simmer until syrup-like.
Pour mixture over the bottom of a 9"x 13" pan.
Slice bread into 12-16 slices; place over the syrup.
Layer as needed.
Beat together the eggs, milk, vanilla, and salt.
Pour over bread.
Cover withsaran wrap.
Refrigerate overnight.
Bake in a preheated oven 350 degree F (175 degrees C) for 45 minutes.
Cut into squares.
Invert and serve.
