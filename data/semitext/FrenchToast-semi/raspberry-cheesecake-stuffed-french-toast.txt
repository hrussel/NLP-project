In a bowl, whisk milk, vanilla, sugar, and cinnamon into the beaten eggs untilwell blended.
Set aside.
In a separate bowl, cream together raspberry pureeand cream cheese until smooth.
Make 'sandwiches' by cutting each slice ofbread in half and spreading raspberry-cheese mixture in the center, then topwith the other half.
Melt butter over medium heat in a large skillet or griddle.
Dip bread into eggmixture, coating thoroughly.
Cook until well-browned on both sides, about 5minutes.
Dust with confectioners' sugar and nutmeg.
Serve immediately.
