In a small bowl, mix together the cream cheese, sugar, and 1/8 teaspoon ofcinnamon until thoroughly combined.
Spread cream cheese mixture onto 4 slicesof raisin bread.
Top each spread slice with another slice of raisin bread tomake 4 sandwiches.
In a shallow bowl, beat the eggs with milk and 1/8 teaspoon of cinnamon.
Dipeach sandwich into the egg mixture on both sides.
Grease a skillet with butter, and place over medium heat.
Brown the sandwichesin the skillet for about 2 minutes per side.
