Mix eggs, milk, salt, and nutmeg together with a fork, until the whole mixtureis pale yellow.
Pour this into a low, flat pan or a large, shallow dish.
If you are using English muffins, cut them in half so that you have sixhalves.
Let the halves or the slices of bread soak on each side in the eggmixture.
Spread a layer of granulated sugar on a plate, and dip both sides ofthe bread in sugar.
Heat a griddle or skillet over medium heat.
Put about half a tablespoon ofbutter in the pan, and let it melt.
Place two muffin halves or slices of breadin the pan.
Cook for 2 to 3 minutes on each side.
Wipe old butter out of pan,and repeat with remaining slices of bread.
