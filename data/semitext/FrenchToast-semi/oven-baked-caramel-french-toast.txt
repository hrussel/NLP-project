In a small saucepan, combine 1 cup brown sugar, 1/2 cup butter and 2tablespoons corn syrup.
Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, untilthickened.
Pour sauce into a 9x13 inch baking dish and sprinkle with 1/2 cup pecans.
Place 6 slices of bread on top of the sauce, sprinkle with the remainingpecans and cover with the remaining 6 slices of bread.
Combine eggs, milk, vanilla, nutmeg, cinnamon and salt in a blender.
Pour eggmixture evenly over bread slices, cover the baking dish, and refrigerate for 8hours or overnight.
The next morning, preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Remove theFrench toast from the refrigerator 30 minutes prior to baking.
Bake inpreheated oven for 40 to 45 minutes, until lightly brown.
Drizzle sauce overthe toast right before serving.
