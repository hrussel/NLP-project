Spread the French bread cubes and cream cheese cubes evenly in a 9x13-inchbaking dish.
Whisk eggs, milk, maple syrup, and cinnamon together in a bowl; pour mixtureover bread and cream cheese.
Cover and refrigerate overnight.
Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Bake in preheated oven until golden brown, about 45 minutes.
