In a saucepan, stir together the brown sugar, butter and water.
Bring to aboil, then reduce heat to low, and simmer for 10 minutes, stirring frequently.
Pour the brown sugar mixture into a 9x13 inch baking dish, and tilt the dishto cover the entire bottom.
Place peaches in a layer over the sugar coating,then top with slices of French bread.
In a medium bowl, whisk together theeggs and vanilla.
Slowly pour over the bread slices to coat evenly.
Sprinklecinnamon over the top.
Cover and refrigerate for 8 hours or overnight.
Remove the dish from the refrigerator about 30 minutes before baking to cometo room temperature.
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Bake for 25 to 30 minutes in the preheated oven, or until the bread is goldenbrown.
Spoon out portions to serve.
