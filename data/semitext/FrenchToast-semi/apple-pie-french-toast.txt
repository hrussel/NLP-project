Place apples in a bowl and drizzle with lemon juice to keep from browning.
Melt 1/2 cup butter in a large saucepan over medium heat; remove from heat.
Stir flour into melted butter until smooth.
Return saucepan to low heat; stirin brown sugar, white sugar, and water until sugars dissolve and mixture issmooth, 3 to 5 minutes.
Increase heat to medium; cook and stir mixture untilboiling.
Reduce heat to low and add apples.
Simmer mixture until apples arecooked but slightly tender, 20 to 25 minutes.
Preheat griddle to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
Whisk eggs, milk, cinnamon, and vanilla extract together in a bowl.
Dip breadslices into egg mixture until completely coated.
Grease heated griddle with frozen butter.
Cook dipped bread slices on the hotbuttered griddle until browned, 2 to 3 minutes per side.
Repeat with remainingfrozen butter and dipped bread.
Serve French toast with apple mixture ladledover top.
