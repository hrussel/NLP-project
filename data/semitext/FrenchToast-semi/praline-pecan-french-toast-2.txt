Mix half-and-half, eggs, vanilla, and 1 tablespoon dark brown sugar togetherin a large shallow bowl; add French bread.
Cover bowl with plastic wrap andrefrigerate 8 hours to overnight.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Place butter in a 9x13-inch casserole dish.
Place casserole dish in thepreheating oven to melt butter, about 5 minutes.
Swirl butter over bottom ofthe dish.
Spread brown sugar, maple syrup, and pecans over the melted butter.
Arrangesoaked bread atop pecan mixture.
Bake in the preheated oven until French toast is cooked through, 30 to 35minutes.
Sprinkle with confectioners' sugar.
