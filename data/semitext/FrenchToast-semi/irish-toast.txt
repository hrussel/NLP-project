Cut the bread into 12 slices.
In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, Irishwhiskey, Irish cream liqueur, and vanilla extract until well blended.
Heat some of the butter in a skillet over medium heat until the butter is hotand the foam has disappeared.
Press each bread slice into the egg mixture,then fry in the hot skillet until nicely browned on both sides, about 2minutes per side.
Add more butter to skillet as needed.
Brush each slice withbutter, and sprinkle with confectioners' sugar.
