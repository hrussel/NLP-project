Place potatoes into a large pot and cover with salted water; bring to a boil.
Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until tender, about 10 minutes.
Drain andset aside to cool.
Mix potatoes, mayonnaise, peas, hard-boiled eggs, bacon, onion, mustard, salt,and black pepper together in a bowl.
Refrigerate to allow flavors to blend andbacon to soften, about 1 hour.
