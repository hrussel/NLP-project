Place the potatoes into a large pot and cover with salted water.
Bring to aboil over high heat, then reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer untiltender, about 20 minutes.
Drain and allow to steam dry for a minute or two.
Finely chop the hard-cooked eggs and the pickles.
When they're cool enough to handle, cube the potatoes and transfer them to a9x13-inch dish.
Stir in the chopped eggs, pickles, mayonnaise, mustard, andthe peas and carrots and mix gently to combine.
Season to taste with salt andpepper.
Serve immediately, or refrigerate the salad before serving.
