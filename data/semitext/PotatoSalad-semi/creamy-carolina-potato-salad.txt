Place potatoes into a large pot and cover with salted water; bring to a boil.
Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until tender, 20 to 25 minutes.
Drain,cool, and chop potatoes into 1-inch cubes.
Combine chopped potatoes and eggsin a large bowl.
Mix sour cream, mayonnaise, pimento peppers, green onions, bacon, mustard,sugar, vinegar, salt, black pepper, celery seed, and garlic powder together ina bowl.
Pour dressing over potatoes and eggs; toss gently to coat.
Sprinklepotato salad with paprika.
