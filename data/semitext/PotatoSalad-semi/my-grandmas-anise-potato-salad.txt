Place potatoes in a saucepan with enough water to cover.
Bring to a boil overmedium heat, and cook until tender, about 8 to 10 minutes.
Remove from heat,drain, and run under cold water to cool.
Drain well.
In a large bowl, mix together the potatoes, celery, onion, mayonnaise, andanise seed.
Season with salt and pepper to taste.
Refrigerate until cold.
Garnish with sliced hard-cooked eggs, and dust with paprika, if desired.
