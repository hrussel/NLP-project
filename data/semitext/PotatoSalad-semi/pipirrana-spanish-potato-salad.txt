Place egg in a saucepan, and cover with cold water.
Bring water to a boil, andimmediately remove from heat.
Cover, and let eggs stand in hot water for 10 to12 minutes.
Remove from hot water, cool, and peel.
Cut eggs into quarters, andset aside.
Meanwhile, bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
Add potatoes, and cookuntil tender but still firm, about 15 minutes.
Drain, and transfer to a largebowl.
Toss potatoes with eggs, bell peppers, onion, tomato, tuna, green olives,olive oil, and vinegar.
Season to taste with salt.
Refrigerate, and serve cold
