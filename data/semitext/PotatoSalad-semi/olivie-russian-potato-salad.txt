Place potatoes and carrots into a large pot and cover with salted water; bringto a boil.
Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until tender, about 15minutes.
Drain and rinse with cold water until cold.
Dice potatoes andcarrots.
Combine potatoes, carrots, sweet peas, pickles, onion, bologna, and eggstogether in a large bowl.
Stir mayonnaise, sour cream, salt, and black peppergently into potato mixture until well combined.
Cover bowl with plastic wrapand refrigerate until cold, at least 30 minutes.
