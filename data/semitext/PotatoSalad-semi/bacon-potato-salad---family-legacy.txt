Place potatoes into a large pot and cover with salted water; bring to a boil.
Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until slightly tender, 15 to 20 minutes.
Drain and cool.
Remove skin from potatoes; quarter and slice.
Place bacon in a large skillet and cook over medium-high heat, turningoccasionally, until evenly browned, about 10 minutes.
Drain on paper towels.
Combine potatoes, bacon, onion, celery, and eggs together in a bowl.
Whiskmayonnaise, milk, sour cream, vinegar, celery salt, salt, and black peppertogether in a separate bowl.
Fold mayonnaise mixture into potato mixture untilincorporated.
Refrigerate 8 hours or overnight to allow flavors to blend.
