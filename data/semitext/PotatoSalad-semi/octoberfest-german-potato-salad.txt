Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
Add peeled and cut potatoes; cookuntil tender but still firm, about 15 minutes.
Drain, and transfer to a largebowl.
Add onions.
In a large bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, oil, vinegar, sugar, parsley,salt and pepper.
Gently stir in the potatoes and onion.
Let stand for 1 hourbefore serving to enhance flavors.
