Grease or butter one 2 quart gelatin mold pan.
In a small saucepan, bring 1 cup of water to a boil; add gelatin and mix untildissolved.
Remove from heat and let cool to room temperature.
Stir inmayonnaise, cottage cheese, pineapples, walnuts and marshmallows.
Pour into molded pan and chill until firm.
Remove onto serving dish andgarnish with halved cherries.
