Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
Add potatoes and cook untiltender but still firm, about 15 minutes.
Drain and allow to cool.
Slice cooled potatoes and layer in a large bowl or dish.
In a saucepan combine water, vinegar and sugar.
Bring to a boil and cook forone minute.
Remove from heat and pour over potatoes.
Cover and chill for atleast 12 hours.
Drain excess marinade and add onions, salt, pepper, celery seed andmayonnaise.
Mix well and serve chilled.
