Place potatoes into a large pot and cover with salted water; bring to a boil.
Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until tender, about 20 minutes; drain andrinse with cold water to cool.
Remove and discard potato skins.
Slicepotatoes.
Whisk vinegar, sugar, salt, and pepper together in a large bowl; add potatoes,onion, and chopped gherkin.
Stir salad dressing into the mixture.
Fold 3/4 theegg wedges into the mixture.
Garnish with the remaining egg wedges andparsley.
Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate 1 hour before serving.
