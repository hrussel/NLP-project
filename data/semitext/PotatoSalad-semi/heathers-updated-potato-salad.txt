Place potatoes into a large pot and cover with salted water; bring to a boil.
Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until tender, about 20 minutes.
Drain andlet cool.
Mix mayonnaise, buttermilk, Dijon mustard, dill, red onion, and celery in alarge salad bowl; gently fold in the potatoes.
Season with salt and blackpepper.
Chill for 4 hours before serving.
