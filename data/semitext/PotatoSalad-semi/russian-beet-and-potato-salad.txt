Bring a large pot of water to a boil, and cook beets until tender, about 30minutes.
Bring a separate pot of water to a boil and cook potatoes and carrotsuntil tender, about 20 minutes.
Drain vegetables, cool, and remove skins.
Diceand place in a large bowl.
Place the diced pickles in the bowl with beets, potatoes, and carrots.
Drizzlethe olive oil and vinegar over the mixture and toss to coat.
Season with salt.
Sprinkle with green onions.
Chill completely before serving.
