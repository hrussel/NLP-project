Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Place potatoes into a pot and fill with enough water to cover.
Bring to a boiland cook until easily pierced with a fork, about 12 minutes.
Drain and pourinto a 9x13 inch baking dish.
Mix with mayonnaise, processed cheese and onion.
While the potatoes are boiling, fry the bacon in a large skillet over mediumheat until crisp.
Drain and break into large pieces.
Place on top of thepotatoes.
Bake for 1 hour in the preheated oven, until cheese is browned.
