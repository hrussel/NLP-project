Toss potatoes in oil with herbs, a generous sprinkling of salt and a fewgrinds of pepper.
Place potatoes, cut side down, in a single layer on a lippedcookie sheet.
Set pan on lowest rack of cold oven; heat oven to 450 degrees.
Roast until cut side is golden brown and potatoes are tender, 20 to 25minutes.
Transfer potatoes to a large bowl; add olives, onion and parsley.
Whisk vinegar, mayonnaise, garlic, a big pinch of salt and a couple of grindsof pepper in a 1-cup glass measuring cup.
Slowly whisk in oil, first indroplets, then in a slow, steady stream.
Pour dressing over warm salad; tossto coat.
Serve at room temperature for best flavor.
