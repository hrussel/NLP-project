Stir salt into water in a large pot and add potatoes; bring to a boil.
Reduceheat to medium-low and simmer until tender, about 20 minutes.
Drain and cool.
Slice potatoes and place in a large bowl.
Cook and stir yellow onion, bacon, celery, and pepper rings in a skillet untilbacon is browned and celery begins to soften, about 10 minutes.
Mix mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, horseradish, pepper juice, lemon juice, andCreole seasoning together in a bowl until well blended.
Add bacon mixture and dressing to potatoes; stir to combine.
Refrigerate for30 minutes before serving.
