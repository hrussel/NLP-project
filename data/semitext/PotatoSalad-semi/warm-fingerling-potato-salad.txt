Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil and add potatoes.
Boiluntil potatoes are tender, 10 to 13 minutes; drain.
Let cool until potatoesare warm.
Whisk yogurt, sour cream, lemon zest, lemon juice, mustard, salt, and blackpepper together in a bowl.
Gently fold potatoes into dressing.
Stir arugulaleaves into potato mixture and serve.
