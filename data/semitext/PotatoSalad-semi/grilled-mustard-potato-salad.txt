Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat.
Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil, and cook potatoes 10 to 15minutes, until tender but firm.
Drain, and transfer to a medium bowl.
In a small bowl, mix canola oil, vinegar, Dijon mustard, celery salt, andpepper.
Toss 1/2 the mixture with the potatoes.
Arrange potatoes in a single layer on a sheet of foil, and place on theprepared grill.
Turning occasionally, cook 7 to 9 minutes, until lightlybrowned.
Remove from heat, and cool.
Toss with remaining oil mixture to serve.
