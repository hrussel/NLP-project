In a large saucepan, cover potatoes with cold salted water and bring to aboil.
Cook until tender, about 10 minutes, adding beans in the last 4 or 5minutes of cooking.
When vegetables are tender, drain.
Place in a large bowl;toss with 2 tbsp olive oil.
Add eggs.
Combine remaining 3 tbsp olive oil, vinegar, mustard, garlic, salt and pepper.
Drizzle vinaigrette over vegetables and eggs; toss gently to combine.
Adjustseasonings, if necessary.
Serve warm.
