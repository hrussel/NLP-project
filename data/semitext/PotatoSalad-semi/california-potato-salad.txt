Place the potatoes into a large pot and cover with salted water.
Bring to aboil; reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until tender, about 15minutes.
Drain and allow to steam dry for a minute or two.
Allow to cool.
Place the eggs into a saucepan in a single layer and fill with water to coverthe eggs by 1 inch.
Cover the saucepan and bring the water to a boil.
Once thewater is boiling, remove from the heat and let the eggs stand in the hot waterfor 15 minutes.
Pour out the hot water and cool the eggs under cold runningwater in the sink.
Peel once cold.
Dice the eggs.
In a salad bowl, lightly toss the potatoes, eggs, artichoke hearts, garlic,Parmesan cheese, croutons, Dijon mustard, rice vinegar, and mayonnaise untilall ingredients are coated with dressing.
