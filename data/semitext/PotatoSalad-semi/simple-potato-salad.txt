Bring a small pot of salted water to a boil.
Add potatoes; cook until tenderbut still firm, about 15 minutes.
Drain and cool.
Place eggs in a saucepan, and cover completely with cold water.
Bring water toa boil.
Cover, remove from heat, and let eggs stand in hot water for 10 to 12minutes.
Remove from hot water, and cool.
Peel the eggs and cut around the egg white; keep the yolk whole.
Dice the egg whites and potatoes.
Combine in a mixing bowl and add the saladdressing.
Toss the potatoes and egg; crumble the egg yolk on top and serve chilled.
