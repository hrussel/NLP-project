Place the potatoes into a pot, and fill with enough water to cover.
Bring to aboil, and cook for about 10 minutes, or until easily pierced with a fork.
Drain, and set aside to cool.
In a large bowl, mix together the sour cream, mayonnaise, sugar, half of theonions, and half of the cheese.
Gently stir in the cooled potatoes.
Top withremaining cheese and onions, and sprinkle bacon bits over the top.
