Place the potatoes into a large pot and cover with salted water.
Bring to aboil over high heat, then reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer untiltender, 8 to 10 minutes.
Drain and allow to steam dry for a minute or two.
Place potatoes in a large bowl.
Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat; grease with cooking spray.
Cookand stir the green beans and onion until tender, about 5 minutes.
Stir in thegarlic; cook and stir until garlic is fragrant, about 1 minute more.
Transfer the green bean mixture into the large bowl with the potatoes.
Add thebalsamic vinaigrette, roasted red peppers, and basil; toss lightly.
Stir inthe goat cheese.
