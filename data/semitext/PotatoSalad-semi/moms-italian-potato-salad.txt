Place potatoes in a large saucepan, cover with water, and bring to a boil overhigh heat.
Reduce the heat to medium-low, and simmer until the potatoes aretender, about 15 minutes.
Drain and cool, then cut into 1-inch cubes.
Combine the potatoes, cucumber, celery, onion, and olives in a large bowl.
Whisk together the olive oil, red wine vinegar, and garlic powder in a smallbowl.
Pour the dressing over the potatoes and vegetables; mix well.
Seasonwith salt and pepper, then chill.
Stir the potato salad again before serving.
