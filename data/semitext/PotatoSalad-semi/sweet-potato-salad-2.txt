Place potatoes into a large saucepan, and fill with enough water to cover.
Bring to a boil, and cook for about 8 minutes, or until tender.
Drain, andcool slightly.
In a large bowl, stir together the mayonnaise and brown sugar.
Stir in thesweet potatoes, walnuts and raisins until evenly coated.
Chill for at least 1hour before serving.
