Place potatoes into a large pot and cover with water; season water with saltand bring to a boil.
Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until potatoes aretender, about 20 minutes; drain.
Put potatoes in a slow cooker.
Place bacon in a large skillet and cook over medium-high heat, turningoccasionally, until evenly browned, about 10 minutes.
Remove the bacon slicesto a plate lined with paper towels.
Crumble bacon and add to potatoes.
Return skillet to medium heat and reheat bacon drippings.
Cook and stir sweetonions in the drippings until translucent, 5 to 7 minutes; add garlic andcontinue cooking until fragrant, 1 to 2 minutes more.
Pour onion mixture overthe potatoes and bacon.
Stir vinegar, water, sugar, and salt together in a bowl; pour over the potatomixture.
Stir potatoes gently to mix.
Cook on Low, stirring occasionally, 3 to 4 hours.
