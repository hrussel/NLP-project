Place the potatoes in a large pot, and fill with about 1 inch of water.
Bringto a boil, and cook for about 15 minutes, or until potatoes are tender.
Throwin the green beans to steam after the first 10 minutes.
Drain, cool, and cutpotatoes into quarters.
Transfer to a large bowl, and toss with fresh basil,red onion, salt and pepper.
Set aside.
In a medium bowl, whisk together the balsamic vinegar, mustard, lemon juice,garlic, Worcestershire sauce and olive oil.
Pour over the salad, and stir tocoat.
Taste and season with additional salt and pepper if needed.
