Place potatoes into a large pot and cover with salted water; bring to a boil.
Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until tender, about 20 minutes.
Drain and cut into cubes.
Combine mayonnaise, Parmesan cheese, Dijon mustard, vinegar, cayenne pepper,salt, black pepper, and paprika in a large bowl.
Add potatoes, red onion, andgreen onions; gently toss to combine.
