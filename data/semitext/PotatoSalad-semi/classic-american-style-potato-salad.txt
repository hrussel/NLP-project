Place potatoes in a pot with water to cover.
Bring to a boil, cover andsimmer, stirring to ensure even cooking, until a thin-bladed paring knife or ametal skewer inserted into a potato can be removed with no resistance, 25 to30 minutes.
Drain, rinse under cold water and drain again.
Cool slightly.
Cut warm potatoes into 3/4-inch dice with a serrated knife.
Layer them in abowl, seasoning with vinegar, salt and pepper as you go.
Cut eggs, celery andpickle in 1/4-inch dice and thinly slice scallions.
Add to potatoes, alongwith parsley.
Stir in mayonnaise and mustard until everything is combined.
Chill, covered, before serving.
