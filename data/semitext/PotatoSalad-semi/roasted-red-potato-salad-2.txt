Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Spread potato pieces onto a baking sheet and drizzle with olive oil.
Roast in the preheated oven until tender, about 45 minutes; remove from ovenand let cool at least 15 minutes.
Place roasted potatoes into a large salad bowl and combine with hard-cookedeggs, celery, green onion, and bacon; stir in mayonnaise and season to tastewith salt and black pepper.
Cover and refrigerate for 1 hour to let flavorsblend.
