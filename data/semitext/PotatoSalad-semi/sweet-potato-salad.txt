Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
Add potatoes; cook until tenderbut still firm, about 15 minutes.
Drain, cool and slice.
In a large bowl, combine the vinegar, olive oil, garlic, dill pickle relishand onion.
Mix and cover with sliced potatoes.
Whisk together mayonnaise, pepper, sour cream and parsley.
Pour over potatoesand chill at least 8 hours.
