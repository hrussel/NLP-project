Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
Butter or grease one 8x16 inchbaking dish.
Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
Add potatoes and cook untiltender but still firm, about 15 minutes.
Drain and cool.
Place bacon in a large, deep skillet.
Cook over medium high heat until evenlybrown.
Add onions, mushrooms, salt and pepper.
Cook until onions and mushroomsare soft.
Slice potatoes and place a thin layer in the baking dish.
Follow with a layerof the bacon mixture and repeat layers until all ingredients are used.
Pourcream and milk over layers; sprinkle with grated cheese.
Bake for approximately 35 minutes, or until cream and milk have almost reducedcompletely.
