Place the potatoes into a large pot and cover with lightly salted water.
Bringto a boil over high heat, then reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmeruntil just tender, about 15 minutes.
Drain the potatoes, and allow to cool toroom temperature.
Dice once cooled.
Place the bacon in a large, deep skillet, and cook over medium-high heat,turning occasionally, until evenly browned, about 10 minutes.
Drain the baconslices on a paper towel-lined plate.
Allow to cool, and crumble the bacon intoa large bowl.
Place the cooled potatoes into the bowl with the bacon, and mix in the sourcream, mayonnaise, Cheddar cheese, chives, salt, and pepper.
Refrigerateovernight before serving.
