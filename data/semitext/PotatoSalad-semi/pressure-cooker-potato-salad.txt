Place potatoes in pressure cooker with water.
Cook on high pressure 3 minutes.
If potatoes are larger, cook for 4 minutes.
Let steam release for 3 minutes.
Then quickly release pressure and open cooker.
Peel and dice potatoes whenthey are cool enough to handle.
Alternate layers of potatoes, onion, and celery in a large bowl.
Season eachlayer with salt and pepper.
Top with the chopped egg and sprinkle with dill.
Mix together the mayonnaise, mustard, and cider vinegar in a small bowl.
Gently fold the mayonnaise mixture into the potatoes.
Chill at least one hourbefore serving.
