Place the potatoes into a large pot and cover with salted water.
Bring to aboil over high heat, then reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer untiltender, about 20 minutes.
Drain and allow to steam dry for a minute or two.
Let the potatoes cool enough to handle, and peel; allow the potatoes to coolthoroughly.
Cut into bite-size pieces.
In a bowl, combine the potatoes, onion, cheese curds, mayonnaise, yellowmustard, and seasoned salt; mix thoroughly.
Top the potato salad with hard-boiled egg slices and sprinkle with black pepper.
Refrigerate at least 2 hoursbefore serving.
