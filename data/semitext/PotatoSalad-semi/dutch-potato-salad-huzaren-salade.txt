Combine 3/4 cup mayonnaise, ketchup, warm water, and vinegar in a large bowland mix well.
Stir in chopped gherkins, cocktail onions, diced apple, andshredded carrot.
Add corned beef, mashed potatoes, and salt and pepper totaste; mix well.
Form the potato salad on a plate as a loaf or a round mound.
Garnish withslices of cucumber, halved cherry tomatoes, and slices of egg.
Decorate withswirls or wavy lines of mayonnaise to add a little flair (see Cook's Note).
Chill for at least 2 hours to allow the flavors to blend.
