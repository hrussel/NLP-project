Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
Add potatoes; cook until tenderbut still firm, about 15 minutes.
Drain, and transfer to a large bowl.
Coolslightly.
Peel and slice into a large bowl.
In a small bowl, combine oil, vinegar, consomme, green onion, parsley, andsalt and pepper.
Gently toss warm potatoes with dressing.
Cover, and refrigerate several hoursor overnight.
