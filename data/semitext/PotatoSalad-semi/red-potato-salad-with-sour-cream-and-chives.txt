Scrub potatoes (don't peel).
If large, cut in half or in quarters.
Boilpotatoes in their skins until fork-tender.
Drain, dry and cut into 1/2 inchcubes.
In a salad bowl, combine the potatoes, sour cream, yogurt, and chives; tossgently to coat.
Add salt, and pepper to taste; refrigerate until chilled.
