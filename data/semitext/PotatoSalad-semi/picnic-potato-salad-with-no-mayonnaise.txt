Place potatoes into a large pot and cover with lightly salted water; bring toa boil.
Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until tender, 10 to 15 minutes;drain.
Whisk vinegar, olive oil, mustard, basil, salt, and pepper together in a largebowl; add the potatoes and onion.
Toss gently to coat.
Let stand until cool,about 30 minutes.
Fold blue cheese and chives into potato salad until blended.
