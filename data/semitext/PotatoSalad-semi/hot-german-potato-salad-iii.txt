Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
Add potatoes and cook untiltender but still firm, about 30 minutes.
Drain, cool and slice thin.
Place bacon in a large, deep skillet.
Cook over medium high heat until evenlybrown.
Drain, crumble and set aside, reserving drippings.
Saute onions in bacon drippings until they are golden-brown.
In a small bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, salt, celery seed, andpepper.
Add to the sauteed onions and cook and stir until bubbly, then removefrom heat.
Stir in water and vinegar, then return to the stove and bring to aboil, stirring constantly.
Boil and stir for one minute.
Carefully stir baconand sliced potatoes into the vinegar/water mixture, stirring gently untilpotatoes are heated through.
