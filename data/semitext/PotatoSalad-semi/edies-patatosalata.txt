In a small bowl, combine olive oil, wine vinegar, oregano, parsley, and onion.
Mix and set aside.
Add potatoes to a large pot of boiling, lightly salted water.
Boil for about20 minutes, or until potatoes are tender.
Drain water and let cool.
When cooled, peel and dice.
Place in a large bowl, add dressing and seasonwith salt and pepper to taste.
Toss to coat, cover bowl and refrigerate forseveral hours or overnight.
