Place potatoes into a large pot and cover with salted water; bring to a boil.
Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until tender, about 20 minutes.
Drain andcube potatoes.
Transfer potatoes to a large bowl; stir in mayonnaise, sour cream, vinegar,sugar, onion, and celery seed until well coated.
Gently mix in eggs and seasonwith salt and black pepper.
Refrigerate until chilled.
