Place potatoes into a large pot and cover with salted water; bring to a boil.
Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until tender, 10 to 15 minutes.
Drain andcool.
Cut potatoes in half.
Heat a skillet over medium heat; cook and stir onions until softened andgolden brown, about 20 minutes.
Add tarragon; pour in white wine to deglazepan.
Simmer until wine evaporates, 2 to 3 minutes.
Place bacon in a large skillet and cook over medium-high heat, turningoccasionally, until evenly browned, about 10 minutes.
Drain the bacon sliceson paper towels.
Mix potatoes, onion, and bacon together in a large bowl.
Fold tzatziki sauce,chives, salt, and black pepper into potato mixture.
