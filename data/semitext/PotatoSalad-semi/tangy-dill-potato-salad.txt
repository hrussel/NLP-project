Bring a large pot of water to a boil.
Add potatoes, and cook for about 10minutes, or until tender.
Drain, and set aside to cool.
Meanwhile, in a large bowl, stir together the salad dressing, mayonnaise,green onions, dill, mustard, lemon juice, and pepper.
When the potatoes arecooled, stir into the bowl until coated.
Refrigerate for a couple of hours toblend flavors before serving.
