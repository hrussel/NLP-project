Place potatoes into a large pot and cover with salted water.
Bring to a boil,then reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until tender enough topierce with a fork, about 30 minutes.
Drain and allow cool, then peel anddice.
Place the diced potatoes into a large mixing bowl and refrigerate untilcold.
Once cold, fold in the mayonnaise, celery, eggs, chicken, mustard,olives, salt, and pickles.
Refrigerate 2 hours before serving.
