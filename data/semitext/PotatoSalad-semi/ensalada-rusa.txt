Place the beets into a saucepan, cover with water, and bring to a boil.
Reduceheat to medium, and boil gently until tender, about 20 minutes.
Remove fromthe boiling water and let cool.
Peel and dice the beets, and chill.
Place the potatoes into a separate saucepan, add water to cover, and stir insalt.
Boil the potato cubes until tender but not mushy, about 15 minutes.
Drain the potatoes in a colander set in the sink, and chill.
Bring a saucepan of water to a boil, reduce heat to medium, and stir in 1 to 2more teaspoons of salt.
Very gently lower the eggs into the boiling water,making sure they don't hit the bottom of the pan and crack; simmer for 15minutes.
Remove the eggs to a bowl filled with ice and ice water, and let theeggs chill until thoroughly cold.
Peel and dice the eggs.
Mix the chilled beets, potato cubes, and eggs together in a salad bowl, andgently stir in mayonnaise to taste.
