Place the beets, potatoes, and carrot into a saucepan, fill with water, andbring to a boil.
Reduce heat and simmer until tender, about 20 minutes.
Removevegetables and allow to cool; peel the beets and potatoes.
Dice the beets,potatoes, and carrot, and place into a salad bowl.
Stir in onion, pickle,sauerkraut, olive oil, and salt.
Chill the salad for 2 to 3 hours; serve cold.
