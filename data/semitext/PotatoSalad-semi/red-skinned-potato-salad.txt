Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
Add potatoes and cook untiltender but still firm, about 15 minutes.
Drain and set in the refrigerator tocool.
Place eggs in a saucepan and cover with cold water.
Bring water to a boil andimmediately remove from heat.
Cover and let eggs stand in hot water for 10 to12 minutes.
Remove from hot water, cool, peel and chop.
Place bacon in a large, deep skillet.
Cook over medium high heat until evenlybrown.
Drain, crumble and set aside.
Chop the cooled potatoes, leaving skin on.
Add to a large bowl, along with theeggs, bacon, onion and celery.
Add mayonnaise, salt and pepper to taste.
Chillfor an hour before serving.
