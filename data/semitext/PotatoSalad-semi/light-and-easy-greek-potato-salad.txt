Place red potatoes into a large pot and cover with salted water; bring to aboil.
Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until tender, about 25 minutes.
Drain and chill in freezer until cold, about 30 minutes.
Slice potatoes and place into a large salad bowl; toss potatoes with greenonion.
Whisk olive oil, red wine vinegar, lemon juice, garlic powder, onionpowder, salt, black pepper, oregano, sugar, rosemary, and red pepper in abowl.
Pour the dressing over the potatoes and toss.
Serve immediately or chilluntil serving time.
