Place the potatoes in a saucepan of lightly salted water, bring to a boil, andcook until tender, about 20 minutes.
Drain the potatoes in a colander, and letcool.
Place the bacon in a large, deep skillet, and cook over medium-high heat,turning occasionally, until evenly browned and crisp, about 10 minutes.
Drainthe bacon slices on a paper towel-lined plate.
Snip the cooked bacon intopieces with a scissors, or chop.
In a large bowl, mix the mini potatoes, chopped bacon, hard-boiled eggs,broccoli, Cheddar cheese, ranch salad dressing, dill weed, salt, and pepperuntil thoroughly combined, and serve.
