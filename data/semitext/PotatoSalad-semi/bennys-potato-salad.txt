Place potatoes in a large pot with enough water to cover.
Bring to a boil, andcook for about 5 minutes, until tender but not mushy.
Drain in a colander, andrun under cold water to cool.
Set aside.
In a large serving bowl, Stir together the eggs, green olives, dill and sweetpickle relishes, green onion, mayonnaise, yellow and brown mustards, and winevinegar.
Season with garlic powder, black pepper, white pepper, salt, celeryseed, dill seed, and dill.
Mix well, and stir in potatoes until coated.
Chillfor at least 2 hours to allow the flavors to blend.
