Place potatoes into a large pot and cover with salted water; bring to a boil.
Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until barely tender, about 10 minutes.
Drain.
Rinse potatoes with cold water to cool completely; drain.
Stir mayonnaise, pickles, eggs, red onion, celery, mustard, vinegar, salt, andpepper together in a large bowl.
Fold potatoes into the mayonnaise mixture.
