Wash and peel the potatoes and cut into 1/2 to 3/4 inch pieces.
Bring a largepot of salted water to a boil.
Add the potatoes and cook until tender butstill firm, about 10 minutes.
Slice the bacon into small pieces and cook over medium high heat in a large,deep skillet until evenly brown.
Do not overcook.
Chop the celery, green onions, stuffed olives and radishes into small piecesand put into a large bowl.
Add the potatoes and bacon and mix together.
Addthe mayonnaise and lemon juice to taste, stir, and place in the refrigeratorfor a few hours to chill before serving.
You may want to add a few sliced hardboiled eggs on top.
ENJOY!!!
