Bring a large pot of water to a boil; add potatoes and carrot.
Return mixtureto a boil and add eggs; cook until potatoes are tender, 20 to 30 minutes.
Drain and slightly cool mixture.
Chop potatoes and carrot; peel and chop eggs.
Mix potatoes, carrot, eggs, pickles, peas, ham, and parsley together in alarge bowl; stir in mayonnaise until salad is evenly coated.
