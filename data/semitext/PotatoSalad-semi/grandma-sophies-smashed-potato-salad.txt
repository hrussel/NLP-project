Boil potatoes until soft, about 25 to 30 minutes.
Remove potatoes to a largebowl.
While the potatoes are still warm but not steaming, stir in the hard-cookedeggs with a fork.
Mix in the dill pickle, then stir in the mayonnaise.
Seasonto taste with salt.
Continue to stir until potatoes are smashed and not manybig chunks remain.
