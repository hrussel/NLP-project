Place red potatoes into a large pot and cover with salted water; bring to aboil.
Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until tender and the peels begin tocrack, about 20 minutes.
Drain and let potatoes cool.
Peel cooled potatoes andcut into 1/8-inch dice.
Place potatoes, shrimp, onions, hard-cooked eggs, sweet pickles, andmayonnaise into a large salad bowl and stir to combine.
Season with salt andblack pepper; sprinkle top of salad with paprika.
Chill salad at least 3 hoursbefore serving.
