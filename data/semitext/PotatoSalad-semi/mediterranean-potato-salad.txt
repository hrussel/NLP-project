Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
Add potatoes and cook untiltender but still firm, about 15 minutes.
Drain, cool and chop.
In a large bowl, combine the potatoes, green peppers, cucumbers, red onion andcheese.
Whisk together the lemon juice, salad dressing, salt and pepper.
Pour oversalad and toss to coat.
Serve with pita bread wedges if desired.
