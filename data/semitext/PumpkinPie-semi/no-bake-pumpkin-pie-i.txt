In a heavy saucepan combine gelatin, cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg, and salt.
Stirin condensed milk and beaten eggs, mixing well.
Let stand one minute, thenplace on burner over low heat, stirring constantly for about 10 minutes, oruntil gelatin dissolves and mixture thickens.
Remove from heat.
Stir in pumpkin, mixing thoroughly, and pour mixture into graham crackercrust.
Chill for at least 3 hours before serving.
