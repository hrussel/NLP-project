Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
In a mixing bowl, combine the pumpkin, condensed milk, egg, 3/4 teaspooncinnamon, ginger, nutmeg and salt.
Pour batter into the pie crust.
Bake at 425 degrees F (220 degrees C) for 15 minutes.
Reduce oven temperatureto 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a small bowl, combine brown sugar, flour, and remaining 1/2 teaspooncinnamon.
Cut in the butter or margarine until the mixture is crumbly.
Stir inwalnuts.
Sprinkle mixture evenly over the pie.
Bake pie for 40 minutes oruntil a knife inserted one inch from the edge comes out clean.
Cool andgarnish as desired.
