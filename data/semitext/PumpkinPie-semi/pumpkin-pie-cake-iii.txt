Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Grease a 9x13-inch cake pan.
Whisk the pumpkin, evaporated milk, brown sugar, eggs, and pumpkin pie spacetogether in a bowl.
Pour into the prepared pan.
Scatter the cake mix over thetop of the pumpkin mixture; sprinkle the nuts over the cake mix.
Drizzle themelted butter over the entire cake.
Bake in the preheated oven until set in the center, about 50 minutes.
