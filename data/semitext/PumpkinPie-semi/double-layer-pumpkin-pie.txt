In a large bowl, whisk together cream cheese, 1 tablespoon of milk, and sugaruntil smooth.
Gently stir in whipped topping.
Spread into bottom of crust.
Pour 1 cup of milk into large bowl, and thoroughly mix in pudding mix,pumpkin, cinnamon, ginger, and cloves.
When thickened, spread over creamcheese layer.
Refrigerate 4 hours, or until set.
