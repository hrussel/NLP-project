Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a medium bowl whisk together egg, sugar substitute, and pumpkin pie spiceuntil well blended.
Add pumpkin and milk to egg mixture, and stir untilsmooth.
Pour mixture into pie shell.
Place pie on a baking sheet and bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, oruntil set in center.
