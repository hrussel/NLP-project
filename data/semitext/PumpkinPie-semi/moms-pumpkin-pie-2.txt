Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
In a bowl, mix the eggs, evaporated milk and pumpkin.
In a separate bowl, mixthe sugar, flour, salt, ginger, cinnamon, allspice and cloves.
Stir into thepumpkin mixture.
Pour into the pie crust.
Bake in the preheated oven 10 minutes.
Reduce heat to 350 degrees F (175degrees C) and continue baking 45 minutes, until a knife inserted in thecenter of the pie comes out clean.
Cool on a metal rack.
