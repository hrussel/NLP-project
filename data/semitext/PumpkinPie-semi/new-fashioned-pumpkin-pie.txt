Heat oven to 425 degrees F.
Place pie crust in 9-inch glass pie pan asdirected on box for One-Crust Filled Pie.
In large bowl, beat eggs with wire whisk.
Stir in remaining ingredients untilwell blended.
Pour into crust-lined pan.
Bake 15 minutes.
Reduce oven temperature to 350 degrees F; bake 40 to 50minutes longer or until knife inserted near center comes out clean.
Coolcompletely, about 2 hours.
Store in refrigerator.
