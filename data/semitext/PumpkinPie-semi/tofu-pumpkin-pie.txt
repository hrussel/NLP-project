Preheat an oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
Place the tofu, pumpkin, sugar, salt, cinnamon, ginger, and clove into ablender.
Puree until smooth.
Pour into the pie crust.
Bake in the preheated oven 15 minutes, then reduce heat to 350 degrees F (175degrees C), and continue baking until a knife inserted into the mixture comesout clean, about 40 minutes more.
Cool before serving.
