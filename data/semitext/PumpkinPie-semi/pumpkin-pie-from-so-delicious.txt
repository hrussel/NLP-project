Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
Place pie crust in an 8-inch pie pan.
Combinepumpkin, coconut milk, sugar, cornstarch, cinnamon, ginger, salt, and allspicein a powerful blender or food processor until smooth.
Pour mixture into pieshell and bake for 60-70 minutes.
Remove from the oven and place on a coolingrack until completely cooled.
Cover in plastic wrap and refrigerate for atleast four hours (overnight is better) before serving.
Garnish with veganwhipped topping and powdered sugar.
