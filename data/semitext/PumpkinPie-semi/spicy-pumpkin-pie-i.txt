Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (220 degrees C).
In a large bowl, mix together butter or margarine, pumpkin, cinnamon, ginger,cloves, and salt.
In a separate bowl, beat eggs until foamy.
Mix flour, brownsugar, white sugar, and milk into eggs.
Blend egg mixture into pumpkinmixture.
Pour half of mixture into each pastry lined pan.
Bake in preheated oven for 15 minutes.
Reduce heat to 375 degrees F (190degrees C) and bake an additional 45 minutes, until toothpick inserted incenter comes out clean.
