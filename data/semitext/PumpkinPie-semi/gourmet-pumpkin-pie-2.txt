Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
When making your favorite pastry for pie shells add 1/4 cup of the finelychopped pecans to the flour before mixing.
Blend the egg, pumpkin and condensed milk.
Pour mixture into the unbaked pieshell.
Combine the brown sugar, flour, chopped pecans, butter and cinnamon with afork until crumbly.
Sprinkle streusel mixture on top of pumpkin mixture.
Bake at 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) for 50 to 55 minutes or until a knifeinserted in near the center comes out clean.
Let pie cool before serving.
