Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Mix brown sugar, ground ginger, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, allspice, and clovestogether in a large bowl; add pumpkin and eggs.
Beat the pumpkin mixture untilsmooth.
Stir evaporated milk into the pumpkin mixture; pour into the piecrust.
Bake in preheated oven until the pie is set in the middle, 35 to 40 minutes.
Cool to room temperature before serving.
