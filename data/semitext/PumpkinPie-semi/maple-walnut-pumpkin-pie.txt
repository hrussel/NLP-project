Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
In large mixing bowl, combine pumpkin, sweetened condensed milk, eggs,cinnamon, maple flavoring, ginger, nutmeg and salt; mix well.
Pour into pieshell.
Bake at 425 degrees F for 15 minutes.
Reduce oven to 350 degrees F; continuebaking 30 minutes.
In medium mixing bowl, combine brown sugar, flour and cinnamon; cut in butteruntil crumbly.
Stir in nuts.
Remove pie from oven; top evenly with crumb mixture.
Return to oven 10minutes.
Cool.
Garnish as desired.
Store covered in refrigerator.
