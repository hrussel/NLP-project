Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C.
)In a large bowl, combine eggs, egg yolk, white sugar and brown sugar.
Addsalt, cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger and cloves.
Gradually stir in milk and cream.
Stir in pumpkin.
Pour filling into pie shell.
Bake for ten minutes in preheated oven.
Reduce heat to 350 degrees F (175degrees C), and bake for an additional 40 to 45 minutes, or until filling isset.
