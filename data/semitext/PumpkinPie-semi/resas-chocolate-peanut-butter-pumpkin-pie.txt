Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
Whisk sugar, cinnamon, salt, ginger, and cloves together in a small bowl.
Beateggs together in a large bowl; stir in pumpkin and sugar mixture until justcombined.
Slowly pour evaporated milk into pumpkin mixture and stir untilcombined.
Arrange peanut butter cups, up-side down, into the graham cracker crust.
Pourpumpkin mixture over peanut butter cups in pie crust.
Bake in the preheated oven for 15 minutes.
Reduce temperature to 350 degrees F(175 degrees C) and continue baking until a knife inserted in the center ofthe pie comes out clean, 40 to 50 minutes.
Cool completely on a wire rack,about 2 hours.
Serve immediately or refrigerate.
