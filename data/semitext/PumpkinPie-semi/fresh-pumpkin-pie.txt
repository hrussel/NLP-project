Cut pumpkin in half, and remove seeds.
Lightly oil the cut surface.
Place cutside down on a jelly roll pan lined with foil and lightly oiled.
Bake at 325degrees F (165 degrees C) until the flesh is tender when poked with a fork.
Cool until just warm.
Scrape the pumpkin flesh from the peel.
Either mash, orpuree in small batches in a blender.
In large bowl, blend together 2 cups pumpkin puree, spices, and salt.
Beat ineggs, honey, milk, and cream.
Pour filling into pie shell.
Bake at 400 degrees F ( 205 degrees C) for 50 to 55 minutes, or until a knifeinserted 1 inch from edge of pie comes out clean.
Cool on a wire rack.
