Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
Fit the pie crust into a 9-inchpie dish.
In a bowl, whisk the egg substitute, sucralose sweetener, sweetener with brownsugar, and pumpkin pie spice until well combined.
Whisk in the pumpkin pureeand evaporated milk until smooth.
Pour the filling into the pie shell.
Bake in the preheated oven until the pie filling is set in the center, about40 minutes.
