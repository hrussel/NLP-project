Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Combine graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter in a mixing bowl untilevenly moistened; press into the bottom and sides of a 9-inch pie pan.
Bake in the preheated oven until the crust is lightly browned and smellstoasted, about 6 minutes.
Remove from the oven and allow to cool.
Spread 1/4 cup caramel topping onto the cooled crust.
Sprinkle 1/2 cup pecanpieces over the caramel topping.
Whisk milk, pumpkin, pudding mix, cinnamon, and nutmeg together in a bowluntil well combined.
Stir 1 1/2 cups whipped topping into pumpkin mixture.
Spread pumpkin mixture into crust.
Spread remaining whipped topping over thetop of the pie.
Refrigerate pie until chilled, at least 1 hour.
Sprinkle 2tablespoons pecans atop the pie and drizzle with 2 tablespoons caramel using afork.
