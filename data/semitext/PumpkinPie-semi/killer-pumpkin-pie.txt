Preheat an oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
Stir together the flour, sugar, and salt in a 9 inch pie pan, and make a wellin the center.
Pour the oil and rice milk into the well, then mix with a forkuntil a dough forms.
Use your hands to press the mixture evenly into thebottom and sides of the pan.
Crimp the edge of the crust.
Stir together the white sugar, brown sugar, cinnamon, salt, ginger, nutmeg,and cloves in a large bowl; set aside.
Whisk together the pumpkin puree, oil,eggs, vanilla, and rice milk in a separate bowl until evenly blended.
Add thepumpkin mixture to the dry ingredients and stir until fully blended.
Pour intothe prepared crust and place on a cookie sheet in the preheated oven.
Bake for 10 minutes.
Reduce temperature to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) andbake for 40 to 50 minutes or until a knife inserted near the center comes outclean.
The center may still wiggle a little but will firm up out of the oven.
Cool on a metal rack.
