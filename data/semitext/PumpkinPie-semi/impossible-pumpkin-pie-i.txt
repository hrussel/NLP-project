Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Grease a 9 inch pie pan.
In a medium mixing bowl, combine pumpkin, milk, sugar, baking mix, meltedbutter, eggs, allspice, cinnamon, cloves, and vanilla extract.
Beat on mediumspeed 2 minutes, until mixture is smooth.
Pour mixture into pan.
Bake in preheated oven for 50 to 55 minutes, until set in center.
Allow tocool before serving.
