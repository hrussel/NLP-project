Place pumpkin seeds, water, and salt in a saucepan, bring to a boil, andreduce heat to medium; boil seeds for 10 minutes.
Drain seeds but do notrinse.
Pat seeds dry with paper towels and spread out onto a work surface or abaking sheet for 30 minutes to dry.
Preheat oven to 250 degrees F (120 degrees C).
Line a baking sheet withaluminum foil.
Melt butter in a large microwave-safe bowl until melted, about 1 minute.
Tossseeds in the melted butter and mix in 1 tablespoon sugar, cinnamon, ginger,nutmeg, cloves, and cardamom; toss again to coat seeds thoroughly with spices.
Spread seeds out onto the prepared baking sheet in a single layer.
Bake in the preheated oven until toasted and crunchy, 45 minutes to 1 hour.
Stir several times during baking.
Sprinkle toasted seeds with 1 tablespoonsugar or as desired; cool before serving.
