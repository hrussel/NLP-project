Pour 1/2 cup caramel topping into bottom of graham cracker crust.
Sprinkle 1/2cup chopped pecans over caramel layer.
Whisk pumpkin puree, cold milk, pudding mixes, cinnamon, and nutmeg togetherin a bowl until smooth; stir in 1 1/2 cups whipped topping.
Spread pumpkinmixture over caramel and pecans in pie crust; top with remaining whippedtopping.
Refrigerate pie until chilled and set, about 1 hour.
Sprinkle 2tablespoons pecans over pie.
Drizzle 2 tablespoons caramel topping over pieusing a fork.
