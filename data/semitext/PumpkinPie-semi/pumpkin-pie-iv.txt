Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
Beat eggs lightly in a medium bowl.
Add pumpkin, sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg,ginger, and salt.
Blend thoroughly, then beat in milk.
Pour into pastry-linedpie plates.
Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes.
Reduce the heat to 325 degrees F (165degrees C) and bake until set, 20 to 30 minutes more.
Chill before serving.
