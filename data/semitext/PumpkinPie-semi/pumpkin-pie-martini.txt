Place graham cracker crumbs in a shallow dish.
Coat rim of martini glass withhoney, and dip into graham cracker crumbs to coat.
Combine milk and pumpkin puree in a cocktail shaker, and shake to combine.
Pour in vodka and creme de cacao, and add ice.
Shake well, and then straininto prepared martini glass.
Garnish with a sprinkle of pumpkin pie spice.
