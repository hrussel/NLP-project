In a large bowl, combine milk, pumpkin, flour, spices, eggs, salt, and sugar.
Mix with an electric mixer until smooth.
Pour filling into pie shells.
Bake at 475 degrees F (245 degrees C) for 10 minutes.
Reduce the temperatureto 350 degrees F (175 degrees C), and bake for an additional 35 minutes.
Servewarm or at room temperature.
