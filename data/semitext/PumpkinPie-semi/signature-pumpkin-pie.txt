Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
Place pie crust on large foil-lined bakingsheet.
Mix pumpkin, milk, eggs and pumpkin pie spice in large bowl until smooth.
Pourinto crust.
Bake 15 minutes.
Reduce oven temperature to 350 degrees F.
Bake 40 minuteslonger or until knife inserted 1 inch from crust comes out clean.
Coolcompletely on wire rack.
Serve with Vanilla Whipped Cream, if desired.
