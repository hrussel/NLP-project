Crumble the sausage into a Dutch oven or soup pot over medium-high heat.
Cookuntil evenly browned, stirring and breaking into smaller chunks.
Drain grease.
Add onion; cook and stir until onion is beginning to soften.
Put in the potatoes, and fill with enough water to cover.
Bring to a boil, andstir in the macaroni.
Pour in the cans of creamed corn and whole corn with thejuice from the can.
When the pasta and potatoes are tender, remove from heat and stir in theprocessed cheese until melted.
Season with salt and pepper to taste.
If thechowder is too thick, stir in milk or water to thin before serving
