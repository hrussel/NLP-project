Fry bacon in a Dutch oven until crisp; remove bacon, reserving 2 tablespoonsdrippings.
Crumble bacon and set aside.
Saute onion in reserved drippings until tender; add potatoes and water.
Coverand simmer 15 to 20 minutes or until potatoes are tender.
Stir in milk, corn, salt, and pepper to taste; continue to cook, stirringfrequently, until heated through.
Sprinkle with crumbled bacon to serve.
