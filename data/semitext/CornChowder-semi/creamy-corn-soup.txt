In a large pot over medium heat, combine the onion, garlic, parsley and butteror margarine.
Saute for about 5 minutes, or until onions are tender.
Add the flour, stirring well, to make a pasty mixture.
Whisk in the milk andthe broth.
Add the corn and the cream cheese and allow to heat through.
Addthe garlic salt, black pepper and cayenne pepper to taste.
Stir together andserve.
