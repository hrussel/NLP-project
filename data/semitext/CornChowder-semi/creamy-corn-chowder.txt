Place potatoes in a large pot of salted water and bring to a boil.
Cook untiltender, about 30 minutes; drain.
To the potatoes add corn, bell pepper, onion, red pepper flakes and vegetablebroth.
Boil for about 15 minutes; remove from heat.
In a food processor or blender puree all but 1 1/2 cups of the vegetable andbroth mixture with the tofu.
Process in batches if necessary.
Combine thepureed mixture with the remaining 1 1/2 cups vegetable and broth mixture, andcook over low heat for 5 minutes; do not boil.
Season with salt to taste.
