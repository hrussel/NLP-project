In a Dutch oven, brown chicken, onion, and garlic in butter until chicken isno longer pink.
Dissolve the bouillon in hot water; Pour into Dutch oven, and season withcumin.
Bring to a boil.
Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 5 minutes.
Stir in cream, cheese, corn, chilies, and hot pepper sauce.
Cook, stirringfrequently, until the cheese is melted.
Stir in chopped tomato.
Garnish withcilantro.
