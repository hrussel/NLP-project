Place potatoes, carrots, celery, onion and salt in a large pot with water tocover.
Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer 20 minutes.
Meanwhile, combine butter, milk and flour in a small saucepan over medium-lowheat.
Stir constantly until smooth and thick.
Pour milk mixture into cooked vegetables.
Stir in corn and cheese until cheeseis melted.
Serve.
