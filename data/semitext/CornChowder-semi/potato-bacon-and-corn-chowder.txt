Cook the bacon pieces in a large skillet over medium heat until the fat hasrendered, and the bacon is crispy, about 8 minutes.
Remove the bacon, leavingthe grease in the pan.
Stir the carrot, celery, and bay leaf into the bacongrease; cook for 5 minutes.
Meanwhile, melt the butter in a large pot over medium-low heat.
Whisk in theflour, and cook for 5 minutes, stirring constantly.
Slowly whisk in the milk,and bring to a simmer over medium-high heat.
Once simmering, cook for 5minutes, whisking frequently.
Add the bacon, cooked vegetables, potatoes, andcorn.
Return to a simmer, then reduce heat to medium-low, and cook until thepotatoes are tender, about 20 minutes more.
Use additional milk as needed toto achieve desired consistency.
Garnish with paprika to serve.
