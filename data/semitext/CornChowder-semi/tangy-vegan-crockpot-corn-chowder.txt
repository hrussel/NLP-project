Place the corn, vegetable broth, potatoes, onion, garlic, red chile peppers,chili powder, salt, parsley, and black pepper in a slow cooker; cover.
Cook onLow for 7 hours.
Pour the vegetable mixture into a blender, filling the pitcher no more thanhalfway full.
Hold the lid of the blender with a folded kitchen towel andcarefully start the blender using a few quick pulses before leaving it on topuree.
Puree in batches until smooth and pour into a clean pot.
Alternately,you can use a stick blender and puree the mixture in the cooking pot.
Onceeverything has been pureed, return it to the slow cooker.
Stir the soy milkand margarine to the mixture; cook on Low for 1 hour more.
Add the lime juiceto serve.
