Heat oil in large saucepan over medium-high heat.
Cook and stir onion,jalapeno peppers, and garlic in the hot oil until the onion is tender, 5 to 7minutes; season with cumin and cayenne pepper.
Add corn, chicken broth, anddiced chicken.
Bring the soup to a boil.
Reduce heat to low, place a cover on the saucepan,and cook at a simmer until the chicken is cooked through, about 15 minutes.
Remove saucepan from heat and let soup cool.
Pour soup into a blender no more than half full.
Cover and hold lid down;pulse a few times before leaving on to blend.
Puree in batches until smooth.
Return pureed soup to the saucepan.
Place saucepan over low heat; warm soup, stirring occasionally, until hot,about 5 minutes.
Garnish servings with lime wedges, cilantro, and sour cream.
