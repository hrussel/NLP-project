In large saucepan, melt margarine over medium high heat.
Add celery and onion;cook and stir until tender, about 5 minutes.
Add chicken broth; bring to a boil.
Add potatoes; cook over low heat for 20 to25 minutes or until potatoes are soft, stirring occasionally.
Stir in corn and chiles; return to boiling.
Dissolve gravy mix in milk; stirinto boiling mixture.
Add cheese; cook and stir over low heat until cheese ismelted.
