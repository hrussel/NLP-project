In a large pot over medium heat, combine water, bouillon, potatoes and garlic.
Bring to a boil, then stir in onion, celery and carrots.
Reduce heat andsimmer 15 minutes.
Stir in corn, peas and green beans and continue to cook on low heat.
Meanwhile, in a medium saucepan over medium heat, melt butter.
Whisk in flourall at once to form a roux and let cook 10 seconds.
Whisk in milk, a little ata time, and cook, stirring, until mixture is thick and bubbly.
Stir in cheeseuntil melted.
Pour this mixture into the large soup pot, stir well and heatthrough.
