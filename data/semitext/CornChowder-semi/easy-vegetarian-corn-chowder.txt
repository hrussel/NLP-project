In a large saucepan over medium heat, melt butter.
Cook onions and celery inbutter 3 minutes.
Whisk in flour and cook 6 minutes more, until light brown.
Whisk in broth and simmer 10 minutes.
Stir in creamed corn, corn, carrot, half-and-half, milk, nutmeg, pepper andsalt.
Simmer over low heat 10 minutes more.
