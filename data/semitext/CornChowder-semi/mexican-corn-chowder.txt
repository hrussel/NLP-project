In a large saucepan over medium heat, melt butter.
Cook celery, onion andcarrot in butter until onion begins to soften.
Stir in garlic, oregano, saltand pepper and cook 2 minutes more.
Stir in corn, chicken broth, cookedchicken and chile pepper and let simmer until heated through.
Stir in milk andheat gently just before serving.
