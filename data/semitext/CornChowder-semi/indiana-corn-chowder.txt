Place the bacon in a large pot and cook over medium-high heat, stirringoccasionally, until evenly browned, about 10 minutes.
Drain the bacon on apaper towel-lined plate.
Stir in the onion, celery, bell pepper, and carrots.
Cook and stir until the onions have softened and turned translucent, about 8minutes.
Add the cream-style corn, whole kernel corn, chicken broth, andpotatoes.
Bring to a boil; reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer untilthe potatoes are tender, about 20 minutes.
Stir in the diced bacon and seasonwith salt and pepper to taste before serving.
