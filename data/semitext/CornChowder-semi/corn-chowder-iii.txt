In a large skillet over medium heat, cook bacon until crisp.
Drain, reserving1 tablespoon of bacon grease.
Place bacon grease in a large stock pot over medium heat.
Cook bell pepper,onion and celery leaves in grease until just tender.
Stir in bacon, corn andwater and bring slowly to a boil, stirring frequently.
Stir in milk and heatgently, without boiling.
Serve hot.
