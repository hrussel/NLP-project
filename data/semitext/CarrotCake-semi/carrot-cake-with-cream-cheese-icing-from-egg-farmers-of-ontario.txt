Cake: Combine sugar, eggs, oil and vanilla in a large bowl; beat with anelectric mixer until combined.
Combine flour, baking soda, cinnamon and saltin a medium bowl.
Add to liquid ingredients; beat until combined.
Stir inpineapple, carrots, walnuts and raisins.
Pour batter into a greased 12-cup (3L) Bundt pan or 13 x 9-inch (33 x 23 cm) baking pan.
Bake in a preheated 350degrees F (180 degrees C) oven until a cake tester inserted in the centrecomes out clean (about 60 to 65 minutes for Bundt pan or 45 to 50 minutes for13 x 9-inch (33 x 23 cm) baking pan).
Let cake cool before icing.
Icing: Combine cream cheese, butter, vanilla and icing sugar in a bowl.
Beatwith an electric mixer until smooth.
Thin with a little milk, adding 1 tbspfor spreading or about 4 tbsp (60 mL) for drizzling over cake.
