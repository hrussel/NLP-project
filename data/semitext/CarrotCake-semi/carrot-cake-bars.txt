Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Grease and flour a 10x15 inchjelly roll pan.
Sift together the flour, baking soda, cinnamon and salt.
Setaside.
In a large bowl, beat sugar, oil and eggs until smooth.
Mix in the flourmixture.
Stir in the baby food carrots.
Spread batter into a 10x15 inch jelly roll pan.
Bake in the preheated oven for20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comesout clean.
Allow to cool.
