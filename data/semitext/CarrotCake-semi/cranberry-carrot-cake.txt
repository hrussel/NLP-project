Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Grease and flour 3 - 8 inchround cake pans.
Whisk the flour, baking soda, baking powder, cinnamon,ginger, and salt in a bowl, and set aside.
Cream together the sugar, mayonnaise, eggs, and vanilla extract with anelectric mixer until blended, scraping the bowl occasionally.
Stir in theflour mixture, then fold in the carrots, pineapple, pecans, and cranberries.
Divide evenly between the prepared cake pans.
Bake in preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes outclean, 30 to 35 minutes.
Cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then remove from thepan, and allow to cool completely on a wire rack.
