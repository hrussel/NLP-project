Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Lightly grease a 9X13 inch panwith non-fat cooking spray.
In large mixing bowl, beat egg whites.
Slowly beat in sugar, then applesauce,skim milk, and vanilla.
Stir in cloves, nutmeg, cinnamon, baking soda, andflour.
Stir in, one ingredient at a time, pineapple (with juice), carrots,walnuts, and raisins.
Pour into the prepared pan.
Bake for 35 - 40 minutes in the preheated oven.
It is done when toothpickinserted in center comes out clean.
