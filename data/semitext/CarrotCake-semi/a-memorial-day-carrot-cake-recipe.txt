Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
Generously grease 2 9-inch round cake pans.
Stir almond flour, sea salt, cinnamon, baking soda, and nutmeg together in alarge bowl.
Beat eggs, agave nectar, and grapeseed oil together in a separate large bowl.
Stir carrots, raisins, and walnuts into the egg mixture.
Stir carrot mixture into flour mixture to form a batter; pour into preparedcake pans.
Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comesout clean, about 35 minutes.
Cool in the pans for 10 minutes before removingto cool completely on a wire rack.
