Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Grease two 9 inch roundcake pans.
In a medium bowl, stir together the flour, cinnamon and sugar.
Add the oil andeggs, mix until blended, then stir in the carrots and nuts.
Divide the batterevenly between the two prepared pans.
Bake for 25 to 30 minutes in the preheated oven.
A toothpick inserted into thecake should come out clean.
Cool cakes on wire racks before removing frompans.
To make the frosting, use the second set of ingredients.
In a medium bowl,cream together the butter and cream cheese, add the sugar and cream well.
Stirin the nuts and vanilla.
Use to fill and frost the cooled cake layers.
