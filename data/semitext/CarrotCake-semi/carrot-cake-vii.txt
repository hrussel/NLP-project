Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
Grease and flour a 10 inch tubepan.
In a medium bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt, nutmeg,cinnamon and allspice.
Set aside.
In a large mixing bowl, combine oil and baby food, then add eggs.
Add sugarand mix well.
Add the flour mixture and mix until well combined.
Pour batter into prepared 10 inch tube pan.
Bake at 325 degrees F (165 degreesC) for 1 hour, or until toothpick inserted into the middle of the cake comesout clean.
Cool for 10 minutes in the pan, then turn out onto a wire rack.
