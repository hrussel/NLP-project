Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Lightly oil a 9x5 inch loaf panwith vegetable cooking spray.
Whisk together the egg whites, sugar, milk, applesauce, vanilla, and almondextract in a small bowl; mix until well blended.
In a large bowl, sifttogether the flour, whole wheat flour, flax seed, cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves,and baking soda.
Mix the egg into the flour; stir until well blended.
Fold inthe walnuts, raisins, carrot, and drained pineapple.
Pour batter into theprepared pan.
Bake in preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes outclean (but not necessarily dry), about 40 minutes.
Set aside to cool for 10 to20 minutes before refrigerating overnight.
