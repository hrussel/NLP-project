In a medium bowl, combine grated carrots and brown sugar.
Set aside for 60minutes, then stir in raisins.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Grease and flour two 10 inchcake pans.
In a large bowl, beat eggs until light.
Gradually beat in the white sugar, oiland vanilla.
Stir in the pineapple.
Combine the flour, baking soda, salt andcinnamon, stir into the wet mixture until absorbed.
Finally stir in the carrotmixture and the walnuts.
Pour evenly into the prepared pans.
Bake for 45 to 50 minutes in the preheated oven, until cake tests done with atoothpick.
Cool for 10 minutes before removing from pan.
When completelycooled, frost with cream cheese frosting.
