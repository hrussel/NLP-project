Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Grease and flour a 9x13 inchpan.
In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamonand salt.
Stir in the wheat germ.
Make a well in the center and add sugar,oil, eggs and vanilla.
Mix well, then stir in shredded carrots, chopped nuts,coconut and pineapple.
Spread batter into a 9x13 inch pan.
Bake in preheated oven for 45 minutes, oruntil the center springs back when lightly tapped.
