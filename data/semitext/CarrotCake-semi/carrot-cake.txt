Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Grease one 9 or 10 inch tubepan.
Combine the sugar, flour, salt, cinnamon, soda, oil.
With an electric mixerbeat in the eggs one at a time.
Stir in the carrots and the walnuts.
Pour thebatter into the prepared baking pan.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 1 hour 20 minutes.
Note: this cakecan be made in two 9 inch layer cake pans just change the baking time to 40minutes.
To Make Frosting: With an electric mixer blend the cream cheese and cream.
Addthe vanilla and confectioners' sugar.
Frosting can be spread and a still warmcake.
