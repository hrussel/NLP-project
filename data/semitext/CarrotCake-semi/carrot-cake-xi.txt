Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Grease and flour a 9x13 inchpan.
Sift together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt and cinnamon.
Set aside.
In a large bowl, mix together the sugar and eggs until thick and pale.
Stir inthe oil, then gradually mix in the sifted dry ingredients.
Fold in the carrotsand nuts.
Spread evenly into the prepared pan.
Bake for 40 to 45 minutes in the preheated oven, or until a toothpick insertedinto the cake comes out clean.
When cool, frost with your favorite CreamCheese Frosting.
