Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
Place sausage in a large, deepskillet.
Cook over medium-high heat until evenly brown.
Drain, crumble and setaside.
Place pizza crusts upside down on cookie sheets and bake for 5 to 7 minutes.
Do not allow to brown.
Beat eggs, milk, salt and pepper.
Scramble eggs until firm.
Remove crusts from oven, turn right side up, spread 1/2 can of cream of celerysoup on top of each crust.
Spread 1/2 of egg mixture on each crust.
Sprinklebacon bits on one pizza and cooked sausage on the other.
Cover each withonions and peppers.
Top each pizza with 2 cups of cheese.
Bake in preheated oven, for 25 to 30 minutes, until cheese is golden brown.
