Place an oven rack in the center of the oven.
Preheat oven to 450 degrees F(230 degrees C).
Brush the entire crust surface with olive oil, and sprinkle with Italian herbmix, parsley flakes, and 1/2 cup of the Parmesan cheese.
Sprinkle 1/4 cup ofmozzarella cheese over the crust.
Place pineapple chunks in a bowl, and toss with remaining Parmesan cheese.
Spread the pineapple chunks evenly over the crust, and sprinkle pizza with thechopped jalapeno.
Top with the remaining mozzarella cheese.
Place the pizza directly on the oven rack, and bake until the crust is crispand the cheese is melted and slightly browned, 10 to 12 minutes.
Cool forabout 5 minutes before serving.
