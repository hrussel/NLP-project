Without separating the dough, roll out both packs of crescent rolls onto abaking sheet, forming them into a single, flat surface.
Bake according to thedirections on the package.
Allow the finished rolls to cool.
In a bowl, Mix together the cream cheese, mayonnaise, basil and garlic powder.
Spread the mixture evenly over the surface of the rolls.
Spread the broccoli,cauliflower and carrots on top of the cream cheese mixture.
Sprinkle the saladseasoning mix over all.
Cut into squares and serve.
