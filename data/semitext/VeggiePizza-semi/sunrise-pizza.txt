Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Place sausage in a large, deep skillet.
Cook over medium-high heat untilevenly brown.
Drain off grease and add eggs.
Cook eggs and sausage togetheruntil eggs are set.
Place pizza crust on a pizza pan or baking sheet.
Spread crust with ricottacheese leaving the outer edge of the crust bare.
Spread the egg and sausagemixture over the ricotta cheese.
Sprinkle with onion, tomato, red pepper andgreen pepper.
Top with mozzarella.
Bake in preheated oven for 15 minutes, or until cheese and edges of crust aregolden brown.
