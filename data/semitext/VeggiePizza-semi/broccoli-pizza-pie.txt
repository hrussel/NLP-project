Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
Bring a pot of salted water to a boil.
Add potatoes and cook until tender butstill firm, about 15 minutes; drain.
Meanwhile, place broccoli in a steamer over 1 inch of boiling water, andcover.
Cook until tender but still firm, about 2 to 6 minutes; drain.
In a large bowl combine potatoes, broccoli, cheese, lemon juice, garlic,basil, oregano and parsley.
On a pizza pan or baking sheet, roll out 1 pizzadough.
Top with potato mixture and cover with second pizza dough; withmoistened fingers seal edges of dough together.
Bake in preheated oven for 20 minutes, or until golden brown.
Serve withwarmed pizza sauce.
