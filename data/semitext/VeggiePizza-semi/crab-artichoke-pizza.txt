Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Lightly grease a pizza pan.
Roll out pizza dough on a floured surface to a 14 or 16 inch circle; placeonto a pizza pan.
Sprinkle dough with red pepper flakes, then top evenly withcrab and artichokes.
Drizzle with olive oil, then sprinkle with garlic,Parmesan cheese, and mozzarella cheese.
Bake in preheated oven until the cheese has melted and the crust is no longerdoughy, about 20 minutes.
Set oven to broil, and cook pizza for 5 minutes moreuntil the cheese has begun to brown.
