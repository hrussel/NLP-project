Working in batches, puree onions, celery, mushrooms, and garlic and 1/4 cupchicken broth in a food processor until smooth, adding more chicken broth asnecessary.
Stir vegetable puree and remaining chicken broth together in large pot; bringto a boil, reduce heat to low, and simmer until flavors blend, about 45minutes.
Stir tomato paste, oregano, black pepper, sugar, celery salt, basil, onionpowder, and seasoned salt into broth mixture; bring to a boil, reduce heat tolow, and simmer until flavors blend, about 45 minutes more.
Whisk oil and flour into tomato mixture until pizza sauce is smooth.
