Preheat grill to medium-high.
Place each pizza crust on a sheet of foil; setaside.
Heat olive oil in a small skillet over medium heat.
Add garlic and onion;cook, stirring frequently, until onion is softened.
Brush pizza crust with olive oil mixture; arrange the onion, tomatoes,artichoke hearts, mushrooms and basil on crust.
Sprinkle with cheese.
Grill pizza on foil sheets in covered grill for 5 to 7 minutes or until cheeseis melted.
