Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Roll out crescent rolls onto a large non-stick baking sheet.
Stretch andflatten to form a single rectangular shape on the baking sheet.
Bake 11 to 13minutes in the preheated oven, or until golden brown.
Allow to cool.
Place cream cheese in a medium bowl.
Mix cream cheese with 1/2 of the ranchdressing mix.
Adjust the amount of dressing mix to taste.
Spread the mixtureover the cooled crust.
Arrange carrots, red bell pepper, broccoli and greenonions on top.
Chill in the refrigerator approximately 1 hour.
Cut into bite-size squares to serve.
