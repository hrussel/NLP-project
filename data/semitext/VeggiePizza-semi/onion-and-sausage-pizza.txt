Remove sausage from casings and saute sausage in 2 tablespoons olive oil untilbrowned.
Drain to remove excess oil.
Remove from skillet and set aside.
Inskillet, heat 3 tablespoons olive oil, add onions, garlic and saute stirringuntil onions wilt down.
Add tomato sauce, salt, pepper and red pepper totaste.
Simmer about 5 minutes on low.
Stir together the sausage and onion and chopped olives.
Toast bread lightlyunder broiler.
Top with sausage mix and sprinkle grated mozzarella over all.
Broil about 3 minutes under broiler until cheese melts.
Watch carefully withoven door open.
Remove from broiler, slice and serve.
