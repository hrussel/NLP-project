Scrape the cheese and toppings off the pizza crust, it may help to heat thepizza in the microwave.
Discard the crust.
Heat the toppings and milk in asmall saucepan over medium heat.
Stir until the topping and milk combine, itshould be a soft clumpy consistency.
Remove from the heat.
Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat.
Whisk the eggs, Italian seasoning,onion powder, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
Pour in eggs and cook withoutstirring for 1 minute.
Add the pizza toppings to the eggs; cook, stirringconstantly, until eggs reach the desired consistency.
