Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
In a medium bowl, dissolveyeast and sugar in warm water.
Let stand until creamy, about 10 minutes.
Stir in flour, salt and oil.
Beat until smooth.
Let rest for 5 minutes.
Turn dough out onto a lightly floured surface and pat or roll into a round.
Transfer crust to a lightly greased pizza pan or baker's peel dusted withcornmeal.
Spread with desired toppings and bake in preheated oven for 15 to 20minutes, or until golden brown.
Let baked pizza cool for 5 minutes beforeserving.
