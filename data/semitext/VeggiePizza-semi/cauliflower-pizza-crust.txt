Place cauliflower pieces through the feeding tube of the food processor usingthe grating blade; pulse until all the cauliflower is shredded.
Place a steamer insert into a saucepan and fill with water to just below thebottom of the steamer.
Bring water to a boil.
Add cauliflower, cover, andsteam until tender, about 15 minutes.
Transfer cauliflower to a large bowl andrefrigerate, stirring occasionally, until cooled, about 15 minutes.
Preheat an oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
Line a baking sheet withparchment paper or a silicon mat.
Stir Italian cheese blend, parsley, egg, garlic, salt, and pepper intocauliflower until evenly incorporated.
Pour mixture onto the prepared bakingsheet; press and shape into a pizza crust.
Bake in the preheated oven until lightly browned, about 15 minutes.
