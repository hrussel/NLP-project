In a large pot of salted boiling water, cook pasta until al dente, rinse undercold water and drain.
In a large bowl, combine the pasta, red bell pepper, green bell pepper,tomato, green onions, mushrooms, pepperoni, olives, garlic, oregano, salt andpepper.
Toss together and refrigerate until chilled.
Before serving, add dressing and cheese; mix together well.
