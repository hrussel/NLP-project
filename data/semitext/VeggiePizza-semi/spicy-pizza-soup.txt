In a large saucepan, place the hot Italian sausage, pepperoni sausage, greenbell peppers, onions, mushrooms, diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato pasteand water.
Bring to a boil.
Boil 30 minutes, reduce heat and simmer 90minutes.
During the final 15 minutes of cooking, stir in the elbow macaroni.
Top with mozzarella and serve.
