Heat oven to 425 degrees F.
Spray large cookie sheet with cooking spray.
Unroll pizza dough on cookie sheet; press into 15x12-inch rectangle.
Bake 6 to8 minutes or until light golden brown.
Spread 1/2 cup plus 2 tablespoons of the cooking sauce evenly over partiallybaked crust (using more sauce will make crust soggy).
Arrange potato slices insingle layer over sauce.
Sprinkle with bacon and cheese.
Bake 8 to 10 minutesor until crust is golden brown.
Meanwhile, heat remaining sauce until hot.
Sprinkle with olives, tomatoes and green onions.
Serve with sauce as a dippingsauce.
