Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (205 degrees C).
Heat oil in a small skillet over medium heat; cook and stir onion and greenpepper until tender, about 5 minutes.
Place tortillas on an ungreased baking sheet.
Top with onion mixture, oregano,garlic powder, tomato, and mozzarella cheese.
Bake in preheated oven until cheese is melted, 8 to 10 minutes.
Cut each pizzainto four wedges.
