Heat oven to 400 degrees F.
In 6-inch nonstick skillet, cook sausage overmedium-high heat 4 to 6 minutes, stirring frequently, until no longer pink.
Stir in mushrooms, tomatoes and pizza sauce.
Remove from heat; cover to keepwarm.
Spray bottom and side of 12-inch cast-iron skillet or other ovenproof skilletwith cooking spray.
Unroll dough in skillet; press in bottom and at leasthalfway up side.
Bake 5 minutes.
Sprinkle 1 3/4 cups of the cheese over dough.
Spoon sausage and vegetable mixture over cheese; top with remaining 1/4 cupcheese.
Bake 15 to 18 minutes or until crust is golden brown.
Sprinkle with greenonions.
Let stand 10 minutes before cutting.
To serve, cut into 6 wedges.
