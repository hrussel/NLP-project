Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a lightly greased 9x13 inch pan or 13 inch round pizza stone, arrange thecrescent roll dough in a single layer.
Pinch together the edges of the dough.
Bake for 12 minutes, or until it becomes a golden brown crust.
Remove thecrust from the oven and allow it to cool.
In a medium bowl, mix together the cream cheese, mayonnaise and dill.
Evenlyspread the cream cheese mixture on the crust.
Sprinkle toppings on top of thecream cheese spread.
Refrigerate for 30 minutes to allow the spread tothicken.
Cut the chilled pizza into wedges or squares with a pizza cutter to serve.
