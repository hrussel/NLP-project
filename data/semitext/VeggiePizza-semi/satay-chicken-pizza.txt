Heat oil in a skillet over high heat.
Saute chicken pieces in hot oil for 6 to7 minutes.
Do not overcook!Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
To Assemble Pizza: Spoon 1/4 of peanut sauce onto each pita.
Sprinkle 1/4 ofthe browned chicken and 1/4 of the scallions on top of each.
Top each 'pizza'with 1 slice cheese.
Place on a lightly greased cookie sheet and bake in thepreheated oven for 10 to 12 minutes, until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
Let stand for 1 to 2 minutes outside of oven before you cut with a pizzacutter.
