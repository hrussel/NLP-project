Heat oven to 400 degrees F for dark or nonstick pan (425 degrees F for allother pans).
Spray 15x10-inch pan with sides with cooking spray.
Unroll dough in pan; starting at center, press dough into 15x10-inchrectangle.
Bake 4 to 6 minutes or until edges begin to set.
In small bowl, stir 6 tablespoons of the pesto with the garlic.
Spread overcrust.
Sprinkle with cheese.
Top with potato slices, and brush remaining 2tablespoons pesto over potatoes.
Bake 8 to 10 minutes longer or until crust is golden brown and cheese ismelted.
Garnish with green onions.
To serve, cut into 6 rows by 3 rows.
