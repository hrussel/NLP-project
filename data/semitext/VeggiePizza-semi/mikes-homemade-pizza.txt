In a small bowl, dissolve yeast in warm water.
Let stand until creamy, about10 minutes.
In a large bowl, combine flour, salt and shortening.
Stir in the yeastmixture.
When the dough has pulled together, turn it out onto a lightlyfloured surface, and knead until smooth and elastic, about 8 minutes.
Lightlyoil a large bowl, place the dough in the bowl, and turn to coat with oil.
Cover with a damp cloth, and let rise in a warm place until doubled in volume,about 45 minutes.
Heat oil in a small saucepan over medium heat.
Saute onion until tender.
Stirin tomato paste and water.
Season with sugar, salt, black pepper, garlicpowder, basil, oregano, marjoram, cumin, chili powder and red pepper flakes.
Simmer 15 to 20 minutes.
RecipeFile makes 2 (12 inch) pizzas.
Divide dough in half, and spread onto pizzapans.
Cover with sauce, and desired toppings.
Bake at 400 degrees for 20minutes, or until crust is golden brown.
