In a mixing bowl, whisk together orange juice, lime juice, shallots, garlic,chili powder, cumin, and sugar.
Pour 1/2 of this mixture into a large,resealable plastic bag, and add the chicken breasts.
Seal, and refrigerate forat least 2 hours.
Refrigerate the remaining dressing.
Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat.
In a large salad bowl, tossromaine lettuce with oranges, celery, and green onions.
Set aside.
Lightly oil grate, and place chicken on grill.
Discard the marinade from thechicken.
Cook for 6 to 8 minutes each side, or until juices run clear whenpierced with a fork.
Remove chicken from grill, and slice into thin strips.
Toss salad with reserved dressing, and top with sliced chicken.
