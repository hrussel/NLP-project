Preheat oven to 325 degrees F ( 165 degrees ).
Mix together chicken, celery, lemon juice, chopped onion, toasted almonds,mayonnaise, sour cream, and grated cheese and place mixture in a lightlygreased 9x13 inch baking dish.
Top with crushed corn flakes.
Bake in preheated oven for 45 minutes to 1 hour, until heated through.
