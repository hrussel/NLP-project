Toss chicken breasts, limeade, and pepper in a bowl.
Cook the chicken breastsin a skillet until no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear,about 8 minutes.
Remove skillet from the heat and set aside.
Combine sugar, vinegar, salt, mustard, and onion in a blender.
Process forabout 20 seconds.
With the blender on high speed, gradually add oil in a slowand steady stream.
Stir in poppy seeds.
Arrange spinach on 4 salad plates and top with strawberries, peas, pecans, andthe cooked chicken.
Serve with the poppy seed dressing.
