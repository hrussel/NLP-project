Place chicken in a large pot of lightly salted water, and bring to a boil;simmer until tender and cooked through, about 25 minutes.
Drain, set aside.
When cool enough to handle, chop coarsely, and place into a large bowl.
In a separate bowl, stir together mayonnaise, curry powder, lemon juice, andsoy sauce.
Stir into the chopped chicken 2 1/4 cups of the toasted almonds.
Stir ingrapes, celery, water chestnuts, and pineapple.
Gently fold in all but 1/2 cupof the dressing.
Cover, and refrigerate salad and reserved dressing forseveral hours (or overnight).
Before serving, adjust dressing to taste, and garnish with paprika andremaining 1/4 cup almonds.
