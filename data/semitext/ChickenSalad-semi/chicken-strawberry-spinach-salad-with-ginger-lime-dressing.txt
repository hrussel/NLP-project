Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat.
Place chicken in skillet, season withgarlic powder and cook 10 minutes on each side or until juices run clear.
Setaside.
In a bowl, mix mayonnaise, lime juice, ginger and milk.
Arrange spinach on serving dishes.
Top with chicken and strawberries, sprinklewith almonds and drizzle with dressing.
Season with pepper to serve.
