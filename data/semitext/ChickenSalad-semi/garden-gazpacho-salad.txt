Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Add pasta and cook for 8to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
Mix the pasta with the chicken, cucumber, bell pepper, tomato, and garlic.
Pour the tomato-vegetable juice cocktail and lemon juice over the salad;gently toss to coat.
Season with black pepper and salt; serve warm or chilled.
