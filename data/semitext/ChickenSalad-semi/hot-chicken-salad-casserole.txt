Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Lightly grease a 9x13 inchbaking dish.
In a large bowl mix the chicken, celery, water chestnuts, mushrooms,mayonnaise, lemon juice, salt, onion, and almonds.
Transfer to the bakingdish, and top with Cheddar cheese and crushed potato chips.
Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes, until lightly browned
