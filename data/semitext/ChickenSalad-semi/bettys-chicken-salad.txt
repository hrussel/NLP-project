Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Cook shell pasta in theboiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to thebite, 8 minutes.
Rinse in cold water until cool and drain.
Combine pasta, chicken, celery, and almonds in a large bowl.
Mix mayonnaise,lemon juice, salt, and pepper in another bowl.
Pour mayonnaise mixture intopasta mixture; stir well.
Fold eggs into pasta.
Cover and refrigerate for 6hours.
