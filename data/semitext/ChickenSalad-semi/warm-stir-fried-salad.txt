Place chicken in a large bowl.
Whisk soy sauce, ginger, tarragon, and brownsugar together in a bowl; pour over chicken.
Cover bowl with plastic wrap andmarinate in refrigerator, 2 to 4 hours.
Remove chicken from marinade with a slotted spoon, reserving marinade.
Heat vegetable oil in a skillet over medium-high heat; saute chicken until nolonger pink in the center, about 5 minutes.
Add marinade; bring to a boil for3 minutes.
Stir cashews and carrots into chicken.
Arrange cabbage and baby kale on 4 plates.
Drizzle sesame oil over eachcabbage-kale serving; top with chicken mixture.
