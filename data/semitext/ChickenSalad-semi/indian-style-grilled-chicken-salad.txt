Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil grate.
Combine the hot sauce, chili powder, coriander, tandoori paste, oregano,vinegar, lemon juice, and oil in a small bowl.
Coat each piece of chicken withthe sauce, then discard remaining sauce.
Cook the chicken on the grill untiljuices run clear and the chicken is cooked through, approximately 10 to 15minutes.
Set chicken aside.
Prepare the dressing by mixing the lemon juice, vinegar, hot sauce, salt andpepper in a small bowl; set aside.
For the salad, combine the lettuce, tomatoes, onion, radishes, carrots, andpepper in a large bowl.
Slice the cooked chicken and add to the salad.
Tossgently with the dressing.
