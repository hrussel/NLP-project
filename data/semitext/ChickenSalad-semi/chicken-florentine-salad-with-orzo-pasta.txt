Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Cook orzo in the boilingwater, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, 10 to11 minutes.
Drain and rinse under cold water.
Mix orzo, spinach, chicken, tomatoes, pine nuts, sun-dried tomatoes, andcapers in a large bowl.
Whisk balsamic vinegar, olive oil, sugar, parsley, basil, garlic, oregano,salt, and pepper together in a bowl; drizzle over salad.
Toss to coat.
Sprinkle Parmesan cheese over salad.
