Preheat grill for high heat.
Lightly oil grate.
Grill chicken 10 minutes on each side, or until juices runclear.
Remove from heat, cool, and cube.
In a large bowl, toss together the chicken, celery, red bell pepper, onion,and corn.
In a small bowl, mix together the barbeque sauce and mayonnaise.
Pour over thechicken and veggies.
Stir, and chill until ready to serve.
