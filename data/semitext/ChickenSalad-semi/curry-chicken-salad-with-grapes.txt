Heat butter in a large saucepan over low heat until melted; remove from heatand cool to room temperature.
Stir mayonnaise, garlic, parsley, and curry powder into butter until evenlyincorporated.
Fold in chicken, grapes, and almonds.
Transfer mixture to aserving bowl and cover with plastic wrap.
Refrigerate until flavors haveblended, at least 1 hour.
