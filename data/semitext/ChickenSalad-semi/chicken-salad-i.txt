Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
Add potatoes; cook until tenderbut still firm, about 15 minutes.
Drain, cool and chop.
In a mixing bowl, combine the potatoes, chicken, peas and corn.
Add enough salad dressing to coat, mix well.
Top with sliced tomatoes, coverand refrigerate for 2 hours.
Season with salt and pepper to taste.
