Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large bowl blend together the chicken, celery, bell pepper, onion,pimento, salt, lemon juice and mayonnaise.
Spread mixture into the bottom of alightly greased 9x13 inch baking dish, sprinkle with cheese and top withpotato chip crumbs.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 25 to 30 minutes, or until cheese ismelted and casserole is bubbly.
