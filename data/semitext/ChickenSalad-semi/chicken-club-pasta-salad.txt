Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Cook pasta in the boilingwater, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, 10 to12 minutes.
Drain and rinse under cold water.
Whisk Italian-style dressing and mayonnaise together in a large bowl.
Stirpasta, chicken, bacon, Muenster cheese, celery, green bell pepper, cherrytomatoes, and avocado into dressing until evenly coated.
