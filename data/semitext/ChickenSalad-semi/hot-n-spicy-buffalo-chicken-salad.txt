In a small bowl, combine crumbled blue cheese and Ranch-style dressing; setaside.
Season cubed chicken with salt and pepper.
Heat a large skillet over high heat, and pour in the vegetable oil.
Justbefore the oil starts to smoke, add the chicken.
Cook to brown evenly, about 3minutes.
Reduce heat to low, and add butter, hot sauce, and salad seasoningmix.
Simmer until chicken is cooked through, about 5 minutes.
Toss together the lettuce and Ranch-style dressing mixture in a large bowl.
Top salad with chicken, and serve.
