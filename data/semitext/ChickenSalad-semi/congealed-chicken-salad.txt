In a large pot, boil chicken until done, drain and cool.
Remove skin and bonesand chop meat into small pieces.
In a bowl, combine chicken, pickles andmayonnaise.
In a saucepan, heat soup and gelatin until gelatin dissolves.
Remove from heatand add cream cheese.
Stir until smooth.
Add chicken, pickles and mayonnaise to cream cheese mixture and mix untilsmooth.
Pour into a greased mold and chill until firm.
