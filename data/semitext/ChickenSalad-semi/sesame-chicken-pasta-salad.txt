Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Add pasta and cook for 8to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
Meanwhile, heat oil in a small skillet over medium-low heat.
Stir in sesameseeds and cook until golden brown.
Remove from heat.
Stir in soy sauce,vinegar, and sugar.
Pour dressing into a sealable container, and set aside.
In a large bowl, mix together pasta, cooked chicken, and 1 cup dressing(reserve remaining dressing).
Cover salad, and refrigerate at least 6 hours.
Directly before serving, stir in parsley, green onions, and spinach.
Toss withremaining dressing, if desired.
