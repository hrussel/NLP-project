Bring the water, bouillon cubes, and garlic to a boil in a saucepan.
Stir inthe quinoa, reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until the quinoa istender and the water has been absorbed, 15 to 20 minutes.
Discard the garlicclove and scrape the quinoa into a large bowl.
Gently stir the chicken, onion, bell pepper, olives, feta cheese, parsley,chives, and salt into the quinoa.
Drizzle with the lemon juice, balsamicvinegar, and olive oil.
Stir until evenly mixed.
Serve warm or refrigerate andserve cold.
