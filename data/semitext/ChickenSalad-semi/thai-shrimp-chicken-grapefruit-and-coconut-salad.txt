Make the dressing by whisking together the lime juice, fish sauce, sugar, andgarlic in a bowl.
Toss together the sectioned grapefruit, shrimp, chicken breast, coconut,shallots, chile pepper, mint, and cilantro in a large bowl.
Drizzleapproximately 3/4 of the dressing into the grapefruit mixture and toss tocombine.
Combine the remaining dressing with the shredded lettuce in aseparate bowl shortly before serving.
Arrange the lettuce on a large platter.
Layer the grapefruit mixture on top ofthe lettuce.
