Bring a pot of water to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer; add rosemary.
Poachchicken in the hot water until no longer pink in the center, 5 to 10 minutes.
An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165degrees F (74 degrees C).
Remove chicken with a slotted spoon and allow tocool.
Discard broth.
Chop chicken into chunks.
Mix chicken, apple, celery, red onion, mayonnaise, chutney, lime juice, currypowder, salt, and black pepper in a bowl.
Chill in refrigerator to allowflavors to blend, at least 2 hours.
Serve cold or at room temperature.
