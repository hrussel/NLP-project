Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Lightly grease a medium bakingdish.
Arrange the chicken in the baking dish, cover, and bake 25 minutes in thepreheated oven, until juices run clear.
Remove from heat, cool, and shred.
In a large bowl, mix the shredded chicken, celery, tarragon, parsley,mayonnaise, raisins, and onion.
Refrigerate 1 hour, or until chilled, beforeserving.
