Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
Add potatoes and cook untiltender but still firm, about 15 minutes.
Drain, cool and chop.
In a large bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise and curry powder.
Add thepotatoes, chicken, onion and artichoke hearts and mix together until evenlycoated.
Refrigerate until chilled.
To serve, arrange over a lettuce bed and sprinklewith salt and pepper.
