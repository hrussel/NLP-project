Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil grate.
Sprinkle chicken breasts with fajita seasoning, and grill for about 4 minuteson each side, or until no longer pink and juices run clear.
Allow to cool,then slice into bite-size strips.
In a blender or food processor, combine mayonnaise, grated Parmesan cheese,sour cream, anchovies, green onions, parsley, Dijon mustard, chipotle pepper,garlic, and Worcestershire sauce.
Blend until smooth.
Place romaine lettuce in a large bowl, and top with croutons, shreddedParmesan, and red, yellow, and orange pepper rings.
Arrange chicken on top,then pour on the dressing.
