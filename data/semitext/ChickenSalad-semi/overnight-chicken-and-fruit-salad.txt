Combine chicken, grapes, pineapple chunks, celery, and water chestnuts in alarge salad bowl.
Mix mayonnaise, soy sauce, lemon juice, and curry powder ina separate bowl.
Pour dressing over salad and toss lightly to coat.
Cover andchill 8 hours to overnight.
Stir almonds into salad just before serving;garnish with papaya and avocado slices.
