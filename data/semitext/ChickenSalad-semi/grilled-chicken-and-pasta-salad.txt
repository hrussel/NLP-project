Preheat the grill for high heat.
Season both sides of chicken breast halveswith steak seasoning.
Lightly oil the grill grate.
Grill chicken 6 to 8 minutes per side, or untiljuices run clear.
Remove from heat, cool, and cut into strips.
Meanwhile, place the rotini pasta in a large pot of lightly salted boilingwater.
Cook 8 to 10 minutes, until al dente.
Drain, and rinse with cold waterto cool.
In a large bowl, mix together the cheese, onion, lettuce, and tomatoes.
Tosswith the cooled chicken and pasta to serve.
