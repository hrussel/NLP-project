Place chicken breast halves in a large resealable plastic bag with lemonjuice, olive oil, and lemon pepper.
Shake to coat, and marinate in therefrigerator for at least 1 hour.
Preheat a grill for high heat.
Lightly oil the grill grate.
Place the Vidalia onions and mushrooms on thegrill, and cook until lightly charred on both sides; set aside.
Place chickenonto the grill, and discard marinade.
Cook for 15 minutes, turning once, oruntil juices run clear.
Remove from heat, cool, and chop.
In a large bowl, thoroughly mix the onions, mushrooms, chicken, andmayonnaise.
Season with hot sauce, salt, and pepper.
Cover, and refrigerateuntil serving.
