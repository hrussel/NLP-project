Divide spinach between 4 salad plates.
In a large deep skillet, melt 1 tablespoon butter over medium heat; add fenneland cook for about 3 minutes or until tender.
Remove from skillet; set aside.
In same skillet, heat remaining butter with olive oil; add garlic and cook for1 minute.
Add chicken livers; cook for 3 to 4 minutes or until no longer pinkin center.
Return fennel to skillet.
Add stock, anchovies, capers, sage, salt and pepper;cook over high heat for 2 to 3 minutes or until sauce is reduced slightly.
Spoon mixture over individual salads and garnish with fennel leaves.
Serveimmediately.
