Place the lime peel, soy sauce, and chicken broth in a skillet over mediumheat.
Place the chicken breasts in the skillet, and bring to a boil.
Cover,and simmer until the chicken is fully cooked.
Remove from heat, and allow tocool.
Shred chicken, and set aside.
In a separate bowl, whisk together lime juice, olive oil, anchovy paste,sugar, salt, and pepper.
Stir in garlic, cilantro, shallots, and chicken.
Mixwell.
