Combine rice, chicken, tomato, cilantro, green onions, and avocado together ina bowl.
Whisk canola oil, lime juice, lime zest, garlic, jalapeno pepper, salt,oregano, cumin, and chili powder together in a bowl; pour over rice mixtureand toss to coat.
Chill in refrigerator, about 1 hour.
Place lettuce in a serving bowl; top with rice mixture.
