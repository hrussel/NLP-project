In a saucepan over medium-high heat, stir together the brown sugar and water.
Bring to a boil, then pour into a medium bowl.
Stir in the garlic chili sauceand lime juice.
Set the dressing aside.
In a large bowl, toss together the chicken, mangos and avocados.
Arrange thespring salad mix on serving plates, then top with a few spoonfuls of thechicken mixture.
Pour dressing over the top.
