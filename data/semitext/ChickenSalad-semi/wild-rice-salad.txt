Cook rice according to package directions.
Remove from heat and set aside tocool.
In a medium bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, vinegar, sugar, salt andpepper.
Stir in rice, turkey, onion and grapes until evenly coated withdressing.
Cover and refrigerate for 1 to 2 hours.
Before serving, sprinkle slivered almonds over the top of the salad.
