Preheat grill for medium-high heat.
In a bowl, whisk together the orange juice, vinegar, olive oil, seasoningblend, and sugar.
Set aside about 1/2 cup for basting.
Lightly oil the grill grate.
Grill chicken for 6 to 8 minutes on each side,basting frequently with the reserved portion of the dressing, or until juicesrun clear.
Cool, and cut into strips.
Discard basting sauce.
In a large bowl, toss together the lettuce, oranges, broccoli, and carrots.
Top the salad with grilled chicken strips, and drizzle with remaining dressingto serve.
