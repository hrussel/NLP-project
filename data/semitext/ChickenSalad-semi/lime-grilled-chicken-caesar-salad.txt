In a shallow baking pan, arrange chicken and pour mesquite-lime marinade overall.
Cover and place in the refrigerator to marinate for 1/2 hour beforegrilling.
Preheat a charcoal or gas grill to medium high heat.
Grill chicken breasts for approximately 15 minutes on each side, or until meatis cooked through and is no longer pink inside.
Remove from heat, cool andslice into strips.
In a large bowl, toss together the lettuce, tomato, onion, pepper, cheese,croutons, dressing and chicken until evenly coated.
Serve immediately.
