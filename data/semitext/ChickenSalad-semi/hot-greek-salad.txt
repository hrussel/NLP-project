Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Cook orzo in the boilingwater, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, about10 minutes.
Drain and keep warm.
Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat; cook and stir chicken,garlic, and Greek seasoning until chicken is no longer pink in the center andjuices run clear, about 10 minutes.
Add spinach, cucumber, tomato, olives, andvinaigrette; lower heat and simmer until spinach begins to wilt, 5 to 10minutes.
Serve over orzo; top with feta cheese.
