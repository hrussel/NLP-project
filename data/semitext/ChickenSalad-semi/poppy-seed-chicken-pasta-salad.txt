Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Cook pasta in the boilingwater, stirring occasionally, until cooked through but firm to the bite, 12minutes; drain, rinse in cold water, and drain again.
Toss pasta, chicken, onion, cranberries, celery, and almonds together in asalad bowl; drizzle with poppy seed dressing and stir to coat.
