In a medium bowl, prepare the dressing according to package directions, usingvinegar and oil, and substituting orange juice for the water; set aside.
Preheat the grill for high heat.
Lightly oil the grill grate.
Grill the chicken 6 to 8 minutes on each side, oruntil juices run clear.
Remove from heat, cool, and slice into strips.
In a large bowl, toss together the chicken, salad greens, strawberries,blueberries, raspberries, peas, and pecans.
Pour in the prepared dressing, andtoss to coat.
