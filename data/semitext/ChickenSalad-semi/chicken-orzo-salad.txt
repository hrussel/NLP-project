Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Add pasta, and cook for 8to 10 minutes or until al dente.
Rinse with cold water, drain, and set aside.
In a large bowl, whisk together oil, orange juice, vinegar, salt, and orangezest.
Stir in cooked chicken until coated.
Stir in cooked orzo, cantaloupe,grape halves, and mayonnaise.
Cover and chill for 4 to 6 hours.
Just beforeserving, stir in cashews.
Serve in lettuce lined bowls.
