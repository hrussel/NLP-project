Heat grill to medium-high heat.
Brush chicken with oil; season with salt and pepper, to taste.
Grill 8 to 12minutes or until no longer pink in the center (165 degrees F), turning once.
Cool chicken enough to handle; slice into thin strips.
Combine salad blend, tomatoes from pouch, cooked sliced chicken, avocado,bacon, egg and blue cheese in large bowl.
Toss with blue cheese dressing, totaste.
