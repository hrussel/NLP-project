Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Add pasta and cook for 8to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain and rinse with cool water.
Pour into alarge dish.
Combine mushrooms, olives, celery, onion, cheese, corn and green bell pepperwith pasta; mix well.
In a small bowl, whisk together dressing and mayonnaise; pour dressing oversalad and toss again to coat.
Gently mix in flaked chicken; refrigerate for a few hours or serve.
