Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Add pasta and cook for 8to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain and pour pasta into a large dish.
Stir in the corn, mushrooms, celery, onions, olives, bell pepper and cheese.
In a separate bowl, whisk together the salad dressing and mayonnaise, thenpour this mixture over the salad and toss again, to coat.
Add flaked chickenand toss gently a final time.
