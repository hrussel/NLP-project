In a jar, combine lime juice, sugar, lime zest, rice vinegar, and mustard.
Shake well, and set aside.
In a small bowl, mix chicken with limeadeconcentrate and pepper.
Heat oil in a skillet over medium high heat.
Cook and stir chicken 4 to 6minutes, or until no longer pink, and juices run clear.
Divide lettuce onto 4 salad plates.
Top with mandarin orange segments, goldenraisins, and walnuts.
Place cooked chicken on top, and drizzle lightly withdressing.
