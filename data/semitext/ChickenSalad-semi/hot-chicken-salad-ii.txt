Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a small skillet, saute the mushrooms in oil.
Mix together the chicken, mayonnaise, soup, sauteed mushrooms, water chestnutsand celery.
Place mixture in a 12x7.
5 inch casserole dish.
Top with thecrushed crackers, tossed with melted butter.
Bake in the preheated oven for 30to 45 minutes.
