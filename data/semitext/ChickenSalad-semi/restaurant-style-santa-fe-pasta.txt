Bring water to a boil and add the Rotini pasta and cook according to packagedirections.
Rinse under cold running water and drain well.
Stir the tomato juice, olive oil, vinegar, chili powder, paprika, salt andblack pepper together.
Toss it with the drained pasta and refrigerate for 2 to4 hours.
Combine the Parmesan cheese, corn kernels, cilantro, scallions, red bellpepper, green bell pepper and diced chicken breast.
Combine with the chilledpasta mixture and cover and refrigerate for at least 8 hours or overnightbefore serving.
