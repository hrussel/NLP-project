Bring the rice and water to a boil in a saucepan over high heat.
Reduce heatto medium-low, cover, and simmer until the rice is tender, and the liquid hasbeen absorbed, 20 to 25 minutes.
Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
Cook the chickenbreasts until no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear, about 25minutes.
An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read atleast 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
Cut the chicken into bite size pieces.
Toss chicken, rice, and lettuce in a large bowl.
Serve immediately.
