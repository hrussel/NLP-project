Combine pineapple juice, soy sauce, brown sugar and garlic with 1 tablespoonprovided sesame ginger dressing in small sauce pan.
Bring to boil and reduceto 1/3.
Heat the grill or grill pan over medium-high heat; brush grill lightly withoil or spray with non-stick vegetable cooking spray.
Baste chicken with pineapple mixture.
Grill chicken, turning once (andcontinuing to baste), 10 to 12 minutes or until no longer pink in center.
Slice chicken.
Place salad blend in large bowl with provided accompanimentsand toss with remaining dressing.
Arrange salad on plates; top with bell pepper, onion, and chicken.
