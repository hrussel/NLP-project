In a large bowl, gently mix the chicken, carrots, red bell pepper, jicama, redonion, corn, cherry tomatoes, avocados, and cilantro.
In a separate bowl, mix the sour cream, mayonnaise, lemon juice, cumin, salt,pepper, taco seasoning, and hot pepper sauce.
Pour over the salad, and toss tocoat.
Cover and refrigerate at least 1 hour before serving.
