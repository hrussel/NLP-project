Place cooking wine and chicken in large saucepan.
Add enough water to cover.
Bring to a boil; reduce heat.
Simmer 10 minutes or until chicken is tender andno longer pink.
Let chicken cool in liquid 45 minutes.
Drain; cut chicken into bite-sizedpieces.
In large bowl, combine chicken, pea pods, celery, onions, mayonnaise, lemonpeel and pepper; mix well.
Cover; refrigerate 1 to 2 hours to blend flavors.
Just before serving, stir in almonds.
To serve, spoon chicken salad ontolettuce-lined plates.
