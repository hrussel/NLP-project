Warm 1 tablespoon olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
Season bothsides of chicken with Cajun seasoning, and place in hot oil.
Stir in crushedgarlic.
Cook chicken 5 minutes on each side, or until juices run clear.
Removechicken from skillet, drain on paper towels, cool slightly, and dice; discardoil and garlic.
In a small bowl, mix remaining olive oil, balsamic vinegar, mustard, salt, andpepper.
In a large bowl, gently toss the dressing with the mixed salad greens,arugula, avocado, sun-dried tomatoes, and olives.
Top with diced chicken toserve.
