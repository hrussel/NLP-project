Place eggs into a saucepan, cover with cold water, and place over high heat.
When water is boiling, cover saucepan with a lid, reduce heat to low, andsimmer for 18 minutes.
Remove from heat and cool eggs under cold running wateruntil eggs can be handled.
Peel eggs.
Cut eggs in half lengthwise and scoop yolks into a mixing bowl.
Beat yolks,cream cheese, creamy salad dressing, pickle relish, hot sauce, salt, and blackpepper until mixture is creamy and smooth.
Spoon yolk mixture into egg whitehalves.
Sprinkle each deviled egg half lightly with Cajun seasoning.
Chillbefore serving.
