Place the eggs in a saucepan in a single layer with enough water to cover by 1inch.
Cover the saucepan and bring the water to a boil over high heat.
Oncethe water is boiling, remove from the heat and let the eggs stand in the hotwater for 15 minutes.
Pour out the hot water, then cool the eggs under coldrunning water.
Peel once cold.
Dry the eggs thoroughly with paper towels, and slice them in half lengthwise.
Remove the yolks, and place in a bowl.
Mash the yolks with a fork, and stir inthe sugar, salt, onion powder, garlic powder, and white pepper.
Add themustard, mayonnaise, salad dressing, and vinegar, one at a time, stirring toincorporate each ingredient before adding the next.
Mound a heaping teaspoonful of the yolk mixture into the cavity of each egghalf, and sprinkle with paprika.
