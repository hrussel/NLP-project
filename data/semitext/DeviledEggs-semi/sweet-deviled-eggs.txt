Place eggs in a saucepan and cover with water.
Bring to a boil, remove fromheat, and let eggs stand in hot water for 15 minutes.
Remove eggs from hotwater and place in a bowl of ice water.
Peel.
Slice eggs in half lengthwise.
Remove yolks and transfer to a bowl; mash witha fork or pastry blender until yolks resemble powder.
Stir mayonnaise, sugar,honey mustard, vinegar, salt, and pepper into yolks until smooth.
Spoon yolkmixture into the egg whites and lightly sprinkle with paprika.
Chill beforeserving.
