Place eggs in a saucepan, and cover with cold water.
Bring water to a boil andimmediately remove from heat.
Cover, and let eggs stand in hot water for 10 to12 minutes.
Remove from hot water, and cool.
To cool more quickly, rinse eggsunder cold running water.
Meanwhile, place bacon in a large, deep skillet.
Cook over medium-high heatuntil evenly brown.
Alternatively, wrap bacon in paper towels and cook in themicrowave for about 1 minute per slice.
Crumble and set aside.
Peel the hard-cooked eggs, and cut in half lengthwise.
Remove yolks to a smallbowl.
Mash egg yolks with mayonnaise, crumbled bacon and cheese.
Stir inmustard.
Fill egg white halves with the yolk mixture and refrigerate untilserving.
