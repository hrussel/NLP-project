Cut eggs in half lengthwise.
Remove yolks; place in medium bowl.
AddNeufchatel cheese, mayo, mustard, vinegar and sugar; mix until well blended.
Spoon mixture into large resealable plastic bag.
Cut small corner from bag andpipe filling into egg white halves.
Sprinkle with paprika.
Add, if desired, your choice of Toppings (see Tips) just before serving.
