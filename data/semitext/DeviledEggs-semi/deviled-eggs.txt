Place eggs in saucepan and cover with water.
Bring to boil.
Cover, remove fromheat, and let eggs sit in hot water for 10 to 12 minutes.
Remove from hotwater and cool.
Peel and cut in half lengthwise.
Remove yolks and combine with mustard, saladdressing and salt and pepper.
Mix together until smooth.
Refill each egg half with the yolk mixture and sprinkle with paprika.
