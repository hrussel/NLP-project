Place eggs in a pot of salted water.
Bring the water to a boil, and let eggscook in boiling water until they are hard boiled, approximately 10 to 15minutes.
Drain eggs, and let cool.
Cut eggs in half, lengthwise.
Remove the egg yolks and mash them together in asmall mixing bowl.
Mix in the paprika, mayonnaise, and dry mustard.
Spoonmixture into the egg whites; cool and serve.
