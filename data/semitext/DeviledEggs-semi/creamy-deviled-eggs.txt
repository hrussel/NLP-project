Place eggs in a large saucepan and cover with cold water.
Bring water to aboil and immediately remove from heat.
Cover and let eggs stand in hot waterfor 10 to 12 minutes.
Remove from hot water, cool and peel.
Slice eggs in half lengthwise and remove yolks.
Place yolks in a medium bowl.
Mash together with ranch-style salad dressing.
Mix in the cream cheese, thenthe onion and dill pickle.
Fill the hollowed egg whites generously with the egg yolk mixture.
Chill inthe refrigerator until serving.
