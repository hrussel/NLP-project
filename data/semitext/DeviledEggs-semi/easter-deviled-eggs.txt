Place eggs into a large saucepan, cover with cold water, and bring to a boil.
Let eggs boil for 3 minutes; turn off heat, cover pot, and let eggs cook inhot water for at least 20 minutes.
Drain and cover eggs with cold water.
Peelcooled eggs.
Cut hard-cooked eggs in half lengthwise and remove yolks; mash yolks in a bowlwith creamy salad dressing, salt, black pepper, hot sauce, and dry mustarduntil smooth.
Place red, blue, and green food coloring into 3 different bowls and add about1 cup water to each bowl.
Steep 8 egg white halves in each bowl, tinting theegg whites pink, light blue, and green.
If color is too pale, add morecoloring to bowls.
Drain colored egg whites on paper towels.
Pipe or spoon egg yolk filling into colored egg white halves; cover and chillbefore serving, at least 30 minutes.
