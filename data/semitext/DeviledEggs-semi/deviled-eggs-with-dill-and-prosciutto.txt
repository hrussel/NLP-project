Place eggs in a saucepan; cover with water.
Bring to a boil, remove from heat,and let eggs stand in hot water for 15 minutes.
Remove eggs from hot water,cool under cold running water, and peel.
Cut each egg in half lengthwise; place egg yolks in a bowl.
Mash yolks with afork; stir in dill, mayonnaise, and Dijon mustard.
Place egg whites cut-side-up on a serving platter.
Cut each prosciutto sliceinto 6 pieces; lay one piece into each egg white half.
Fill egg whites withyolk mixture; sprinkle with black pepper, top with a cucumber slice, andsprinkle with paprika.
Refrigerate, covered, for 30 minutes before serving.
