Measure 1 cup beet juice from reserved juice.
Combine 1 cup beet juice, sugar, vinegar, cloves, salt, pepper, and bay leavesin a saucepan; bring to a boil.
Reduce heat and simmer until sugar isdissolved, about 5 minutes.
Place beets and eggs into a large bowl.
Pour beet juice mixture over beets andeggs.
Refrigerate for 3 days.
Drain and discard pickling liquid.
Cut each egg in half lengthwise and scoop yolks into a bowl.
Mix mayonnaise,relish, and mustard into egg yolks until smooth.
Spoon egg yolk filling backinto each egg white.
Serve beets and eggs on a platter.
