Place the eggs in a pan of cold water and bring to a boil.
Once they boil,remove from the heat, cover and let stand for 10 minutes.
Drain, cool andpeel.
Place the potato in a saucepan with enough water to cover.
Bring to a boil,and cook until tender.
Drain.
Cut the eggs in half lengthwise, and remove the yolks; reserve for other usesor discard.
Place the potatoes in a medium bowl.
Mash with a whisk untilsmooth, then mix in the relish, sugar, mayonnaise and mustard.
Spoon into theegg whites, and dust with paprika.
Refrigerate or serve.
