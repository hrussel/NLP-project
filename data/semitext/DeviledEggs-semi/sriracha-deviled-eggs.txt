Place eggs in a saucepan; cover with water.
Bring to a boil, remove from heat,and let eggs stand in hot water for 15 minutes.
Remove eggs from hot water,cool under cold running water, and peel.
Cut eggs in half lengthwise; place egg yolks in a small bowl.
Mash yolks witha fork; stir mayonnaise, Sriracha sauce, dry mustard, and cayenne pepper intoyolks until smooth; season with salt.
Spoon yolk mixture into a resealableplastic bag; snip off one corner of the bag.
Place egg whites cut-side up on a serving platter.
Pipe the yolk mixture intoegg white halves; sprinkle with smoked paprika.
Refrigerate deviled eggs,covered, until ready to serve.
