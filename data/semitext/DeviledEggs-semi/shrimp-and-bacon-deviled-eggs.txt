Place eggs in a saucepan and cover with water.
Bring to a boil, remove fromheat, and let eggs stand in hot water for 15 minutes.
Remove eggs from hotwater, cool under cold running water, and peel.
Place bacon in a large skillet; cook over medium-high heat, turningoccasionally, until evenly browned, about 10 minutes.
Drain bacon on papertowels; crumble bacon when cool enough to handle.
Cut each egg in half lengthwise; place half the egg yolks in a bowl, reservingremaining yolks for another use.
Mash yolks with a fork; stir in potatoflakes, mayonnaise, chives, Dijon mustard, seafood seasoning, black pepper,and red pepper until smooth.
Stir shrimp, parsley, and cilantro into yolkmixture.
Arrange egg whites cut-side-up on a serving platter.
Spoon about 1 roundedtablespoon yolk mixture into each egg white half; sprinkle with crumbledbacon.
