Place eggs in a saucepan and cover with cold water.
Bring water to a boil andimmediately remove from heat.
Cover and let eggs stand in hot water for 10 to12 minutes.
Remove from hot water, cool and peel.
Cut the eggs in half lengthwise and carefully remove the yolks to a bowl.
Mashthe yolks with the mayonnaise and mustard and season with pepper and ranchdressing mix, blending until smooth.
Spoon the mixture into the egg whitehalves and garnish with a sprinkle of paprika.
