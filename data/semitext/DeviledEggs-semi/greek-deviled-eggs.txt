Place eggs in a saucepan; cover with water.
Bring to a boil, remove from heat,and let eggs stand in hot water for 15 minutes.
Remove eggs from hot water,cool under cold running water, and peel.
Cut each egg in half lengthwise; place egg yolks in a bowl.
Mash yolks with afork.
Stir mayonnaise, feta cheese, basil, tomato, black olives, and balsamicvinegar together in a bowl; stir in mashed yolks.
Spoon yolk mixture into aresealable plastic bag; snip off one corner of the bag.
Place egg whites cut-side up on a serving platter.
Pipe yolk mixture into eggwhites.
Season deviled eggs with black pepper; garnish with additional choppedbasil and crumbled feta cheese.
