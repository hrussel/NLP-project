Place eggs in a medium saucepan and cover with cold water.
Bring water to aboil and immediately remove from heat.
Cover and let eggs stand in hot waterfor 10 to 12 minutes.
Remove from hot water, cool, peel and cut lengthwise.
Remove yolks from eggs.
In a medium bowl, mash the yolks and mix together withmayonnaise, onion, sweet pickle relish, horseradish and mustard.
With a fork or pastry bag, fill the egg halves with the yolk mixture.
Garnishwith paprika, salt and pepper.
Chill until serving.
