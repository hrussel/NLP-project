Place eggs in a saucepan with enough water to cover.
Bring water to a boil andremove saucepan from heat.
Let eggs stand in hot water for 15 minutes.
Drain,cool, and peel.
Cut the eggs in half lengthwise and remove the yolks.
In a bowl, mash theyolks and mix with mayonnaise, paprika, mustard powder, and Old Bay seasoning.
Fold in the crabmeat.
Spoon the mixture evenly into the hollowed egg whitehalves.
Chill at least 1 hour before serving.
