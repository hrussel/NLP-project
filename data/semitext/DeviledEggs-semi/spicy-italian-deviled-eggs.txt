Place eggs in a saucepan and cover with cold water.
Bring water to a boil andimmediately remove from heat.
Cover and let eggs stand in hot water for 10 to12 minutes.
Remove from hot water, cool and peel.
Slice eggs in half, and remove the yolks.
Place the yolks into a medium bowl,and mix with the mustard, Italian dressing, mayonnaise, and pepper.
Arrangethe egg white halves on a plate, and sprinkle with salt.
Spoon the yolkmixture into the egg halves, or use a pastry bag and pipe the filling in for amore professional look.
Sprinkle with paprika, and refrigerate until serving.
