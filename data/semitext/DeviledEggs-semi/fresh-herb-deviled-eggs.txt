Muddle thyme, sage, oregano, rosemary, garlic salt, and salt together with amortar and pestle.
Pour vinegar into mortar bowl and swirl around to mix withherbs.
Scoop egg yolks from eggs and transfer to a bowl; mash egg yolks using a forkuntil texture is very fine.
Stir chives and black pepper into mashed eggyolks.
Pour vinegar-herb mixture into egg yolks and mix well.
Mix mayonnaise,sour cream, mustard, and sucralose sweetener into egg yolk mixture untilsmooth.
Spoon egg yolk mixture into egg whites using a teaspoon.
