Place egg in a saucepan and cover with cold water.
Bring water to a boil andimmediately remove from heat.
Cover and let eggs stand in hot water for 10 to12 minutes.
Remove from hot water, cool, and peel.
Cut eggs in half lengthwise, place the yolks into a mixing bowl, and set thewhites aside.
Mash the yolks with the deviled ham, mustard, mayonnaise, andblack pepper.
Spoon or pipe filling into egg white halves.
Garnish eachdeviled egg with an olive slice, then sprinkle with paprika.
