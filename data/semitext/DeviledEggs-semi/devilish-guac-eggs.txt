Cut peeled eggs in half horizontally and scoop egg yolks into a bowl.
Scoopavocado from peels and mash the flesh into egg yolks.
Stir onion and garlicinto yolk mixture; add lime juice, mayonnaise, and salt.
Stir until creamy.
Spoon about 1 tablespoon avocado filling into hollow of each egg white half.
Place eggs onto a serving platter and sprinkle each deviled egg with a pinchof chili powder.
Sprinkle Cheddar cheese over each appetizer and top each with2 slices of olive.
Cover and chill for 1 hour before serving.
